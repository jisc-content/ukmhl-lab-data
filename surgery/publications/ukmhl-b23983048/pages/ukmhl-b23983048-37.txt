AN HISTOHIALL EXPOSTULATION: Against the beastlye Abusers, bothe of Chyrurgerie, and Physyke, in oure tyme: with a goodlye Doctrine and Instruction, necessarye to be marked and folowed, of all true Chirurgiens: Gathered and diligently set forth by JOHN HALLE, Chyrurgyen. Imprinted at London in Flete Streate, nyghe unto Saint Dun- stones Churche, by Thomas Marshe. An. 1565.
