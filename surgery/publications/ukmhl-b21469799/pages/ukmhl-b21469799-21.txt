To Ambrose Pare we are indebted for a better theory, and a better mode of treating such wounds. I beg leave, Gentlemen, to call your atten- tion to this great man, in whose professional career, we shall find a circumstance which connects him in a particular manner with the late Mr. Hunter. The physiological and philosophical principles of Pare, are nearly those of Galen and of the Arabian Physicians. Vesalius was his master in Anatomy.
