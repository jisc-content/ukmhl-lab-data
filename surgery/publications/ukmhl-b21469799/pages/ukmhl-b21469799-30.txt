Surgery does not appear to have kept equal pace with it. From the time of Am- brose Pare, nearly a century elapsed before Wiseman made his appearance. He is one of the first surgeons in England who has produced any thing like a connected treatise on Surgery. He improved the treat- ment of wounds, and simplified their manage- ment, by his attention to the saving of skin.
