To Mr. Hunter the world is indebted for his operation for the popliteal aneurism, an operation founded entirely on his anatomical and physiological knowledge. He had in the early part of his life assisted his brother. Dr. William Hunter, in his dissections in pro- secution of his discoveries in the lymphatic system, and had conceived ideas of the powers of that system, greater than had yet been contemplated.
