96 Intestinal Surgery- Ill. The Treatment of Artificial Anus or Faecal Fistula The difference between these two terms is rather vague. The term ‘ faecal fistula ’ is sometimes applied to any communication between the small intestine and the abdominal wall, and the term ‘ artificial anus ’ con- fined to a similar condition of the large bowel. There is a considerable amount of difference in treatment of an artificial anus in the small and large intestines.
