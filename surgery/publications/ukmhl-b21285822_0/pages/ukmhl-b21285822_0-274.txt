Dr. Sharpey, Dr. Sprott Boyd, Dr. Todd, Mr. Nasmyth, Dr. Barry, and Mr. Toynbee are all distinguished in this field of research."" In the present article I have endeavoured to combine with all the authentic information I could obtain from these sources, the results to which I have been brought by a two years' study of the anatomical characters of the whole mucous system. So rapid, however, are the daily advances of knowledge, that it is possible much has been omitted which is already in some shape before the public, and on the other hand, that a few years may greatly modify the general views that are here set forth. As the anatomical details, however, have been all substantiated by my own observations, except where otherwise stated, I am enabled to speak with more con- fidence of their correctness. BIBLIOGRAPHY.
