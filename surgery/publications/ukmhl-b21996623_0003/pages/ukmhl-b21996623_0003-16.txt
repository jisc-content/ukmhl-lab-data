DEDICATION. X is peculiarly directed to the service of the army, I am led by my situation, my duty, and my feelinys, to address it, with all humility, to Your Majesty. That Your Majesty may long live to enjoy the love and esteem of a happy people, is the fervent wish of Your Majesty's Most faithful subject, And most dutiful Servant, JOHN HUNTER. Leicester Square, May 20, 1793.
