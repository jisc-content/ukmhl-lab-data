If, on the other hand, there is evidence that the bullet has carried clothing in with it, or if it is producing symptoms of pressure or inflammation, the proper course would be to remove it if possible. When no operation is advisable, or when the injury to the cord is irremediable, the treatment will be similar to that described under fracture-dislocation of the spine.
