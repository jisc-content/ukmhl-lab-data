When removal is undertaken, special pains must be taken not to open the rectum, which may very readily happen, owing to the connection of the tumour with that structure. It must also be borne in mind that there may be a free communication between the tumour and the spinal canal, and consequently cerebro-spinal fluid may escape during the operation.
