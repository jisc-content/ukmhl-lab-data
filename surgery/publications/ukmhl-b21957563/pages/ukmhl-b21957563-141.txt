The opening in the linea alba is then closed with a few stitches, the line of sutures being buried by a second row which unites the aponeurosis over it. This second row must be continued at least half an inch above and continued for half an inch below the first row of stitches ; and sometimes it must be continued from close to the ensiform cartilage to the umbilicus.
