Great care must be taken in the disinfection oi the instruments in these, cases, as once a cystitis is set up it is likely to lead to fatal pyelitis or pyelonephritis. The catheter should be passed regularly at least once a day or oftener in bad cases, so as to make sure that there is no accumulation of urine. If the urine becomes foul, the bladder must be washed out two or three times daily, and urinary antiseptics administered (see p. 446).
