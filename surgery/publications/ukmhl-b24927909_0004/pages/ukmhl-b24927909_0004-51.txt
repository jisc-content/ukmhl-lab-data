As I have preserved no notes, and do not perfectly recollect any case of a tumour of this structure occurring in a distinct form, unless some of those about the breast may be so considered; and as I wish to show that all these diseases occur distinctly as well as in glands, I shall, as an instance of a pan- creatic appearance in a distinct tumour, refer the reader to the curious Case published in London by Dr. Bouttatz of Moscow, of a tumour which grew beneath the conjunctiva of the eye, and protruded it between the eyelids.
