Seeing that the really painful part of the operation is the section of the optic and ciliary nerves, it is quite possible to perform this step under chlorid of ethyl or even nitrous oxid, having executed all those preceding under cocain. For subjects who are unfit for ether or chloroform this method is highly to be recommended. Indeed, as regards the youngest class of sub- jects, it is practicable to make the entire operation under the ethyl chlorid. Highly inflamed and sensitive eyes do not admit of using local anesthesia at any stage of the procedure.
