Then to try and examine the patient when she is off her guard. Much care and experience are required in diagnosing such cases. We should never be in too great a hurry to operate, when there is any doubt as to the real nature of the disease. As regards the treatment of hysterical disease of joints, the warm or cold douche is very beneficial, and internally, chalybeate tonics are the best medicines.
