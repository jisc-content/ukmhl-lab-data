TO SIR JOSEPH BANKS, Bart., PRESIDENT OF THE ROYAL SOCIETY, &c. &c. &c. Dear Sir, As the following Observations were made in the course of those pursuits in which you have so warmly interested yourself, and promoted with the most friendly assistance, I should he wanting in gratitude were I not to address them to you, as a public testimony of the friendship and esteem ivith which I am, Dear Sir, Your obliged and Very humble Servant, JOHN HUNTER. Leicester Square, Nov. 9,1786.
