467 HERNIA TESTIS. An elliptical incision is then made to embrace the cicatricial opening, which is dissected out right down to the herniated mass, after which the testicle is freed from the dartos and it and the hernia are reduced and the incision in the scrotum is sewn up. A small drainage tube should be put into the lower part of the wound for 48 hours in case it has not been possible to render the mass aseptic. Anti-syphilitic remedies should be persevered with. The hernia only requires to be shaved off when it is so large that it cannot be reduced in this manner.
