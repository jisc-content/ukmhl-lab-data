The skin is scrupulously purified, and the front part of the foot should be wrapped up in gauze soaking in a 1-2000 sublimate solution, so that the hands of the assistant holding it will not be infected and therefore cannot endanger the wound. This precaution should be adopted for all amputations about the foot. The incision should commence about a finger's-breadth below the tip of the external malleolus. From that point it extends forwards parallel to the outer border of the foot as far as the level- of the tubercle of the fifth metatarsal bone.
