When patients have had these com- plaints for a length of time, we frequently find benefit pro- duced by removal to the sea-side, and employing a course of sea-bathing; but after all, this healthy state, when it is pro- duced, is not so much from the means just mentioned as from the complaint ultimately wearing itself out.
