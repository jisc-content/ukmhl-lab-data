Sir Astley is now about to resign his situation as Surgical Lec- turer, which he has held with so much honour to himself, and advantage to those who have received instruction from him. Confident, therefore, that a correct and authentic copy of his Lectures will be acceptable to the profession, and having, as his apprentice, and subse- quently, had the best opportunities of becoming acquainted with his profes- sional opinions, I have undertaken the publication of them.
