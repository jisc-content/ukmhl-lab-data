DEDICATION TO THE FIRST EDITION OF THE TREATISE ON STRICTURE OF THE URETHRA. TO JOHN ABERNETHY, ESQ. F.R.S. Surgeon to ST. BARTHOLOMEW'S HOSPITAL, &c. &c. &c.
