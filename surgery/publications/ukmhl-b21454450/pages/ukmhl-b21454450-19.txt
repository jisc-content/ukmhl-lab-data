PREFACE TO THE PRESENT VOLUME. The natural anxiety felt by every writer to engage the attention or conciliate the appro- bation of his readers will, I trust, be considered a sufficient apology for the observations with which I am desirous of introducing the present Volume. The favourable reception given by the Profession to the First Edition of the Treatise on Stricture of the Urethra, has rendered me satisfied with the principles on which it was written. I have, nevertheless, subjected it to a careful revision, and as I have not been able to make many useful additions, I must rest content in having found nothing which I have any wish to expunge.
