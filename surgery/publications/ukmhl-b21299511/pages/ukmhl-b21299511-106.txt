Various accidental cir- cumstances may derange the instrument, and a portion of intestine, or more partici>4arly of omentum, may slip out under the pad. For this reason bodily exertion should be avoided as much as possible; and the patient, when obliged to make any considerable effort, should press on the pad with his hand. If a protrusion should occur, let him immediately take off the truss, lie down, and either return the part himself, or send for his surgical attendant.
