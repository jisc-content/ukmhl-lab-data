CHAP. V. OF FISTULA ANI. By a fistula is meant a narrow channel or sinus, secreting purulent or serous dis- charge, and having an external opening near the anus through which the matter has vent. The opening of a fistula is often extremely small, so that there may be difficulty in finding it. The channel it- self, or fistula, is usually a little larger than a common probe: it is sometimes straight, sometimes crooked : its length may vary from half an inch to several inches.
