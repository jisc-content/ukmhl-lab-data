OBSERVATIONS ON INJURIES AND DISEASES OF THE RECTUM. CHAPTER I. OF LACERATION OF THE RECTUM. The simplest affection of the rectum is its partial or complete rupture. The bowel may be torn through by violence applied either from within or from without. Partial laceration of the bowel is the consequence of force applied to its inner surface.
