Pain in the Rectum. 57 teeth and in the rectum was not constant, but would come on frequently during the day, without any assignable cause. It had lasted a year and a half, during which he had remained perfectly free from symptoms of lues. This patient, who was otherwise in good health, suffered his mind to be greatly distressed by the continuance of the neuralgia. He was anxious to try every plan which held out the least promise of benefiting him. But of all the remedies which he tried, he appeared to experience relief from one only, which was a course of sarsaparilla.
