THE SAPHENOUS OPENING 104 an oval-shaped aperture, of considerable size, is observed in the fascia lata, immediately below and a little external to the root of the tuberosity of the os pubis ; the edge of which is sharp, and very clearly defined upon the external and inferior part of its circumference. This opening has received the appellation of the saphenous aperture of the fascia lata,'f* from the circum- stance of the great saphena vein entering by it to join with the trunk of the common femoral vein.
