The more extensive of the rules of therapeutics are here pretty plainly indicated, but none of them have appeared to me to be comparable in scope with the few physiological and pathological laws which I have endeavoured to explain. The merit of originality is perhaps never rightly estimated, and the praise of it is little worth seeking. There may be abundant authority for the statements, and even example for the plan here pursued. I do not know, however, where they are to be found; and yet I can almost hope that some may follow these opinions to the end without dissent, for I am not conscious of Avandering far from a clear and practicable track.
