190 Fistula in Ano. I am induced to offer some observa- tions on fistula in ano, from a convic- tion, that many surgeons have failed in curing the disease rather from neglecting constitutional means, than from an im- perfect operation, or subsequent inat- tention to the mode of dressing the wound. Fistula in ano is an abscess taking place in the cellular membrane, about the anus ; and lias been generally divided into simple and complicated. The first species is that in which matter becomes collected in the neighbourhood of the rectum and anus, and is discharged by one or more openings, having no communication with the gut.
