But even if cancer cannot be cured by the knife, there can be no question that life may be prolonged and the health improved by an operation; and in addition to this the patient may die from the implication of internal organs, and therefore his death is easier and entails less suffering than if an external cancer has been left to ulcerate, fungate, and bleed, and cause intolerable pain and suffering.
