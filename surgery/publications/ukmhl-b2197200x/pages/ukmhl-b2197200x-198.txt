156 EXTRACTS FROM OFFICIAL REPORTS. TALLAHASSEE, FEOKIDA. Extract from Weekly Report of Cortipanies " G" and "K,"7th U. S. Infantry, Tallahassee, Florida. Actinf/ Assistant Surgeon T. Artaud. October 5, 1867. Private C. Brady, Company "K," 7th U. S. Infantry, died October 1st, 1867, of well characterized yellow fever; a sporadic case brought from Madison, Florida. I will remark that no yellow fever was existing at that place, nor is there any existing in this place. 0
