The first concerns the bringing of an ulcer into this state ; the second, the management of it when there ; and it is plain that the second must largely depend upon the first, and that certainly one part of the treatment of a healing sore must consist in obviating the tendency to departure from it. Now the bringing of an ulcer into a healthy state necessaril}' involves the counteraction to its causes.
