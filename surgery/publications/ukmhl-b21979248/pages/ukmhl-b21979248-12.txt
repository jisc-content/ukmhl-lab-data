PREFACE TO THE FOURTH EDITION. iv Under the heading " Anaesthetics " the conclusions arrived at by the second Hyderabad Chloroform Commission have been given, together with some views which present a different conclusion. The rules for the examination of sight for candidates for the Govern- ment Services have been given in full. Lastly, as a help to Officers preparing on short notice for active service, the regulation '' Kit" is detailed in Appendix V. In conclusion, the writer begs sincerely to thank his friends and colleagues for their friendly criticisms of the Third Edition, which have been of much assistance to him in the preparation of the Fourth. C. H. Y. GODWIN. Oakhurst, Netley, Arn-il, 1891.
