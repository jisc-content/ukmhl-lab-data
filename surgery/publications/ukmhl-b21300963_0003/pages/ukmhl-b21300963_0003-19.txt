He caused the sentiment of the philanthropic Chremes, in the Heautontimorumenos of Terence, to be inscribed on the walls of the hospital-sur- gery, that students should have constantly before them an admonition to humanity, drawn from a reflection on their own wants : Homo sum; humani nihil a me alienum puto. I could with pleasure enlarge on this theme, but I check myself, because 1 am aware that what I am now saying may rather annoy than gratify the feelings of my preceptor.
