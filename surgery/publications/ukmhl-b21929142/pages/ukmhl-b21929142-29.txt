Note.—I am not yet phrenologist enough to determine whether the science of phrenology is to be at all a help to us in this matter. The advocates for the science say it will; but I am not sure that all they assert is correct, being a little sceptical as to the correctness and perfectability of their system. Time, however, which makes us better acquainted with all things, will show its truth or fallacy.
