They should have some kind of knee-cap, with a little pad made to fit against the cartilage, because where the carti- lage has once slipped out it is apt to slip out again, and by a support of this kind it is possible to prevent it. In cases in which the cartilage is dislocated and fixed, or when the dislocation frequently recurs, an operation for removal of the carti- lage by an incision into the joint under strict antiseptic precautions ofters the most satis- factory method of treatment.
