Necessarily, these cliemiotactic and phagocytic powers do not explain all the phenomena seen in inflammation, but they surely form an im- portant part of such explanation, and further investigation may very probably make clear some of the points which are still obscure. At all events, no statement of inflammation is complete without a sum- mary of the knowledge thus far obtained in this direction.
