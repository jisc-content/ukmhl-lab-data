PREFACE. Jt was my original intention to have il- lustrated some of the following pages, with engravings from drawings of many valuable preparations collected by myself and my friends; but, after considerable progress had been made, I found that the expence would have seriously exceeded what I either wished or proposed, when I first an- nounced my design of appearing before the public. Since that period also, a number of valuable cases have been sent to me, which have considerably augmented my materials. I therefore thought it much bet- ter, to supply the place of engravings, by letter-press description, than farther to en- hance the price of a book, the object of which has been neither the acquisition of profit, nor the gratification of vanity.
