83 BAKED CABBAGE. Ingredients Callage. Salt. 1 oz. Butter. Pepper. Boil a cabbage in water with a lump of salt and a small piece of soda. When cooked, drain in a colander till quite free from water. Chop up finely, season with pepper and salt. Mix in an ounce of butter and bake for an hour in the oven. CABBAGE AND MILK. Ingredients Callage. Milk. Flour. Pepper. Salt. Butter. This is another way to cook cabbage. Cut the cabbage up small, boil in milk thirty minutes, then thicken the milk with a little flour, add pepper, salt and a piece of butter. TOMATO PUDDING. Ingredients Tomatoes. Potatoes. Pepper. 1 Egg. Salt. Bread Grumls. Butter a pie dish well. At the bottom of the dish put a layer of sliced tomato, then a layer of mashed potatoes, and so on till the dish is full. Season with pepper and salt, pour over a beaten egg and sprinkle the top with bread crumbs. Bake brown.
