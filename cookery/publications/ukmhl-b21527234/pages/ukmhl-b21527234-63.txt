59 HARICOT BEAN SOUP. Ingredients 1 pint Haricot Beans. 1 Onion. 3 Gloves. 1 Carrot. Powdered Sugar. 1 oz. Butter. Put the white haricots in cold water overnight and put a small piece of soda in the water. Next morn- ing put them in a saucepan with three pints of water, an onion stuck with three cloves, a carrot, and salt. When the beans are quite tender pound them and rub the whole through a wire sieve, first removing the onion and carrot. Add a little powdered sugar and the butter just before serving. Some cooks put a little rice in this soup. Slice the parsnips and put into one quart of boiling stock, with an onion and a few Bticks of celery. Simmer until quite tender, about an hour. Then rub the whole through a wire sieve, season with pepper and salt, and, if possible, add a little cream. Boil up again and serve with fried crofitons of bread. If this soup be found too sweet, add a little vinegar. But this is entirely a matter of taste. PARSNIP SOUP. Ingredients 1 quart Stock. Pepper. 1^ lbs. of Parsnips. Salt.
