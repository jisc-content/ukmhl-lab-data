so STUFFED POTATOES. Ingredients 6 Potatoes. Sausage Meat. Salt. Butter. Take six large potatoes. Peel and wash. Cut off the small end of each potatoe so they may stand upright. Then take a potato scoop and empty out a large hole in the inside of the potatoes. Fill this hole up with sausage meat. Lay the potatoes in a tin baking dish, sprinkle with salt, and moisten with butter. Bake in a good oven till a nice brown, taking care to keep the potatoes well basted.
