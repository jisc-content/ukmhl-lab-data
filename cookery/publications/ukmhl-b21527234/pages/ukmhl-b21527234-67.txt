Ingredients :â€” 2 lbs. Turbot. 1 pint Milk. Thyme. Parsley. Bay Leaves. 2 ozs. Butter. 1 oz. Flour. 1 Egg. Pepper. Salt. Bread Crumbs. Potatoes. Boil the turbot; pick all the flesh away from the bones. It is always easier to bone fish while quite hot than afterwards. Make a sauce with a pint of milk, a little thyme, parsley and two bay leaves. Let it simmer for twenty minutes. Rub two ounces of butter into an ounce of flour. Stir gradually into the sauce until it thickens, then add the yolk of an egg, pepper and salt. Line the bottom of a pudding dish with this Bauce ; put on this a layer of fish, and so on alternately till the dish is full.v Sprinkle the last row with bread crumbs and some pieces of butter, and round the edges of the dish put a border of mashed potatoes. Bake in the oven till brown. If liked, a little grated cheese may be used in this dish.
