39 SAVOURIES 69. Cheese and Potato Pudding. German Dish. Ingredients. Three ounces of Parmesan Cheese. The whites of six Eggs Six ounces of Potato. Half a pint of Cream. Three ounces of Butter. Pepper. The potatoes should be prepared as potato snow Melt the butter slightly and beat it to a cream, then mix it well with the potatoes. Make the cream hot and pour it over the Parmesan, but do not stir it. When it is nearly cold mix it with the potatoes and the well-beaten whites of eggs ; add pepper to taste. Put the mixture into a well-buttered mould and bake in a moderate oven. Turn out, and serve very hot. 70. Cheese Pudding (No. i). Ingredients. Six ounces of grated Cheese. Half a pint of Milk. Three-quarters of a pound of Two ounces of Butter. Bread-crumbs. The whites of four Eggs. Pepper. Melt the butter in the milk, then pour it over the bread-crumbs and the cheese. Let these cook for twenty minutes, then add a dust of pepper. Beat .he whites of eggs to a stiff froth, and add them to the other ingredients, being careful to mix all well together. Butter a baking dish and pour the mixture into it, and bake in a quick oven for about tluee-quarters of an hour.
