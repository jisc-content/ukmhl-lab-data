Mix thoroughly and pour into a well-greased mould and steam for three hours. Turn out carefully, and serve with jam or sauce. 226. Candied Fruit Pudding. Ingredients. Quarter of a pound of Two ounces of Butter. candied Fruit. Two ounces of Flour. Quarter of a pound of Two whites of Eggs. Bread-crumbs. Half a pint of Milk. Any kind of candied fruit that is getting a little damp to use at dessert will do for this pudding; if it is large it should be shredded, but if cherries are used they may just be cut in halves. Mix the flour with the bread-crumbs and the candied fruit. Make the milk hot and melt the butter in it, and pour it over the bread-crumbs, &c. Now beat the eggs to a stiff froth and mix it well with the other ingredients. Have ready a buttered mould and pour in the mixture, and steam for three hours. One of the wine sauces, or plain sweet white sauce, may be served with this pudding. 227. Carrot Pudding (No. i). Ingredients. Twelve ounces of Carrots. One and a half ounces Sugar.
