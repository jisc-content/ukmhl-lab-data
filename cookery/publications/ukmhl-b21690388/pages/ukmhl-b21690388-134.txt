THE APSLEY COOKERY BOOK 9fi 185. Potatoes (Saute). Ingredients. Two pounds of cold Potatoes. Teaspoonful of chopped Parsley Three ounces of Butter. Pepper to taste. This is a good way to use up cold potatoes. Cut the potatoes into slices about a quarter of an inch thick. Put them into a saucepan with the butter, cover them, and allow them to cook for twenty minutes. Add the parsley, and pepper last thing, and serve very hot. 186. Potato Snow. Ingredients. One pound of Potatoes. Boil the potatoes according to recipe No. 175 to ensure their being quite dry. Put them through the potato masher (see "Hints on Cooking"), and let them drop lightly into the vegetable dish, which should be hot. They should not be touched after they are in the dish or their appearance will be spoiled. 187. Savoy Cabbage (Stewed). Ingredients. A medium sized Savoy Cabbage. | Half a pint of Brown Gravy. Pepper. Put a Savoy cabbage in plenty of salted boiling water and boil until tender. Drain it well and chop it. Put it in a stewpan with the gravy and a seasoning of pepper.
