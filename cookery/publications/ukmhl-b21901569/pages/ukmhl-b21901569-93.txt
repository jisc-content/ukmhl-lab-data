79 Vegetable Salad. by morsels of liaddock, sardine, &c. Well known and appreciated as the Snlade de legume's* always is at a Paris restaurant, most persons here would prefer the vegetables simply sliced, so as to preserve some of their natural form and texture, rather than cut into innumerable small cubes, as usually pre- sented. In all cases, such salads should be kept very cool, and be dressed immediately before serving in warm weather. * For receipt, see Appendix.
