No. 77. Lobster Butter. Procure a full-sized hen lobster, and quite full of spawn, , which take out and pound weU in a mortar; then add a quarter of a pound of fresh butter, mix them well together, then rub it through a hair-sieve, and put it on a plate upon the ice or in a cold place untU wanted. No. 78. Anchovy Butter. Take the bones from ten anchovies, wash the fillets; dry them upon a cloth, and pound them well in a mortar; then add a quarter of a pound of fresh butter; mix well together, and proceed as in the last.
