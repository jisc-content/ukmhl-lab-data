THE AETILOR’s DIRECTIONS FOR MAKING GOOD BEEF-TEA. Chop fine or mince with a machine, a pound and a half of gravy beef from which the fat has been removed. Place it in an earthen jar with a lid, add a pint of cold water, and let it remain an hour, occasionally stirring and pressing the meat firmly with an iron spoon. Then place the jar, with the lid on, in a sauce- pan large enough to cover it, with water sufficient to reach nearly to the lid, but not near enough when gently boiling to rise above it and enter the jar.
