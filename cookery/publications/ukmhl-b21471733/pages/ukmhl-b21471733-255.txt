Let the scorzonero be well washed, then boil them sufficiently to enable you to wipe off the skin, cut them of an equal length, and stew them tender in a little broth, with lemon juice; when they are done add two spoonsful of sauce tournee (No. 6); give them a boil, then dish the scorzonero, skim the sauce, and pour it over them. 407—Salsijis, or Scorzonero, fried- Boil the salsifis or scorzonero until it is quite tender, wipe off the skins, and put them into a marinade of oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper, in which they are to remain for a few hours ; then drain them on a cloth, dip them in batter (No. 411), fry them in clean lard, and serve them on a napkin.
