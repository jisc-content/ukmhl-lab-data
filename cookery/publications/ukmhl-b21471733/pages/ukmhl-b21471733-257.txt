Boil the beans and peel off the skin, and put them into some sauce tournee (No. 6), with a bunch of green onions and parsley, and a small lump of sugar; boil them for a quarter of an hour in the sauce, then take out the herbs, and thicken the sauce with two yolks of eggs beat up with cream. FEVES A LA MAITRE D'HOTEL. White Beans, d la Maitre d'Hotel. Boil and peel the beans as above, take some bechamel sauce, reduce it over the fire, then mix in it some chopped parsley and lemon juice; throw the beans into the hot sauce, give them a turn over the fire, and dish them immediately.
