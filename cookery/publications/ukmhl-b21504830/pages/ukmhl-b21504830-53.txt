53 SOUPS, BROTHS, PUREES. Rabbit Broth. Ingredients. 3 quarts of water. 1 rabbit .... 1 onion ) Half a turnip / Half a pound beef-steak 1 bay leaf . . \ A bunch of parsley ( Salt . . . | 8 pepper-corns . ) Directions. 1. Put 3 quarts of cold spring water in a saucepan. 2. Take the rabbit, wash it well, joint it and put it in the saucepan. 3. Cut the beef-steak into small pieces and put into the pan also. 4. Bring slowly to the boil and add the salt and peppercorns just before the liquor boils. 5. Skim the broth well. 6. Add the vegetables and herbs when the broth boils. 7. Allow to simmer for three-and-half hours. 8. Strain and put into a cool place to get cold, then skim off the fat. 9.
