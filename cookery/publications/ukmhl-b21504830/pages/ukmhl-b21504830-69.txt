69 SOUPS, BROTHS, PUREES. Directions. 1. Wash and peel the potatoes and onions. 2. Put them in a stew-pan with the butter for five minutes. 3. Pour the stock on to the vegetables, add season- ing and boil until perfectly soft. 4. Rub through a hair sieve, add the cream which has been previously boiled or the milk. 5. Return the stock in the saucepan, stir smoothly till quite hot and serve with fried dice of bread handed with it. Palestine. Ingredients.
