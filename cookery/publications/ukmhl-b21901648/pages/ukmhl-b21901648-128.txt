Put half an ounce of cream of tartar, the juice of one lemon, and one grain of saccharin into a jug, and pour over a quart of boiling water. Cover till cold. Egg and Chablis. 368. Beat up with a fork an egg till it froths; add a very small quantity of saccharin, and two tablespoonfuls of water; mix well, pour in a wineglassful of chablis, and serve before it gets flat.
