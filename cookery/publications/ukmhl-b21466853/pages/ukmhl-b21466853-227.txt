A CHARLOTTE RUSSE. A SPANISH CUSTARD. Stir till quite smooth three table-spoonfuls of arrow root, with a little cold milk, and pour it into an English quart of boiling milk, stirring it; sweeten to taste, and add half an ounce of dissolved isinglass, the yolks of two well beaten eggs, and a seasoning of bitter almonds; pour into a wetted mould, and when cold turn it out, and ornament it with sliced blanched almonds.
