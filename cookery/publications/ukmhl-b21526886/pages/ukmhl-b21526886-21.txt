This, when intended to be introduced upon the breakfast-table, should, previously to boiling, be care- fully boned, and skewered together in as nice a form as possible, and when cold, arranged in a white cloth. If served in slices, it should not be cut quite so thin as ham would be. Vide Vinaigrette, Bemolade, Persillade, Cold Beef, Potted, etc. Some people make bubble-and-squeak with slices of cold boiled beef, fried with cold cabbage, but it is an inelegant and not over-wholesome dish. COLD GAME OK POULTRY. When either poultry or game is dressed expressly to appear at table cold, particular care should be taken to make it look as well as possible.
