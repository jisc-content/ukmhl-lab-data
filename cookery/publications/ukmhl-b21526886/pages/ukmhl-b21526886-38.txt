A very small quantity of cold meat goes a great way when scalloped, bread-crumbs, chopped egg, mashed potatoes, or forcemeat being added to fill up, Bake, broil, or do them in a Dutch oven before the fire, and serve in the shells. SMOKED SALMON'. Dried salmon needs very little cooking. It may be sliced and broiled in oiled paper cases, or tossed in butter or olive oil, and served with lemon juice; or throw it into boiling water, and at the end of a few minutes take it up, and when cold arrange it as a salad, or with a rcmolade or ravigote sauce.
