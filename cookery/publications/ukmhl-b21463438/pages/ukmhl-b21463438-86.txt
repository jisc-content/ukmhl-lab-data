Out of stale bread stamp half a dozen two and a quarter inch rounds a quarter-inch thick ; fry them a golden yellow in butter, drain, and canafes. dry them. When quite cold butter them and spread a layer of fresh caviar over them, mask the surfaces of the canapes completely with mayonnaise sauce as cold as possible, and serve on a flat dish garnished with watercress. To prepare the caviar, a few drops of lemon-juice and a sprink- ling of Nepaul pepper are alone necessary.
