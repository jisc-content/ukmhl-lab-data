FIFTY LUNCHES. 122 Put into the syrup of the pineapple three tablespoon- fuls of apricot jam, a liqueur-glass of rum, the juice of a lemon and a teaspoonful of cornflour ; boil up, strain, and cool; when cold pour over the pineapple slices, and cover the surface with whipped cream, with which an ounce of pounded pistachio nuts may be blended.
