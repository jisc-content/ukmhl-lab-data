66 FIFTY LUNCHES. into the custard, fill the moulds three-quarters full, steam gently till set, and when cold turn them out. Arrange them neatly on a flat dish, garnish with broken aspic and garden cress, and serve with a sauce mayonnaise made without mustard, plain red wine vinegar being used instead of tarragon, so as not to interfere with the flavour of the asparagus.
