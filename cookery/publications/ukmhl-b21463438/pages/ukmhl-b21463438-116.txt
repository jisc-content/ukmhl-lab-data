Meanwhile reduce the syrup over the fire, adding two tablespoonfuls of apricot jam to it; when like honey strain it, and when cold begin to baste the figs with it. The cold will cause the glaze to mask the fruit, and when that has been done let the figs remain over ice till wanted, then serve with whipped cream in a separate dish. This may be flavoured with any liqueur, with brandy or with rum.
