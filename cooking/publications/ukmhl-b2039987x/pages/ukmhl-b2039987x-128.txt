124 Bakewell Tart. Eg-g- I. Bread crumbs 2 oz. Castor sugar 2 oz. Butter 2 oz. Raspberry jam. Almond flavouring. Pastry | lb. Line a tart dish with pastry, place a layer of jam over the bottom, cream the butter, mix in the castor sugar and bread crumbs, moistening with the egg beaten separately, add flavouring. Spread this mixture over the jam and bake in quick oven about half an hour.
