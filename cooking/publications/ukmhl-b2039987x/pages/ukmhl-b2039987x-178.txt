Pour on the milk, which must be almost boiling, and while doing so whisk the sauce quickly till it froths. It must be served immediately. Lemon Sauce. Lump sugar ... i oz. Lemon i Water (boiling) ... rather more than half-pint. Water (cold) ... 2 tablespoonfuls. Arrowroot a small dessertspoonful. Rub the sugar on the lemon rind, till all the outer rind is removed. Put it into the boiling water. Mix the arrowroot with the cold water, pour in the boiling water, return to the saucepan and boil for one minute, add the lemon juice. If the lemon is a very large one, more sugar will be required.
