20I Coffee Icing- May be made in the same manner, using one table- spoonful of coffee essence, and one tablespoonful of water. Other icings may be made in the same way, using noyeau, maraschino, or other liquor instead of lemon juice. Chocolate Icing Is made by adding two tablespoonfuls of grated choco- late to half a pound of icing sugar, and sufficient water to melt it. Finish as for Lemon Water Icing.
