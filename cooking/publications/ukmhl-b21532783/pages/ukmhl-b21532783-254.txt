Potatoe Cheesecakes. Boil six ounces of potatoes, and four ounces of lemon-peel; beat the latter in a marble mortar, with four ounces of sugar; then add the potatoes beaten, and four ounces of butter melted in a little cream. When well mixed let it stand to grow cold. Put crust in pattypans, and rather more than half fill them. Bake in a quick oven half an hour; sifting some double-refined sugar on them when going to the oven. This quantity will make a dozen. Almond Cheesecakes.
