Boil one pint of cream, half a pint of milk, with mace, cinnamon, and lemon-peel, a little of each. When cold, mix the yolks of three eggs ; sweeten, and make your cups or paste nearly full. Bake them ten minutes. Lemon Custard. Beat the yolks of eight eggs till they are as white as milk: then put to them a pint of boiling water* the rinds of two lemons grated, and the juice sweet- ened to your taste.
