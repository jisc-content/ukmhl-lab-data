The chine and rib-bones should be wiped every day ; and the bloody part of the neck be cut ofl', to preserve it. The brisket changes first in the breast; and if it is to be kept, it is best to rub it with a little salt, should the weather be hot. Every kernel should be taken out of all sorts of meat as soon as brought in : then wipe dry.
