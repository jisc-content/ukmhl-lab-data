Pare thin two Seville or China oranges, boil the peel tender and shred it fine; pare and core twenty apples, put them in a stew-pan, and as little water as possible; when half-done, add half a pound of sugar, the orange-peel and juice,; boil it pretty thick. When cold, put it in a shallow dish, or pattypans lined with paste, to turn out, end be eaten cold. Prune Tart.
