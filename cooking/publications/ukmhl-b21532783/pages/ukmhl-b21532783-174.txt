Macaroni Soup. Boil a pound of the best macaroni in a quart o^ good stock till quite tender; then take out half, and put it into another stew-pot. To the remainder add some more stock, and boil it till you can pulp all the macaroni through a line sieve. Then add to- gether that, the two liquors, a pint or more of cream boiling hot, the macaroni that was first taken out, and half a pound of grated Parmesan cheese ; make it hot, but do not letit boil.
