Prices for such vary from 2 s. 6 d. to 7 r. 6 d. per head, according to the number of guests and the style of catering required. The quantity and quality of provisions supplied must, oj course, be regulated according to the price paid per head. This generally includes the hire of all plate, glass, linen, china, cutlery, and table plant. Table decorations and attendance are, as a rule, charged extra, unless the number of guests is large enough to enable the Caterer to provide the whole at an inclusive price.
