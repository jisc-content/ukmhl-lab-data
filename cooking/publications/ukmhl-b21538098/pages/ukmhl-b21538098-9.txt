PREFACE. CONSIDERABLE interest attaches to the collection of recipes which we now present to the reader. The Plasmon Exhibit, consisting of a large variety of Plasmon dishes, Plasmon pastry, cakes and bread, shown at the Food and Cookery Exhibition, held at the Royal Albert Hall in April, 1903, was awarded the highest certificate of merit.
