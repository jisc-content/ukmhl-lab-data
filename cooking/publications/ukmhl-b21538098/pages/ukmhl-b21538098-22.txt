LENTIL SOUP.—Ingredients: i pint lentils; i small onion ; x stick of celery; 2 oz. blitter; 1 gill Plasmon stock (recipe No. 2) ; 2 quarts water; salt and pepper to taste. Method.—Wash the lentils, and soak them in cold water for 3 or 4 hours. Put the butter in a saucepan ; add the lentils, onions (peeled and finely sliced), celery (cleaned and chopped small), stir over the fire for about 5 minutes, then add the water, boil up, skim, and cook gently till the lentils are soft. Pass all through a sieve; add Plasmon, salt, and pepper; re-heat, and serve with bread cut in dice. Note.—Peas, haricots, beans, etc., can be used in the same way as lentils. 5.
