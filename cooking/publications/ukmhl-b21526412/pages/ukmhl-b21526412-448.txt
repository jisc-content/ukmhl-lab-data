GRAVIES AND SAUCES. 438 Egg Balls.—(No. 3S1.) Boil four Eggs for ten minutes, and put them into cold water, put the yolks into a mortar with the yolk of a raw egg, a teaspoonful of flour, same of chopped parsley, as much salt as will lay on a shilling, and a little pepper, rub well together, roll them into small balls, (as they swell in boiling,) and boil them a couple of minutes.
