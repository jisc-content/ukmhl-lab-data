Obs.—This will keep some time; and make the finest punch, &c., by only adding sugar, spirits, water, &c. to the palate. Essence of Lemon-Peel. — (No. 407.) Wash and brush clean the Lemons; let them get perfectly dry; take a lump of loaf sugar, and rub them till all the yellow rind is taken up by the sugar; scrape oft’ the surface of the sugar into a preserving pot, and press it hard down; cover it very close, and it will keep for some time. In the same way you may get the essence of Seville orange-peel. Obs. — This method of procuring and pre- serving the flavour of Lemon-peel, by making an Oleu-sacchanm, is far superior, to the common practice of paring off the rind, or grating it, and pounding or mixing that with sugar: in this process, you obtain the whole of the fine, fi'agrant, essential oil, in which is contained the flavour.
