420 GKAVIES AND SAUCES. Sauce for Hashed or Minced Veal. (No. 361.) Take the bones of cold roast or boiled veal, dredge them well with flour, and put them into a stewpan, with a pint of broth or water, a small onion, a little grated lemon-peel, or the peel of a quarter of a small lemon pared as thin as possible, half a teaspoonful of salt, a couple of blades of pounded mace, and to thicken it, rub a tablespoonful of flour into half an ounce of butter; stir it into the broth, and set it on the fire, and let it boil very gently half an hour, strain through a sieve, and it is ready to put to the veal to warm up.
