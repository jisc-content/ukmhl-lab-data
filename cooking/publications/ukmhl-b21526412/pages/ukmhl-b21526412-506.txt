I would recommend parsley and fennel to be dried rather quicker than the other herbs, and rubbed clean from the stalks before they are bagged; these are not generally dried, but those who have ex- perienced the goodness of them in this state, will not willingly omit preserving them. Basil is in the best state for drying from the middle of August, and three weeks after, see (No. 397.) Knotted Marjoram, from the beginning of July, and during the same.
