Cut off their heads, open them, and take out the roes, and clean them thoroughly, rub them on the inside with a little pepper and salt, put the roes in again, season them (with a mixture of powdered allspice, black pepper and salt, well rubbed together) on both sides, and lay them close in a baking pan, lay a couple of bay leaves on the top, cover them with equal quan- tities of cold vinegar and water, tie them down with strong white paper doubled, and bake them for an hour in a slow oven. They will keep for a fortnight.
