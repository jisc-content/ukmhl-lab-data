You may make this soup to most advantage the day after a joint of mutton has been boiled ; put the liquor the mutton has been boiled in, into a soup-pot, with four large leeks cut into pieces an inch long, season with pepper and salt, and let it boil slowly for an hour, then mix a quarter of a pound of oatmeal with half a pint of cold water, till it is quite smooth; pour this into the soup, let it simmer gently half an hour longer, and send it up hot. lUce Soup. — (No. 206.)
