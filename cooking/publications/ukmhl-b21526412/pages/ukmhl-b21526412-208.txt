ROASTING. 198 Wheat Ears. — (No. 81.) These birds are dressed in the same way ai larks. Lobster. — (No. 82.) See receipt for boiling (No. 176.) We give no receipt for roasting lobster, being of opinion with Dr. King, who says, “ By roasting, that which our forefatliers boiled, And boiling, what they roasted, much is spoiled.”
