Plaice or Flounders Fried. — (No. 165.) Flounders are perhaps the most difficult fish to fry nicely. Clean them well, and wipe them on a dry cloth to absorb all the water from them; flour them and fry them in hot lard, and send them up with plain butter, or anchovy .sauce in a boat.
