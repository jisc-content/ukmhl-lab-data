Put an ounce of Cayenne pepper into half a pint of wine or proof spirit; let it steep for a fortnight, and then pour off the clear liquor. This is nearly equal to fresh Chili juice. Obs. — A few drops, will be found extremely convenient for the extempore seasoning and finishing of Soup, Sauces, &c., its flavour being instantly and equally ditt’nsed.
