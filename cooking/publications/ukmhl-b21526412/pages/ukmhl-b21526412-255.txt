Perch Fried.—159-) Wash the fish well, and wipe them on a dry cloth. Hour them lightly all over, and fry them ten minutes in hot lard or drippings; when they are fried, lay them on a hair sieve to drain; send them up on a hot dish, garnished with sprigs of green parsley.
