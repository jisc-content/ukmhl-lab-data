Stuffing for Roast Turkey, Capon, or Fouls. (No. 376.) Chop fine, a quarter of a pound of beef or veal suet, the same quantity of fine bread cmmbs, an ounce of fat ham or bacon (that has been boiled), a drachm of lemon-thyme, same of parsley and lemon-peel, all chopped fine, or gra- ted, a small shallot, a little grated nutmeg, pepper and salt to palate: mix thoroughly together, with the yolk and white of an egg, to bind it.
