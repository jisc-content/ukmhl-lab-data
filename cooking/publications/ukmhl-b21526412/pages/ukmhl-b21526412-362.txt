Savoury Thickening. — (No. 258.) Put a quarter of a pound of Butter into a stewpan with the meat of half a dozen Ancho- vies, three minced shallots, and four large blades of pounded Mace; stir together, and by degrees add as much Flour as will make it a stiff paste; put it into a pot to heighten the flavour of your Brown Sauces. Obs.—The cook will vary the spices, &c. according to the taste of those she works for.
