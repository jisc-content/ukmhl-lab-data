Take care to have a fresh sweetbread; for it spoils almost sooner than any thing, therefore should be parboiled as soon as it comes in. This is called blanching, or setting it. Mutton kidneys may be broiled and sent up with the sweetbreads. The time they will take depends on the thickness of the cutlet and the heat of the fire; half an inch thick will take about fifteen minutes.
