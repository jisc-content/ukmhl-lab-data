202 PRACTICAL GASTRONOMY Hachis de mouton a la Portugaise. Sliced roast leg of mutton browned slightly in butter with ham, carrots, shallots, a little garlic and parsley, braised in Madeira wine sauce. Haricot de mouton. Pieces of loin or neck of mutton, fried in butter with a few carrots, turnips, and onions, drained and stewed in brown sauce, dished up in the centre of a dish surrounded with vegetable garniture and fried bread croutons.
