SEASONABLE SUGGESTIONS IN “ THE DECALOGUE OF GASTRONOMY ” PUT INTO RHYME. I. Were there no life, the universe were naught; Life to sustain, apt food must still be sought. II. Beasts feed, man eats ; the man of cultured sense Alone gives eating its due eminence ! III. The fate of nations on their food depends ! IV. Say what you eat —I’ll know your tastes and friends !
