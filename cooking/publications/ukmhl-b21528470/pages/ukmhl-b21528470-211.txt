199 ENTREES : MOUTON MUTTON Cdtelettes de mouton sauca piquante. Fried mutton cutlets with piquante sauce. Cdtelettes de mouton d la chasseur. Broiled mutton cutlets with finely chopped mush- room sauce. Cdtelettes de mouton d la princesse. Par-broiled mutton cutlets covered with forcemeat, mixed with finely chopped mushrooms and truffles, egged, crumbed and fried in clarified butter, served with Perigord sauce. Cdtelettes de mouton a la Vatel. Stuffed mutton cutlets with chicken forcemeat and finely chopped truffles fried and garnished with finan- ciere ragout. Cdtelettes de mouton d la Reforme. Mutton cutlets crumbed in white bread crumbs mixed with finely chopped ham ; fried in butter, garnished with mushrooms, truffles, piccalilly pickles, ham and hard-boiled white of eggs, all cut into fine j ulienne strips and served with poivrade sauce, mixed with red currant jelly, or with sauce a la Reforme. (See Sauces.) Cdtelettes de mouton a la Singarat. Fried mutton cutlets larded with strips of smoked ox-tongue. Cdtelettes de mouton a la Vicomtesse. Mutton cutlets, grilled and pressed, immersed in a well reduced Madere sauce, egged, crumbed and fried. Dished up in a circle with a mixture of spring vegetables in centre and served with demi-glace sauce.
