201 MANUAL FOR ARMY COOKS. LEMONADE, No. 2. Rind of 2 lemons. Juice of 3 large or 4 small lemons. \ pound loaf sugar. 1 quart boiling water. Rub some of the lumps of sugar on the rinds of the lemons nntil all the oil is imbibed and put them with* the rest of the sugar into a vessel, and pour in the lemon juice (but not seeds); then pour in the boil- ing water. When thoroughly dissolved strain; and when cool it is ready for use.
