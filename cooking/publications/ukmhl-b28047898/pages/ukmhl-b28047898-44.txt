Kashmsr© Chutney.—G-rind to a paste the following:— quarter pound each of mustard seed, stoned raisins, chillies, and salt; half a pound each of green ginger and garlic. Dissolve one pound of moist sugar in two quarts of vinegar; mix with the paste; bottle and use after having been kept in the sun for three weeks.
