5o OYSTERS A LA MODE Woodcocks and Oysters. Becasses a la Poulette aux Huitres. Stuff a brace of woodcocks with oysters and roast them, and serve them with a stew of oysters round, prepared by bleaching the oysters in their own liquor, reducing the liquor with a liaison of eggs and cream, adding two pats of butter worked with flour, and cooking the oysters in this with pepper and lemon-juice. Serve on toast, and send to table very hot
