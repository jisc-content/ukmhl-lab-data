Rice Croquettes 3 oz. rice, i oz. sugar, i pint of milk, strip of lemon rind, egg and crumbs, Method— Boil milk, rice, and lemon rind until cooked and quite thick. Remove rind, and add sugar. Spread on a wet plate. Divide into portions. When cold, form into balls. Coat with egg and crumbs, and fry in deep fat. LESSON XX Sponge Pudding | lb. flour, 2 oz. butter, 2 oz. castor sugar, 2 eggs, i teaspoonful of baking 2 tablespoonsful of powder, water. Method.—Whisk the sugar, eggs and water over a pan of hot water for fifteen minutes. Fold in the butter (melted, but not hot) and the flour (sifted), and baking powder. Pour into a buttered pie-dish (No. 6), and bake in a moderate oven. When cooked, turn out and spread with jam.
