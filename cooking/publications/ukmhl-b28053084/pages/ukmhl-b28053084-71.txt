Method.—Boil the spawn in the sauce, tammy, add the chopped lobster and cream. Hollandaise Sauce 3 tablespoonsful of vinegar, 2 oz. butter, 3 tablespoonsful of water, salt, 2 yolks of eggs, pepper. Method.—Boil the vinegar and water for five minutes. Add one ounce of butter and the yolks. Place in a double pan or jar set in boiling water. Stir till it thickens. Add the other ounce of butter in small pieces. Season and serve in a sauceboat. Note.—If allowed to get too hot it will curdle.
