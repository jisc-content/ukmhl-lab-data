62 COOKERY RECIPES LESSON II Soups White Stock 2 lbs. knuckle of veal, | lb. lean ham, 5 pints cold water, 2 onions, 4 inches lemon-rind, 2 pieces celery, 20 peppercorns, | blade mace, i teaspoonful of salt, | turnip, bunch of herbs. Method.—Cut veal and ham into small pieces. Add salt and cold water. Bring slowly to the boil. Skim carefully. Simmer for three hours. Add vegetables, &c. Simmer for two hours longer. Strain through a wire sieve. When cold, use, after removing the fat. Note.—Use the meat and bones for second stock. Method.—Cut the meat and ham finely and remove marrow from bone. Add cold water and salt. Simmer gently for three hours, skimming as it comes to the boil. Cut onions into fine rings.
