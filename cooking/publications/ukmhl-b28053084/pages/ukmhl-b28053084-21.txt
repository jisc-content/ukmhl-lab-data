COOKERY RECIPES THE FIRST COURSE OF COOKERY LESSON I Sauces White Sauce i oz. flour, \ pt. milk, i oz. butter, or milk and stock, salt and pepper, Method.—Melt the butter in a lined pan. Cook the flour well in it, but do not let it brown. Remove from fire and add liquid gradually. Boil well—stirring all the time. When thoroughly cooked add seasoning, and if too thick a little more liquid. A squeeze of lemon juice may also be added. Parsley Sauce Above sauce made with stock and milk, plus one dessertspoonful of finely chopped parsley.
