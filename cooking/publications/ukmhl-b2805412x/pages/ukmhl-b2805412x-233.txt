Pour half a pint of boiling milk on the crumb of a French roll, beat it up with a quarter of a pound of good cheese grated, and the yolks of two eggs well beaten. Just as it is ready for the oven, stir in the whites of four eggs beaten to a solid froth. Bake in a quick oven, either in a silver or tart dish, with a high band of paper round.
