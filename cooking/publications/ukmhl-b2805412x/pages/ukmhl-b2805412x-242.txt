Orange Custard Pudding. Boil a pint of new milk, pour it on to three eggs lightly beaten, mix in the grated peel of an orange and two ounces of loaf-sugar. Beat all together for ten minutes, then pour the custard into a pie-dish, set it in another containing a little water, and put it in a moderate oven. When the custard is set (it generally takes about half an hour), take it out and let it get cold.
