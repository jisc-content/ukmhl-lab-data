They should be thoroughly washed first in hot and then in cold water, and dried in the open air each time they are used. When mention is made, “No stock required,” it does not mean that stock or liquor in which meat, fowls, etc., have been boiled must not be used, but that it need, not be used. Meat stock is always better than water.
