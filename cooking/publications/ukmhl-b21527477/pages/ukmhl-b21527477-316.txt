274 LEMON SYRUP. Take a pint of fresh lemon juice, add to it two pounds of lump sugar ; simmer it for a few minutes, and remove the scum till the surface is quite clean, then add an ounce of thin cut lemon-peel ; let them all simmer very gently for a few minutes, and strain it through a flannel. When cool, bottle, and keep it in a cool place. ORANGE SYRUP. Squeeze the oranges, and strain the juice from the pulp ; to a pint of the juice, add two pounds of sugar ; give it a boil, skim it well, strain it through a flannel, and let it stand till cold, and then bottle it.
