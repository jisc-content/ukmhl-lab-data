The same process is to be repeated, with addi- tional layers of salt, and shreds, till the whole be packed. They are then to be covered with a layer of salt, or till the barrel be filled within two inches of the top, over which the outside leaves of the cabbages are to be spread. About two pounds of salt are required for twenty middling sized cabbages. The head of the barrel, which should have been previously well fastened toge- ther, is lastly to be put within the barrel above the leaves, and loaded with stones, to prevent the mixture from rising during the fermentation.
