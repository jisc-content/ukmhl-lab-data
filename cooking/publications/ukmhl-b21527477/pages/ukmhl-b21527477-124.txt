ROASTING ON A STRING Is usually performed by means of the useful contrivance called a bottle jack, a well-known machine, so named from its form. It only serves for small joints, but does that better than the spit. It is cheap and simple, and the turning motion is pro- duced by the twisting and untwisting of a string.
