APRICOT PASTE, Peach Paste J Chery Paste Plum Paste | Quince Paste. Take ripe apricots, boil them till quite soft, mash them, and rub the mass through a splinter sieve, put the pulp into a pan, and to every pound put half a pound of pow- dered loaf sugar; put it again on the fire to simmer till the paste drops off easily from the spoon, then take it from the fire and pour it on a slab. Peach, quince, plum, and cherry paste, may be prepared in the same manner.
