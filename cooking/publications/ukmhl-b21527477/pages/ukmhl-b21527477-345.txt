303 Coffee* The beverage which we call coffee, is said to have been drank in Ethiopia from time immemorial. The Galla, a wandering nation of Africa, in their excursions on Abyssinia, being obliged to traverse im- mense deserts, and being also desirous of falling on the Abyssinians, without warn- ing, that they may be incumbered as little as possible with baggage, carry nothing with them to eat, but coffee roasted, till it can be pulverised, and then mixed with butter into balls ; one of these, about the size of a billiard ball, is said to keep them during a whole day’s fatigue.* The liquor, called coffee, was introduced Bruce’s Abyss.
