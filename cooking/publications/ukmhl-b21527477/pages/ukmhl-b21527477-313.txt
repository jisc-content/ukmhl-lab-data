271 GOOSEBERRY JELLY. Take two quarts of bruised gooseberries, simmer the mass with one pint and a half of water for about a quarter of an hour, then put it into a flannel bag to strain off the juice, and to one pint add one pound and a half of lump sugar ; simmer it, as stated under the article currant jelly.
