Strawberry, currant, gooseberry, and mulberry jam, may be prepared in a like manner. APRICOT JAM. Take ripe apricots, cut them into pieces, and remove the stones ; mash the fruit in a marble mortar, to form it into a smooth pulp ; heat it over the fire, and when nearly boiling hot, rub it through a splinter sieve ; add to one pint, by measure, of the pulp, one pound of powdered sugar; stir the mixture together, and suffer it to simmer over the fire till it comes clear from the bottom of the pan, taking care to stir the mixture all the time.
