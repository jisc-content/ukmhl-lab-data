The first care in the preservation of succulent vegetables, therefore, is to prevent them from losing their natural moisture. In regard to the tender succu- lent vegetables this is not altogether pos- sible ; because there is a constant exhala- tion from their surface, while the supply of moisture is cut off. The principle of pre- serving them, then, is to retard and diminish the exhalation.
