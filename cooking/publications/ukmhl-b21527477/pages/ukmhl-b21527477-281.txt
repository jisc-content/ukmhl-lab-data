239 PICKLED RED BEET-ROOT. Boil the root till sufficiently done; peel it and cut it into thin slices. Put it into a stone jar, and pour over it white vinegar, seasoned with long pepper, horse-raddisb, cut into small slices, allspice, cloves, and salt. PICKLED MUSHROOMS. Having peeled small button mushrooms, put them in a strong brine of salt for three or four days ; strain off the brine, and pour over them boiling hot vinegar : season with long pepper, ginger, and mace.
