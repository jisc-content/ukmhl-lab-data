Ingredients.—4oz. of lean ham, 1 bay leaf, 3 or 4 green onions, 2 sprigs of parsley, a sprig of thyme, \ of a pint cf French vinegar, 3 tablespoonfuls of brown, sauce, j a pint of red wine, 4 a pint of good stock, 1 spoonful of red cur- rant jelly, the juice of an orange, a pinch of pepper. Chop the ham, put it into a stewpan with the bay leaf, onions, parsley, thyme, and vinegar, boil on the fire till it is reduced to half its quantity, then add the sauce, wine, and stock, stir on the fire till it boils, skimming it well. Now strain it through a pieve into a small stewpan containing the red currant jelly, set it on the fire to boil, add the orange juice and pepper, and serve. This sauce is served with venison and is also very nice with haunch of mutton. CHATEAU B RI AND SAUCE.—SAUCE CHATEAUBRIAND.
