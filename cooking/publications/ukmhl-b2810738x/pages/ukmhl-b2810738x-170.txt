Ingredients.—Ripe currants, cherries, strawberries, or grapes, castor sugar, white of egg, 1 tablespoonful of water. Beat the white of egg to a froth with the water on a plate. Dip each bunch of fruit in this, then roll the fruit in the powdered sugar till quite covered, lay them on white paper placed on a sieve and put it before the fire or in a cool oven till the frosting is dry and hard This makes a nice dessert dish, or a pretty garnish for cakes.
