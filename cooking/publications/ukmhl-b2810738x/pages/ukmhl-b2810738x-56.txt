52 TROUT A LA COUR.—TRUITE A LA COUR. Ingredients.—Trout, 1 pint of light French wine, \ pint of water, 1 sliced onion, 2 cloves, a bouquet garni, i.e., parsley thyme, green onion, lemon peel, and bay leaf. 2 pepper- corns, salt to taste, 1 sliced carrot. Clean the fish well, dry it, and boil with the above ingre- dients for 20 to 30 minutes according to size. Lay the trout on a hot dish and pour over it some of the liquor in which it has been boiled or the liquor can be thickened with a little butter and flour and served separately. Salmon and red mullet are equally nice cooked this way.
