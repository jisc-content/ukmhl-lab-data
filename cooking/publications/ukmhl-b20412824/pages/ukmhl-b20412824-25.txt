Ingredients, Three quarts of Second Stock. Four Onions. Six Apples. Two Shalots. One Leek. Six Cardamom Seeds. One tablespoonful of Mustard Seed. Three tablespoonfuls of Curry Powder. Two tablespoonfuls of Curry Paste. One tablespoonful of Salt. Juice of one Lemon. Put all the ingredients in a stewpan, with the exception of the lemon juice; add the stock, and let it boil for two hours. When it is cold, add four whites of eggs, beaten up with a little cold water. Let the soup boil up again, and, when clarified, strain through a clean cloth; then add the lemon juice, and serve with some pieces of cooked chicken and well-boiled rice; the chicken served with the soup, the rice served separately.
