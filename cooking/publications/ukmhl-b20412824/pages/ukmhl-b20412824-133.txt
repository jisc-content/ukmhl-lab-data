127 HIGH-CLASS COOKEEY, Brandy Sauce. Ingredients, Three Eggs. One gill of Cream or Milk. One wineglassful of Brandy. One dessertspoonful of Castor Sugar. Put the yolks of egg into a milk saucepan, add the cream, brandy, and sugar ; whisk all well to- gether for six or eight minutes. Be careful that it does not curdle. One ounce of Castor Sugar. One gill of Water. One tablespoonful of Jam. One wineglassful of Sherry. Half a teaspoonful of Lemon-juice.
