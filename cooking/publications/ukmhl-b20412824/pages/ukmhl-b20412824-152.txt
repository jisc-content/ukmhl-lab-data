HIGH-CLASS COOKERY. 146 Coffee Cream. Ingredients, Two Eggs. Twelve sheets of Gelatine. Three ounces of Loaf Sugar. Half a pint of Milk. Half a pint of Cream. Half a gill of strong Coffee. Make half a pint of custard with the eggs and milk. Dissolve the gelatine and sugar in the coffee; add the custard, and strain; whip the cream, and stir lightly into the custard when cool.
