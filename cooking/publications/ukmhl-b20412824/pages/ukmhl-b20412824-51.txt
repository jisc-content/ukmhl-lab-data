45 HIGH-CLASS COOKERY. One tablespoonful of Parsley. Lemon-juice. Cayenne Pepper. One teaspoonful of Anchovy Sauce. A little Glaze from Fish. Half a pint of Brown Sauce. One glass of Sherry. Put the mushrooms and shalots in a stewpan, add the sherry, and reduce it; then add the brown sauce, anchovy sauce, lemon-juice, cayenne, liquor from the fish, and salt if necessary. Pour this over the mullet, and serve. Soles, whiting, &c., can be cooked in the same way. Lobster Soufflee. Ingredients. Three Whitings. One Hen Lobster. One gill of Cream. Two ounces of Butter. Two ounces of Rice Flour. One gill of Fish Stock made from bones of the Whitings. Two Eggs and one Yolk.
