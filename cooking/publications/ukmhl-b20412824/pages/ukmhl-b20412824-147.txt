Turn it quickly towards the handle of the pan,, turn it over for a second, and then on to a hot plate. ^^Sweet Omelet. Ingredients, Two Eggs. Half an ounce of Butter. One tablespoonful of Castor Sugar. One teaspoonful of Orange-Flower Water. Salt. Put the orange-flower water and the sugar into a stewpan, and stir over the fire ; let it boil quickly for three minutes; then put it into a basin to cool. When cool, add the yolks of egg and beat to a cream. Put a pinch of salt to the whites of egg, whip them to a stiff froth, and stir them lightly to the yolks of egg. Melt the butter in an omelet-pan, and pour in the eggs; put this on the fire for two minutes, and then in the oven for three or four minutes.
