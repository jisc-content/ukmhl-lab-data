A piece of Shalot. One ounce and a half of Butter. Skin the tomato, cut it up into small pieces, ^ith a piece of shalot cut fine. Take the eggs, heat the yolks and whites separately; mix the tomato, shalot, pepper, and salt with the yolks. Put the hutter into the omelet-pan and make it quite hot; then stir the whipped whites of egg lightly into the yolks, &c.; put the mixture into the omelet-pan and cook for two or three minutes, stirring all the time.
