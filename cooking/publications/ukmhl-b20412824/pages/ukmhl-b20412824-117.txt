CEufs farcie a la Russe. Ingredients. Six hard-boiled Eggs. Caviare. Tomatoes. Oil. Vinegar. Cut the eggs in half, and take out some of the yolk from the centre of each half; fill this space in with caviare, and cover over with the yolk rubbed through a sieve. Serve on slices of tomato, with a little salad oil, vinegar, and pepper and salt over them. CEufs a la Suisse. Ingredients. Two ounces of fresh Butter. Eight Eggs.
