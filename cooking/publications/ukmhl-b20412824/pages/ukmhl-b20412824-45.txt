HIGH-CLASS COOKEEY. 39 Sole a la Portugaise. Ingredients, One large Sole. One ounce of Butter. One Shalot. Half a teaspoonful of Parsley. Half a teaspoonful of Anchovy. Skin the sole, make an incision sufficiently large to admit of the stuffing; put the butter on a plate, add the shalot, parsley (chopped very fine), and anchovy sauce; put this stuffing in the sole, and put it on a buttered gratin dish.
