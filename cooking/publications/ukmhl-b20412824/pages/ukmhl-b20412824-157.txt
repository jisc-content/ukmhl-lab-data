The cream should never be added without being whipped. Pine-apple Water Ice. Ingredients, One small Pine-apple, tinned or fresh. One pint of Water, if tinned. One pint and a half of Water, if fresh fruit. Half a pound of Loaf Sugar. One tablespoonful of Lemon-juice. Pound the pine-apple in a mortar, put it in a stewpan with the water and sugar; let this boil ten minutes, strain into a basin, add the lemon- juice, and when cold freeze.
