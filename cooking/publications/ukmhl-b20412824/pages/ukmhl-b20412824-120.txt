Asparagus. Pick off the loose leaves, scrape the stalks, and wash them in cold water ; cut all the stalks the same length, and tie them together in small bundles, the heads all the same way. Put the asparagus into hot water with a little salt, and boil gently for about twenty minutes till tender, taking care not to break the heads.
