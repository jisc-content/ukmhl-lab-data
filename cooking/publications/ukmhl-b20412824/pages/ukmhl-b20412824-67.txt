ENTREES. Beef Olives. Ingredients. One pound and a half of Fillet of Beef or Rump Steak Three ounces of Bread-crumbs. Two ounces of Beef Suet. One teaspoonful of chopped Parsley. Quarter of a teaspoonful of chopped Thyme and Marjoram. Nutmeg and Lemon-rind grated. One Egg. Salt and Pepper. One pint of Brown Sauce. Cut the fillet of beef into pieces of half an inch thick and four inches long, and beat them out with a wet cutlet-bat. Chop up the trimmings of the beef, the suet, parsley, thyme, and marjoram, and mix them in a basin with the bread-crumbs, the grated lemon-rind, nutmeg, salt, pepper, and the egg; stuff each piece of beef with this mixture, roll it up, and tie it round with a piece of string.
