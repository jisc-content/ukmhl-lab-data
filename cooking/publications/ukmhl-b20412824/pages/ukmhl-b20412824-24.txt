HIGH-CLASS COOKERY. 18 Tomato Puree. Ingredients, Three pints of Stock. Two ounces of lean Ham. One Onion. Little Celery. One Shalot. One ounce of Butter. One dozen ripe Tomatoes. One tablespoonful of Vinegar. Salt, Pepper, and Nutmeg. Put all these ingredients, except the tomatoes and the stock, into a saute pan and saute them for five minutes, then add the tomatoes sliced; let them simmer, and when cooked pass through a fine sieve; add to the stock, and let it boil about five minutes. This soup can be made of tinned tomatoes. Consomme au Riz. Ingredients, Two quarts of White Stock. Four tablespoonfuls of ground Rice. Half a pint of boiling Cream. One teaspoonful of Sugar.
