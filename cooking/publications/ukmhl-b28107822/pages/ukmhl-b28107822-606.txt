Favourite Scones.—Ingredients: 1 lb. flour, 1 tablespoon- ful Rizine Baking Powder, 2 oz. butter, 2 oz. castor sugar, 2 oz. Rizine, 2 oz. sultanas, 1 cup of milk. Mode: Soak Rizine ten minutes in milk, rub butter into flour, stir in sugar, baking powder, sultanas, and a pinch of salt, and mix to a light dough with Rizine and milk, divide in three parts, roll each piece to size of a small plate, brush over with egg, cut in four, place on greased tin, and bake twelve to fifteen minutes.
