Cut half a pound of ham into dice ; fry them in salad oil, and, when a good colour, put in some morels ; moisten with half a pint of sherry, a gill (quarter pint) of Madeira, season with a mixture composed of a saltspoonful of salt, half a teaspoonful of pepper, and a teaspoonful of capsicum powder, to which add a dessert-spoonful of finely-minced parsley. Cook all this for forty-five minutes, then dish up morels in a pyramid and pour the sauce over it, which must be thickened with flour and flavoured with lemon-juice. Mushroom Baskets.
