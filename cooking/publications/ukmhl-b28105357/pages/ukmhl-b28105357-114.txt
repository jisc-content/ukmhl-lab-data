Croutes de Jamhon () VIndienne.—Stamp out . rounds one and a half inches across from thin slices of bread, and have half as many rounds of ham cut to match. Butter the bread lightly, then spread it with good chutney, place the ham on the bread, covering it with the rest, press them together, dip them in frying batter, fry a golden brown, drain well, and serve garnished with fried parsley.
