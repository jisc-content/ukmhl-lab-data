THpe. 263—SAVOUEY TEIPB. 1 lb. tripe i teacupf ul white sauce J lb, butter 1 lemou (juice) Cayenne. Mode.—Put the butter into a saucepan with a salt- spoonful of cayenne, squeeze in the juice of a lemon, cut the tripe into very small pieces and put also in the sauce- pan, allow it to cook in the butter for about half an hour ; then add the white sauce, stir it all and allow it to come to a boil. Serve on squares of hot toast with a slice of lemon.
