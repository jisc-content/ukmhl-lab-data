Pastry, Pies and Puddings. 616—YEEMICELLI POEEIDQE. 1 qt. milk 3 ozs. vermicelli Salt Sugar 1 egg. Mode.—Bring the milk to the boil, shake in the ver- micelli, stirring until all is mixed; give it half an hour's cooking with frequent stirring ; add a pinch of salt; turn on to small hot plates ; sift sugar over and eat with hot or cold milk. If an egg be added a still greater delicacy is obtained.
