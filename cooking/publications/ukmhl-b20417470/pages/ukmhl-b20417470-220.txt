Mode.—Grate the cheese and mix well with the corn- flour, then rub in the butter, moisten with the egg (well beaten), add cayenne and salt ; roll out very carefully and cut with a very small round shape ; lift with a knife and place on to a buttered tin j bake in a moderate oven for ten minutes.
