To every lb. of pineapple add 1 lb. of sugar and a little water. Mode.—Peel the pineapple and cut into thin slices ; put the sugar into a preserving pan, allowing a quarter of a pint of water to every pound of sugar ; when it is boiling drop in the slices of pineapple, and allow it to boil for about half an hour ; put the pineapple into jars and cover with syrup; close down securely and stow it away until required. 758—PINEAPPLE JAM.
