Soups. 30—POTATO SOUP. No. 2. 2 qts white stock i lb. potatoes Pepper and salt 2 carrots 1 turnip J pt. milk Sippets of toast or fried bread. Mode.—Boil the potatoes, carrots and turnip in the stock for an hour and a half, then rub all through a sieve, return it to the saucepan and add the milk ; allow it to come to the boil and serve with sippets.
