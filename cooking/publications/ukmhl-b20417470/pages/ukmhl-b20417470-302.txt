Whites of 4 eggs 8 ozs. fine sugar Essence. Mode.—Beat the whites to a stiff froth, put in the sugar and essence, beat until they are stiff enough not to move when the plate they are put on is turned over; put on greased paper in the shape of an egg, and bake in a cool oven for an hour to an hour and a half ; whip some cream and put between two of the meringues, or, if liked, preserve may be used instead, or the meringues may be made larger, and no filling will be then needed.
