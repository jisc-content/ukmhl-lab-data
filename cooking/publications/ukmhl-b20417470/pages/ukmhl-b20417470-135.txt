Veal. into a small frying pan with half the butter; when it is well cooked, cut the sweetbreads into the size you require (about two inches square) and place them in the pan with the onion and the remainder of the butter, fry them for about fifteen minutes. When nearly cooked put in the champignons each rolled in flour, sprinkle freely with pepper and salt, pour over these the sherry and shake the pan well so as to prevent them burning; allow it to simmer lor two or three minutes. Turn out into a hot entree dish, sprinkle with the parsley and serve at once.
