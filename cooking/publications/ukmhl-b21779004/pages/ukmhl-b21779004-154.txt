In Spain, they make very good chocolate ; Italian chocolate is not so pleasant to the taste, the berry being generally overroasted, which gives a bitter flavour. To make good chocolate, dissolve about an ounce and a half, for one cup, in water heated gradually, stirring it with a wooden spoon. Let it boil for a quarter of an hour, and serve up hot.
