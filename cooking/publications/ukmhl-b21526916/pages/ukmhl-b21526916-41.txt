CAMP COOKERY. 37 a little salt, popper, butter, and half a cup of water to a small lobster. Stew about ten minutes. Curried Lobster. Prepare the lobster as for stew; when it comes to a boil, add a mixture of a heaping tablespoonful of flour, and half a teaspoonful of Indian curry mixed with cold water. Let this boil eight minutes, then serve. Lobster Salad. Lobster salad is made the same as chicken, using lobster instead of chicken, and lettuce instead of celery.
