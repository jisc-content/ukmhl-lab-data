Minute Pudding. One pint of milk, one of water, nine tablespoonfuls of flour, one teaspoonful of salt, two eggs. Set the milk into a basin of hot water, and when it comes to a boil add to it one pint of boiling water. Have ready the flour, made into a smooth paste with one cup of milk, and mix with this paste, after they are well beaten, the two eggs ; now take the basin in which the milk and water are, and set upon the fire ; let it boil up once, and then stir in the thickening ; beat it well, that it may be smooth, and cook three minutes longer.
