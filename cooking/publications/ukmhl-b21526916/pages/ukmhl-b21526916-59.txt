55 GAMP COOKERY. Beets. Wash clean, but do not scrape ; if you do they will look white when cooked. When young they will cook in two hours ; but old ones will require four or five hours. When done, plunge them into cold water, and the skin will peel off easily. Cut in thin slices. Pickled Beets. Cut the beets that are left from dinner into thin slices, aud lay them in an earthen vessel, and cover with cold vinegar. Shelled Beans. Wash in several waters, and put them in a basin with boiling water. Boil one hour. Do not drain them very dry. Season with butter and salt. Boston Baked Beans.
