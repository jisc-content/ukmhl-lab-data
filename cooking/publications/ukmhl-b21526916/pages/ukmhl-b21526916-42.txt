38 CAMP COOKERY. EGGS. Poached Eggs. Break and beat up two eggs, and stir in- to them two tablespoonfuls of milk and half a teaspoonful of salt ; put them into a basin, with half a spoonful of butter, and set over the fire. Stir until it thickens, and then serve. Scrambled Eggs. Beat together four eggs, and then turn in- to a pan with one spoonful of melted butter Stir quickly over a hot fire one minute, and serve.
