CAMP COOKERY. 27 Broiled Halibut. Grease the gridiron with a little butter, place the halibut upon it, sprinkle a little salt over it, and place over clear coals. Cook one side ten minutes, then turn and cook upon the other side ten more. Have the dish warm ; put the fish upon it, season with pepper and butter, and send to the table. Smoked Halibut. Broiled the same as the fresh, omitting the pepper and salt. Smoked salmon cooked in the same way. Fried Salmon. The same as halibut. Broiled Salmon. The same as halibut. Salmon Trout.
