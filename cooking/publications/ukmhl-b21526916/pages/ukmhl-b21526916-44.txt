40 CAMP COOKERY. MEATS. Fried Salt Pork. Cut salt pork into slices a quarter of an inch thick, cut off the rind, and then pour over them boiling water, in which let them stand ten minutes; then turn off the water, and fry until they are brown on both sides. Broiled Salt Pork. Prepare as for fried, and broil ten min- utes over clear coals. Salt Pork Fried in Batter. Fry the pork as before directed ; dip in batter, and fry in the pork fat, to which should be added two spoonfuls of drippings or lard. Make the batter in the following manner: Mix gradually with one cup of flour one cup of milk, and then add one well beaten egg and a little salt.
