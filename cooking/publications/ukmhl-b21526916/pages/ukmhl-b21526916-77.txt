73 CAMP COOKERY. CAKE. Tea Cake. One spoonful of butter, one cup of sugar, one of milk, one pint of prepared flour. Beat the sugar and butter together, and then the two eggs ; next stir the milk with them, and then stir in the flour. Turn it, about an inch deep, into shallow pans, and bake in a quick oven. To be eaten warm. Berry Cake. Make the same as tea-cake, only pint and a half of flour, and stir in one pint of blue- berries. Plain Cup Cake.
