If you will give champagne, give it good, or they will all laugh at you—they will indeed, they will laugh. Young men, bachelor friends of your son-in-law, will say, “ Did you taste that fellow’s wine? Wasn’t it awful?”—which will call forth the remark, “ Ah, I don’t suppose he had opened many before.” Therefore, whatever you do, give good champagne, or none.
