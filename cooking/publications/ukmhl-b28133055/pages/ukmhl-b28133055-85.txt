TIIE CURRY COOK’S ASSISTANT. 59 No. 47.—TOAS1 CURRY. Prepare some Curry gravy, same as Madras Curry, No. 4. Now toast two slices of bread ; cut thin, and in diamond shape. After toasted, dish the toast on a vegetable dish, and pour over the gravy you prepared, and send to table hot, with Curry and rice, samball, etc.
