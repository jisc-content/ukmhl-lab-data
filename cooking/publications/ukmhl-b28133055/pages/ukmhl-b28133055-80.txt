54 THE curry cook’s assistant. No. 43.—LEMON (HOT) SAUCE. Juice of a Large Lemon. i Dessertspoon Cayenne. i Dessertspoon Pounded White Sugar; some Salt. Mix all together in a cup and use. If required to be kept, boil the whole in an enamel-plated saucepan; when cold bottle it. This sauce is very nice with cold meat or with made dishes.
