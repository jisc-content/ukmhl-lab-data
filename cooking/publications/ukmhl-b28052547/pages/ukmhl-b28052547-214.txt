Croutons a la Russe.—Stamp out half a dozen fancy rounds of stale bread with a pastry cutter. Fry these in boiling lard till of a bright brown colour; then spread thickly with shrimp or lobster paste. Cover with a layer of rich horseradish sauce, dust with cayenne pepper, and serve as hot as possible. N.B.—Although in the above menu I have made a vegetable “ braise ” follow meat cooked after the same mode, yet it will be found that the flavour is so widely different that the sameness in the method of preparation will not be noticed.
