291 MENUS AND RECIPES Supreme sauce—or rich white sauce can be used in its stead, if flavoured with mace ; egg and bread-crumb in the usual way, and fry in deep fat. Drain carefully, and serve with a border of French beans dressed ci la Frau false. French Jelly Roll.—Take a cup and a half of flour, a cupful of sifted sugar, and half a cup of cream or milk. Reserve the flour, and mix the other ingredients thoroughly, and then add an ounce and a half of butter and the well-beaten yolks of four eggs ; add the flour, and, finally, the whites of live eggs which have been whipped to a stiff froth and flavoured with vanilla. Bake on a large well-buttered baking-sheet in a quick oven. When cooked, place on a clean serviette, spread thickly with any jelly best liked, roll and serve up immediately.
