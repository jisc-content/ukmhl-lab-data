184 CHAPTER XXV NEW DISHES Mindful of my masculine readers, I append two menus, each containing a recipe for an entirely new and original dish, viz., “ Beignets de Laitance a la Propert” and “ Crab a l’Hollandaise.” Housekeepers in search of novelties may like to note this fact. Special Dinner. Menu. Puree de Navets. Turbot en Matelotte Normande. Selle d’Agneau a la Godard. Pommes de Terre. Concombres a l’Espagnol. Cailles Roties. Salade. Petits Clioux au Caramel. Beignets de Laitance a la Propert. Dessert. Popular Dinner. Menu. Scotch Broth. Crab a l’Hollandaise. Grenadines of Veal, with Spinach. Saute Potatoes. Rice, Polish fashion. Currant Sago, Custard Sauce. Beignets of Sardines. Dessert.
