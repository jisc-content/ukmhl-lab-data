183 TWO LOYAL MENUS beat it till of the consistency of thick cream. Next add to it twelve ounces of well-dried and sifted flour, six ounces of finely-chopped sweet almonds, the grated rind of two lemons, a pound of caster sugar, and a large wineglassful of Kirchenwasser. Mix thoroughly, and use to fill a sufficient number of small moulds, previously well buttered. Bake till of a light fawn colour. Then cover thickly with either apricot jam or orange marmalade, and serve with whipped and sweetened cream, handed separately.
