Then stir the whole mixture carefully for about three minutes over a slack fire, till the paste leaves the sides of the pan ; take off the fire, and when the paste is getting cool add a few drops of essence of vanilla, or any other essence pre- ferred, and shape into small round balls and bake to a light golden colour. When done the petits choux may be served plainly with sugar sprinkled over them, in which case they are generally sent to table hot, or split open when cold and filled with whipped and sweetened cream.
