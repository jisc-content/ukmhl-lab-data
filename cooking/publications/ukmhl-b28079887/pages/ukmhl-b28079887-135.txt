BREAK a bar of chocolate into small pieces, and pour over it a pint and a half of milk, let it remain until it is dissolved, and then boil it slowly for ten minutes. Well beat the yolks of five eggs with a spoonful and a half of moist sugar, mix it with the milk, and pour it into cups. Stand them in a stcwpan of boiling water, which must only cover half way to the edge of the cup, and let them remain simmering twenty minutes with the cover of the stewpan kept on. When done, place them in a very cold place.
