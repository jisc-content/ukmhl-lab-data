Put the spinach in the stewpan, and add about half a saltspoonful of pepper, and a salt- spoonful of salt or more, according to taste, and half a gill of cream or milk, and mix all together with a wooden spoon. Put the stewpan on the lire and stir the spinach until it is quite hot. Cut a slice of crumb of bread, about a quarter of an inch in thickness, and cut it up into triangular pieces. Take a frying-pan, and put into it two ounces of butter or lard, and put it on the fire to heat. When quite hot, throw in the pieces of bread, and let them fry a pale brown.
