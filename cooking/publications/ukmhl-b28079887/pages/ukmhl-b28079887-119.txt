BOIL three quarters of a pound of carrots, and pulp them, add half a pound of bread crumbs, a quarter of a pound of stoned raisins, four ounces of suet chopped very fine, a little nutmeg, and three ounces of sugar pounded. Well beat the three eggs, and add them to a sufficient quantity of milk to make the ingredients into a thick batter, then put it into a buttered pie-dish and bake it for one hour. When done, turn it out, and sift sugar over it.
