128 GINGER SNAPS. WORK a quarter of a pound of butter into a pound of fine flour, then mix it with half a pound of treacle, quarter of a pound of brown sugar, one tablespoonful of ground ginger, and one of caraway seeds. Work it all well together, and form it into cakes not larger than a dollar, place them on a baking tin in a moderate oven, and bake for twenty minutes, when they will be dry and crisp. Jtli? BEAT eight ounces of fresh butter to a cream before the fire, and add eight ounces of best sugar pounded and one pound of arrow-root, beating the mixture all the time. When well mixed, stir in the whites of seven eggs whisked to a very stiff froth, and a little flavouring of essence of lemon to taste.
