142 — ANOTHER RAISIN CAKE. ONE cup of treacle, one of butter, one of milk, three of flour, two of chopped raisins, and one teaspoonful of soda. Spice to taste. Soften the butter, and beat it and the treacle together; then add milk, and then the flour in which the soda is mixed, and lastly the raisins.
