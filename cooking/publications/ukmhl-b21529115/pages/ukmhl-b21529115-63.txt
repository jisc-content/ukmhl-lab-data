Cream Toast.—Take slices of Zwieback, moisten with hot cream, to which a little salt has been added, pack immediately in a hot dish, and serve. Cream Toast, No. 2.— Pour hot cream, or milk thickened with corn-starch to the consistency of cream, over toasted Granose Biscuit. Nut Gravy Toast.—-Over moistened slices of Zwieback pour a gravy made by adding three or four tablespoonfuls of Nut Meal to every cup of water used.
