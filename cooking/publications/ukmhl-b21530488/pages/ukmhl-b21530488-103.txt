Preserve the venison dry, wash it with milk and wa- ter very clean, and dry it with clean cloths till not the least damp remains, then dust pounded ginger over - every part, which is a good preventative against the fly. By thus managing and watching, it will hane a fortnight. When to be used, wash it with a little luke- warm water, and dry it. Pepper is likewise good to keep it. To dress Venison.
