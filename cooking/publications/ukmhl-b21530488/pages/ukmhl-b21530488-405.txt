To prevent thv creaking of a Door. Rub a bit of soap on the hinges. A strong Paste for Paper. To two large spoonfuls of fine Hour, put as much pounded rosin as will lie on a shilling; mix with as much strong beer as will make it of a due consistence, and boil half an hour. Let it be cold before it is used. Fine blacking for Shoes.
