83 VEGETABLES. a teaspoon of chopped parsley, season with pepper, salt, and a small pinch of mace. Cut a moderate-sized vegetable marrow in half lengthways and scoop out the seeds, fill each half with the stuffing, place the halves together, and wrap up the vegetable marrow in buttered paper and tie with tape, put it in a deep buttered tin or a pie-dish, and cover with another dish ; bake in the oven for an hour; take it carefully out of the paper, put in a silver dish, and pour clear gravy or tomato sauce over it.
