With each pound of meat put two tablespoons of bread that have been rubbed through a wire sieve, and add a teaspoon of salt, half a teaspoon of pepper, and half a teaspoon of dried sage. Mix the herbs and seasoning with the bread-crumbs, and then add the whole to the meat, being careful to blend the ingredients thoroughly. Moisten the mince with a tablespoon of gravy to each pound of meat. The meat is then ready to be made into balls 5 flour them lightly before frying.
