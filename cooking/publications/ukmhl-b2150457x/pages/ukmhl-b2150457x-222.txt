[ 210 ] WINE FINGERS. 3 oz. flour. 2 oz. butter. 1^ oz. sugar. i yolk of egg. i tablespoonful sherry. Decorate. —Chopped almonds. Rub the butter into the flour, add sugar, and mix to a firm paste with the yolk of egg and sherry. Roll out thinly and cut into •fingers. Brush with white of egg, and sprinkle with chopped almonds ; bake in a fairly hot oven till brown and firm, twelve to fifteen minutes. N.B.—These biscuits may be decorated with small pieces of cherry, angelica, etc., or they may be decorated with royal icing before being put into the oven.
