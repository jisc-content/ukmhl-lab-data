C 85 ] LOBSTER CUTLETS. 4 oz. cooked lobster. 1 oz. butter. 1 oz. flour. 1 gill fish stock or water. 1 yolk of egg. Cayenne, salt, lemon juice. ^ oz. lobster butter or carmine. 1 beaten egg. Bread-crumbs. Fried parsley. Make a thick sauce with the butter, flour, and stock. Add the lobster, finely chopped, and the other ingredients. Cook over a gentle heat till the mixture binds; then turn out on to a plate to cool. Form into neat cutlets; coat with egg and bread-crumbs, and fry in deep fat. Dish neatly and garnish with fried parsley. Lobster Butter. See Lobster Bisque, p. 17. Recipe and preparation the same as for Lobster Cutlets, substituting twelve oysters for the lobster and omitting the colouring. OYSTER CUTLETS.
