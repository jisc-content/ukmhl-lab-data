[ 199 ] CHOCOLATE CAKE. 3 oz. sugar. 3 oz. butter. 4 oz. flour. 3 oz. chocolate. 1 teaspoonful baking powder. 3 small eggs. Vanilla essence. Dissolve chocolate in two tablespoonfuls of milk. Cream butter and sugar; add eggs with a little flour. Stir in dissolved chocolate with remainder of flour and baking powder ; add a few drops of vanilla essence. Pour mixture into a greased and prepared tin ; bake in a moderate oven for about one hour. Ice when cold with chocolate icing. Sieve the flour, cream the butter and sugar, and add the grated lemon rind. Add the flour.
