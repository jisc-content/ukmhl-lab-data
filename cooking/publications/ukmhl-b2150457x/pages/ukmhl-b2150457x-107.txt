( es ] TOMATO SALAD. 3 large firm tomatoes. 3 sticks celery, or 1 bunch watercress or small cress. French dressing. Dip the tomatoes into boiling water; skin and cut them across into thin slices. Shred the celery and leave in cold water for half an hour; wash the cress well. Arrange the tomatoes in a neat circle in a salad dish, pour the dressing over. Pile the celery or cress in the centre.
