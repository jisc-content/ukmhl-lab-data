[ 109 ] FIG PUDDING. 4 oz. bread-crumbs. 2 oz. flour. 3 oz. sugar. 3 oz. suet. 6 oz. flgs. 2 oz. sultanas. 1 oz. almonds. 1 oz. mixed peel. 1 oz. cherries. Grated rind of one lemon. 3 eggs. ^ gill cream. Milk if necessary. Chop the suet, figs, peel, cherries, and almonds, and mix all the dry ingredients together. Bind with the beaten eggs, cream, and milk, and mix to a dropping consistency. Steam in a greased mould for four hours. Turn out, and serve with custard, mousseline, or wine sauce.
