BREAD SAUCE. ^ oz. butter. 1 gill freshly made bread- crumbs. ^ pint milk, 1 small onion. 2 cloves. 6 peppercorns. Salt. 1 or 2 tablespooufuls cream. Put the milk, peppercorns, and onion stuck with cloves into a saucepan, and infuse at the side of the fire for fifteen minutes, then strain. Return the seasoned milk to the pan with the butter and bread-crumbs. Stir till boiling and of a dropping consistency. Add the cream and serve.
