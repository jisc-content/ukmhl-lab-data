[ 192 ] ROUGH PUFF, ^ lb. flour. 6-6 oz. butter. Salt. Lemon juice. Cold water. Sieve the flour and salt into a bowl; add the butter broken into pieces the size of a walnut. Add the lemon juice and sufficient cold water to make the mixture adhere. Turn on to a floured board, and roll the pastry into a long strip. Fold in three and press the edges together. Half turn the pastry, and again roll it into a strip. Fold in three, and repeat this until the pastry has had four rolls, folds, and half-turns. It is then ready for use. Rough puff* pastry is suitable for meat pies, patties, sausage rolls, etc. Sieve the flour and salt into a basin, and mix to a firm paste with the lemon juice and cold water.
