[ 12 ] Wash and soak the turtle for four or five days. Rinse and cut in small pieces. Put it on with the stock and vegetables, and simmer for four to five hours till the turtle is tender. Strain through a hair sieve. Thicken with the arrowroot broken with the sherry ; add brandy, lemon juice, cayenne, and turtle cut in dice.
