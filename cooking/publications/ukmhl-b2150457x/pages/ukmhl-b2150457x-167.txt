[ 155 ] ANCHOVY STRAWS A LA ROYALE. Anchovy biscuits as above. | Anchovy butter (2 oz.). Spread a little butter on the anchovy biscuits and sandwich two together. Decorate with the butter piped in a pretty design on the top. FARCED OLIVES. 6 olives. 6 cheese biscuits. 1 oz. anchovy butter. Cress or chervil. 3 anchovy fillets. Stone the olives and farce them with the butter. Place them on the biscuits, and twist a half anchovy round each. Pipe the remainder of the butter round and decorate with cress. Maitre d hotel butter, foie-gras, etc., may be substituted for the anchovy butter.
