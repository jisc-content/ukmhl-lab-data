[ S8 ] PUR^E DE MARRONS. ^ Ib. chestnuts. 1 onion. oz. butter. 1 quart white stock. 1 gill cream. Pepper and salt. Pinch of sugar. Cut a small piece off each chestnut, and boil for ten minutes. Remove the skins, and break the chestnuts in pieces. Melt the butter, add the chestnuts and onion, and saut^ for a few minutes, but do not brown. Add the stock, and cook one hour, or until the chestnuts are tender. Rub through a hair sieve; reheat; add the sugar, seasoning, and cream, and it is ready to serve.
