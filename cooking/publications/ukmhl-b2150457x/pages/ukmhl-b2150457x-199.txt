Put the water, sugar, orange rind, and gelatine into a stew-pan. When dissolved, infuse for ten minutes ; then strain through muslin into a basin. Add the orange and lemon juice, and mould. When set, turn out and serve. Put all these ingredients into a large rinsed pan. Stir over a clear fire till the gelatine is dissolved. Whisk briskly till a good froth is formed.
