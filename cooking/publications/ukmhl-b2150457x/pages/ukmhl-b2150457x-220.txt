[ 208 ] SULTANA CAKE. 8 oz. flour 6 oz. butter. 6 oz. castor sugar 4 oz. sultanas. Grated rind of half a lemon. 2 oz. candied peel. 1 teaspoonful baking powder. 2 eggs. A little milk. Prepare and bake as for Plum Cake. SHORTBREAD. 4 oz. flour. 4 oz. butter. 2 oz. rice-flour, 2 oz. castor sugar Sieve the flour, rice-flour, and sugar into a basin, add the butter, and work all together with the hand till of the consistency of short crust. Form into a round cake either with the hand on the pastry-board, or in a short- bread mould. Place the cake on a greased and papered tin; prick it well, bake it in a good steady oven till beginning to colour, and allow it to crisp off slowly for about one hour.
