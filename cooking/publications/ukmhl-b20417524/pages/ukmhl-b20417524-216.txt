To be eaten with hard sauce. MOLLY PUFFS. Mrs. George B. Gushing. One cup Indian meal scalded; when it cools add two cups of rye meal, two eggs, one tablespoon of brown sugar, and a small half teaspoon of soda; fry them, dropped from a spoon in boiling lard.
