Line the bottom of a pudding dish with stale sponge cake, slice upon the cake six oranges ; make a custard of one quart of milk and five eggs, leaving out the whites of four; beat the whites to a stiff froth, adding sugar, put on top of pudding, and put in the oven until brown.
