Four eggs, three teacups flour, not quite two of sugar, one teaspoon of soda, two of cream tartar, or three of baking powder; mix flour, sugar and cream tartar together; dissolve the soda in one cup good cream and add with the beaten eggs to the mixture. Flavor with lemon or vanilla. Bake twenty minutes. Is made quickly and very nice. COEN STARCH CAKE. Mrs. Dickinson. One cup of butter, two cups sugar, one cup sweet milk, two-thirds cup corn starch and fill it up with flour, two cups flour, two teaspoons baking powder, whites of seven eggs. COEN STAECH CAKE.
