(214) PUDDING SAUCES. " I crack my brains to find out tempting sauces, And raise fortifications in the pastry." PUDDING SAUCE. Anonymous. One cup of butter, one cup of milk, one cup of sugar, three eggs, flavor to taste. PUDDING SAUCE. Mrs. Dunham. One-half cup of butter, one cup of sugar, two eggs well beaten, and pour on one cup of boiling water; flavor with nutmeg or other flavor. PUDDING SAUCE. Mrs. A. R. Scranton. Four tablespoons of white sugar, two tablespoons of butter, one tablespoon of flour; beat all to a cream and add the white of one egr^ well beaten; then add one gill boiling water ; stir well; flavor to taste.
