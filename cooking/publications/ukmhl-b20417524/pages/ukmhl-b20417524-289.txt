BEOWN" BEEAD. Mrs. Lamkin. One and one-half cups of rye meal, one and one-half of Indian meal, one-half cup of molasses, two and one- half of cold water, even teaspoon of soda, a little salt; steam four and a half hours, then put it in the oven for a very few minutes, just to take the moisture from the top.
