VALUABLE EECIPES. 34T CLAY CAKE. Mrs. Ada Sturtevant. One cup butter, two and one-half cups sugar, one cup sweet milk, four cups flour, the yolks of five eggs and the whites of seven, two spoons cream tartar, one spoon soda, one spoon extract of lemon; stir the butter and sugar till it looks like cream; beat the yolks separately and well, the whites to a stiff froth, adding the whites and flour last, and beat all very thoroughly. This will make two cakes. If you lack time, and wish variety, by changing the flavoring and adding fruit to one, you will have two cakes entirely unlike, and very good. HAEEISON CAKE. Mrs. B. F.
