71 YALTJABLE RECIPES. CAEAMEL, OR BURNED SUGAR. Put two ounces of brown or white sugar in an old tin cup over a brisk fire, stir this until it is quite dark and gives forth a burned smell, then add a half a cup of cold water; let it boil gently a few minutes, stirring well and all the while.
