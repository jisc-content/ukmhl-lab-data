VALUABLE EECIPES. 399 Allowance of Supplies for an Entertainment. In inviting guests, it is safe to calculate that out of one hundred and fifty, but two-thirds of the number will be present. If five hun- dred are invited, not more than three hundred can be counted upon as accepting. Allow one quart of oysters to every three persons present. Five chickens [or, what is better, a ten pound turkey, boiled and minced], and fifteen heads of celery, are enough for chicken salad for fifty guests ; one gallon of ice cream to every twenty guests ; one hundred and thirty sandwiches for one hundred guests ; and six to ten quarts of wine jelly for each hundred.
