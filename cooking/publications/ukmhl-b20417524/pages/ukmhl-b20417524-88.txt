THE HOME COOK BOOK. 84: FRICASSEED OYSTERS. Mrs. W. P. Brown. For a quart can, drain the oysters dry as possible; put a piece of butter the size of an egg into your spider, and let it get quite brown ; put in your oysters and as soon as they commence to cook, add as much more butter, which has been previously well mixed with a tablespoon of flour; let it cook a moment and add one egg, beaten with a tablespoon of cream ; let this cook a moment and pour all over toasted bread. MACARONI WITH OYSTERS. Mrs. F. B. Orr. Boil macaroni in salt water, after which drain through a colander ; take a deep earthern dish or tin, put in alter- nate layers of macaroni and oysters; sprinkle the layers of macaroni with grated cheese; bake until brown.
