APPLE FEITTEES. M. One teacup of sweet milk, one tablespoon of sweet light dough dissolved in milk, three eggs beaten separately, one teaspoon of salt, one and one-half teacups of flour, one tablespoon of sugar, and the grated peel of a lemon, peeled apples sliced without the core ; drop into hot lard with a piece of apple in each one; sprinkle with powdered or spiced sugar.
