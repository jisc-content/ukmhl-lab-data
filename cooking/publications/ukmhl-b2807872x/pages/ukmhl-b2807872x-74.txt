Blanc de volaille aux concombres — slices from the breast of a roast fowl, with pieces of cucumber, white sauce, cream, &c. Cotelettes de volaille d la Dawpliine are made with the legs of fowls from which the thigh-bone has been removed. They are dipped in Allemande sauce, breadcrumbed and fried. Served with a dressed vegetable in the centre and white sauce. Legs of fowl are also boned, stuffed with forcemeat, and served as Guisses de volaille, or poulardes, a la jardiniere, with a Jardiniere garnish; aux con- combres or aux petits pois, with dressed cucumber or peas.
