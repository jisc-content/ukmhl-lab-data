Compotes^ &c., of Fruit. 153 Beignets souffles d- la vanille are small fried balls of a batter flavoured with vanilla. aux fleurs d’oranger are flavoured witli orange-flower water. aux confitures are served with, preserve. au maizena are made with maizena. Crepes are pancakes. aux confitures are spread with preserve and rolled. au riz are made of rice with sugar, cream, eggs, &c. au maizena are made with maizena. d la Frangaise—a batter of eggs, sugar, cream and flour, fried and folded over preserve. COMPOTES, &c., OF FRUIT. Compote d'abricots—apricots boiled in syrup. It is also made of green apricots. d’ahricots d la Breteuil — half apricots sprinkled with sugar and broiled; served with apricot and raspberry syrup. Abricots au riz are stewed apricots with rice. Served round a shape of rice, with apricot syrup, they are called d la Conde. Pain d’ahricots is a mould of apricot cheese ; it is served with cream in the centre.
