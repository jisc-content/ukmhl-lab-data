Bake for about 25 minutes in a well buttered “Express” paper-bag, placed on a wire grid in a fairly hot oven. Milk Rolls. Take 8 ozs. of flour, 1 oz. of butter, half an ounce of castor sugar, one tea-spoonful of baking powder, salt, and milk to mix. Mix the flour and salt in a basin, rub in lightly the butter, add the sugar and baking powder, and mix with enough milk to form a dough; turn on to a floured board, make up into desired shapes, and brush over with sweetened milk; place them in a buttered “Express” paper-bag, put it on a wire grid and bake in a fairly hot oven from 15 to 20 minutes.
