Choose firm, not over-ripe, bananas, peel them, and cut each in half lengthwise ; dip in milk and then in seasoned oatmeal. Brush over the inside of a paper-bag with clarified butter or olive oil. Put in a layer of thinly cut bacon, and over these the bananas, sprinkle a little clarified butter over the bananas, close up the bag and fasten with clips; bake on a grid shelf for about 15 minutes. Take up the bananas, season them lightly with Krona or paprika pepper, dish up, garnish with slices of fried bacon and slices of lemon, and serve. Bananas with Bacon. This makes an excellent breakfast dish. Take six bananas, not over ripe, peel them, and cut each in half lengthways; season with salt and pepper, and put each half on a slice of streaky bacon. Place these in a well buttered paper-bag, fasten the ends securely as above directed, and bake in a hot oven for about 12 minutes or longer; it will not spoil. Bananas and Cream.
