Then place the prepared pasties in a buttered “Express” paper-bag, and bake on a grid shelf in a fairly hot oven for from 10 to 12 minutes. Savoury Banana Pasties. Proceed the same as directed in the fore- going recipes, but omit the sugar, cinnamon, and apricot marmalade. Instead, spread the paste with a little paprika butter before folding in the bananas. Then place in a buttered “Express” paper-bag and bake for about 10 minutes. Paprika Butter. This is made by mixing a tea-spoonful of paprika with about an ounce of butter and a little salt, knead it well and use as directed. Cheese Straws. Take some trimmings of puff paste and roll out to an oblong shape; sprinkle over carefully with finely grated Parmesan or Gruyere cheese, then cut the paste into neat even-sized strips; place them on a small greased baking tin, slip this carefully into a buttered “Express” paper-bag, and bake in a hot oven for about 10 minutes.
