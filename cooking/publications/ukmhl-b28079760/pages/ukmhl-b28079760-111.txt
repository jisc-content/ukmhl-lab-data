Take four bananas, not over ripe, peel them, put them in a pie-dish, and pour over a little lemon juice. Have ready a buttered souffle dish. Separate the yolks from two eggs, adding 2 ozs. of castor sugar, and beat up to a cream. Next cream up in a basin 2 ozs. of fresh butter, add slowly 2 ozs. of sifted flour, then stir in the yolk of eggs. When well mixed, add the bananas previously passed through a sieve. Beat all well together, lastly add the stiffly whisked whites of eggs. Put the souffle quickly in an “ Express ” paper-bag, fold and fasten the end of the bag with clips. Bake in a hot oven for about 20 minutes. Send the souffle to table quickly. Bananas served in this way can be easily digested by the most delicate person, and are always received with delight.
