Put two tablespoonfuls of tapioca into a basin with four lumps of sugar, a grain of salt, and a lump of sugar rubbed on the rind of a lemon; pour a gill of boiling milk over these ingredients, and cover them up with a saucer to steep ten minutes ; then add one egg; beat up all together, and boil in a buttered teacup tied up in a cloth for nearly half-an-hour; turn out and serve.
