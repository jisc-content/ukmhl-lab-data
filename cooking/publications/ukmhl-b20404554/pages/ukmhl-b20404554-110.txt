Cut it into neat joints, and either dust it over with flour or crumb it like a cutlet, and fry in the same way for ten minutes. Eabbit being in itself rather insipid, is generally served with bacon, and in every receipt for dressing it for people in health bacon forms an essential feature; so if the invalid does not object, a little fried bacon may be served up with the boiled rabbit, and in the fricassee some bits of bacon may be added, but this must be done with caution.
