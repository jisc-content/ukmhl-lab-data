CONVALESCENT COOKERY. 46 RECIPES, &c. EXTRACT OF BEEF. Take one pound of lean beef, cut it up into small pieces, eacli about as large as a dice, put the meat into a bottle or jar with a little salt. Tie a piece of bladder over the neck of the bottle, or cork it closely. Put the bottle into a saucepan of cold water, and put the saucepan on a slow fire. As soon as the water boils, set tbe sauce- pan in some part of the fire which will keep the water boiling-, but no more, and as the water in the saucepan boils away, fill up with boiling water.
