86 Cake Decoration and Flower Piping. The basket is made by baking some pound-cake mixture in a basket-shaped mould, and when done iced over with pale green sugar and piped. Of course, in the pre- sent case, the basket whilst piped is only “ a dummy,” the handle is pulled sugar, and the flowers are almond paste.
