37 Plaque Piping. CHAPTER VI. PLAQUE PIPING. The plaques or panels being now prepared, proceed in the following manner : Fig. 13. —Country Lane and Cottage. Country Lane and Cottage.—Fill your cornet of paper with some liquid pale yellow sugar, and mark out the sky, leaving a few parts untouched for the lights of your picture, and go over the entire surface, the above portions indicated excepted ; then with some yellow icing in cornet mark out the lane, cottages, and hedges and finger-post and background, and then proceed to fill in and raise the cottages, hedges, &c.
