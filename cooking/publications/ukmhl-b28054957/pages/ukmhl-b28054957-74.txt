62 Almond Souffle 4 ozs. almonds 7 eggs 4 ozs. sugar 1 table-spoon lemon sugar 24 ozs. flour Blanch and pound the almonds with one whole egg. Add the sugar, six yolks, lemon sugar (see p. 164). Beat for quarter of an hour. Sift in the flour lightly, avoiding lumps, and add the whites beaten to a stiff froth. Pour into a buttered mould and steam about an hour.
