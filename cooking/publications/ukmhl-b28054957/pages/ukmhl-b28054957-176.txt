164 Coloured Sugars On to some powdered sugar drop a few drops of any colouring. Stir well and quickly so that the sugar may be evenly coloured. When dry, crush between paper if necessary. Keep in a tin in a dry place. Orange and Lemon Sugar ^ lb. loaf sugar 3 oranges or lemons Pare off the yellow rind of oranges, tangerines or lemons very finely. Let it dry thoroughly. Pound it in a mortar with the sugar. Pass through a fine sieve and keep in a dry place. Or the rind may be rubbed off on lumps of sugar, which must then be dried and very finely crushed.
