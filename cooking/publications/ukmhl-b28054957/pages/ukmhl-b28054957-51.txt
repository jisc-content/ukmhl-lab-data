Pastry Book 39 Rice Pancakes r lb. rice | pint cream or milk 1 table-spoon powdered sugar 4 eggs 2 ozs. butter Flour Boil the rice in water until very tender. Pound it and put it through a sieve. When cold add the cream or milk, sugar, well-beaten eggs and butter, which should be warmed and creamed. Add Hour, if necessary, to make a fairly stiff batter. Fry in butter.
