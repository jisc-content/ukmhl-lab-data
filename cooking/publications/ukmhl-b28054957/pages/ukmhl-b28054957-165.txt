Pastry Book 153 Lemon Water Ice 6 lemons b lb. lump sugar 1 quart water 1 lb. powdered sugar Whites of 4 eggs Rub off the peel of the lemons on to lump sugar. Make a syrup with the lump sugar, powdered sugar and water (see p. 151). Add the juice of the lemons. Strain. When cool add the whites beaten to a froth, and freeze. Maraschino Punch 5 lb. powdered sugar 1 quart water b pint maraschino 1 lemon 1 orange Make a syrup of the sugar and water (see p. 151). When cold add the maraschino and the juice of the lemon and orange. Strain. Freeze.
