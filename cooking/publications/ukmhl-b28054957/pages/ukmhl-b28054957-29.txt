Pastry Book B 17 "Tapioca Custard 2 table-spoons tapioca 1 pint milk 2 eggs 5 cup powdered sugar i tea-spoon vanilla Soak the tapioca for two hours in hot water in a double-boiler. Drain. Put back and add the milk. Cook until tender and clear. Carefully mix with the tapioca the two yolks beaten well, the sugar and a little salt. Cook until the custard thickens. Take off the fire and stir in the whites beaten to a stiff froth. When cool add the vanilla. Serve very cold.
