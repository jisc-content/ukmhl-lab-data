80 Banana Sauce 1 cup water 4 table-spoons powdered sugar 4 bananas 1 dessert-spoon maraschino or 1 wine-glass of wine Put the water and sugar in a sauce-pan and stir until the sugar is dissolved. Add four bananas which have been mashed with a silver fork. Boil for two minutes. Put through a sieve, and add the wine or maraschino.
