125 Plain Pastry—I 12 ozs. Hour 3 ozs. butter 3 ozs. lard Salt Cut up the lard and butter, very finely, in the Hour. With a knife, mix with a little water. Roll and fold up three or four times. Plain Pastry—II 1 lb. flour b lb. butter Cold water Rub the butter into the flour. Add eold water gradually to make a stiff paste, mixing with a knife. Roll out quickly. Plain Pastry Crust—III (For Tarts) 4 ozs. butter 2 ozs. lard 10 ozs. flour Beat the butter to a cream. Rub butter and lard very carefully into the flour, adding a little salt. Mix with as little water as possible.
