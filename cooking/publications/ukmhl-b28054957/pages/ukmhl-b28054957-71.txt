Pastry Rook 59 the flour is cooked (the mixture should boil for at least five minutes). Add the butter whilst boiling. Strain and set aside to cool. Beat the yolks till creamy. Beat them together with the milk, etc. Add the flavouring. Beat in the whites, whipped to a stiff froth, very lightly. Bake in buttered souffle dish in a moderate oven for twenty-five minutes. This souffle may be flavoured with the syrup of preserved ginger, one table-spoon of maras- chino, the juice and grated rind of a lemon, or with orange flower water.
