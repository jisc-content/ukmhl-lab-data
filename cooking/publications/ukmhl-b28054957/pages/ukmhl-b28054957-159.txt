Pastry Book 147 Brown Bread Cream Ice 1 quart cream 1 tea-cup brown bread-crumbs ^ lb. sugar Dry the brown bread and crumble and sift to very fine crumbs. Add them to the cream and sugar. When the sugar has melted, mix well together and freeze. "Caramel Cream Ice 8 ozs. powdered sugar 6 yolks of eggs 1 pint milk 1 pint cream I dessert-spoon vanilla Put the sugar in an iron sauce-pan with a very little water, and leave it until it becomes a rich brown. Let it become cold. Pound it and add the milk and yolks. Stir the mixture in a double boiler until it thickens. Strain, and when cold add the vanilla and cream.
