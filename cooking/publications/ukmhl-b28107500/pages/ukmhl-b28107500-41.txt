Naples macaroni f pint stock or water 1 small shallot or onion 1 blade of mace Yolks of 2 eggs 3 tablespoonfuls grated Parmesan cheese Salt, pepper, and cayenne Skin and joint the chicken neatly, put it into a sauce- pan with the stock (boiling), the shallot, mace, sweet herbs, and simmer slowly 30 minutes to one hour. Meanwhile wash the macaroni and break into pieces one inch long, throw it into a pint of boiling water with 1 oz. of the butter and boil until tender, then strain from it the water, and add a little pepper, salt, and cayenne, and one tablespoonful of Parmesan cheese. Arrange this round the edge of a dish, and the chicken in the centre. Put the butter into a pan and melt, stir in the flour until smooth, then add the liquid from the chicken, pepper, salt, and cayenne, stir until thick, and boil three minutes, strain to the yolks of two eggs, add the remainder of the Parmesan cheese; put back into the pan, re-heat, but do not allow it to boil, pour over the chicken, and serve. The dish may be garnished with a very little parsley if liked. D
