Aunt Molly’s Cookies. 1 cup of butter. 2 cups powdered sugar. 4 eggs. 4 cups of prepared flour, or enough for soft dough. 2 tablespoonfuls of cream. Nutmeg and mace. Roll into a thin sheet, and cut into small cakes. Bake in a quick oven until crisp and of a delicate brown. Brush them over while hot with a soft bit of rag dipped in sugar and water, pretty thick.
