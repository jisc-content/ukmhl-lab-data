Pare, wash and grate 12 large raw potatoes. Mix with 1 tablespoonful of salt and the yolks of 3 eggs. Beat the whites to a stiff froth ; add the potatoes slowly to the whites. Place a large frying-pan with 1 tablespoonful of fat over the fire as soon as hot. Put small por- tions from the mixture with a spoon into the hot fat to form cakes the size of a saucer; bake light brown on both sides. Serve on a hot plate.—Mrs. Gesine Lemcke. 21.—Stuffed Mutton Chops. Trim 8 or 10 mutton chops which have been cut very thick. With a sharp knife split each chop in two, without separating the meat from the bone.
