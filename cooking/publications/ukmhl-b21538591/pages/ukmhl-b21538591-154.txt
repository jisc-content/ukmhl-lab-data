November. 146 platter, garnished with parsley and toast points.—From “ Table Talk,” Philadelphia. 30.—Pannikins. Warm minced ham or tongue or veal in a thick cream sauce, and pile it in the centre of a platter. Heat and butter some earthen cups; break an egg in each and bake until the egg is firm. Turn them out and arrange around the meat.—Mrs. M. J. Lincoln.
