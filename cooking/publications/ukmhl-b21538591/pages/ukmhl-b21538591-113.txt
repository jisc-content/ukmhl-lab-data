Let all cook together for a few minutes; then stuff each chop with this mixture after it has cooled ; press them tightly together and broil. 22.—Veal Hash. Put 1 tablespoonful of butter and 2 table- spoonfuls of chopped onion over the fire ; let this cook for 5 minutes; add 1 tablespoonful of flour and stir and cook a few minutes longer ; then add 1 pint of fine chopped veal, 1 pint of chopped boiled potatoes, salt and pepper, and 1 cup of stock. Let simmer for 10 minutes and serve on slices of buttered toast. 23. —Spanish Omelet. Beat 6 eggs very light; add to them 1 cup- ful of fine chopped ham and 2 small onions chopped fine. Pour into a buttered dish and let cook until set; turn out on a hot platter. 24. —Rice Croquettes. Put 1 pt. of cold boiled rice in the double- boiler, and add to it 3 tablespoonfuls of milk ; mix lightly.
