When it is melted, put into it 1 lb. of calf’s liver cut fine, salt and pepper; brown care- fully and cook for 18 minutes. Take up the liver and place it on a hot platter. Add 1 slice of onion to the gravy and let it cook 1 minute. Add 1 tablespoonful of flour and cook, stirring until it begins to froth. Draw the pan back and gradually add 1 pint of milk and cook 1 minute, stirring all the time. Put the liver in the pan with the gravy and stew very slowly 5 minutes longer. 20.—Omelet Rudolph.
