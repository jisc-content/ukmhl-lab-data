134 NOVEMBER. i.—Mock Sausage. Chop i lb. of cold beef fine; add 3 tablespoonfuls of mashed potatoes, 1 egg, 1 tablespoonful of flour and a small slice of moistened bread; stir all together ; add salt, pepper and a little sage. Make in small cakes and fry first on one side and then on the other. 2.—Rice and Mutton Scallop. Cut cold mutton into dice (about 1 pt.). Add 1 pt. of cold boiled rice. Into a sauce- pan put 2 tablespoonfuls of butter; when it is melted put in the meat and rice; season with salt and pepper ; when it is hot stir in 2 slightly beaten eggs; stir until the whole is very hot and serve. 3.—Salt Codfish Omelet.
