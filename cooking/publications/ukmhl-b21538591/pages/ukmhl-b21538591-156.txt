Put in a saucepan a tablespoonful of butter and a tablespoonful of chopped onion and cook slowly together until the onion changes color. Add a tablespoonful of flour and a teaspoonful of curry powder; mix with the butter ; then add ^ of a pt. of milk. When smooth add more seasoning if liked ; draw to one side and simmer 10 minutes. Add 1 lb. of canned or boiled salmon freed from bones and skin and broken in good-sized pieces. Gently cook 15 minutes longer.—From “Table Talk,” Philadelphia. 5.—Oysters a la Reine.
