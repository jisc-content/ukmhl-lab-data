Season with salt and pepper, dust with flour, dip in beaten egg, roll in bread crumbs and fry in hot fat. 15.—Egg Gems. To 1 cup of fine chopped meat add 1 cup of fine bread crumbs, 1 spoonful of fine chopped onion. Season with pepper and salt and a spoonful of melted butter ; add enough milk to bind together. Have large gem pans well greased and nearly fill with the mixture; break an egg carefully on the top of each one ; dust with salt and bake 8 minutes.
