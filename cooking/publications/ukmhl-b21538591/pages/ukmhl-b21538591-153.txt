Cut 1 lb. of beef liver into inch pieces, wash and scald. Drain, place in a saucepan with 1 slice of onion, y2 a teaspoonful of salt, of a teaspoonful of pepper, 1 tablespoon- ful of walnut catsup and just enough boiling water to keep from burning. Cover closely and simmer until tender. Brown 1 table- spoonful of flour in 1 tablespoonful of butter; add gradually the gravy from the liver and stir until smooth and thick.
