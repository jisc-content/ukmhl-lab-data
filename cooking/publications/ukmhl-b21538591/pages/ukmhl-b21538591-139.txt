Cover with a pt. of stock; season with salt and pepper and a tablespoonful of chopped parsley; cover with another pan and let it cook for ^ an hour. After it has been in the oven about 15 minutes add to the gravy 1 can of French peas. When the chicken is cooked take it out and lay on a hot platter.
