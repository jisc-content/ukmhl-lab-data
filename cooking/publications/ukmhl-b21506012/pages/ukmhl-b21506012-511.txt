Buy a pound jar of the best French brandy68 Â°herry plums (prunes) and a bottle of good cherry brandy, take out the plums, put them into a clean stew-pan with as much light claret as will cover them, and two ounces of sugar; stew till soft and the wine has been nearly absorbed : replace them in the jar with what liquor may remain, and pour as much cherry brandy into it as the plums will admit. Finally screw on the top, and serve at dessert. Never let the jar be empty, but re-fill it as the plums are eaten, adding cherry brandy from time to time.
