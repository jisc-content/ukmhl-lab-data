8o POTATOES, SAUTA Peel several new potatoes and cut them into thin slices. Melt in the blazer two tablespoonfuls of butter, or of really good dripping. Chicken fat or dripping is especially good for this purpose. When it is very hot, put in the potatoes, and cook them to a light brown. LYONNAISE POTATOES. Slice a small onion very thin. Fry it in the chafing-dish in two tablespoonfuls of butter or dripping. Slice half a dozen cold boiled potatoes and fry them with the onion.
