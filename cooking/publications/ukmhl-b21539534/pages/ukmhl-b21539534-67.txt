Cniefftceang 53 CREAMED LOBSTER. One pint lobster. Half pint milk. Half pint cream. One tablespoonful butter. One heaping tablespoonful flour. Juice of half a lemon. Even teaspoonful salt. Pinch of cayenne. Make a sauce by cooking together over boiling water the butter and the flour, and adding to it the milk and half the cream. Put the lobster into this sauce, add pepper and salt, and stir until it is smoking hot. Put in then the rest of the cream, cook just long enough to heat this, squeeze in the lemon-juice, and serve. This is a delicious preparation of lob- ster.
