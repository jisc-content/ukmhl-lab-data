One pair sweetbreads. Parboil, blanch, and slice them. One dozen mushrooms, chopped coarsely. Half pint cream. One gill mushroom liquor. One tablespoonful butter. One tablespoonful flour. Yolks of two eggs. Salt and white pepper. Cook the butter and flour together over boiling water, pour upon them the cream and mushroom liquor. Stir until smooth, and lay in the sweetbreads.
