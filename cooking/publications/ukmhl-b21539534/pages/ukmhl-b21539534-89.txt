CHAPTER VIII MISCELLANEOUS DAINTIES FRIED TOMATOES. Cut the tomatoes into thick slices. Do not peel them. Put in the blazer two tablespoonfuls of butter, and lay the to- matoes in this. Turn them quite often, and cook until tender. Sprinkle with salt and serve. These make an excellent accompaniment to cold meat. DEVILLED TOMATOES.
