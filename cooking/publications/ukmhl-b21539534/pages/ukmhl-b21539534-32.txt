18 EGGS WITH CHEESE. Five eggs. Two heaping tablespoonfuls dry grated cheese. Parmesan or old English cheese is best for this. One tablespoonful butter. Half teaspoonful salt. A dash of cayenne pepper. Beat the eggs light, add the cheese, the salt, and pepper. Have the butter melted in the blazer, turn in the eggs, and stir until thick and smooth. Serve on toast or crackers. EGGS ON ANCHOVY TOAST. One tablespoonful butter. One tablespoonful flour. Half pint milk. Six eggs. One scant teaspoonful salt.
