SWEETBREADS, SAUTA Parboil and blanch the sweetbreads, cut them in half lengthwise, and then cut each half into two pieces. Sprinkle these with salt and pepper. Beat up one egg in a saucer with a tablespoonful of water. Dip each slice of sweetbread first in this and then in cracker dust. Have ready in the blazer two tablespoonfuls of but- ter, and when this is very hot lay in the sweetbreads. Cook until well browned.
