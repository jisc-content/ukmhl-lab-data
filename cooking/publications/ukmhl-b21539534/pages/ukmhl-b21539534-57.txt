They will brown very quickly, and must be watched closely and turned promptly, or they will scorch. Should they stick to the pan, add a little more butter. FRIED OYSTERS. For this also large oysters should be chosen. Drain them, sprinkle them with a little salt and white pepper, and roll them in flour or cracker dust. Heat four tablespoonfuls of olive-oil in the hlazer, and when it is boiling hot, drop in the oysters. Turn them when hrown on one side and cook on the other. Lift out with a fork.
