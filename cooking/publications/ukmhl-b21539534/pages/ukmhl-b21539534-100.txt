CHAPTER IX RECHAUFFES CHICKEN AU SUPREME. Cold roast or boiled chicken, cut into slices. The white meat is preferable, but both may be used. Two tablespoonfuls olive-oil. One tablespoonful butter. One tablespoonful flour. Half pint milk. One gill cream. Salt and white pepper. Lay the chicken in the oil for an hour before using. Turn it once, that each piece may be well coated.
