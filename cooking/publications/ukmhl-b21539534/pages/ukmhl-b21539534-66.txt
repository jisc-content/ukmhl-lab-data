CHAPTER VI WITH THE CRUSTACEANS CURRIED LOBSTER. One pint lobster. One pint weak soup stock. One teaspoonful onion, minced fine. Two teaspoonfnls curry powder. Scant teaspoonful salt. Brown the onion in the butter, add the salt, the curry powder, and the stock, and let them boil together for five minutes. Put in the lobster then, and serve as soon as it is heated through. If possible, boiled rice should be served with this.
