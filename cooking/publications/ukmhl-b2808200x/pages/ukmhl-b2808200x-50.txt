34 MENUS MADE EASY. SPRATS. Esprots a VAnglaise are fried. au gratin are baked with herbs and breadcrumbs. a la Diablc are devilled. STURGEON. Esturgeon en fricandeau is cut in thick slices, larded, stewed with bacon and mushrooms, and browned. d la Boyale—tied up in the form of a bolster, covered with forcemeat, and ornamented with pieces of truffle, tongue, etc. Dame d’esturgeon au four is a baked slice of sturgeon. braisee is larded and braised. grillee sauce piquante is broiled, and served with Piquante sauce. d la Bourguignotte is served with Bourguignotte sauce. TENCH. Tanchefrite is fried. sur le gril is grilled. au court-bouillon—boiled in court-bouillon, and served with caper sauce. farcie—stuffed and baked. TROUT. Truites au bleu are turned blue with boiling vinegar, and boiled in it with wine and vegetables; served with oil and vinegar. frites are fried.
