Petits Clioux a la Creme. 2 oz. flour. 2 oz. butter. 4 pint cream. 1 oz. castor sugar. Put the butter and water in a small saucepan, bring it to the boil, add gradually the sieved flour, stir till quite smooth over the fire, and till it no longer sticks to the sides. Turn it out to cool and add the eggs, well beaten. Beat the mixture for 5 to 10 minutes, and then place dessertspoonsful on a greased baking-tin. Bake in a moderate oven 30 to 40 minutes. Turn them on to a sieve to cool, after cutting a slit in the side. Whip, sweeten and flavour the cream and fill the buns with it through the slit, when they are cold. Dust sifted sugar over them and serve. BLACKCURRANT CREAM. Creme de Cassis. 14 oz. blackcurrant jam. 1 gill cream. Juice of half a lemon. Soak the isinglass in a teacupful of the milk.
