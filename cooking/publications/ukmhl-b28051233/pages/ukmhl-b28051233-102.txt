Sauce aux Framboises. 1 pint of water. 4 tablespoonsful raspberry jam. 1 lemon. 1 tablespoonful sherry. Put the water into a saucepan, squeeze the juice of the lemon into it, add the jam, mix well, and let it boil. Remove it from the fire, stir in the sherry, and serve.
