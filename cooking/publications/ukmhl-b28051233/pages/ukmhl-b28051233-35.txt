Add carmine to improve the colour if necessary. PORTABLE SOUP. 4 lbs. knuckle of veal. 3 lbs. gravy beef. 4 lbs. shin of beef. 3 large onions. Boil the meat in as much water only as will cover it. Remove the marrow from the bones, break them up, add a little spice and the onions, and boil all together. When the meat is done to rags, strain off the liquor and let it stand in a cold place. Take off the fat from the top when cold, and put the soup in a saucepan. Boil it fast and uncovered for 8 hours, stirring it constantly. Pour it into a basin and let it stand a day. Then place the basin in a pan of boiling water on the fire, let it boil again till the soup is thick and ropy. Pour it into flat, plain patty pans to form cakes. When cold turn out on to flannel to dry. Keep the cakes in tin canisters. Use as required, adding boiling water and flavouring. TURKEY SOUP. Break up the bones of a cold turkey and grill them. Place them in the stock-pot with 4 lb. lean smoked beef, a little bruised celery-seed, a small bunch of parsley and thyme, G dried mush- room stalks, a thin piece of lemon peel, pepper and salt to taste, and 2 quarts cold water. Let it come to the boil, skim well, and simmer gently for 4 hours. Strain into a basin and set aside till next day; then remove the fat, place the stock in a clean saucepan, and let it boil up.
