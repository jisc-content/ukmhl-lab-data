Oeufs farcis ciux tomates. 4 hard-boiled eggs. 8 anchovies. 3 tomatoes. Breadcrumbs. 2 oz. clarified fat. Cut the eggs in half and remove the yolks. Place the yolks in a basin with an equal quantity of tomato pulp and a small piece of butter. Pound them all together, and season with salt and cayenne. Stir it over the fire till hot, and then fill each half white with the mixture. Decorate with 2 fillets of anchovy on each egg and serve on a bed of browned breadcrumbs, heated with dripping, pepper and salt. FOIE-GRAS. 3 oz. smoked beef. Truffles. 6 oz. liver or poultry livers.
