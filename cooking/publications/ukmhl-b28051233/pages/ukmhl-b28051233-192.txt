LEMONADE. 4 lemons. | 1 quart of boiling water. Loaf sugar to taste. Rub some lumps of sugar on the lemons, to get the oil or essence of lemon. Put them into a large jug, squeeze the juice of the lemons over them. Pour the water over it, and let it stand ^ an hour. If not sweet enough, add more sugar. Strain, and use when cold.
