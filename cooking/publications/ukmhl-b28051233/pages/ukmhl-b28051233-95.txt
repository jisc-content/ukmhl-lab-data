Melt the butter, stir in the flour, add the milk, boil up, add seasoning, lemon juice, cream, anchovies pounded (or the essence), tammy, reheat, and serve. APPLE SAUCE. Compote lie Pommes. 1 lb. good cooking apples. 1 teaspoonful lemon juice. 1 oz. brown sugar. 1 wineglassful claret, if liked. Water. Peel, core, and quarter the apples, and put them into a sauce- pan with just sufficient water to keep them from burning. Add a little sugar and lemon juice. (A wineglass of claret is con- sidered a great improvement.) Rub it through a sieve, reheat, and serve. BECHAMEL SAUCE A LA CREME.
