SWEET DISHES, ETC. 107 APPLES A LA HEINE. Put a border of paste round a dish ; mix a quarter of a pound of very finely bruised sweet almonds with half a pound of sugar and six yolks of eggs; butter the dish, put this on it; pare and core some apples, put on each a bit of butter, and on the top put the whites of the eggs beaten to a froth ; bake it half an hour. APPLE FRITTERS. Pare some apples, cut them in thin slices; have ready a batter made of two eggs, a little milk, and flour; dip the slices in this, and fry them in lard; serve with sifted sugar over them. APPLE FRITTERS. Beat two eggs, put them in a pint of milk, make this into a thick batter with flour; take some apples and cut them (after they have been pared) in thin round slices ; dip each slice in the batter, and fry them in boiling lard, over a quick fire. APPLE MERINGUES. Make a pyramid of apples stewed in sugar, beat the whites of two eggs with two ounces of sifted sugar, put in on the apples, and bake it two hours. BUTTERED APPLES.
