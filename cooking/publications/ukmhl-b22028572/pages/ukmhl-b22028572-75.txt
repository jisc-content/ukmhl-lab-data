N CARROTS A LA MAlTRE DHOTEL. Boil the carrots tender, put a piece of butter into a stewpan with a little flour; when it is browned add a little gravy; then put in the carrots and let them stew a little : turnips can be done the same way, only they do not require so much gravy. STEWED CARROTS. Half boil some carrots, scrape them nicely, and slice them into a stewpan; put in some gravy, a little salt, and some cream; let them simmer until quite tender, but not broken; before serving rub a very little flour with a bit of butter, and warm up with the carrots. PUREE OF TURNIPS. Cut some turnips in slices, put them with two or three ounces of butter into a stewpan, stir it, put in half a pint of broth, dredge with flour, and add, if required, some cream.
