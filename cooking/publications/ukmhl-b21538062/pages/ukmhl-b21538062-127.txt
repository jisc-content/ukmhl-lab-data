Broiled Kidneys. Skin the kidneys, and make a deep incision in each,, rub them over with fresh butter, put a skewer through to keep them open, broil them on a gridiron over a slow fire; beat up to a cream two ounces of fresh butter with a dessert-spoonful of vinegar, add a little chopped parsley; put a teaspoonful of this mixture into each kidney, and shut them like a book.
