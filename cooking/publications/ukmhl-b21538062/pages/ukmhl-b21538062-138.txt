PIES. Venison Pastry, Cut a neck or breast into small steaks, rub them over with a seasoning of parsley, shallot, and a sprig of thyme minced very fine, add grated nutmeg, pepper and salt, roll the pieces of venison separately in the herbs, fry them slightly in butter, line the sides and edges of a pie-dish with puff-paste, place the pieces of venison in the pie-dish, add about half-a-pint of rich gravy made from the trimmings of the venison, add half a glass of port wine and the juice of half a lemon, cover the dish with puff paste, and bake it nearly two hours in a moderate oven ; when nearly done, open it a little at the top or side and pour in a little more gravy into the pie before serving it up.
