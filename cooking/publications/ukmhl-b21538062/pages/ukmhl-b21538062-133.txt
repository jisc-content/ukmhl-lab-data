Pork. ii 7 To Cure Hams, as in Ireland. To each ham put one pound of coarse brown sugar, one and a-half of salt, two ounces of black ground pepper, two ounces of allspice, and half an ounce of saltpetre; mix all well together and rub the ham well with it. Turn and rub them daily with their own pickle for six weeks, and hang them up to dry. They are much improved by being smoked. i
