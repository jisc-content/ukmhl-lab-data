Beat six ounces of sugar with two ounces of butter; add four yolks of eggs, one at a time, and beat till light; add the grated rind of two lemons or oranges and a little salt. Mix eight ounces of pressed curd with two ounces of ratafia biscuits, crushed; mix in lightly four whites of eggs, previously well beaten. Next, line two dozen or more tartlet pans with puff paste; put a little of the mixture on the paste; place a strip of candied lemon-peel on the top of each. Put them on a baking sheet, and place them in the oven, at moderate heat. Bake of a light brown colour. When done, shake a little sugar over them. Dish them on a napkin, and serve hot. Almond Cheese-Cakes. Line about a dozen and a-half of tartlet pans with puff paste. Skin and pound four ounces of sweet almonds and one ounce of bitter, with five ounces of white sugar, passed through a fine wire-sieve. Beat the whites of four eggs to a snow; mix the almonds and' sugar lightly with the whites of eggs; add one teaspoonful of cold water. Fill the centre of the tartlet pans three parts full; place them on a baking sheet; put them in the oven, at moderate heat. Bake for about half-an-hour. Dish on a napkin, and serve cold.
