238 Miscellaneous Recipes. Pomade for the Hair. Take one pint of salad oil, three ounces spermaceti, six drams bergamot, one dram palm oil, and four drops otto of roses. Mix these together in a basin ; dissolve before the fire, and put it into pots. Gold-Coloured Ink. Eight ounces of water, one ounce of pale bronze, one ounce of gum-arabic, one dram of ox-gall, and one dram of honey. Mix them all well together. Put into a bottle, and shake frequently until dissolved.
