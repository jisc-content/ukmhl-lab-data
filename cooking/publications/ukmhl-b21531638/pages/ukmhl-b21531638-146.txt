Mind to keep the meat hot till the gravy is quite ready. China Cliilo. Mince a pint-basin of undressed neck of mutton, or leg, and some of the fat; put two onions, a lettuce, a pint of green peas, a tea-spoonful of salt, a tea-spoonful of pepper, four spoonfuls of water, and two or three ounces of clarified butter, into a stew-pan closely cover- ed; simmer two hours, and serve in the middle of a dish of boiled dry rice. Cut off the chine-bone from the breast; and set it on to stew with a pint of gravy. When the bones will draw out, put it on the gridiron to grill; and they lay it in a dish on cucumbers nicely stewed.
