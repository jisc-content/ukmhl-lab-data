174 Menus Made Easy. Creme or eau de marrons—Chestnut cream or water ice. de gingembre — Ginger cream or water ice. In addition to these there are: Eau de ponche—Punch water ice. de melon—Melon water ice. de grenade—Pomegranate water ice. d’e'pine-vincite—Berberry water ice. de mille fruits—Mixed fruit water ice. de candle—Cinnamon water ice. If orange ice is made of Chinese oranges it is called de Ckinois. For water ices fresh fruit is necessary, as preserved fruit can ouly be used for cream ices.
