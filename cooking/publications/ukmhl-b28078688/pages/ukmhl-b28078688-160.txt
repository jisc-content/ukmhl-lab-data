In Fanchonettes a la Vanille the custard is flavoured with vanilla. Mirlitons aux fieurs cV or anger are tartlets filled with a hatter of eggs, sugar, butter, pounded macaroons, candied orange flowers, &c. They are flavoured in various ways— aux amancles, with almonds instead of the orange flowers; or au chocolat, with chocolate. Aux confitures they are half filled with jam before the hatter is put in. Talmouses are cheese-cakes. au citron—lemon cheese-cakes. aux amancles are made of pounded almonds with sugar, butter, ground rice and eggs. aux oranges—made of pounded almonds, orange-flower water, sugar, butter and eggs, with orange marmalade.
