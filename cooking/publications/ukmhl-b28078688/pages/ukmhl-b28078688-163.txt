c 149 Puddings. Ponding auxfirjues— a boiled mould of pounded figs, suet, milk, eggs, breadcrumbs, &c. a Vananas — Pine-apple pudding. Whip sauce, with the pineapple syrup, is served with it. an gingembre—Ginger pudding. an citron—Lemon pudding. aux oranges is made in the same way as lemon pudding, with oranges instead of lemons. an chocolat—a steamed mould of sponge cake in crumbs, butter, sugar, eggs, chocolate, &c. a 10 caramel—custard pudding in a mould with burnt sugar. It is usually served cold, with burnt-sugar sauce, or with burnt brandy and sugar. d la marmelade—a steamed mould of flour, brown sugar, milk, eggs, marmalade, &c.; served with marmalade sauce. souffle—a very light steamed pudding; it is served with wine or marmalade sauce, or with a syrup of fruit. cle cabinet, or Cabinet ponding—Cabinet pudding. This is also iced and served as Ponding de cabinet glace, an biscuit de Savoie—a sponge cake with brandy, steamed in.a mould with custard and dried cherries.
