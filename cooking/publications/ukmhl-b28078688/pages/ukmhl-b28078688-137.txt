123 Vegetables. Champignons a la mace arc stewed, and served in white sauce. a la Bordelciise are soaked in oil, broiled, and served in the oil with chopped parsley, &c. farcis or an grcitin are filled with stuffing, sprinkled with breadcrumbs and baked. They are served with or without brown sauce. Cut in slices they are dressed en rag obit, with broth, chopped parsley, &c.; or a la creme, with butter, cream, &c. For Puree cle champignons they are chopped and served with butter, gravy, &c., garnished with croutons. ONIONS. Stewed Portugal onions are served as Oignons aa jus. Stewed in a white sauce with puree of ham they are en ragoilt. For Oignons farcis the centres are cut out, they are stuffed, and cooked with bacon, &c. Small onions served in Poulette sauce are a la poulette. Au sirop dure they are stewed with butter, sugar, &c., and arc served in the sauce. A puree of onions with stock is called Puree
