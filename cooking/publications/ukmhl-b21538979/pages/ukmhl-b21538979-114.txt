The Cake and 102 *Little Biscuits J lb. flour 2 eggs i lb. butter J lb. powdered sugar A small tea-spoonful baking powder A small wine-glass of sherry Put the flour, sugar and baking powder into a basin and stir well together. Rub in the butter and add the well beaten eggs. Mix with the wine into a paste just firm enough to roll out on a paste-board. Cut out in little rounds with a small wine-glass. Bake on a floured tin until a delicate colour, like nicely baked pastry. *Little Dutch Cakes 4 ozs. butter 4 ozs. white sugar 4 ozs. flour Vanilla 2 yolks 1 white of egg 1 oz. almonds blanched and chopped Mix the butter and sugar thoroughly together. Add the flour. Flavour with a few drops of vanilla. Beat the yolks and add them to the mixture. Roll out the paste. Shape it into rings. Dip each in the white of egg and sprinkle over them the chopped almonds.
