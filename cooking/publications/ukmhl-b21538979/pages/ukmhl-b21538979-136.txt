Cake and Biscuit Book 124 Yorkshire Cakes 1 lb. flour 2 spoonfuls yeast 1 egg 3 ozs. butter \ pint warm milk Rub the butter into the flour. Add the yeast, egg and milk. Beat the whole well together. Set to rise in a warm place for three-quarters of an hour. Cut into round cakes. Set to rise again. Bake in a moderate oven. Wash over, when baked, with milk and sugar.
