The Cake and Boiled Icing 1 cup powdered sugar 1 cup water Whites of two eggs Flavouring Boil the sugar and water together until it spins a thread from a spoon. Then take the syrup off the fire and pour it into a basin. Add the whites (beaten to a stiff froth) to it gradually, beating hard all the time. Continue beating until the mixture is cold. Add flavouring. Soak the gelatine in the cold water for half-an- hour. Add to it the hot water and dissolve it in the same. Add it to the sugar and beat till smooth and firm. Add the milk to the sugar in a saucepan. Stir it till it boils.
