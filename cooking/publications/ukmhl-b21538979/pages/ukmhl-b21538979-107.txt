Coburg Cake 3 cups flour 1 cup sugar i cup syrup ^ cup water 1 cup butter milk (or sour milk) lb. butter 3 eggs 2 tea-spoons soda 2 tea-spoons ground ginger 2 tea-spoons ground cinnamon 1 tea-spoon allspice 2 ozs. sweet almonds Beat the butter and sugar to a cream. Whisk the eggs till light and creamy and mix with the butter. Add the syrup, water, spices and flour, mixing thoroughly, and lastly the soda dissolved in a little boiling water. Blanch and chop the almonds, and sprinkle them in the bottom of the patty pans. Pour in the mixture and bake in a quick oven for about twenty minutes.
