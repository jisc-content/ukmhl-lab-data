Two cups brown sugar, scant half cup butter, two eggs (save out one white for frosting), one cup sour milk, one teaspoon soda, two and a half cups flour, cloves, cinnamon and nutmeg to taste. Make two layers. For filling make a boiled frosting of white of one egg and three-quarters cup of sugar. 'Add one cup chopped raisins. This can be used without the frosting as two separate cakes.
