143 PASTRY. Make meringue by beating whites of two eggs to a stiff froth, and add one tablespoonful of powdered sugar and a little lemon juice. Spread over top, put in oven and slightly brown. Mrs. Walter S. Newhall. LEMON PIE. Three lemons, grated rind and juice, three cups sugar and yolks of four eggs well beaten.
