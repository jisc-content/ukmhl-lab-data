When soft and dry, beat them briskly with a little salt and two tablespoonfuls of butter till white and creamy. Now add the yolks of two eggs well beaten, and two tablespoonfuls of cream. About twenty minutes before the potato is wanted whip the whites of. two eggs till firm, mix them lightly with the mashed potato, turn into a deep dish and bake till brown. If successfully managed, the potato will be light, puffy and delicious. Mrs. William S. Cross. POTATO PUFF.
