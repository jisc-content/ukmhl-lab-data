MEAT, POULTRY, GAME. 11G 303. Tendons de Veau a la Ravigote. Stew the tendons very tender in broth; let them cool, turn them, arrange them neatly in your dish, and pour over a cold ravigote. Border the dish with beurre de Montpellier or aspic. 304. Veal Cutlets. Take a leg of veal, cut the cutlets a neat size and shape, and beat them flat; strew over them a little black pepper and salt; beat up an egg with a little minced parsley and shallot; dip the cutlets into this, and then into bread- crumbs, and fry them a good brown. Serve with shallot- gravy or sauce piquante. 305. Macaroni Pie. Bone a fowl and cut it into quarters; blanch it, boil it tender with a few onions, thicken the gravy with the yolk of an egg. Wash the macaroni clean ; boil it for a quarter of an hour; have about two ounces of Parmesan cheese grated ; mix all together with pepper, salt, and a little good butter ; then put it into a raised paste, and bake in a sharp oven for one hour.
