Put half a pound of hartshorn shavings into three quarts of water ; boil to a quart; then add an ounce of the strained juice of Seville oranges or lemons, four ounces of Malaga wine, and half a pound of fine sugar; boil down to a consistence fit to drink when warm, or jelly when cold. 804. Iceland-Moss Jelly. Boil three-quarters of an ounce of Iceland moss (un- washed) in a pint and a half of water until only a pint remains; add the juice of a Seville orange, and take a wine-glassful two or three times a day. (Very nourish- ing and good in consumptive cases.) 805. Ivory-Dust Jelly. Boil one pound of ivory-dust in three quarts of water for eight hours. It will require no skimming. When done strain through a jelly-bag.
