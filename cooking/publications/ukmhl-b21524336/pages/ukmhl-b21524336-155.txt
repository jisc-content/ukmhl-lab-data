MEAT, GAME, POULTRY. 127 336. Godiveau, or Yeal Forcemeat. Scrape one pound of veal, half a pound of fat bacon, pound it fine in a mortar; add the crumb of a French roll, a teaspoonful of mace, and the same of nutmeg; a table-spoonful each of chopped onions, parsley, and mushrooms, or truffles, some pepper and salt; mix all this together, with two whole eggs, and rub it through a sieve. It is used on all occasions; for pies and forcemeat stuffing.
