Take some spinach that has already been served stewed ; mix two yolks of eggs with it, lay it on the toast, pass a knife dipped in egg over the top bread-crumb, and fry them. French beans are good dressed in the same way. The grease that runs from a goose when roasting should always be kept, as it is particularly good to dress spinach with. 397. Stewed Water-Cresses (Cornish Receipt). Lay the cresses in strong salt and water ; pick and wash them well, and stew in water for about ten minutes; drain and chop them ; return them to the stew-pan, with a bit of butter, some pepper and salt; stew till tender.
