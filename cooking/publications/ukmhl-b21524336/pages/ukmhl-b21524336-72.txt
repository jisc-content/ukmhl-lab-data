Put it into a stew-pan, and stir with a spoon over the fire till it boils. Good with all sorts of meat. 117. Green Sauce for Boiled Chicken. Take a teacupful of spinach-juice, add the juice of one lemon, two yolks of eggs beat up, and a teaspoonful of sugar. Just heat, and serve. 118. A Good Gravy for Game or Fowl. Boil some veal gravy with pepper and salt, and the juice of a Seville orange and a lemon. 119. Onion-Sauce. Peel the onions and boil them tender; squeeze the water from them ; chop, and add to them butter that has been melted rich and smooth with milk instead of water. Boil it up once. For boiled rabbits, shoulder of mutton, etc. 120. Onion-Sauce Brown. Roast four large onions, peel and pulp them into a rich stock with salt, cayenne, a glass of port wine, a little vinegar, or the juice of half a lemon; simmer and stir into it a small piece of butter.
