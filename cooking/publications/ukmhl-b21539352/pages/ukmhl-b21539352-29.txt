SAVOURIES AND SWEETS. 13 Anchovy Toast with Egg. For Four Persons.—6 anchovies; 6 rounds of fried bread; 2 eggs. Pound and pass through a sieve six anchovies, spread on rounds of fried bread; boil hard two eggs and pass the yolks through a sieve, sprinkle thickly over the anchovy toasts and serve very hot.
