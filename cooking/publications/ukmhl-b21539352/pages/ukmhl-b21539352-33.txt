For Four Persons.—3 eggs; 3 tablespoonfuls of rice; 2 ozs. of butter; 1 teaspoonful of chopped parsley; pepper and salt to taste; 1 onion; some slices of buttered toast. Two or three hard-boiled eggs chopped very fine; mix thoroughly with three tablespoonfuls of rice well boiled, but each grain must be dry and separate. Put into a stewpan with a bit of butter, a little chopped parsley, pepper, salt, and a little onion; when quite hot serve on a slice of fried or buttered toast. Gascony Butter. For Four Persons.—1 oz. of chopped parsley; 2 ozs. of anchovies; 2 ozs. of butter; cayenne to taste. Take equal quantities of parsley picked and par- boiled, of anchovies washed, boned, and pounded, also fresh butter; mix the ingredients well together, add cayenne, and pass all through a hair sieve; shape into little balls and garnish with parsley.
