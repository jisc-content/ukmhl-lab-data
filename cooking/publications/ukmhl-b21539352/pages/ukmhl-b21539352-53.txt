37 SAVOURIES AND SWEETS. Buttered Eggs. For Three Persons.—2 eggs; 1 oz. of butter; pepper and salt to taste; three slices of buttered toast. Take two eggs and one ounce of butter; melt the butter and add the eggs, stirring it the whole time. As soon as it thickens sufficiently, put it on slices of hot buttered toast, season with cayenne and salt. Kidney Omelet. For Two Persons.—3 eggs; 1 kidney; 2% ozs. of butter; cayenne and salt to taste. Take three eggs, one kidney, two and a half ounces of butter; skin the kidney and cut it very small, fry it in some of the butter till cooked. Mix the in- gredients for the omelet well, taking care to whisk the yolks and whites separately; season with pepper and salt, add the kidney. Melt the butter in the omelet pan and fry the omelet till nice and brown, turn and serve.
