SAVOURIES AND SWEETS. *4 good Mayonnaise sauce, in which you should have a little chopped parsley, chervil, tarragon, and chives. Sardines en Caisse. For Four Persons.—5 ramequin cases; 5 sardines; a dessertspoonful of anchovy sauce; 1 oz. of butter; 1 table- spoonful of cream; 1 oz. of fried bread crumbs; cayenne; black pepper and salt to taste. Bone and pound some sardines, add a little cream, anchovy sauce, cayenne, black pepper and salt, and some melted butter; mix all well together, place in the cases and put into the oven to heat thoroughly. Sprinkle bread crumbs on the top of each. Anchovy Toast aux Tomatoes. For Four Persons.—6 anchovies; a tablespoonful of tomato sauce; cayenne; 4 or 5 squares of fried bread. Take six anchovies, bone and pass through a sieve; mix with a little tomato sauce and cayenne, spread thickly on fried squares of bread, and serve very hot. Lobster k la Creme.
