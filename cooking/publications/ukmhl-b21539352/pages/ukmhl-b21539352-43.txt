Make a thick white sauce with a lump of butter in it and a little anchovy sauce, add cayenne pepper, pour over the shrimps, put bread crumbs on each, and brown in the oven. Puffed Eggs. For Six Persons.—4 eggs; 1 pint of cream or new milk; y2 oz. of butter; 1 red chilli; 3 olives; 6 capers; pint of good gravy. Beat four eggs lightly, and mix with them about a pint of cream or new milk; season with salt and pepper. Butter some small moulds and pour in the mixture, put the moulds into a saucepan with about two inches of water at the bottom of it; steam twenty minutes, turn them out and serve with a thick brown gravy; chop two red chillies, three olives and a few capers and add to the gravy.
