For Six Persons.—6 apples; 6 eggs; 1 quart of milk; 2 bay leaves; y2 saltspoonful of vanilla; a lemon; 6 cloves; 1 wineglassful of brandy; 2 ozs. of chopped almonds. Take six large apples, dig out the cores, make a custard (six eggs to a quart of milk, two bay leaves, and a few drops of vanilla); place a little finely cut lemon peel inside each apple, and one clove. Lay the fruit in a dish, pour the custard over, and bake half an hour.
