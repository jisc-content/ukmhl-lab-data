For Four Persons.—i slice of onion; half a beetroot, or ,the whole of a small one; teaspoonful of salt; J4 tea- spoonful of dry Finch’s mustard; teaspoonful of anchovy sauce; 3 tablespoonfuls of cream; 3 tablespoonfuls of milk. Fry a slice of onion in butter, then mix together half a teaspoonful of salt, half of dry mustard, half of anchovy sauce, one of flour, three of cream, and (three of milk; pour it over the onion in the saucepan 'and boil five minutes; lastly, slice in a moderate- sized boiled beetroot. If you have no cream, use double quantity of milk and a little more flour. This is to be eaten cold. Eggs in Pipkins.
