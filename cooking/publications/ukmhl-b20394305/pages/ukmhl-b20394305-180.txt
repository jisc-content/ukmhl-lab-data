Bake in but- tered pans three-fourths of an hour. Serve hot. GrRAHAM BrEAD. Three quarts of unbolted wheat flour, one quart of warm wa- ter, one gill of molasses, one teaspoonful of soda, dissolved in a little cold water; mix these well together; add a small teaspoonful of salt, and one gill of good fresh yeast. Make this into two loaves, and bake one hour, and cool gradually. No bread, or cake, should be put in an oven that is too hot; as it prevents them rising as they should. Delicious Rolls.
