326 Drinks and Food for the Sick. Carragheen Moss. To one quart of milk add half an ounce of moss; boil it till thick; strain it^ and pour it into moulds; flavor it with any- thing you prefer. Scotch Brose. Stir into a pint of nice chicken broth half a pint of best oat- meal, and a little salt; let this cook until the meal is entirely done, stirring continually. When done, if the invalid can take it, a little sweet butter is nice stirred in; this is nutritious and palatable.
