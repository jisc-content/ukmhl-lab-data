Plasmon powder ; 6 oz. suet; salt; x | lb. steak ; \ lb. kidney ; seasoning Method.—Put the flour, Plasmon, and salt in a basin, add the suet (finely chopped), and mix all with cold water into a stiff dough. Line the basin with half the paste ; cut the steak and kidney in nice pieces, season to taste, and put in basin till nearly full, adding a little water for gravy. Then cover oyer with remainder of the dough, join the edges round the basin, and trim off neatly. Wring your pudding-cloth out in boiling water, tie it over the basin, allowing room for the dough to swell. Put the pudding in a saucepan of boiling water, and boil 2 hours.
