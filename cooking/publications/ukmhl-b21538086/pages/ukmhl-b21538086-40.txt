PLASMON CHOCOLATE MOULD (without milk). —Ingredients : i tablespoonful cornflour; i tablespoonful Plasmon cocoa; g pint tepid, water; g pint boiling water; 3 oz. sugar; vanilla flavouring to taste. Method.—Put the cornflour and Plasmon cocoa into a basin, mix with the tepid water gradually; have the water boiling with the sugar in it (milk may be used in place of water if preferred). Add the mixed cornflour and Plasmon cocoa and vanilla. Let it boil for 5 minutes, stirring all the time. Pour into a wetted mould to set.
