Coat it with Cardinal sauce and serve hot. Anchovy Sauce. Sauce aux Anchois. Take one ounce of butter and half an ounce of flour, and mix them well together ; add one gill and a half of cold water. Put the stewpan over the fire and stir well with a wooden spoon till the mixture is quite smooth and boils. Take it off the fire and add a good dessertspoonful of anchovy essence, and stir it well into the butter. Six drops of chili vine- gar is an immense improvement to this sauce. Brown Butter for Broiled Fish. Beurre Noir.
