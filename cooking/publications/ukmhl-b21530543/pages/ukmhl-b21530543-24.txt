22 If you have a garden make the most of it. A bit of leek or an onion makes all dishes savoury at a small expense. If the money spent on fresh butter were spent on meat, poor families would be much better fed than they are. If the money spent on tea were spent on home-brewed beer, the wife would be better fed, the husband better pleased, and both would be healthier. Keep a little Scotch barley, rice, dry peas, and oatmeal in the house. They are all cheap, and don't spoil. Keep also pepper and ginger. Pay your debts, serve God, love your neighbour. THE ENDc
