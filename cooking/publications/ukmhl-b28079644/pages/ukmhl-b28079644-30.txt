Cut some cold mutton into thin slices, sprinkle with flour, salt, and pepper, and put them in a greased pie dish with alternate layers of sliced tomatoes. Moisten with stock or water. Cover with a layer of mashed potatoes, decorate the top suitably, brush over with beaten egg, and bake in a fairly hot oven for about three-quarters of an hour. PARMENTIER TURNOVERS.
