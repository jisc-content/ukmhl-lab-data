SAVOURY ROLY-POLY PUDDING. Ingredients.—\ lb flour, 6 ozs. sausage-meat, one table- spoonful cream, a pinch of cayenne, gill rich tomato or piquante sauce, 4 ozs. chopped beef suet, one yolk of egg, one pinch of powdered sage or mint. Method.—Mix the flour and suet together, add a pinch of salt, and work into a paste with the needful quantity of water. Mix the sausage-meat with the egg-yolk, cream, sage or mint, and cayenne, and work together till quite light. Roll out the paste on a floured board, spread over with a layer of sausage-meat as above pre- pared ; wet the edges of the paste, roll up, and wrap in a wet and floured cloth ; tie the ends tightly with string, and boil for one hour and a half, or steam for two hours. Take it up, untie, remove the cloth, cut the ends of the roll, and place on a hot dish, pour over some hot tomato or piquante sauce, and serve. BACON OLIVES WITH SHARP SAUCE.
