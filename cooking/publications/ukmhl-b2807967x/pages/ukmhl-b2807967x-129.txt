Beat 3 eggs thoroughly, add a cup of castor sugar, 1 ditto of flour; stir them well together, 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar, a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda. The soda to be dissolved in a very little warm water. Have a tin about f yard long, a ^ wide, grease the tin with almond oil, pour the mixture in, bake quickly, place a sheet of writing paper over the top to prevent its scorch- ing ; it must be quite yellow when done. Slip it out of the tin on a clean cloth, the bottom side up, spread over it raspberry or strawberry jam or orange marmalade, then gently roll it up and cut it as wanted.
