No. 389.—To Make Tea Cakes. 1 lb. of roll dough, ^ lb. of fresh butter, ^ lb. of sugar, 1 egg beat into a cup of milk, warm the butter in the milk, then mix all well together; a small pinch of salt, divide the mixture into 4 pieces, shape them round, bake them in a brisk oven for 20 or 30 minutes.
