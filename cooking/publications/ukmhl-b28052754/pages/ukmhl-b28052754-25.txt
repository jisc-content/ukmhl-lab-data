9 Season as usual, but instead of “garnish” sprinkle lightly with chopped fennel and marjoram. Ham Sandwiches and Salad. Toast some thick slices of bread on both sides, then cut them lengthways with a sharp knife. When cold spread slices of ham or tongue on the untoasted side of one piece and butter the other. Spread a little mustard over the meat, and cover it with cress or chopped lettuce. Put the buttered piece over it, and press with the bottom of a plate so as toi get the two pieces to adhere. Then cut into small sandwiches.
