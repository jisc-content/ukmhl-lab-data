210 MENUS MADE EASY. Canapes d'ceufs are biscuits or tartlets of cheese or anchovy pastry spread with buttered eggs. With curried eggs, they are a la Madras. h, Madame—tartlets of anchovy pastry filled with chopped potato in Mayonnaise sauce, garnished with fillets of anchovies, and aspic. a la Prince de Galles—very small rolls filled with chopped ham, anchovies, gherkins and truffles, with oil, vinegar, etc. The tops covered with aspic or Mayonnaise sauce. Croustades a la Versailles are little souffles of anchovy on croustades of anchovy pastry. d la Mancelle—croustades of anchovy pastry filled with a savoury puree of chestnuts.
