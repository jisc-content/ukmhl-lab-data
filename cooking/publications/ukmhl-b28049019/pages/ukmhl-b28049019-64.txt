MENUS MADE EASY. 48 Tournedos d la sauce poivrade. Slices of cooked fillet dressed in a crown alternately with fried slices of bread of the same size; Poivrade sauce in the centre. This may be served with olives or French beans, instead of Poivrade sauce; and would then be called Tournedos aux olives, or aux haricots verts. Slices of the fillet served with Piquante, Hol- landaise or other sauce, are called after the sauce with which they are served. 2 la Naples, they are served with a cheese sauce; aux pruneaux, with a compote of prunes. Mironton de hosuf are slices of cooked beef in a savoury brown sauce, covered with bread- crumbs and baked. This dish is often called Escalopes de hoeuf au gratin. It may be served aux cdpres—with a sharp sauce and capers, and d la St. Jacqiies—with a garnish of mushrooms and cherries. Mironton de hoeuf aux clioux is the dish known as Bubble-and-Squeak. Salade de hoeuf d la Chateau. Little rolled slices of beef served on an aspic border with a mixed vegetable salad in the centre. Bouilli en matelote.
