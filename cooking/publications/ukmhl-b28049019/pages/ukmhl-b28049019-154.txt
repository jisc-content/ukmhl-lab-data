138 MENUS MADE EASY. Quails—Cailles. They are hardees aux feuilles de vignes in the same way as pigeons. Rabbit—Lapin. Young rabbit—Lapereau. A brace of young rabbits—Accolade de lapereaux. Ruffs and Reeves—Paons de mer. Shovellers—Rouges de riviere. Snipes—Becassines. Teal—Sarcelle. They are served au cresson with watercress; and d la Bigarade, with Bigarade sauce. Turkey—Dindon (m.), or Dinde {{.). Turkey poult—Dindonneau. They are pique, larded; harde, barded; and farci, stuffed with forcemeat and truffles. Widgeon—Canard siffieur. They are sometimes called Macreuses. Wild Duck—Canard, or Caneton, sauvage. Woodcocks—Becasses. Served d la Piemontaise, they have game sauce and slices of truffles.
