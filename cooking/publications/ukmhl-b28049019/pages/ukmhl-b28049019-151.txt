REMOVES OR RELEVES. 135 Larks or other small birds are dressed as Boicdin d la Chipolata, with Chipolata garnish in suet crust. Pate froid de gihier is a cold pie made of the meat of several kinds of game. Pate a la Yorkshire is composed of goose, hare, grouse, and snipes, with spices, sausage-meat, and slices of ham.
