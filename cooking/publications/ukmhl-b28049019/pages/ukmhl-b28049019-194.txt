Ponding aux marrons—a steamed pudding of pounded chestnuts with butter, milk, eggs, etc. It is served with apricot syrup or jam. aux pommes—a pudding of alternate layers of breadcrumbs and stewed apples. It can be baked or steamed. With the addition of powdered ginger and spice it is called Pond- ing de pommes au gingenibre; with ginger syrup and pieces of preserved ginger, a Vlndienne; or with almonds and currants, d la Suisse. aux figues—a boiled mould of dried figs, suet, milk, eggs, breadcrumbs, etc. Aux duties is made in the same way with dates; aux raisins, with raisins. a Vananas—Pineapple pudding.
