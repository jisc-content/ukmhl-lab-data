They are also served with tomato, Jardiniere, or other sauce, or with dressed cucumber or asparagus, and named after the sauce or garnish with which they are served. Veal cutlets dressed in this way are often called Grenadins de veau instead of cotelettes. a la St. Garat—larded with bacon and tongue, and braised.
