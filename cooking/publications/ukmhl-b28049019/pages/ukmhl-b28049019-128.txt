A la Bourgeoise, it is stewed with vegetables. A la Milanaise, it is braised, and served with a Milanaise garnish. Boasted, it is served d la puree de celeiis, with stewed celery, and celery sauce. A la St. Clair, with slices of fried ham and tomato sauce. A la Bruxellaise, with Brussels sprouts and white sauce. A la Piintaniere, with spring vegetables. A Breast of veal as— Poitrine de veau rotie is roasted; d la Soubise, it is served with Soubise sauce. Boned, stuffed, rolled, and braised, it is served d la Jardiniere—young vegetables; or d la Chipolata—mth Chipolata garnish.
