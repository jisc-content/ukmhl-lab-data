Cbeese Mafets. Twelve wafer biscuits, two ounces grated Parmesan cheese, ounce of butter, cayenne and mustard. Mix the grated cheese well with the butter, a little cayenne-pepper and mustard ; spread this cheese-paste over the biscuits. Put them on a baking-sheet, and bake for five minutes in a hot oven.
