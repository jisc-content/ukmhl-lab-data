Cut the vegetables in halves, and boil them in three quarts of water for two hours. During this time soak the barley in the gill of water. Strain the broth through a fine sieve, putting aside a piece of carrot and celery to be cut up in thin shreds. Return the clear liquor to the saucepan, put in it the barley, pepper and salt, and boil for two hours, or until the barley is quite soft. Strain again through a sieve.
