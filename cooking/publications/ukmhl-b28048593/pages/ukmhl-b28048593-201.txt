One pound of dour, nine ounces of butter, five ounces of currants, five ounces of white sugar, the yolks of three and whites of two eggs, cinnamon and nutmeg to davour. Rub the butter into the dour, add the sugar, the yolks and whites of the eggs well beaten, then the currants and davouring. Mix well together, roll once across on a pastry-board, cut out round biscuits, and bake in a moderate oven.
