Cbocolate ipubbino* One and a half ounces of ‘ chocolat Menier’ grated, three-quarters of a pint of milk, two eggs, a tablespoonful of milk. Mix the chocolate smoothly with the table- spoonful of milk ; pour on to this the three- quarters of a pint of milk boiling, and mix well together. Boil quickly for five or six minutes, by which time the chocolate will be dissolved. Remove the pan from the fire, and when it has cooled a few minutes, add the eggs well beaten previously.
