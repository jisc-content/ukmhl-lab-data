Half a pint of Patna rice ; half a pint of milk ; six eggs ; peel of one lemon ; a spoonful of ratafia flavouring ; sugar to your taste, and some sweet almonds. Boil the rice in the milk until very tender ; then add the eggs well beaten, the pounded sugar, the peel of a lemon grated, and a flavouring of ratafia, or essence of lemon. Mix all the ingredients well together, and boil them in a mould for an hour and a half. When done, turn it out, cut the almonds (after they have been blanched) into long shreds, and stick them all over the pudding. Serve it with a very rich custard poured over it. Potato Pudding.
