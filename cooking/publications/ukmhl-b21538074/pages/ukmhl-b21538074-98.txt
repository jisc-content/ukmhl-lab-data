One egg. Half a gill milk. Three tablespoonfuls marmalade. Make the bread crumbs, shred and chop the suet finely, shred the candied peel, and grate the rind of the lemon. Mix all the dry ingredients, beat up the eggs, add the marmalade and the milk, and pour the liquid into the dry ingredients.
