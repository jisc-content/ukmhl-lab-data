Pour this mixture into the omelet pan, and shake the pan over a gentle heat until the omelet is beginning to set and rise; then place the pan in a steady oven, and cook until the omelet is well risen and of a light brown colour. When ready, turn the omelet out on to a piece of sugared paper, put the jam in the centre, fold the omelet over, and serve at once.
