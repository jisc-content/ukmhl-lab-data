No. 290.—Macaroni Pudding. Three ouuces of macaroni; one pint of new milk; quarter of a pint of cream ; four ounces of powdered white sugai’; two ounces of butter, and four eggs. Simmer the macaroni in the milk; beat the eggs well, adding the cream, sugar, and the butter, melted; when well mixed, pour in the macaroni and boiling milk, gradually, and bake immediately in a buttered dish, in a moderately hot oven, till lightly browned.
