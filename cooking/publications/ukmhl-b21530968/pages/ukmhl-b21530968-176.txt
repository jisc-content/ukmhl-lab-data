No. 408.—Gooseberry Custards. Three pints of green gooseberries; quarter of a pound of sugar; four eggs, and two table-spoonfuls of orange-flower water. Set the gooseberries in cold water over a slow fire, and simmer till soft; then di'ain the water away, and rub them through a sieve; to a pint of pulp add the eggs, the sugar, and orange-flower water; set it over the fire, stirring constantly till it becomes thick, aud when cold, serve in custard glasses.
