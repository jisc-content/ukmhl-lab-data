One pound and a half of flour ; three eggs; half a pound of sugar; one large tea-spoonful of carbonate of soda ; four ounces of butter; six ounces of currants, or Sultana raisins; the grated rind of a lemon, and half a pint of butter-milk. Kub the soda very fine, and mix it well in the flour, adding a pinch of salt, then the sugar, grated lemon-peel, and currants, or raisins, the butter, melted, and the eggs, well beaten; mix all well togethci- with the butter-milk, and bake immediately in buttered tins, in a rather quick oven.
