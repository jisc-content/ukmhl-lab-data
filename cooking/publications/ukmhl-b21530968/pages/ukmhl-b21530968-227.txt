No. 575.—JUMBALES. One pound of flour; half a pound of butter; three quarters of a pound of powdered sugar, and two eggs. Rub the butter into the flour, adding the sugar and the eggs, well beaten ; make it into a stiff paste ; roll it rather thin; cut it in shreds, and twist them into knots, rings, or any other form; lay them on baking tins; sift fine sugar over them, and bake in a moderately hot oven.
