Two pounds of flour; one pint of new milk ; quarter of an ounce of Miller’s baking-powder; one egg, and one large tea-spoonful of salt. Rub the baking-powder very fine and smooth; mix it, and the salt well in the flour; heat well the egg, and mix it, with the milk, into the flour; roll out the cakes in the usual way; let them remain quarter of an hour; and bake immediately in a quick oven.
