No. 335.—Bread Fritters. Half a pound of bread-crumbs; one pint of milk; the yolks of five eggs, and two ounces of sugar. Pour the boiling milk on the bread-crumbs; cover it with a plate, and when cold, beat it smooth, adding the yolks of eggs, the sugar, and a little grated nutmeg; fry it in fritters, nicely brown ou both sides, and serve with sifted sugar over. No. 330.—Carrot Fritters. Quarter of a pound of carrot; two ounces of flour; two ounces of sugar; two eggs, and two table-spoonfuls of cream. Boil, and then mash the carrots till smooth, adding the flour, sugar, a little nutmeg and salt, the cream, or milk, and the eggs, well beaten; beat all well together; fry in fritters, lightly brown on both sides, and sift sugar over them.
