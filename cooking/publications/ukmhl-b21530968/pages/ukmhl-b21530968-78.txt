No. 61.—Carrot Fritters. Quarter of a pound of carrot; two ounces of bread-crumbs; two table-spoonfuls of cream, and two eggs. BoU, and mash the carrot till perfectly smooth; add the bread- crumbs and cream; season with pepper and salt; add the eggs well beaten, immediately before frying; fry in fritters, and serve with brown sauce. No. 62.—Egg Frittebs. Six eggs, and a quarter of an ounce of eschalot, or sweet leeks. Beat the eggs well, adding one table-spoonful of spring water, the eschalot, chopped fine, and season with pepper and salt. Melt some butter in a frying-pan; pour in the eggs; fry them on both sides of a light brown colom-; cut them into squares, and serve with mint sauce.
