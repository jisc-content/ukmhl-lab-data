No. 225.—Potted Cheese. One pound of cheese; three ounces of butter, and one tea-spoonful of mixed mustard. Grate the cheese and mix it with the other ingredients; add a little mace and Cayenne pepper; beat all together with a potato-masher; put it into small jars, and pour clarified butter over it. Keep it in a dry place. No. 220.—Potted Cheese.
