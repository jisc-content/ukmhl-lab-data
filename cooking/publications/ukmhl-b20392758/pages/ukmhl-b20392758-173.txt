THE EOACK, The Eoach.—This fish is found throusfhout Europe, and the western parts of Asia, in deep still rivers, of which it is an inhabitant. It is rarely more than a pound and a half in weight, and is in season from September till March. It is plentiful in England, nnd the finest are caught in the Thames.
