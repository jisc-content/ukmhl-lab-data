Hare soup.—Cut a large hare in pieces, put it m an earthen jar, with three blades of mace, two large oni- ons, a little salt, a red herring, six large morrels, a pint of red wine, and three quarts of water. Bake it three hours in a quick oven, and strain the liquor into a stewpan, put in a quarter of a pound of French barley, ready boil- ed ; scald the liver, and rub it through a sieve with a. wooden spoon; put in the soup, place it on the fire, and keep it stirring till near boiling.
