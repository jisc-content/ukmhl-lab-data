Soak three biscuits and squeeze the water out well; cut up three large apples into small thick pieces; take two ounces of currants, the same quantity of stoned raisins, and melted fat, a table- spoonful of chopped lemon-peel, and a quarter of a pound of moist sugar. Mix altogether with a sufficient quantity of beaten eggs, place in a greased dish, and bake in a good oven. Lemon- juice should be put into the sauce served with it. Napoleon Cake. Mix half a pound of flour, six ounces of butter, two ounces of pounded sweet almonds, and two ounces of sugar together, and bind with the yolk of one egg. Set in the cold for an hour, then roll it out thinly ; bake it and spread each slice with rasp- berry jam ; then place one on the other sandwich fashion, paint the outside all over with some of the jam diluted, and ornament with pistachio-kernels.
