SEMOLINA PUDDING (with Caramel Sauce) Ingredients One pint of milk. Two teaspoonfuls of cream. Four dessertspoonfuls of semo= Sugar. lina. Vanilla essence. Two eggs. Two ounces of loaf sugar. Two ounces of butter. Put the milk into a saucepan, set it on the fire; when it boils, add the semolina, and stir till thick; then take it off, and stir into it the eggs lightly beaten, the butter, cream, and sugar to taste; add a few drops of vanilla or lemon essence if liked. Put two ounces of loaf sugar into a copper or tin saucepan, with enough water to cover it, and boil till it is a rich brown colour, then pour it into the tin or mould; when it is quite cold, put in the mixture, and steam for an hour and a half.
