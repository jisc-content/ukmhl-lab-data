154 OCICLYE COOKERY BOOK TANGERINE GIN Ingredients Half a gallon of gin. The rinds of three dozen Tan- Two pounds of sugar. gerine oranges. Cut the rinds of the oranges very thin, put them into a large-mouthed jar, with the sugar, and pour the gin over them. Let it stand for three days, stirring it three or four times a day; strain it off and bottle it. It should be kept a year or more before it is used.
