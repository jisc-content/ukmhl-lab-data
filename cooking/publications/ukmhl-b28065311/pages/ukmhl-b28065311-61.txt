Peel and cut up the onion into very small dice, put it into a stewpan with the butter, and stir it over the fire till rather brown; add the common vinegar, let it boil, add the stock and broth, and let it simmer at the corner of the stove for ten minutes; skim it well, then stir it over a sharp fire till rather thick, add the mustard, Tarragon vinegar, and a very little sugar and salt.
