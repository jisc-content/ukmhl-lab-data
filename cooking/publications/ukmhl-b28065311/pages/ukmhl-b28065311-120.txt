CHEESE SOUFFLES Ingredients Three eggs. Two ounces of grated parmesan A gill of cream. cheese. Pepper and salt. Beat up the eggs in a basin, add the cream, pepper, salt, and grated cheese, mix all well together. Put the mixture into small buttered moulds, and steam them care- fully for a quarter of an hour. Turn them out of the moulds and let them cool, then egg and breadcrumb them, and fry them a light brown in boiling lard; serve very hot. If kept waiting they must stand in the oven.
