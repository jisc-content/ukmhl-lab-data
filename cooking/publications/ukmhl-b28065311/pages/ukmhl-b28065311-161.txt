BREAD, CAKES, AND PASTRY 149 ALMOND ICING Ingredients Two pounds and a half of ground almonds. Three pounds and three=quarters of icing sugar. The whites of eight eggs. One teaspoonful of orange flower water. One teaspoonful of vanilla. Pass the sugar through a hair sieve, add the ground almonds, flavourings, and whites of eggs; work it into a stiff, dry paste, and use.
