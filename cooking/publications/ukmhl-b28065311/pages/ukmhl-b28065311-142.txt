This quantity makes eight good-sized loaves. WHOLE MEAL LOAF Ingredients Half a pound of wholemeal. One ounce of butter. Half a pound of flour. Salt. Nearly half an ounce of yeast. Half a teaspoonful of castor sugar. Rub the butter and flour into the meal, and a pinch of salt; cream the yeast in a basin with the sugar, add about half a gill of tepid water, make a well in the flour and pour in the yeast; let it stand for a quarter of an hour covered over with a cloth, then mix it well with nearly half a pint of tepid water; let it stand for rather more than an hour, make it up in any shape required, and bake it in a moderate oven for one hour.
