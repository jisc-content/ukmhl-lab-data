SCALLOPED ARTICHOKES Ingredients Jerusalem artichokes. Breadcrumbs. White sauce. Butter. Boil as many artichokes as are required, till they are very soft; then cut them up in pieces the size of a large pea; make a good white sauce with flour, butter, milk, and cream; season with pepper and salt, mix in the artichokes; butter some scallop shells and nearly fill them with the mixture; cover them with breadcrumbs, put a few bits of butter on the top of each, and bake in a hot oven for a quarter of an hour.
