Three=quarters of an ounce of Two onions. flour. One ounce of butter. Half a pint of stock. One dessertspoonful of curry Half a gill of cream, powder. Rice. Lay four nicely poached eggs on a hot dish, with or without little rounds of hot buttered toast under them. Pour the following curry sauce over them, and serve with a border of plain boiled rice : Chop the onions very fine and fry them in the butter for about five minutes, till a golden brown; add the flour, curry powder, and stock, and stir it over the fire till boiling; put the lid on, draw it to the side, and let it cook gently for about half an hour; then add the cream and use. CURRIED SARDINES Ingredients One teaspoonful of curry powder. Sardines. A little cayenne pepper. Buttered toast. Roll the sardines in the curry powder and cayenne pepper; lay them on a buttered baking tin, with a buttered paper over them; put them in a moderate oven for ten minutes, and serve on fingers of buttered toast.
