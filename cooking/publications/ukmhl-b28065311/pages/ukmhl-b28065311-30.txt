18 OCKLYE COOKERY BOOK WHITE SAGO SOUP Ingredients Two pints of good stock. A gill of cream. Two tablespoonfuls of small sago. Put the stock into a saucepan and let it come to the boil; then add the sago, and let it boil gently for about a quarter of an hour, stirring it occasionally; add the cream and serve. Clear soup may be used, but good white stock is best.
