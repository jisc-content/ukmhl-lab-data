OCKLYE COOKERY BOOK 112 MACARONI WITH SPANISH SAUCE Ingredients Two ounces of macaroni. One ounce of butter. One ounce of flour. One ounce of ham or lean bacon. One small carrot. Two eschalots. Two sprigs of parsley. One small bayleaf. One small sprig of thyme. Six peppercorns. Half a blade of mace. One clove. Two gills and a half of good brown stock. Parmesan cheese. Put the macaroni into a saucepan of boiling water with a pinch of salt, and boil it wrell for rather more than two hours. While it is cooking, put the butter into a stewpan, add the bacon, and the eschalots and carrot cut into thin slices; fry them for about five minutes, add the flour, and stir it over a gentle fire till it is a golden brown; add the stock, herbs and spices, and let it simmer gently for half an hour; strain it, skim off the fat, put it into a clean saucepan and add the macaroni; let it get thoroughly hot and serve. Grated cheese to be handed with it. MACARONI AND TOMATO Ingredients A quarter of a pound of macaroni. One ounce of grated parmesan Onion. cheese. Parsley. Tomato sauce. Boil the macaroni with a bit of onion and a little parsley for two hours; put it in a hot dish, sprinkle the cheese over it, adding pepper and salt. Have ready a good tomato sauce and pour over it all. Serve very hot.
