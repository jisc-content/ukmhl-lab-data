Wash and peel three or four fresh mushrooms, chop them and fry in half an ounce of butter, blended with a small chopped shallot ; season with salt and pepper to taste, and keep hot. Prepare a plain omelet, when ready to fold put the mushroom puree in the centre, then fold in the ends and allow the omelet to take colour ; turn it out on to a hot dish, and serve. Whisk up four or six fresh eggs in a basin, season with salt and pepper and pour the eggs into a well buttered fireproof souffle dish ; bake in a fairly hot oven till the omelet is set and of a nice golden brown.
