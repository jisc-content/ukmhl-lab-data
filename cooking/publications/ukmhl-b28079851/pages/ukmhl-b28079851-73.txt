Slice thinly IV lbs. of cold boiled potatoes, melt two ounces of butter in a fireproof earthenware frying or omelet pan, when hot put in the potatoes, and toss over the fire till they are thoroughly hot and slightly browned. Season with pepper and salt, shape neatly, dish up, sprinkle with finely-chopped parsley, and send to table. Curried Vegetables. Cut six small cooked and peeled potatoes into thick slices, to these add two small cooked turnips, four small cooked carrots, all cut into dice-shaped pieces. Mince finely a small onion. Heat up an ounce of butter in a brown fireproof dish, add the onion, and fry a nice brown.
