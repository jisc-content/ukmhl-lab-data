Cut up about a pound of cold roast beef; season it with a small teaspoonful of salt, a saltspoonful of pepper, a saltspoonful of celery seed, pounded, a small onion, chopped, and either a mustardspoonful of mustard or a teaspoonful of grated horse-radish. Put it into a pie dish with a teacupful of gravy or water, a teaspoonful of vinegar, and a tablespoonful of any kind of ketchup; cover it over with another dish, and bake it in a slow oven for nearly an hour.
