Cut your potatoes in slices, melt some butter in a frying-pan, and then put in your potatoes. Add salt, pepper, and chopped parsley; stir your potatoes occasionally till a good colour and crisp, then serve. You may also fry them in batter for a change; shake a little salt and pepper over them when frying. POMMES DE TEREE EN PUREE. Take some boiled potatoes, peel and mash them, then put them into a stewpan with some butter, salt, pepper, or sugar; stir the mixture gently, adding milk to thin it; let it boil up, and serve.
