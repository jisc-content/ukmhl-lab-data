Orange Jelly Infuse I ounce & a quarter of Isinglass in a pint of water, then add the juice of five large china oranges and the juice of half a lemon or Seville orange paired thin, a quarter of a pound of fine sugar ; boil all together and strain it, when near cold then put it into your mould.
