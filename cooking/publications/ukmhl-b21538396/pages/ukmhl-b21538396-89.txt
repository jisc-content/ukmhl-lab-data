AN OLD-WORLD RECIPE BOOK 85 To Cure Hams I lb. Bay Salt, ^ lb. Common Salt, one ounce of black pepper, 2 ounces of Saltpetre ; beat these all very fine and dry them all well before the fire, rub the Flour before the fire with these ingredients for five or six days, then put a pound and a half of treacle to the Ham, rub and turn the Ham every day for five weeks, then take it out of the pickle, and soak it 24 hours in cold water, let it hang to dry a month, when it comes home put a thick paste over it, and tye it up in brown paper. N.B. The above receipt is for a large Ham of 30 lbs.
