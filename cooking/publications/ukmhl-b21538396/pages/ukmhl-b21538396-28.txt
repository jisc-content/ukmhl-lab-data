A Spanish Onion Soup Take four Onions, pare and slice them, put them in a Saucepan with as much water as will cover them, put in a thick slice of Crumb of Bread, when well boiled, rub them through a sieve. Then put about Two oz. of Butter in the pan, fry it and add two spoonfuls of flour to thicken the butter, scald a pint of Milk in the same pan with the butter and put the onions to it.
