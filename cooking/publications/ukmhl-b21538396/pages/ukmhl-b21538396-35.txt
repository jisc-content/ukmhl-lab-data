To Stew a Neck of Mutton in Stakes Cut the Mutton in stakes, pepper and salt them, fry them a little brown, then take them out of the pan and put them into a stew pan, fill the frying pan with water, let it boil, and pour it upon the stakes, when it boils throw in peas, onions, herbs, turnips and carrots cut small, let them simmer till they are enough, then add more pepper, salt, or water if required. 3'
