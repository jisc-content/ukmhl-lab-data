An Excellent Lemon Pudding Beat well together four Oz. of fresh Butter, creamed, and eight of sifted Sugar, to these add gradually the yolks of six, and the Wliites of two Eggs, with the grated rind and strained juice of one large lemon. The last must be added by slow degrees, and stirred briskly to the other ingredients. Bake in a dish lined with Paste, three-quarters of an hour.
