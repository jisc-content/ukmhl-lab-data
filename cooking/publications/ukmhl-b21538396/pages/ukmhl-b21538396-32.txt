AN OLD-WORLD RECIPE BOOK 28 Onions, when brown put into a stew Pan with one pint of Stock, let it simmer, add a small spoonful of Currey Powder, more or less accord- ing to taste, a small I Tea spoonful of Cayenne Pepper, a small quantity of flour and butter. Pieces of rabbit to remain in the soup. Peas Soup Put ye meat into a pan with water to cover it. Let it stew gently, put in a few cloves & skim it well; the herbs must be stewed seperate for half an hour with a ^ lb. of Butter, then to be added to the liquor with the peas, and be stewed for an hour with pepper & salt.
