AN OLD-WORLD RECIPE BOOK To Stew Cheese Two ounces of scraped Cheese, a piece of Butter the size of a walnut, a middle-sized tea cup full of cream, and one egg, beat these together, put them into a pan, stirring it till it is melted, then pour it into the dish. Fromage Soufflee Equal portions of grated cheese and bread crumbs, the yolk of a raw Egg, some pepper and salt, all mixed up with some clarified butter, put them into a saucepan and let them just boil, then pour it into a dish. 6i
