Old Sportsman’s Receipt to Pot Grouse Pluck, singe, draw, wipe out with a dry cloth. Put whole into a brown pot, with pepper & salt inside each bird, also a piece of butter the size of a hen’s egg ; put the rest of the butter over and about them, tie two or 3 folds of paper tight over the pot, & bake slowly 2 hours.
