The Pudding To three eggs and two ounces of sifted Sugar, a quarter of a pound of Butter melted in a large teacupful of water, three tablespoonfuls of the Orange Paste, and the juice of one Lemon. The Butter must be cooled before it be mixed with the eggs. Put a nice Paste at the bottom of the dish, and bake the Puddings an hour.
