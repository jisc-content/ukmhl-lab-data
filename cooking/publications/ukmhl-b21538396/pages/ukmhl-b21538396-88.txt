Strain the Orange and lemon skins well through a strainer before the wine is put into the barrel, then put in 2 lbs. of Raisins. When the Wine has done working bung it down, and let it stand three months, when it should be drawn off, and take out of the Barrel all the peel and pulp, wash it out with a little of the Wine, and strain the bottom through a flannel bag, put it in the barrel again with ^ oz. oi Isinglass, bung it down as before, and let it stand (at least) 2 or 3 months before it is bottled.
