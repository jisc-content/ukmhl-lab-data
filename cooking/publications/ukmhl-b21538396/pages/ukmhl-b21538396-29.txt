AN OLD-WORLD RECIPE BOOK 25 Sugar and season with a little Mace. Ox Tail Soup Cut Two Tails in pieces, fry them a light brown, take some Onions and fry, rather brown, put some flour in the pan, keep the pan moving, till the flour and onions are brown, put all on, boil in as much water as will cover the Tails, boil all very tender, put sweet herbs, whole pepper, three blades of Mace, and a knuckle of ham. When enough, strain it off to stand all night. Take off the fat. Serve it up with Cut Carrot, Celery and Cayenne pepper. The pieces of Tail must be put in the Soup Tureen.
