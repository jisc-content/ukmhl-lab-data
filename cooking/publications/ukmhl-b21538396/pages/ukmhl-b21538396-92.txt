Fish Sauce Two spoonfuls of water, one of vinegar, two or three slices of an onion, 2 anchovies, some horseradish, put them into a saucepan and boil them till the anchovies are dissolved, strain the liquor and mix it up with butter and flour; you may add a little nutmeg and lemon peel.
