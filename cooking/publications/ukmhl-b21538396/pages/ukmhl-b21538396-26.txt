AN OLD-WORLD RECIPE BOOK 22 Peas Soup Put Lettice leaves, pea-scods, old Peas, mint, an onion or two, some pepper corns & the crumb of a penny loaf, into a boiler of water & when these are all boiled tender rub them through a sieve; then chop the heart of the lettice small and put into the boiler with young peas & thicken your soup with flower & butter, salt it to your taste.
