Dutch Milk Take a quart of Milk, a quarter of a lb. of Raw sugar, add Cinnamon to your taste, put sippets into your dish, pour your milk over it, and set it over the fire, simmer gently until your bread be soft. Take the yoks of two Eggs, beat them well, and mix them with a little milk, simmer altogether gently.
