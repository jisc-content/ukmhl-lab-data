AN OLD-WORLD RECIPE BOOK D 49 Orange Cream Take the peel of one Orange, the juice of three, four yolks, and two whites of eggs well beat and a quarter of a pint of spring water ; put to the juice half a lb. of sugar, then put in the eggs, stir it well and run it through a muslin strainer, put it over a slow fire and keep stirring till it is the thickness of cream.
