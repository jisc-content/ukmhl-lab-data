AN OLD-WORLD RECIPE BOOK 82 Spoons full of Yeast, bottle it in stone bottles and tie the corks down closely. English Frontignac Boil 18 lbs. white sugar in 6 gallons of water, with 2 whites of eggs well beaten, skim it, and put in a quarter of a Peck of Elder Flowers, do not keep them on the fire ; when nearly cold, stir it, and add 6 table-spoonsful of lemon juice, and 2 oz. of German Yeast on toast, beat the whole well into the liquor, stir it every day : put 6 lbs. of the best raisins, stoned, into the cask, and then the wine, stop it
