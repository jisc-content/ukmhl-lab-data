The wine made from the elderberries was a rare treat, when warmed up in a saucepan and taken hot, to a weary party of young ones, coming in late in the winter twilight, after a long walk home from a day’s skating. “It will warm the cockles of your heart,” as a dear old friend used to say to us, and we thoroughly agreed with that wholly inexplicable phrase !
