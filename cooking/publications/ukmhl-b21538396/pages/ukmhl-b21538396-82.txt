To Clarify Isinglass Put a quarter of a lb. of isinglass in a small stew pan, just cover it with a little spring Water, and add a piece of Sugar the size of a Walnut, shaking the stew pan to prevent its sticking to the bottom, when upon the point of simmering add the juice of half a lemon, let simmer about a quarter of an hour, skim, and pass through a strainer.
