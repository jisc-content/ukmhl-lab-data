2S COOKERY FOR INVALIDS. Egg Soup. Slice two onions, fry a light brown in a little butter, add to them three pints of water and let it reduce in boiling to one quart; when the onions are tender, strain them out of the liquor and put them aside. Let the liquor boil and add to it one table- spoonful of flour mixed smooth in a quarter of a pint of milk, half a teaspoonful of salt, a pinch of pepper, and a pinch of sifted sugar; stir until the soup has thickened, then remove it from the fire, and stir in gradually the yolks of four eggs. Have ready in the soup tureen two ounces of pulled bread or slices of roll dried in the oven; pour the soup on to the bread and serve. Oatmeal Soup.
