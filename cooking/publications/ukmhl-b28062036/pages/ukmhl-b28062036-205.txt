As bananas are so much with us all the year round it leaves us small occasion to use the dried bananas which are sent over from the West Indies, yet when these are stewed tender, and a little sherry or juice of limes and sugar added to them, they are extremely nice, and stewed banana added to a fritter batter is every whit as good as slices of the fresh fruit.
