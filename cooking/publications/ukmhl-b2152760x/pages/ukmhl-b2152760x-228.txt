202 THE IMPERIAL AND CHOCOLATE TART. Rasp a quarter of a pound of chocolate and a stick of cinnamon ; add to them, fresh lemon-peel grated, a little salt, and sugar; take two spoonfuls of fine flour, and the yolks of six eggs, well beaten and mixed with milk; put all this into a stew- pan, and let them be a little time over the fire; then take it off; put in lemon-peel, cut small, and let it stand until cold : beat up enough of the whites of eggs to cover it, and put it into puff-paste : when baked, sift sugar over, and glaze it with a sala- mander.
