THE IMPERIAL AND 12 RICE SOUP WITH A CHICKEN. Blanch about half a pound of rice, and put it into a stewpan, with one or two chickens, and a quart of stock ; set it on the stove to boil very slowly, until the chickens are very tender, and the rice the same ; then put as much stock as will fill the tureen; skim the fat very clean from the soup.
