There is not the smallest doubt that French beans are a very good and wholesome vegetable to take to sea on long voyages, as the salt is very easily ex- tracted, by putting them in warm water for about two hours before using: the ex- pense of trying them would be very trifling; the early part of September is the time they are very plentiful, and generally cheaper, or as cheap, as any other vege- table.
