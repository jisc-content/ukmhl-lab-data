Boil half a pound of rice to a jelly in a small quantity of water; when cold, mix it with a pint of cream, eight eggs, salt, and nutmeg, stirring a quarter of a pound of butter just warmed; add as much flour as will make the batter thick enough: fry them in as little lard as possible. PINK COLOURED PANCAKES. Boil beat-root till tender, and then beat it fine in a mortar; add the yolks of four eggs, two spoonfuls of flour, and three or four of cream ; sweeten it, and grate in half a nutmeg: add a glass of brandy : mix all well together, and fry your pan- cakes in butter: garnish them with green sweetmeats.
