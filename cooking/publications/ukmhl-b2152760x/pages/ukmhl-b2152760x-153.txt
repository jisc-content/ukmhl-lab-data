Raise the skin from the breast-bone of a fowl with your finger ; take a veal sweet- bread, oysters, mushrooms, an anchovy, pepper, nutmeg, and lemon-peel ; chop them small, and mix them with the yolk of an egg ; stuff this between the skin and the flesh, but do not break the skin; put oysters in the body of the fowl, paper the breast, and roast it ; make good gravy, and garnish with lemon.
