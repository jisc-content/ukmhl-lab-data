Cut a slice, three inches thick, from a round of beef, with some fat to it; stew it in a quart of second stock, and add a glass of white wine; season with salt, pepper, cloves, sweet herbs, and a bay leaf; boil it till the liquor is nearly gone, and send it to table cold.
