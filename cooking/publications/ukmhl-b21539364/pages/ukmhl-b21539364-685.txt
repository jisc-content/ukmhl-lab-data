591 Trussing of Hare. Thirdly, cut the sinews of the legs and bring them forward, well against the body, running two skewers right through, one fixing the two fore, and the other the two hind legs. SKEWERING OF LEGS, Fourthly, lay the head rather back and skewer it there, passing the skewer through the shoulders, and see that it is skewered firmly and evenly; that it may present a good appearance when cooked.
