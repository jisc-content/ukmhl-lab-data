Average Cost, id. 2504.- NEAPOLITAN CAKE. Ingredients.- ^ lb. of butter and the same quantity of flour, sugar and almonds, apricot or any other jam, a few preserved cherries, 2 eggs, and £ lb. of icing sugar. Mode.—Rub the butter with the flour, and add the almonds, blanched, chopped and pounded to a smooth paste, and mix with the yolks of the eggs. Roll the paste rather thin and cut it in rounds and bake till yellow in a moderate oven on a buttered tin. When cold, spread each round with jam, and pile one over the other evenly. Beat the icing sugar with the whites of the eggs and ice the cake, decorating it with the cherries. Time.—About 15 minutes to bake the cakes. Average Cost, is. $d. Seasonable at any time. 2505.—A NICE CAKE. Ingredients.—1 lb. of flour, 4 lb. of ground rice, | lb. of fine white sugar, 5 eggs, lemon to taste. Mode.—Mix the flour, rice and sugar thoroughly, beat the eggs, yolk and white separately, 20 minutes, add the yolks to the white, then sift in the other ingredients ; bake in a buttered tin from 35 to 45 minutes, according to the heat of the oven. Time.—J to f hour. Average Cost, 9d. Seasonable at any time. 2506.-PICCOLOMINI CAKE, Ingredients.—1 cup of butter, cups of sugar, 4 eggs, 1 teacup of milk, 4 cups of flour, % teaspoonful of soda, 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar. Mode.—Beat the butter and sugar to a cream; add the eggs, beaten to froth, then milk, with the soda dissolved in it. Put cream of tartar dry into the flour, season with rosewater and nutmeg, or extract of almond. Time.—40 minutes. Average Cost, is. Seasonable at any time.
