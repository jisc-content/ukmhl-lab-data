Ingredients.—2 oz. of butter, 2 small onions, 1 carrot, £ small teacup- ful of flour, 1 pint of new milk; salt and cayenne to taste. Mode.—Cut up the onions and carrot very small, and put them into a stewpan with the butter; simmer them till the butter is nearly dried up; then stir in the flour, and add the milk ; boil the whole gently until it thickens; strain it, season with salt and cayenne, and it will be ready to serve.
