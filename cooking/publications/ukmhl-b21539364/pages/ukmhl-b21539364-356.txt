Recipes for Cooking Fish. 276 590.—FISH MACARONI. Ingredients.—Fresh-boiled or cold remains of fish, 2 lbs.; j a lb. of macaroni, 3 oz. of grated cheese, 1 oz. of butter, pepper, salt. Mode.—Flake the fish carefully, removing all skin and bones. Boil the macaroni till tender in salted water, then cut it in small pieces. Mix the fish, macaroni and grated cheese together, then put the whole into a but- tered dish, smooth the top, grate some cheese over it, put some bits of butter on the top, brown before the fire or with a salamander, and serve up very hot. Time.—Altogether, £ hour. Average Cost, with fresh fish, is. 6d. Seasonable all the year. Sufficient for 4 persons. FISH ENTREES. 591.—LOBSTER CREAM. (Fr —Creme d’Homard.) Ingredients.—I lobster, 3 eggs, 2 teaspoonfuls of anchovy sauce, a cupful of bread-crumbs, 1 of a pint of cream, £ a pint of milk, a little cayenne. Mode.—Boil the milk and pour it over the bread-crumbs. Chop up the lobster very fine and beat the eggs, and when the bread-crumbs are nearly cold, mix with them the lobster, the sauce, the seasoning of cayenne and a little salt, and lastly the cream. Butter a mould, and fill it with the mixture, put a buttered paper over the top and steam for 1 hour. Any good fish sauce can be served with this, or it can be eaten without. Average Cost, 2s. Seasonable all the year round. Sufficient for 6 persons. 592-—CURRIED LOBSTER. (Fr.—Homard a Plndienne.)
