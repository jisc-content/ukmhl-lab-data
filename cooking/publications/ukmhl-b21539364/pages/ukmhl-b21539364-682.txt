The bird having been well plucked and singed : First, cut off the feet at the joint, the pinions at the first joint, and the neck close to the back, leaving enough skin to turn over the back. GOOSE READY FOR TRUSSING. Secondly, loosen the liver, &c., at the throat end, and cut the bird open between the vent and the rump ; draw it, wash and wipe the inside and beat the breast bone flat with a rolling-pin. TRUSSING OF GOOSE. Thirdly, put a skewer through the under part of one wing and bring it through the other. Skewer the legs by passing the skewer through the first joint and carry- ing it through the body to secure the other.
