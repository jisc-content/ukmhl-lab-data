Mode.—Have ready a clear brisk fire, and put down the joint at a sufficient distance from it that the fat may not burn. Peas, spinach, or cauliflowers are the usual vegetables served with lamb, and also a fresh salad. Time.—A shoulder of lamb rather more than 1 hour. Average Cost, 10d. per lb.
