Marlborough Pudding. 853 1810.—MACARONI PUDDING. (Simple Method.) ingredients.—£ lb. of macaroni, pint of milk, 3 eggs, 3 tablespoon- fuls of brown sugar. Mode.—Boil the macaroni till tender in a pint of milk ; then put it into a buttered pie-dish, put in the sugar, the remainder of the milk and the eggs, beaten very thoroughly ; bake for half an hour. Time.—1 hour. Average Cost, 7d. Sufficient for 3 or 4 persons. Seasonable at any time. 18x1.—MANNA KROUP PUDDING. Ingredients.—3 tablespoonfuls of manna kroup, 12 bitter almonds, 1 pint of milk, sugar to taste, 3 eggs. Mode.—Blanch and pound the almonds in a mortar; mix them with the manna kroup ; pour over these a pint of boiling milk, and let them steep for about a quarter of an hour; when nearly cold, add the sugar and the well-beaten eggs; mix all well together ; put the pudding into a buttered dish and bake for half an hour. Time.—\ hour. Average Cost, 9d. Sufficient for 4 or 5 persons. Seasonable at any time. 1812. —MANSFIELD PUDDING.
