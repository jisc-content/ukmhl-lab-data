Ingredients.—1 large fowl, 1 dozen ears of green corn, £ lb. of rice, a bunch of parsley, pepper and salt to taste. Mode.—Cut up the fowl into joints, and put them in the soup-kettle with the water, boil 1 hour, or more if the fowl is tough; cut the corn from the cob, add it to the soup and stew for another hour. Take out the fowl when perfectly tender, cut the meat from the bones, and then into dice, add it with the parsley chopped, the rice and the seasoning to the soup, boil 20 minutes and serve without straining. Time.—2| hours. Average Cost, 4*. Sufficient for 8 or 10 persons. Seasonable from July to September. 2828.—CLAM SOUP.
