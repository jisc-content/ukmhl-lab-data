ALMOND SOUP. ^HOP into small pieces a neck of veal and the scrag end of a neck of mutton; put them into a large pan with five quarts of water, a cut turnip, and a blade or two of mace. Set on the fire and allow to boil gently till the whole is reduced to two quarts. Now strain through a hair sieve into a clean pot, and put in 6 oz. of blanched and beaten almonds and half a pint of thick cream, and season to taste with cayenne pepper. Place three small French rolls, with blanched Jordan almonds stuck in them, in the bottom of the tureen, and pour the almond soup over them.
