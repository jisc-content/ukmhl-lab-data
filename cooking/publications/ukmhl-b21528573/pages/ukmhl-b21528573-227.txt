209 Delectable Dinners. HAM SAVOURY. pOUND 2 oz. of lean, cooked ham, and put into a saucepan with two whole eggs, i oz. of butter, one good-sized tomato (skinned), and one table-spoonful of cream. Stir over a hot fire till it thickens, and then pour into little pastry-cases. Serve very hot. If you like, place a small fried mushroom on the top. CHEESE SAVOURY. pUT into a saucepan J pint of milk, with 2 oz. of butter and a pinch of salt. Bring to the boil, add oz. of fine flour, and stand at the cool side of the fire for a quarter of an hour. It must not boil after the flour has been put in. Now add one large whole egg and half a tea-spoonful of mustard. Spread the mixture on a plate to cool. When cool, wrap up in the batter square dice of American or any other kind of cheese. Now flour over the pieces and fry in boiling lard, roll in grated Par- mesan cheese, and serve hot.
