Fill the goose, and fasten it in securely at both ends by passing the rump through a slit made in the skin, and tying the skin of the neck into the back. Roast before a nice brisk fire for i\ hours—longer if the bird is large. Baste frequently with its own gravy. Take out the skewers. Pour over on a hot dish.
