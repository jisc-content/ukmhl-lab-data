Pour the sauce over and serve hot. 4—CALF'S BRAINS, a la Mattre d'Hotel. Order. Ingredients. Quantities. Method. Brains Boiling water Cold water .. Butter Flour or Stock, water, white wine... 2 oz. I dessert spoonful I pint .., Dress and skin. Put them in a dish. Pour over the brains till covered. Let them steep 2 or 3 hours. Throw away the water. Pour over the brains. Let them steep 10 minutes. Drain through a sieve. Melt in a saucepan over a slow fire. Sprinkle over the butter, stirring with a wooden spoon. Pour over the butter, still stirring, and see that the butter does not brown. When the sauce is well mixed, put in the brains with a skimmer.
