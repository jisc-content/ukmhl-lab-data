445 PICKLES, 9.-PICKLED GHERKHINS. Ingredients. Gherkins Salt Vinegar Vinegar Pepper Tarragon Fennel Cloves Capsicums Quantities. Method. Pick them in September, and see that they are very fresh, and of equal size. Cut off the stalks, and put them in an earthen pan. Cover them v^ell. Let them stand 24 hours, but turn them 3 or 4 times. Put them to drain, and wipe them perfectly dry. Put them in pickle jars. Pour in till they are covered. At the end of a week, pour off the vinegar. Let the gherkins drain. Pour fresh vinegar on them. I Put a little of each into all the jars j to give flavour. Stopper the jars closely, and keep them in a cool place. At the end of two months they will be ready for use. lO.-PICKLED LEMONS. Order.
