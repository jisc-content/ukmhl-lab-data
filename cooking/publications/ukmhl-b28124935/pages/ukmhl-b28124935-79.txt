MEATS. 75 Sweetbreads roasted.—Parboil two large ones; when cold, lard them with bacon, and roast them in a Dutch-oven. For sauce, plain butter and mushroom-ketchup. Sweetbread Ragout.—Cut them about the size of a walnut, wash and dry them, and fry them of a fine brown; pour to them a good gravy seasoned with salt, pepper, allspice, and either mushrooms, or mushroom-ketchup; strain, and thicken with butter and a little flour. To roast a Leg of Pork, Choose a small leg of fine young pork; cut a slit in the knuckle with a sharp knife; and fill the space with sage and onion chopped, and a little pepper and salt.
