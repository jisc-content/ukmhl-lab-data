Keep the mixture in small wide-mouthed bottles, well-corked, and in a dry cool place. Apple Sauce for Goose and roast Pork. Pare, core, and slice some apples ; and put them in a stone jar, into a saucepan of water, or on a hot hearth. If on a hearth, let a spoonful or two of water be put in to hinder them from burning. When they are done, bruise them to a mash, and put to them a bit of butter the size of a nutmeg, and a little brown sugar. Serve it in a sauce-tureen.
