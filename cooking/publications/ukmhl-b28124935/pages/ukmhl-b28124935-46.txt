43 DOMESTIC COOKERY. To scallop Oysters. Put them with crumbs of bread, p epper, salt, nutmeg, and a bit of butter, into scallop-shells or saucers, and bake before the fire in a Dutch oven. Fried Oysters, to garnish boiled Fish. _ Make a batter of flour, milk, and eggs, season it a very little, dip the oysters into it, and fry them a fine yellow-brown. A little nutmeg should be put into the seasoning, and a few crumbs of bread into the flour. Oyster Sauce. See Sauces. Oyster Loaves.
