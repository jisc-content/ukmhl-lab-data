( 67 ) VEGETABLES, etc. 129.—Plain Fried Potatoes. Peel them, wash them, slice them, and di’ain them; when perfectly dry, put them in boiling fat and fry them a pale gold colour. Sprinkle with salt, and serve at once. 130.—Potatoes “a la Lyonnaise.” Make a thick soubise sauce. Boil the potatoes; peel them, and cut them in slices. Pour the soubise sauce over them and serve. 131.—Puree of Potatoes. Boil and peel the potatoes; pass them through a wire sieve; put them into a saucepan, with a lump of butter, pepper, salt to taste. Stir in enough milk to make the pur^e smooth. Boil it up once, being careful to stir from the bottom, and serve.
