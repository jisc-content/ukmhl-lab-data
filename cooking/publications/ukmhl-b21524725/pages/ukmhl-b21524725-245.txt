Mix all thoroughly together, and bake. 548.—French Sponge Cake. 5 eggs, the weight of 4 eggs in castor sugar, the weight of 3 eggs in flour. Beat the 5 yolks up, and then gradually beat in the sugar. Whisk the 5 whites up to a very stifi" froth, then mix them to the yolks and sugar, but do not beat them; lastly, add in the flour in small quantities at a time, mixing very lightly; flavour with a few drops of essence of vanilla, or any other essence liked. Put it at once into a well- buttered cake-tin, and bake for 1 hour in a moderate oven. 548a.—French Cake.
