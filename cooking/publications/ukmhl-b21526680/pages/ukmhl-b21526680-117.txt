To Roast Ortolans. Pick and singe, but do not draw them. Tie on a bird- spit, and roast them. Hares, If properly taken care of, will keep a great time; and even when the cook fancies them past eating, may be in the highest perfection : which if eaten when fresh killed they are not. As they are usually paunched in the field, the cook cannot prevent this; but the hare keeps longer, and eats much better, if not opened for four or five days, or according to the weather.
