Fish Sauce without Butter. Simmer very gently a quarter of a pint of vinegar, and half a pint of water, (which must not be hard), with an onion, half a handful of horse-radish, and the follow- ing spices lightly bruised; four cloves, two blades of mace, and half a tea-spoonful of black pepper.
