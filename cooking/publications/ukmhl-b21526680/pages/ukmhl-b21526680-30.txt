To broil Salmon. Cut slices an inch thick, and season with pepper and salt; lay each slice in half a sheet of white paper well buttered, twist the ends of the paper, aud broU the slices over a slow fire six or eight minutes. Serve in the paper with anchovy-sauce. To pot Salmon.
