A tea-spoonful of sugar to half a pint of mustard is a great improvement, and softens it. Kitchen Jdepper. Mix in the finest powder one ounce of ginger; of cinnamon, black pepper, nutmeg, and Jamaica pepper, half an ounce each; ten cloves, and six ounces of salt. Keep it in a bottle; it is an agreeable addition to any brown sauces or soups. Spice in powder, kept in small bottles close stopped, goes much further than when used whole. It must bo dried before pounded, and should be done in quantities that may be wanted in three or four months.
