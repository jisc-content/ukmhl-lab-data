i8 SWEETS A LA MODE Cranberry Jelly. Wash the cranberries thoroughly ; take a pound of them and cook them in a stewpan with half a pint of water and half a pound of sugar, and let them boil for half an hour ; add a few drops of cochineal and half an ounce of best French gelatine, and a wineglassful of maraschino syrup. Pour it into a mould set on ice for an hour or so, and turn out when required. Danish Jelly. Gelee Danoise. Take three-quarters of a pint of claret, half a pint of marsala, half a pint of brandy, half a pint of syrup of cherries, two ounces of castor sugar, the juice and peel of one lemon, and one ounce of gelatine which has been soaked in a gill of cold water. Mix these ingredients well, boil, and strain into a mould. The jelly must not boil quickly, or the colour will be spoilt. Place on ice and turn out.
