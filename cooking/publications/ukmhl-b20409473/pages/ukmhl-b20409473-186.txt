Ingredients: 10 oz. of sifted sugar, 8 oz. finely chopped, or ground almonds, 6 oz. of flour, 12 yolks, and 14 whipped whites of eggs, 2 oz. shred candied orange peel, a teaspoonful of ground cinnamon, a tea- spoonful of ground cloves, and a teaspoonful of lemon sugar. First work the sugar and the almonds with the yolks of eggs, the cinnamon and lemon sugar, for ten minutes ; then add the flour and the shred orange peel, and lastly incorporate the whipped whites of eggs; bake the batter in a strong paper case, moderate heat; and when cold, cut the cake in thin slices to be dished up. These biscuits may also be baked in small moulds buttered and floured. No. 336.—Princess Drops. Ingredients : 8 oz. of ground almonds, 8 oz. of flour, 8 oz. of sifted sugar, 6 oz. of butter, 4 eggs, and a small glass of curagoa.
