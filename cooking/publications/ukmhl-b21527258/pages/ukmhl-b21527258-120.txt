Bespeak a calf’s head with the skin on, cnt it in half, and clean it well; then half boil it, take all the meat off in square bits, break the bones of the head, and boil them in some veal and beef broth to add to the richness. Fry some shejot in butter, and dredge in flour enough to thicken the gravy ; stir this into the browning, and give it one or two boils ; skim it carefully, and then put in the head ; put in also a a pint of Madeira wine, and simmer till the meat is quite tender.
