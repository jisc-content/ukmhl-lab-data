Oranije Pudding, Grate the rind of a Seville orange; put to it six ounces of fresh butter, six or eight ounces of lump sugar pounded : beat them all in a marble mortal-, and add as you do it the whole of eight eggs well beaten and strained ; scrape a raw apple, and mix with the rest ; put a paste at the bottom and sides of the dish, and over the orange mixture put cross bars of paste. Half an hour will bake it. Another.—Mix of orange paste two full spoons, with six eggs, four of sugar, four ounces of butter warm, and put into a shallow dish with a paste lining. Bake twenty minutes. Beat the yolks of four eggs ; add four ounces of white sugar, the rind of a lemon being rubbed with some lumps of it to take the essence ; then peel and beat it in a mortar with the juice of a large lemon, and mix all with four or five ounces of butter warmed.
