domestic cookery. m Transparent Pudding. Beat eight eggs very well; put them into a stew- pan, with half a pound of sugar pounded fine, the same quantity of butter, and some nutmeg grated. Set it on the fire, and keep stirring it till it thickens. Then set it into a basin to cool ; put a rich puff paste round the edge of the dish ; pour in your pudding, and bake it in a moderate oven. It will cut light and clear. You may add candied orange and citron if you like. Bailer Pudding.
