To dress Pork as Lamb. Kill a young pig of four or five months old ; cut up the fore-quarter for roasting as you do lamb, and truss the shank close. The other parts will make delicate pickled pork ; or steaks, pies, &c. Pork Steaks. Cut them from a loin or neck, and of middling thickness ; pepper and broil them, turning them often ; when nearly done, put on salt, rub a bit of butter over, and serve the moment they are taken off the fire, a few at a time".
