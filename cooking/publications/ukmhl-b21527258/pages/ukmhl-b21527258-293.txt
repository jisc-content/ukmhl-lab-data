Pare thin and halve four pounds of apricots, weigh- ing them after ; put them in a dish ; strew among them three pounds of sugar in the finest powder. When it melts, set the fruit over a stove to do very gently ; as each piece becomes tender, take it out, and put it into a china-bowL When all are done, and the boiling heat a little abated, pour the spup over them. In a day or two remove the syrup, leaving only a little in each half. In a day or two more turn them, and so continue daily till quite dry, in the sun or a warm place.
