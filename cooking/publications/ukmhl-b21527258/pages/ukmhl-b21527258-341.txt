Put two ounces of cream of tartar, and the juice and paring of two lemons, into a stone jar ; put on them seven quarts of boiling water, stir and cover close. When cold, sweeten with loaf-sugar, and, straining it, bottle and cork it tight. This is a very pleasant liquor, and very wholesome ; but, from the latter consideration, was at one time drank in such quantities as to become injurious.
