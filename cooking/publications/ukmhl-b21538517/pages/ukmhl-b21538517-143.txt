A pretty way of serving fresh ripe currants, when the bunches are large and fine, is to wash them, and when dry to dip them in white of egg and roll in powered sugar until well coated, then dry again in the sun. In thinking of fruits, let us not forget how greatly our range is added to in winter-time by the variety of dried fruits we have to use, and that cooked prunes and figs, raisins and dates, all are most nourishing and beneficial for health.
