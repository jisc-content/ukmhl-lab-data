83 AT HOME AND ABROAD. said the captain; “ you must own I have given you time and exercise sufficient, for we have had a delightful voyage.” “ Delightful ! I have been nearly drowned, burned, tatooed, scalped, impaled, roasted, toasted, chewed, devoured, and digested, and all this in consequence of your very pressing invitation to dinner.” From this time John Smith rigidly took his chop in the City. Moral. Beware, when dining abroad, of an invitation to take “pot-luck” with a friend.”
