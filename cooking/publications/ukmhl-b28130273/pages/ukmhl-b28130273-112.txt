You fumble for the congreve-box ; but how horrible it is to find that one solitary lucifer alone remains. What words can express the unspeakable anguish of that moment—the torture of that discovery ? A fresh source of perplexity arises. Which— which is the right end? Your fine sense of touch at last securely puts you in possession of the un- phosphorised extremity. Premature haste or impetuosity of friction would now be fatal. It is a crisis for cool judgment and consideration alone.
