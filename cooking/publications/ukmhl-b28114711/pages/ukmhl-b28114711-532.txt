Graham Hasty Pudding. 1 cup Graham flour, wet up with cold water. 1 large cup boiling water and same quantity of milk. 1 saltspoonful of salt. Stir the wet flour into the boiling water, slightly salted. Boil fifteen minutes, stirring almost constantly. Add the milk and cook, after it has come again to a boil, ten min- utes longer. Give with sugar and milk for breakfast. Eaten with cream, nutmeg, and powdered sugar, this is a good plain dessert for grown people as well as children.
