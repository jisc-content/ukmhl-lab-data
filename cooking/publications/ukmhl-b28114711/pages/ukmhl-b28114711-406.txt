Cocoanut Pudding. i lb. grated cocoanut. i cup stale sponge cake, crumbed fine. 1 cup sugar. 1 large cup rich milk—cream, if you can get it. 6 eggs. 2 teaspoonfuls vanilla, or rose-water. Cream the butter and sugar, and add the beaten yolks. When these are well mixed, put in the cocoanut; stir well before adding the milk, cake-crumbs, flavoring ; and lastly, the whites of three eggs. Whip the other whites stiff with three tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar ; flavor with vanilla, and just before taking the pudding from the oven, spread this meringue over the top, and close the oven until the icing is slightly browned.
