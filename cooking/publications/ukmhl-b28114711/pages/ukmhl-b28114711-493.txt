Green Tomato Soy. >J< 2 gallons tomatoes, green, and sliced without peeling. 12 good-sized onions, also sliced. 2 quarts vinegar. 1 quart sugar. 2 tablespoonfuls salt. 2 “ ground mustard. 2 “ black pepper, ground. 1 tablespoonful allspice. 1 “ cloves. Mix all together, and stew until tender, stirring often lest they should scorch. Put up in small glass jars. This is a most useful and pleasant sauce for almost every kind of meat and fish.
