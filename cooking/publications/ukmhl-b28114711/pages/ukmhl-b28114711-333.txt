Black or Wedding Cake. 1 lb. powdered sugar. 1 “ butter. 1 “ flour. 12 eggs. 1 lb. currants well washed and dredged. 1 “ raisins seeded and chopped. i “ citron cut into slips. 1 tablespoonful cinnamon. 2 teaspoonfuls nutmeg. 1 “ cloves. 1 wineglass brandy. Cream the butter and sugar, add the beaten yolks of the eggs, and stir all well together before putting in half of the flour. The spice should come next, then the whipped whites stirred in alternately with the rest of the flour, lastly the brandy.
