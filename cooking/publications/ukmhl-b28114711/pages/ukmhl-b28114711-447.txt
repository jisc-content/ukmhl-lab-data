Wine Jelly (boiled.) 1 box Coxe’s gelatine, soaked in 1 pint of cold water one hour. 1 quart of boiling water poured over this, and stirred until the gelatine is dissolved. 1 * lb. white sugar. 2 lemons—juice and peel. 1 pint of wine. Put all over the fire, boil up once well, and strain through a double flannel bag into moulds.
