Velvet Cakes. 1 quart new unskimmed milk—half cream and half milk is preferable. 3 eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately, and very stiff. 1 teaspoouful salt. Rice flour. Mix the beaten yolks with the milk, add the salt, then rice flour to make a batter as thick as that for flannel cakes; lastly, whip in the stiffened whites very lightly, and bake immediately.
