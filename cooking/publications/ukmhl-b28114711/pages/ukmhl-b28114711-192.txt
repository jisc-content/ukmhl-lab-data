This is a “ stock ” sauce, being suitable for so many dishes, roast or boiled. Mint Sauce for Roast Lamb. 2 tablespoonfuls green mint, chopped fine. 1 tablespoonful powdered sugar. Half a teacupful cider vinegar. Chop the mint, put the sugar and vinegar in a sauce boat, and stir in the mint. Let it stand in a cool place fifteen minutes before sending to table. Mushroom Sauce. 1 teacupful young mushrooms. 4 tablespoonfuls butter. 1 teacupful cream or milk. 1 teaspoonful flour. Nutmeg, mace, and salt to taste. Stew the mushrooms in barely enough water to cover them until tender. Drain, but do not press them, and add the cream, butter, and seasoning.
