I was undeceived, five minutes later, when her angry tones reached me from the kitchen, the door of which she had left open. “ I’ll never believe a person has a good heart, or deserves to be called a Christian, who names an honest, respectable girl, who tries to do her duty, a servant! ‘A faithful servant! ’ says she ; ‘ as if she was a queen, and meself a beggar! ’ ” “ What did you say to the ungrateful wretch ? ” asks a listener, indignantly. “ Nothing.
