Gingerbread without Butter. Mix with two pounds of treacle a quarter of a pound each of candied ginger, orange and lemon peel, all sliced very thin, half an ounce of carraway seeds, half an ounce powdered ginger, and as much flour as will mix it into a soft paste ; lay it on tin plates in con- venient-sized cakes, and bake in a quick oven. 684. Honeycomb Gingerbread. Bub together half a pound of flour, a quarter of a pound of butter, a quarter of a pound of moist sugar, a tea- spoonful of ground ginger, one each of allspice and cinnamon in powder, the rind of one lemon grated, and as much treacle as will make it into a paste to spread very thinly on tins ; bake it gently while hot; cut it in squares, and roll it over a stick like wafers till cold. Keep them in a dry place, or they will lose their crisp- ness. 685. Gingerbread Nuts.
