FOODS AND THEIR SEASONS. XXI11 APRIL. Meats. Beef, mutton, lamb, pork, and veal. Exsh. Carp, chub, crab, crayfish, gudgeon, herring, lobster, mackerel, mullet, salmon, skate, soles, tench, turhot. Poultry and Game. Chickens, ducklings, fowls, leverets, pigeons, pullets, rabbits. Vegetables. Artichokes, asparagus, beet, broccoli, burnet, carrots, celery, endive, lettuces, radishes, spinach, salads, sea-kale. Fruits. Scarce in this month—depend on last year’s supply. MAY. Meats. As in April, except pork. Fish. As in April, also whitebait. Poultry and Game. As in April. Vegetables. As in April, with early potatoes, peas, French beaus, early cabbages and cauliflowers. Fruits. Gooseberries and strawberries. JUNE. Meats. As in May, with the addition of venison. Fish. Carp, conger eel, crabs, crayfish, dabs, dace, eels, gudgeon, lobsters, mackerel, mullets (red), plaice, prawns, salmon, sea bream, shad, soles, tench, trout, turbot, whitebait. Poultry and Game. As in April, with green geese, plovers, turkey poults, wheatears, wood-pigeons. Vegetables. Artichokes, asparagus, beans, and vegetable marrows— otherwise as in April. Fruits. Cherries, currants, gooseberries, green apricots, melons, strawberries, forced grapes, nectarines, peaches, pines. JULY. Meats. As in May, with buck venison. Fish. Carp, crab, crayfish, dace, eels, gudgeons, lobsters, mackerel mullet, plaice, sahnon, sole, trout, turbot, whitebait. Poultry and Game. As in May and June. Vegetables. As in June. Fruits. As in June, with raspberries, plums, and damsons. AUGUST. Meats. As in July, without venison. Poultry and Game. As in July, with wild duck, and, after the l‘2th, grouse. Fish. As in July. Mackerel is not good in August. Vegetables. As in July. Fruits. As in July, with figs, mulberries, and filberts.
