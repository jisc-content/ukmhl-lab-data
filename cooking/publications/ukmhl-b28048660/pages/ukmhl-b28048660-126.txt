Roast three partridges, leave them to cool, cut them in pieces, put them in a pan with four shallots, a little tarra- gon and burnet minced, four spoonfuls of oil, three large ones of broken aspic jelly, a little pepper and salt, and a large spoonful of tarragon vinegar. Mix all well together, dish the partridges, putting the breasts and hacks at the bottom, and the other pieces round, cover with a good mayonnaise (see Sauces), and garnish with aspic jelly, 01 beurre de Montpellier.
