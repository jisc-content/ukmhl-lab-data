Take the saucepan off the fire. Take a large spoonful of the frothed whites and mould it into the form of an egg with another spoon, then drop it into the milk. Use up all the whites in the same way. Pour the milk remaining in the saucepan gently over the yolks, and stir them well the same way with a wooden spoon.
