Coarse sugar is best. Powder fine, and mix with the sugar. Rub the beef well with these at intervals, for two days. Dry well, beat, and add to the pickle. Rub well into all parts of the meat for three weeks. Before boiling the beef wash off the salt. This is sufficient for 20 lbs. of beef, either the round, the brisket, or the aitch bone. 2.—PICKLE FOR BEEF. Order. Ingredients. Quantities. Method. I...
