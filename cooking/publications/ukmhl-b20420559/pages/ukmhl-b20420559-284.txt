Use with colourless sauces, and for heating remains of poultry and white meats. 19.-TO CURRY MEAT. When you wish to add curry to any stew, sprinkle your meat with curry powder before you put it in the stewpan. The sauce is generally poured over boiled rice which is placed round the dish. The gravy for curry ^ould always be prepared the day before it is required, and care- fully skimmed when cold.
