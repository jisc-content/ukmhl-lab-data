Of scraped beef free from skin and strings, weigh 21b., 41b. of suet picked and chopped, then add 61b. of cur- rants nicely cleaned and perfectly dry, 31b. of chopped apples, the peel and juice of tw7o lemons, a pint of sweet wine, a nutmeg, a quarter of an ounce of cloves, ditto mace, ditto pimento, in finest powder; press the whole into a deep pan when well mixed, and keep it covered in a dry cool place. Half the quantity is enough, unless for a very large family. Have citron, orange, and lemon-peel ready, and put some of each in the pies when made. Mince Pies without Meat.
