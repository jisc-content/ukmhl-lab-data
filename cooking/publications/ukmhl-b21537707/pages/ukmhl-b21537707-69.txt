Black Currant Tea. Ingredients. One tablespoonful of Black Currant Jam. Half teaspoonful of Lemon Juice. Half pint of boiling Water. Mode of Work.—Put the jam into a jug, pour the boiling water over. Cover and let it stand till cold. Strain and serve. Linseed Tea. Ingredients. One ounce of Linseed. One quart of Water. Half ounce of Liquorice. Half ounce of Sugar Candy. Strip of Lemon Rind. Mode of Work.—Wash the linseed, put it into a saucepan with the cold water and lemon-rind. Boil gently for one hour. Add liquorice and sugar candy. Strain and serve. Toast Water. Ingredients.
