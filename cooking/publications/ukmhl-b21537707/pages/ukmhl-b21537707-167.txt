8i Cambridge Pudding. Ingredients. One pound of Flour. One and a half pints of skimmed Milk. One Egg. Two ounces of Sugar. Half-pound of Apples peeled and sliced. Make a smooth batter of the flour, milk and egg, add the sugar and the apples. Grease a basin, pour in the mixture, tie a floured cloth over the top, and boil for two hours.
