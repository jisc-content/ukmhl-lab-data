Two ounces of Flour. Half a pint of Milk. Three Eggs. Three ounces of Butter. Boil the rice in a little water till it is a jelly; stir the milk into the flour, be careful to have no lumps; when the rice is cold stir the flour and milk into it, also the eggs lightly whipped and the butter just warmed. Let this batter stand a little while, and fry as pancakes. Rich Suet Pudding. Ingredients. Quarter of a pound of Suet. Two Eggs. Three ounces of Flour. Half a pint of Milk. A little grated Ginger. Pinch of Salt. Mince the suet as finely as possible, and mix it thoroughly with the flour; beat up the eggs, mix them with the milk, stir in the suet and flour, tie it loosely in a cloth, and boil one hour and a quarter.
