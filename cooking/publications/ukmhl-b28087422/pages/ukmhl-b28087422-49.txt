CEnfs d la Portugaise.—Choose ripe, rich-coloured tomatoes, all of a size, either large enough to hold an egg in each half or else just the size for one. Set these tomatoes on a well-buttered baking tin, break a fresh egg carefully into each, and put the pan into the hot oven till the eggs are poached.
