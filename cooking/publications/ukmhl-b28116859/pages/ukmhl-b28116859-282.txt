We whip the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth. 13. When the pudding is a light brown, we take it out spread the whipped whites of the eggs over the top, and sift about a dessertspoonful of castor sugar over it. 14. We put the dish back in the oven till the icing is a light brown ; the pudding is then ready for serving.
