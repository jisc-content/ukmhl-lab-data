Lessons on Cooking Meat.—Beef Steak Pudding. 125 Now it is finished. 26. When the water in the saucepan is quite boiling, we put in the pudding and let it boil for two hours. N.B.—The lid should be on the saucepan. N.B.—We should keep a kettle of boiling water and fill up the saucepan, as the water in it boils away. 27. After that time we take the pudding out of the sauce- pan and take off the cloth. 28. We place a hot dish on the top of the pudding, turn the basin and dish quite over, and carefully raising the basin, we should leave the pudding in the middle of the dish unbroken. N.B.—This pudding might be made of beef skirt or Australian beef.
