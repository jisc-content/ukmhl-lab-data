Now we will show you how to make a Vennoise Pudding. 1. We take a piece of stale crumb of bread (about five ounces), put it upon a board, and cut it up in the shape of dice. 2. We put the bread into a basin with three ounces of castor sugar and three ounces of sultana raisins. 3. We take a lemon, wipe it clean with a cloth, and grate the rind of it into the basin. 4. We chop up two ounces of candied peel ^nd put it into the basin. 5. We pour in a wineglassful of sherry. 6. We put a saucepan of warm water on the fire to boil. 7.
