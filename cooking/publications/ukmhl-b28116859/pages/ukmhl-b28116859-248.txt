Half a pint of melted butter 5 yolks of eggs Salt and Cayenne pepper ., ) 2 teaspoonsful of lemon juice / Time required, about ten minutes. Now we will show you how to make Dutch Sauce. 1. We take half a pint of melted butter and put it into a stewpan. 2. We add the yolks of five eggs. 3. We stand the stewpan in a saucepan of hot water over the fire, and stir well with a wooden spoon. 4. We season it with salt according to taste, and Cayenne pepper (enough to cover very thinly half the top of a threepenny piece). 5. We must stir continually until it thickens, and we must not let the sauce boil or it will curdle. 6. Just before the sauce is finished we stir in two tea- spoonsful of lemon juice.
