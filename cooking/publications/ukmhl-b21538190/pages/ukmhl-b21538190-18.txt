A good sized cauliflower, i quart white stock, i oz. butter. A grate of nutmeg. i oz. flour. Yolks of two eggs. Seasoning. A small piece of sugar. Break the flower in small branches and soak it in salt and water. Heat the stock; mix the flour and butter in a saucepan; add the stock, stir until it boils. Throw in the cauliflower, and boil with the lid off till quite tender. Press all through a hair sieve and warm it again.
