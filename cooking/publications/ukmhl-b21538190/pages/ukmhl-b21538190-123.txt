119 COLD LEMON PUDDING. 3 tablespoonfuls cornflour. I 3 eggs. Rind and juice of a lemon. I 2 teacupfuls sugar. Mix the cornflour with a little cold water, and pour over boiling water till it is clear, just as you would make starch. Add the juice and grated rind of the lemon, the sugar, and the yolks of the eggs. Pour into a crystal dish. When quite cold pile up the whites of eggs beaten to a stiff froth, with a very little sugar added. Decorate with a few cherries" or a little red jelly. Mrs. Baird, Dalmuir. KUSSEL.
