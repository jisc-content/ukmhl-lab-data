Put water on to boil with sugar, lemon rind and juice, when it boils take out rind. Mix cornflour with a little water and stir it in. Boil 3 minutes and turn it into a basin. Beat whites to a stiff froth and stir into the contents of basin. Turn out into a wet mould and serve with sauce. Sauce. 2 yolks of eggs.
