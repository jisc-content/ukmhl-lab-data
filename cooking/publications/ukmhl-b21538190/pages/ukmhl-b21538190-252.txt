248 FRIED SALSIFY. Wash, scrape, and trim a bundle of salsify. Put in clean water with a little acid as each piece is cleaned. Cook in salted water till tender and then drain. Season with oil, vinegar, and finely-chopped parsley. Dip in butter and fry in hot fat. Garnish with fried parsley. Salsify may be boiled and served with melted butter or parsley sauce.
