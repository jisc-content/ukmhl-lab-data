Warm the mortar and pestle by plac- ing them on a stove (or fill the mortar with hot ashes, and when heated turn them out), or char- coal may be used, until they are so hot that you can scarcely bear your hand against them. Wipe the mortar out clean, and put any convenient quantity of your prepared nuts in it, which you pound until they are reduced to an oily paste into which the pestle will sink by its own weight.
