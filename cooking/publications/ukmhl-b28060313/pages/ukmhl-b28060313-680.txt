To destroy Bugs in a few hours.—Take i lb. of oil of turpentine, and with a brush rub the beds, walls, or any places infested with them ; if they are in great numbers, brush the places over several times, which will not only destroy the bugs, but also their eggs. Although the smell of turpentine is strong, yet it is not unhealthy, and goes off' in a short time. To test Mushrooms.—Rub the upper skin with a gold ring or any piece of gold : the part rubbed will turn yellow if it is a poisonous fungus.
