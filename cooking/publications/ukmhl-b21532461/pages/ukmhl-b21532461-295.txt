FowL(Stewed).—Truss a fowl the same as for boiling; put it into a stewpan, with a bit of butter, chopped parsley, shalot, and mushrooms; stew it on a slow fire about a quarter of au hour, turning it often. Then put it into another stewpan, with slices of veal and ham, and all the first seasoning. Cover it with slices of bacon; stew it gently for a quarter of an hour longer, then add a little whole pepper, and some salt, a little stock, and some white wine, and finish it on a slow fire; then skint and strain the braise.
