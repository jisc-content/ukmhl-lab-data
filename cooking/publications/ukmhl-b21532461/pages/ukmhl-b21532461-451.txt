Pig Olives.—Bone, and cut off the head and feet of a fine pig ; take a part of the flesh, and mince it with some beef suet, then pound these with some bread crumbs, parsley, shalots, mushrooms (all shred), and add some cream, a spoonful of brandy, the yolks of six eggs, pepper, salt, and nutmeg. Cut the skin of the pig into pieces, in each of which roll some of the farce, tie up the olives, and cook them in some stock; add a glass of white wine, and a few slices of peeled lemon.
