237 FRENCH COOKERY. Pink-coloured Fritters. Boil a large beet-root until it is tender ; beat it fine in a marble mortar. Add the yolks of four eggs, two spoonsful of flour, and three of cream, the juice and peel of half a lemon, half a nutmeg, and a glass of brandy. Mix all well together, and fry the fritters in butter. Garnish them with green sweetmeats, preserved apricots, or sprigs of myrtle. Plain Fritters.
