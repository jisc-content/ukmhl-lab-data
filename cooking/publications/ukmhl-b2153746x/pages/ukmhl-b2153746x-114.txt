The milk jugs and vessels, too, in which any food is kept should be scrupulously clean, and covered with washable muslin. Children should not be allowed to suck anything that is handy, as they are usually allowed to do. The sterilizing of milk, and washing and dressing of the baby, shown on a toy doll, often proves a useful and instructive lesson, and one in which the children are very keenly interested.
