INSTRUCTION IN COOKERY i6 is that called the Perfection oil stove. The oven, which is detachable, is heated by two large lamps with round wicks about 2 inches in circumference, allowing a free passage of air up the centre. This makes the flame as hot as a Bunsen flame is. It is consequently better than an ordinary flame, and much cleaner, and can be used for baking quite large batches of bread or cakes.
