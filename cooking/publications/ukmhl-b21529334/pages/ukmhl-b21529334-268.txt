THE DUDLEY RECIPES 226 “DUXELLE” FOR STUFFED VEGE- TABLES, TOMATOES, MUSHROOMS, Etc. T)UT six tablespoonfuls of dry Duxelle into a small stewpan and add to it three table- spoonfuls of half-glaze sauce, containing plenty of tomato, crushed garlic, the size of a pea, and two tablespoonfuls of white wine. Set the above to simmer until the required degree of consistency is attained. A tablespoonful of fine fresh breadcrumbs may be added to the Duxelle in order to thicken it.
