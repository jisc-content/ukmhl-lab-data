OIX eggs, two ounces of butter, a small glass of ^ rum, one tablespoonful of castor sugar, one tablespoonful of cream and a pinch of salt. Break the eggs into a basin ; add the salt and sugar ; beat up well together with the cream and a little rum. Melt the butter in an omelet pan ; when hot, pour in the eggs, etc. Stir, cook, roll and brown the omelet to a light colour ; turn carefully on to a dish ; dust with sugar ; pour the rum round the omelet; light it, and serve quickly.
