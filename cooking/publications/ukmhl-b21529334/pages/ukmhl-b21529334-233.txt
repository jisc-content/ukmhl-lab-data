LITTLE SARDINE ROLLS CAREFULLY remove the skins from the number of sardines required and wrap each neatly in a piece of puff paste, previously spread over with a little anchovy paste. Brush each roll over with yolk of egg; sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and place on a baking-sheet. Mark the surface of the paste crossways with the point of a knife, and bake in a sharp oven for ten minutes.
