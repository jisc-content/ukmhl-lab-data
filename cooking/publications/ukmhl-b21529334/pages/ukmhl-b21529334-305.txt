R 261 50 SWEET DISHES AND PUDDINGS CREME BRULEE ONE pint of cream, four yolks of eggs. Beat the yolks in a basin, and having boiled the cream for a very short time, pour it on the eggs. Put it all into a saucepan and allow it to come to the boil, stirring all the time; then pour it into a fireproof dish, and allow it to become cool; then strew a thick crust of castor sugar over it, and brown with a red-hot salamander. Serve cold. EXCELLENT RECIPE FOR BAKED APPLES rpAKE nice large apples, and core them without splitting. Place in a china dish and fill each apple with castor sugar, then put a piece of fresh butter the size of a nutmeg on top of each apple. Cover bottom of dish with warm water, and bake in a medium oven for three-quarters of an hour. Serve with apricot sauce.
