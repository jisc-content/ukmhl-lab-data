POULTRY AND GAME DISHES 99 Have ready a large stewpan ; put in some slices of pork or bacon, the carrot and onion cut into slices, cloves and bouquet; lay the partridges on this ; put the cabbage on top ; then some more bacon or pork ; add the wine and stock ; season with pepper, salt and grated nutmeg; cover and cook slowly for thirty to forty-five minutes. Dress the cabbages on a dish to form a bed ; place the partridges on this (whole or cut up); garnish with slices of carrot and bacon or pork. Skim and strain the gravy ; add the sauce ; boil up and sauce all over.
