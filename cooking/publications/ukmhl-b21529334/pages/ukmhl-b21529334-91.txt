FISH DISHES 53 SOUFFLE OF LOBSTER mWO hen lobsters, six eggs, half a pint of well- reduced creamy Bechamel sauce, pepper, salt, nutmeg and a little cayenne. Split the lobsters ; remove the flesh from the shell, and pound in a mortar until very fine. Make the Bechamel sauce hot, and add it by degrees to the lobster, also the yolks of the eggs and the season- ing. Rub all through a sieve into a basin. Beat up the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth ; mix all lightly together ; put it into a well-buttered mould, and steam for an hour and a quarter.
