Add lemon and sugar to taste. r« FOR EXTRACTING GAS FROM EFFERVESCING DRINKS : A “MOSSER” A LTHOUGH effervescing drinks are most at- tractive and refreshing, many people object to the gas, which induces distention and flatulence. A small wooden stick with a wheel cut at the end and used as a whip in a glass of aerated water or champagne, with which to dispel the gas, will, at the same time, retain the briskness of the beverage. These “ Mossers ” can be obtained at Messrs. Farrow and Jackson, 8 Haymarket, W., or 16 Great Tower Street, E.C.
