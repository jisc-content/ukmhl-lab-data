THE DUDLEY RECIPES 128 GERMAN BEEFSTEAK CAKES TAKE one pound of fresh fillet of beef chopped finely; add three ounces of fresh butter, salt, pepper, some chopped parsley and a grate of nutmeg; form this into flat l'ound cakes ; dip them into beaten egg and then into breadcrumbs. This should be done twice ; then fry them in hot fat and serve with fried parsley as a garnish. Serve with them some Risotto and in a sauceboat some Sauce Colbert. Sauce Colbert is prepared as follows :â€” One and a half gills of Espagnol sauce, one glass of Madeira wine, one tablespoonful of meat glaze, one and a half ounces of fresh butter, one tea- spoonful of lemon juice, one dessertspoonful of chopped parsley, tarragon and chervil mixed. Put the sauce into a small stewpan ; stir over the fire until hot; add the wine and allow to boil for a few minutes ; remove to side of stove and stir in gradually the butter and the meat glaze ; beat up with a small whisk, but do not allow it to boil again. Last of all add the lemon juice and chopped herbs. If desired richer, half an ounce more butter can be used. 9
