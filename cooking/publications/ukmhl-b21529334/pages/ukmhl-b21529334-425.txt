TEA CAKES, ETC. 377 out some rounds or ovals, according to taste and fancy. Place these on a greased baking tin. Spread each with a thin layer of icing to which a little orange-flower water has been added ; strew over with chopped pistachio nuts or almonds and bake in a moderate oven for about twenty minutes. As they come out of the oven, brush over with liquid icing, which will set as the biscuits get cool
