Roulade de Bceuf a la Napolitaine.— Take a pound of cold roast beef, free it from skin and fat, etc., and pass it through the mincing machine. Add to it 6 oz. of finely minced (cooked) ham or bacon, fat and lean together. Season highly with pepper and salt. Add a teaspoonful of finely minced olives, a few drops of tabasco, a teaspoonful of minced parsley, a little grated lemon peel, and a dust of nutmeg. Mix all together; then add to it 4 oz. of previously cooked macaroni which has been cut into 1 in. lengths and tossed in a little butter. Mix again. Add the well- beaten yolks of two eggs, and finally the whites whipped to a stiff froth. Mix thor- oughly ; roll up in a piece of clean well-greased paper, place in the bag, fold and clip, put on the broiler, and bake in a moderate oven for 25 minutes. Serve with the same sauce as in the foregoing recipe.
