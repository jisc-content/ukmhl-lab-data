Remedy for the Ring-worm. Dip the finger in lemon-juice, and apply it well to the part affected; some common gunpowder (not glazed) pounded and passed through a sieve, to be sprinkled on the lemon-juice whilst wet, so as com- pletely to cover the wound, and repeated every second day. Three or four applications have gene- rally produced a cure, but in cases where this trouble- some disease has been inveterate, a longer time has been required.
