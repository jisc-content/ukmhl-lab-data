Boil eight eggs hard, and put them in cold wrater, then peel them without breaking the whites, cut a small bit off the end of four, as they will stand up- I right on the dish; split the other four through the middle, and lay them round the others; put a little flour, water, butter, and catsup into a stewpan, and make it hot; put a little chopped parsley in it, and pour it over the eggs. N.B. The parsley should not be boiled, either in the sauce or before it is chopped. Garnish with small branches of curled parsley. 102. Fried Eggs with Parsley.' Boil some eggs hard, slice and fry them with olive- oil and butter, brown a little butter in the pan with a little flour dusted in it, pour in a little water and salt, let it boil and pour it on the eggs.
