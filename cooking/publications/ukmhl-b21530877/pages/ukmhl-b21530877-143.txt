129 PUDDINGS, &C. . 376. Gooseberry Pudding. Scald a quart of green gooseberries, rub them through a sieve, stir in a quarter of a pound of butter, sweeten it, add two or three Naples biscuits, four eggs well beaten, mix it well, bake it half an hour. 377.
