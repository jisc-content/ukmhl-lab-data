Buttered Eggs, Beat five eggs, put three ounces of butter in a basin with a little cream or new milk, and set the basin in boiling water till the butter be melted, then pour it into a saucepan with the eggs, hold it over a slow fire, shaking it one way as it begins to warm ; pour it into a basin and back, then hold it again over the fire, stirring it constantly and pouring it into the basin, more perfectly to mix the egg and butter, until it is hot and thick without boiling. Serve it on toasted bread. 106. Scotch Dish of Eggs. Eggs with Sorrel and Lettuce.
