2 7 Recipes for the Breakfast Table. No. 14. MILK ROLLS. Average cost of “Milk Rolls" {about Twelve). Ingredients. s. d. 1 lb. self-raising flour ... ... 0 3 2 oz. butter ... 0 Milk 0 1 0 6 Time required, about half-an-hour. 1. Put one pound of self-raising flour into a basin and rub two ounces of butter into it with your hands. 2. Add sufficient milk to make it into a lithe firm dough. 3. Sprinkle flour over a board, and turn d e dough out on it. 4. Take a knife, dip it in flour, and cut the dough into twelve pieces. N.B.—Keep your hands floured to prevent the dough (rom sticking to them. 5. Form each piece into a small roll. 6- Flour a baking-tin. 7. Put these rolls on to the tin, and put the tin in the oven to bake for twenty minutes. 8. The milk rolls will then be ready for use.
