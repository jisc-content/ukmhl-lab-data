Why, then, should the good things offered to us by Providence be neglected, especially if we look upon them as perishable things ; more especially, if they awaken our gi-atitude towards the Creator of all things. No less cogent reasons come to the support of the above. Can we receive too hospitably those who guide us in the right path? Ought we not to invite often, and be kind to those whose object is so praiseworthy? Sometimes the gifts of Cornus drop in quite un- sought for; a souvenir from a college chum, a gift from an old friend, a peace offering from a peni- tent. How refuse to accept such offerings ? It is sheer necessity. Moreover, it has always been so.
