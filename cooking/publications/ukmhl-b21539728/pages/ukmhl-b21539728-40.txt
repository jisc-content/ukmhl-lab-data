Treacle or Golden Syrup. 6 ozs. of Butter. 2 ozs. of Sugar. 2 ozs. of Lemon Peel. 1 oz. of Ground Ginger. 1 teaspoonful of Carbonate Soda. 1 small teacupful of Milk. 2 Eggs. Method:—Put treacle, butter and sugar into a saucepan over the lire to dissolve. Break the eggs into a cup ; beat them up. Cut up the peel very finely. Dissolve the soda in the milk. Put the Hour into a basin, mix in the ginger and peel, then stir in the eggs, treacle, etc.; beat well. Pour in the milk and soda, and beat well again. Pour the mixture into a greased shallow tin, bake in a moderately hot oven about f hour. When done, turn it from the tin and cool on a sieve.
