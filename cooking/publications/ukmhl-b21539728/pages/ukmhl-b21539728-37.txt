Method:—Mash the potatoes very finely; put them into [a basin, add the Hour and salt, then rub the dripping or lard lightly in with the fingers; make it into a paste with the water (very little required, as the potatoes are moist). Put it on a floured paste-board, roll it out thinly, line some tart-tins with it. Bake the pastry in a quick oven about | of an hour ; when done put in some jam. Serve hot or cold.
