Simmer the vegetables well.' Add the water; stew slowly for 20 minutes, or until the vegetables pulp, then add the flour (previously mixed into a smooth paste with a little of the milk); add the rest of the milk. Stir the soup until it boils, then let it simmer for 10 minutes. Stir frequently. Pour the soup into a basin, and serve with small squares of toast.
