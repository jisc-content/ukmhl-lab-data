Put into the chafing - dish a heaping tablespoonful of butter, a saltspoonful of dry mustard, as much salt, and a couple of dashes of cayenne pepper. Stir in the lobster next. The meat of one large or two small ones may be used, or the con- tents of a can. When it is smoking hot, put in a wineglassful of sherry and the juice of half a lemon, cook a minute longer, and serve. The wine may be omitted if desired, but its use adds much to the flavor of the dish.
