Cold chicken or turkey, sliced or cut into small pieces. Have about a pint of the meat. Half cup canned mushrooms, sliced. Two tablespoonfuls butter. One tablespoonful flour. Half pint milk. One gill mushroom liquor. Yolks of two eggs. Salt and white pepper. Make a sauce over boiling water of the butter, flour, milk, and mushroom liquor, add the chicken, cook three minutes, put in the mushrooms, and cook two minutes longer. Add the eggs then, very slowly.
