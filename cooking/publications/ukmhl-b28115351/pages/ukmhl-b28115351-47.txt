CHAPTER IY. A PRETTY KETTLE OF FISH CREAMED SALMON'. Two hours before it is needed turn the contents of a can of salmon out upon a platter. Pick it to pieces with a fork, re- moving all bits of bone or skin, and drain off the liquid. When you are ready to prepare the dish, cook together in the chafing-dish over hot water a tablespoonful of butter and one of flour.
