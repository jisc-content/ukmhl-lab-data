CHAPTER YII A CHAPTER ON ENTRIES BROILED SWEETBREADS. Sweetbreads must always be parboiled and blanched before using. Wash them well, lay them in boiling water, and let them simmer ten minutes. Take them out and plunge them in cold water. Leave them in this for ten minutes. All this should be done before the time when they are to take their place in the chafing- dish.
