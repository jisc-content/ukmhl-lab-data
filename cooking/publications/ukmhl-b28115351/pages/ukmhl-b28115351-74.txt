Two tablespoonfuls butter. One tablespoonful flour. One pint milk. Scant teaspoonful salt. A little cayenne. Cook together the butter and flour, add the milk, and cook until the sauce is smooth, put in the shrimps, and simmer until they are heated through. Season and serve.
