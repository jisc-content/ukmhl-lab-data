13 SOUPS. Potage Dupoise, or Mussel Soup. Ingredients, Two pints of Fish Stock. Three pints of Mussels (prepared as for Dressed Mussels). Two ounces of Butter. Two ounces of Flour. A few Parsley Stalks. Half a teaspoonful of Sugar. Cayenne, Salt, Mignonnette Pepper. Lemon Juice. Half a pint of Cream. PrejDare the mussels as for dressed mussels. Melt the butter in a stewpan, add the flour, and mix them well. Pour the fish stock and the liquor from the m,ussels to the butter and flour; add the parsley stalks, &c. Let it boil, and skim well; then strain, and add the cream and the mussels, Ee-heat without boiling, and serve. Potage aux HuTtres. Ingredients. Three dozen of Oysters. Two Whitings, or one Eel or two Flounders. Parsley Boots. One Carrot. One Stick of Celery. A little Thyme. One Blade of Mace. A few Peppercorns. Two Anchovies. Two ounces of Butter.
