HIGH-CLASS COOKERY. 74 Cutlets a la Reforme- Ingredients, Best end of a neck of Mutton. Two lean slices of Ham (chopped fine). One Egg. Bread-crumbs. One Carrot (cut in strips). Four Gherkins „ Four Mushrooms „ Four Truffles „ Whites of two Eggs (previously cooked). Cut the carrot into strips to cook. Cut the vegetables up neatly, put them in a stewpan, and keep them warm in a bain-marie. Cut the cutlets, trim, mix the ham with the bread-crumb, pepper and salt, egg and bread-crumb them. Fry them a nice brown in about three ounces of butter; arrange them on a dish in a circle. Put the chips of vegetables in the centre of the cutlets, and pour Eeform sauce round.
