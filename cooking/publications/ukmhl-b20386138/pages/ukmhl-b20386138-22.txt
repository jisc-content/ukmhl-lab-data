HIGH-CLASS COOKERY. 10 Kidney Soup. Ingredients, Four Sheep's Kidneys. The weight of the Kidneys in lean Beef. Three pints of Brown Stock. Two tablespoonfuls of Ric6. Chopped Parsley. One Onion. Four ounces of Butter. Crolites of Bread. Pepper and Salt. Cut up the kidneys and the beef into small dice, season with pepper and salt, add the onion chopped up and some parsley, saute all these for about ten minutes in the butter; pour off the grease, add the stock and rice, and boil till quite tender, then strain; pound all the meat and rice in a mortar, then pass through a tammy or fine hair sieve; stir this into the stock again, make it very hot and serve with croutes of bread. The soup must not boil after it has been passed through the tammy. Potage aux Abatis. Ingredients. Two sets of Duck Giblets. Half a head of Celery. One Carrot. One Turnip. Two Onions. A Bouquet Garni. Two Cloves.
