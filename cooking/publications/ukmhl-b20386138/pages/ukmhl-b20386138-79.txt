Chicken Saute a la Marengo. Ingredients, One Chicken. One gill of Salad Oil. A few Truffles. Six Chives, cut small. Six Mushrooms. One gill of Tomato Sauce. One gill of Brown Sauce. Croutons of Puif Paste, Cut the chicken in six pieces; put it in a stew- pan with the salad oil; let it brown, which will take about ten minutes, then pour away the oil; add the mushrooms, the tomato, and the brown sauce, also the chives and the truffles; it is best to put the stewpan in the oven, with the lid on, and let simmer for about half an hour. This should be nicely arranged on a silver dish, and served with croutons of puff paste; poached eggs may also be used as a garnish.
