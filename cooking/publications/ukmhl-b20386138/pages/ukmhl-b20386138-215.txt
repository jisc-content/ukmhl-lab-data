Maraschino or Sherry. One ounce of Castor Sugar. Whites of five Eggs. Vanilla Essence. Cut the sponge cakes into three pieces length- ways, putting jam between each layer ; pile up on a glass dish, and well soak them with either sherry or Maraschino. When soaked, beat up the whites of egg to a very stiff froth; add sugar and vanilla; mask the cakes entirely over with the egg and put in a cool oven to dry; when dry, garnish with red jelly.
