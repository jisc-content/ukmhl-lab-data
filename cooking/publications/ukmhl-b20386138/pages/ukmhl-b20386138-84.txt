Stir the tongue, truffles, and macaroni into the white sauce, and serve in the centre of the cutlets; pour the brown sauce round, and serve. Mutton Cutsets a !a Provencale. Ingredients. Three pounds of the best end of a neck of Mutton. Half a pint of White Soubise Sauce. Six Mushrooms (chopped fine). One Shalot „ One small Onion „ Half a Clove Garlic „ One tablespoonful of Parsley Yolks of four Eggs. Pepper and Salt, to taste. Brown Bread-crumbs. One teaspoonful of Parmesan (grated). Trim your cutlets, fry them, and press them until cold; remove all outside pieces, so that each cutlet may be the same size and shape. Put the soubise sauce in a saute-pan ; add the mushrooms, shalot, onion, garlic, pepper, and salt; when they have cooked ten minutes, take out the garlic, stir in the yolks of egg, and cook well, also the parsley; this will now form a stiff paste, which must be put on the cutlet on one side only.
