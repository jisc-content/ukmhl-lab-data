167 OMELETS. SoufTlee Omelets Ingredients, \ Six Eggs. Half an ounce of Flour. Half an ounce of Butter, Three ounces of Castor Sugar. Fifteen drops of Essence of Vanilla, Put four yolks of egg into a basin, add the castor sugar, and beat with a wooden spoon for ten minutes; then stir in the flour and vanilla; whisk the six whites of egg to a stiff froth, and mix lightly to the yolks of egg. Butter a half- pint soufflee-tin, pour in the mixture, and bake in a quick oven for ten minutes; send to table quickly. If jam is used, it must be put in the centre of the soufiiee before it is baked.
