One pint of White Stock. One Egg. Bread-crumbs. Pepper and Salt. Well wash the rice and boil it in the stock until quite tender ; then pack it down in a saute- pan and press it till quite cold. Now cut out patty cases of the rice with a pastry cutter, making a lid with a smaller cutter. Egg and bread-crumb these cassolettes, fry them a nice brown in very hot fat. Lift the lid and fill the cases with any savoury mixture you like, replace the lid, and serve with fried parsley. Cauliflower au Gratin. Ingredients. A nice Cauliflower.
