FANCY BREAD AND CAKES. 215 Walnut Cake. Ingredients, Quarter of a pound of Vienna Flour. Half a pound of Castor Sugar. Half a pound of Butter. Eight Eggs. i Half a pound of Walnuts or Almonds. One teaspoonful of Baking-powder. One teaspoonful of Yanilla Eseence. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream, then add the flour, sifted, and mix well; add the walnuts or almonds chopped, and lastly the whites only of the eight eggs, whipped to a stiff froth. Bake in a moderate oven for one hour.
