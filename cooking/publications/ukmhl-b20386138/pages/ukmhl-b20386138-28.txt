16 HiaH-CLASS COOKERY. Shred the vegetables all of the same length and size. Melt the butter in a saucepan, and fry all the vegetables (except the lettuce, tarragon, and chervil), a light brown; add three pints of boiling consomme, the sugar and salt. Skim well until all grease is removed ; then add lettuce, tarragon, and chervil, let it boil a few minutes, and serve. Ordinary clear consomme is required for this soup. Potage a la Puree Lievre. Ingredients, One Hare. One ounce of Flour, Two ounces of Butter. Two Shalots. One smaU Onion. SmaU bouquet Garni. Blade of Mace. Quarter of a pound of lean Ham, Half a pint of White Wine, Salt, Whole Pepper, Cayenne. Two pints Second Stock. Cut up the hare in joints after it has been skinned and paunched. Do not wash it. Cut the bacon up in small pieces and butter a sauce- pan with the butter. Put the bacon and the hare in the stewpan and fry a light brown; then add the onion, &c., also the wine; boil ten minutes, add the stock, and let this simmer gently for about one hour and a half; strain through a sieve.
