25 SOUPS. serve. Put the stock on to boil. When boiling add the salt, then the peas, and other ingredients except the cream; boil until the vegetables are tender, then pass them through a hair-sieve with the stock they were boiled in, and again pass through a tammy-cloth, if desired very smooth; put all back into a clean stewpan to boil up, and just before serving add the cream and a teaspoonful of castor sugar. Puree k la Palestine. Ingredients. ^ Two pounds of Jerusalem Artichokes, Two ounces of Butter. One pint of White Stock. Half a pint of Cream, Pepper and Salt. Wash, peel, and slice the vegetables. Melt the butter in a stewpan, put in the vegetables, squeeze over them the lemon juice, and cook carefully for about five minutes; being careful the vegetables do not brown. Add the stock and boil until the artichokes are tender, then rub them through a hair-sieve. Eeturn the soup to the saucepan, warm it up, add the cream, and it is ready to
