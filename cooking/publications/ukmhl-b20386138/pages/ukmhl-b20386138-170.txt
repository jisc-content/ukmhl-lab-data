HiGH-Ct-AgS COOKERY. 158 PASTEY. Cheese Pastry. Ingredients. \^ Two ounces of Butter. Two ounces of Flour. Two ounces of Parmesan Cheese. One ounce of Cheddar Cheese. One Egg. Salt and Cayenne. Put the flour on a paste-board, sprinkle in a little salt and cayenne, and rub in the cheese, grated, and the butter; mix all these together with the yolk of an egg into a smooth stiff paste. EoU this out into a strip one eighth of an inch thick and five inches long; cut this into strips of equal sizes, also some rings. Grease a baking sheet, put the straws and rings on it, and bake in a hot oven ten minutes till they are a pale brown. For serving, stand the straws through the rings like a bundle of sticks.
