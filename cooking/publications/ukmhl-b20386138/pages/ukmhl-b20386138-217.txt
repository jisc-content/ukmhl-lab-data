( 205 ) EANCY BEEAD AND CAKES. Arrowroot Cake. Ingredients, One pound of Butter. One pound of Arrowroot, One pound of Sugar. Eight Eggs. Bind of one Lemon. Beat the butter to a cream; beat the yolks and whites of the eggs separately a long time; add the sugar rubbed through a sieve and the grated lemon-peel. Beat all well together for one hour, and bake in a moderate oven. Cocoanut Buns. Ingredients.
