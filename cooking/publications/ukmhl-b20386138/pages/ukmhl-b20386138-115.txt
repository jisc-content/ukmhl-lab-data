103 ENTREES. Rissoles a la Victoria. Ingredients. One gill of Supreme Sauce. Half a Chicken, minced. Half a Sweetbread „ Six Truffles Six Mushrooms „ Three Eggs. Mix all this into the sauce; stir in, when over the fire, the yolks of three eggs. Let this thicken, pour out on a dish, and when cold make up into rissoles. Egg and bread-crumb, and fry them a nice brown in boiling lard or fat.
