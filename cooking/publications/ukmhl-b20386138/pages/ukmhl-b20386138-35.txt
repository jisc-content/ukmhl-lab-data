Fried Bread, Wash all the vegetables, and cut them into thin slices. Melt the butter in a stewpan ; put in the sliced vegetables. Place the stewpan on the fire and let the vegetables cook for five minutes; then add the stock and milk, and boil slowly until the vegetables are quite tender. Now rub the vege- tables through a tammy-cloth or hair-sieve. Put the soup back in a saucepan, add the cream, and when hot it is ready to serve.
