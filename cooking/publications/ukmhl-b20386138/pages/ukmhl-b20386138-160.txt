HiaH-CLASS COOKEEY. 148 Croutes de Fromage Glace. Ingredients. Three ounces of grated Parmesan. Two ounces of Gruyere. Half a pint of whipped Cream. A gill and a half of whipped Aspic. Cayenne, Salt, and Pepper. Mix all these ingredients well together in a basin. Freeze this mixture in a mould or ice-box for about one hour, turn out^ cut into small square pieces, and serve on croutons of fried bread. Homard a la Creme. Ingredients, One Lobster. One gill of White Sauce* One tablespoonful of Mayonnaise Sauce, Pepper, Salt, Nutmeg. One gill of Aspic Jelly. Browned Bread-crumbs. Mince half a pound of the meat of the lobster finely, put it into a basin with the white sauce, Mayonnaise, aspic jelly, pepper, salt, and nutmeg. Mix well, steam until quite hot, serve in Eamequin cases with browned bread-crumbs sprinkled over it.
