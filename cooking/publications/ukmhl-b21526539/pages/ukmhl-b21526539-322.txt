Cover the bottom of a small oval paper form with ver}' thin slices of fat bacon : cut down the back six or eight nicely-washed small trout, and having removed the bones, lay the fish, open, flat upon the bacon, sprinkled with dropped parsley, pepper, salt, a little mace, and two cloves finely pounded. Care must be taken, when split- ting the fish, to leave half the tail of each attached to each half of the body, and to curl them inwards towards each other.
