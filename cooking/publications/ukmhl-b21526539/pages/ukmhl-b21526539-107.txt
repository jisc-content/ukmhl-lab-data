Chop the flesh of a large lobster, or two small ones, and mix with it a very little lemon-peel, pepper, salt, nutmeg, or mace, a little butter, cream, ^.nd a very few crumbs of stale bread. Roll the mass, and cover it in small quantities, the size of sausages, with a light puff’ paste. Rub them over with the finest yolk of egg, and dip them in the finest crumbs of bread.
