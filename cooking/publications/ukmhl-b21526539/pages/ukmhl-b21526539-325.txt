Do them slowly on a grid- iron, and serve in the cases. Any of the vegetable sauces may be served to eat with them. Piecls de Veau en fricassee.—Calf’s Feet fricasseed. Soak two fine calf’s feet in cold water six hours ; then simmer in a sufficiency of milk and water to cover them completely, until the meat can be easily removed from the bones in nice pieces ; sprinkle with cmmbs of bread, pepper, and salt, having previously dipped them in yolk of egg: fry of a beautiful colour, and serve in fricassee- sauce. Pieds de Veau en Sauce d’ Oignons, oxi en Sauce Tomate. —Calf’s feet in Onion or Tomata Sauce. Boil the feet of a good colour in milk and Mater; drain, and serve in three divisions, with the sauce between.
