For the ice :—Dissolve two ounces of chocolate in a pint of boiling milk, when quite smooth, and cold, add half a pint of cream, a tea-spoonful of vanilla essence and the yolks of ten eggs : make a custard of the mixture adding two ounces of sugar. Stir in half an ounce of dissolved gelatine while the custard is warm, and whip it briskly and put it into the freezer. Freeze, mixing into the half- frozen custard a coffee-cupful of whipped cream. Turn it out when ready, cover with the case, and serve. Note.—To adapt this menu, you need only insert after the fish, Boeuf a la mode, vide page 123.
