Potatoe Cheesecal-es. Boil six ounces of potatoes, and four ounces of lemon- peel ; beat the latter in a marble mortar, with four ounces of sugar ; then add the potatoes, beaten, and four ounces of butter melted in a little cream. When well mixed, let it stand to grow cold. Put crust in pattj-pans, rather more than half fill them. Bake in a quick oven half an hour; sifting some double refined sugar on them when going to the oven. This quantity will make a dozen. Almond Cheesecakes. Blanch and pound four ounces of almonds, and a few bitter, with a spoonful of water; then add four ounces of sugar pounded, a spoonful of cream, and the whites of two eggs well beaten, mix all as quick as possible; put into very small pattypans, and bake in a pretty warm oven under twenty minutes.
