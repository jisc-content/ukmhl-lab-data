For io save venysone fro restyivj Take venesone when hit is new slayn, And cover hit alle with feme playn pat no wynde enter per to; And whenne pou hast covered hit so, Lede hit home, selor hit lay pat wynde ne sone ne ughe’^ hit may; Dresse hit wele and wassh hit dene, Sythen, lay hit in water alle by dene, per in be half a day to lye; penne take hit oute on flore to drye, penne after take salt a quantite; Boyle hit in dene water so fre.
