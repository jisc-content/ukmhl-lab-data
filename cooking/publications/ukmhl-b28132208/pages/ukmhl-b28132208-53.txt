Bruise the bones, and return them to the soup, and boil for half an hour. Mix a little butter and flour with the livers, and stir them into the soup to thicken it Strain the soup, return it to the saucepan with the pieces of pigeon; boil very gently for ten minut^, skim- ming if required, and serve in a tureen, very hot.
