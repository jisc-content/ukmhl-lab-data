This is a drink which is used to cause perspiration, in cases of cold, or other ailments where there is no inflamma- tory tendency in the patient. Put the milk on the fire in a saucepan, and directly it boils, add the white wine, wdth a little sugar dissolved in it. A light floating curd will be instantly seen. Boil for a few minutes; then strain it through a hair-sieve, so that the whey may run from the curd. Serve the whey hot.
