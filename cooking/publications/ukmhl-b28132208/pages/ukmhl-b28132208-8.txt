The present work is designed entirely with a view to practical utility, for the use of young Housewives who study simplicity and economy in the preparation of food, and who require explicit directions for their guidance. Care has been taken to exclude costly dishes, and the use of expensive materials, or those which cannot easily be procured. The book is a Manual of plain directions to he used in the kitchens of the middle or less affluent classes; the endeavour being to show how tasteful and nutritious dishes may b(> prepared at little expense, in order to render home happy and attractive.
