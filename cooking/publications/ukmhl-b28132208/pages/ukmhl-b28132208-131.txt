Cream. Rub the butter into the flour, salt, and baking-powder. Beat the eggs to a froth, add to them a gill of cream, and stir these into the flour; if necessary, add a little more cream to form the mixture into a stiff dough; then turn the dough on to a floured board, roll it out to the thickness of three-quarters of an inch, and cut it into small rounds. Prick them on the top with a fork, and bake in a quick oven for fifteen minutes. Serve hot. DROPPED SCONES. 2 breakfast-cups of flour. i breakfast-cup of sugar.
