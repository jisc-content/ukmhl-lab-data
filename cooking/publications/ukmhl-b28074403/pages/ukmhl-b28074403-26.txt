He left the table with a worried brow, and I saw him talk to the proprietor —old Gerot gone to his reward—then he returned with a smile, and “it’s all right until to-morrow ! ” Emerson has said, “ We walk alone in the world.” I don’t believe it. “ Men talk of unkind hearts, kind deeds With coldness still returning. Alas ! the gratitude of men Has often left me mourning.”
