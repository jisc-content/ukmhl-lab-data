80 The Bachelor and the Chafing-dish. Four tablespoons of cold gravy, one of chutney paste, one of ketchup, one of vinegar, two tea- spoons of made mustard, two of salt, and two of butter. Mix these ingredients first smoothly in a soup-plate. Deviled Duck.—A dish that is generally liked is deviled duck. To prepare it remove the skin and bones of cold roast duck and cut the meat into moderate-sized pieces. Boil the livers and mash to a paste, and put 'in a saucepan with one table- spoonful of dry mustard, one teaspoonful of salt, a dash of Cayenne pepper, and two tablespoonfuls of lemon juice. Mix thoroughly and gradually; add two tablespoonfuls of melted butter and the same amount of water. In this put one and one-half pints of the cold duck and one gill of Madeira. Place the saucepan over the fire and stir until the mixture is smoking hot. Turn on a hot dish.
