30 The Bachelor and the Chafing-dish. Second.—To make French coffee : Simply pour hot water on well-ground coffee through the top strainer. If chicory is added it will help flavor and color. Delmonico and all best restaurants use it. Third.—Same as first, except when putting into it a cup of cold water add rich milk. This will bring a perfect golden color to your coffee and also saves extra serving. This is known on the menus as cafi au lait.
