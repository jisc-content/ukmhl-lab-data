Curried Rice.—One of the simplest and most palatable of dishes is curried rice. Wash half a pound of rice in two waters and pick it very clean. Put it into two quarts of boiling water, in which two teaspoonfuls of salt have been thrown ; stew slowly, uncovered, keeping down the boiling by adding a cup of cold water once or twice, for fifteen f]) \ *v minutes, taking care the grains do not break.
