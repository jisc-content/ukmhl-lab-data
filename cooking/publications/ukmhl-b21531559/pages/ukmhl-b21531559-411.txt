CRACK NUTS. Mix eight ounces of flour, and eight ounces of sugar 5 melt four ounces of butter in two spoonsful of raisin-wine ; then, with four eggs beaten and strained, make into a paste; add caraways, roll out as thin as paper, cut with the top of a glass, wash with the white of an egg, and dust sugar over.
