GAME. 153 TO POT BABBITS. Cut up two or three young, but full-grown ones, and take the leg bones off at the thigh ; pack them as closely as possible in a small pan, after seasoning them with pepper, mace, Cayenne, salt, and allspice, all in very fine powder. Make the top as smooth as you can. Keep out the heads and the carcases, but take off the meat about the neck. Put a good deal of butter, and bake the whole gently. Keep it two days in the pan, then shift it into small pots, adding butter. The livers also should be added, as they eat well. TO BLANCH RABBIT, FOWL, &C., is to set it on the fire in a small quantity of cold water and let it boil j as soon as it boils it is to be taken out and put into cold water for a few minutes.
