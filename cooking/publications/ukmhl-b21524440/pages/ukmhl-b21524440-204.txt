Calf’s Foot Jelly 2 Calfs Feet | 2 Quarts Cold Water Put the feet (which must be well cleaned) into a saucepan with the water; let them boil; skim off the scum that rises to the surface; let them boil gently for four hours; strain the liquor from the feet; let it get quite cold. When cold, remove every morsel of fat; dip a cloth in warm water ; squeeze it out; wipe over the surface of the jelly; put it into a pan ; flavour and clear as directed for lemon or wine jelly (p. 179). Then add the vegetables, herbs, celery seed, and salt; let all boil gently for an hour; strain it; let it get cold ; take off all the fat; wipe the sur- face over with a cloth dipped in warm water; put it in a pan ; clear as directed for lemon jelly (p. 179).
