Muttonettes. 1. Baked Mutton Pudding*. Ingredients.—Underdone remains of cold mutton, \ lb. bread crumbs, seasonings, 2 eggs, browned bread crumbs. Method.—Mince or chop about one pound meat with a little fat or cold bacon, soak the bread crumbs in stock and press dry, blend these, season with lemon peel, two teaspoonfuls curry paste worked with a little tomato pulp or sauce, salt and pepper, and a fried chopped onion, beat in two yolks and lastly the stiffly beaten white of one egg. Place in a greased deep baking dish in a steady oven until the pudding is set, but no longer, turn out, and dredge the outside with fried bread crumbs or raspings, unless it is sufficiently brown on the outside.
