Baked Tomatoes 22. Tomatoes, Baked. Ingredients.—Tomatoes, bread crumbs, butter, seasonings. Method.—Halve six or eight ripe tomatoes, season with pepper and salt, sprinkle bread crumbs lightly over them, and add a few drops of tarragon vinegar, if you have it. Divide about an ounce of butter into little pieces, and place these here and there upon the tomatoes. Bake in a moderate oven till browned, and serve very hot. 4i
