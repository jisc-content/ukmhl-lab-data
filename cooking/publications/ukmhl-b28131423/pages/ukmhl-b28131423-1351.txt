Prep. Cut slices of bread, toast and butter them; then cover them with slices of rich cheese, spread a little mus- tard over the cheese, put the bread in a cheese- toaster before the fire, and in a short time serve it up very hot. WELD. Syn. Woald. The Reseda luteola (Linn.), an herbaceous annual employed by the dyers. A decoction of the stems and leaves gives a rich yellow to goods mordanted with alum, tartar, or muriate of tin. See Yellow Pigments.
