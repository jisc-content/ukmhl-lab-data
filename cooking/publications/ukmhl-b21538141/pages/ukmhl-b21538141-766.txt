A real French omelet should never be ready before it is required for table. The guests should wait for an omelet. Fresh eggs and fresh butter are indispensable for making a really good omelet. Break six to eight eggs into a basin, add to them a tablespoonful of cream or milk, beat well with a fork for four or hve minutes and add a pinch of salt and a teaspoonful of castor sugar.
