Cut out the centres of 8 or 9 tomatoes, making the openings as small as possible, and remove the pulp. Fry a chopped shallot in half an ounce of butter, add two ounces of finely chopped cooked ham, fry a little longer, next add the tomato pulp, a glass of sherry, a teaspoonful of chopped parsley, six.chopped mushrooms, and a small handful of fresh breadcrumbs, mix well over the fire, season with pepper and salt, and bind with one ladleful of veloutee sauce and two yolks of eggs, stir again over the fire for a minute or two. Fill the tomatoes with this preparation, sprinkle with breadcrumbs and grated cheese, drop a little oiled butter on top of each, bake in the oven for half an hour. Dish up on a hot dish, reduce a little brown sauce with the gravy from the tomatoes, pour over, and serve.
