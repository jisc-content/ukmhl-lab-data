Hour, pint of milk, a ])inch of salt, 3 oz. castor sugar, 6 eggs, 2 tablespoonfuls raspberry jam, 6 thin pancakes. Melt the butter, stir in the flour, cook a little, add the milk (boiling), stir till it boils, season with a pinch of salt and add the sugar, cook and stir for five minutes longer, and let it cool. Work in six yolks of eggs and the jam, previously heated and strained. Butter a plain charlotte mould and dredge with flour. Line the bottom and sides of the mould with pancakes. Whisk four whites of eggs to a stiff froth and stir it lightly into the above mixture, also add the remainder of pancakes, cut into shreds. Turn into the prepared mould and bake in a moderate oven for forty-five minutes. Dish up, and serve with a suitable sauce. Caramel sauce goes well with this pudding.
