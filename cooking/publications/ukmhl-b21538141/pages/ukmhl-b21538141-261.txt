Cotelettes de Saumon a la Danoise [Salmon Cutlets with Danish Sauce). 2-J- lb. middle cut of salmon, anchovy paste, egg, breadcrumbs, fried parsley, Danoise sauce. Cut the salmon into half-inch-thick slices, and each slice in two lengthways. Trim neatly into cutlet shapes, season each with pepper and salt, spread a little anchovy paste on each side of the cutlets, dip them in flour and brush over with beaten egg, and cover with breadcrumbs.
