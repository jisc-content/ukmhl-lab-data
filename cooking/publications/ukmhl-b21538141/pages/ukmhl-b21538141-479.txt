Pigeons en Casserole a la Beige. 3 Bordeaux pigeons, 2 oz. butter, i gill gravy, I gill brown sauce, i gill Marsala or sherry wine, i small cabbage, 6 thin slices bacon, i small onion, seasoning. Truss the pigeons as for roasting, put them in an earthenware stewpan containing the butter (heated), brown them quickly, cook in the oven for ten minutes, take them up, untruss, and cut them into neat joints. Peel the onion, fry a nice brown in the butter, and pour off the butter. Blamh the cabbage and divide it into six portions, fry the bacon, roll up each slice of bacon in a portion of cabbage, arrange these in a layer in the earthenware pan, place the joints of pigeon on top, moisten with the stock, sauce, and the wine. Season with pepper, salt, and a little grated nutmeg, cover the pan, and set it in the oven to cook for about half an hour. Remove any fat that may float on the surface, and send the casserole to table as it leaves the oven.
