Cut the meat into six equal-sized slices of even thickness, pare them neatly into round or oval shapes, and flatten with a cutlet-bat. Season to taste, add the chopped parsley and a few drojis of lemon-juice, place the fillets in this, and shake the pan gently over the fire, but the sauce must on no account be allowed to boil, else it wall curdle. Dish up the fillets in a row' on a thin bed of mashed potatoes. Sauce over carefully, sprinkle over a few' drops of liquid meat glaze, and serve.
