Kippers Broil these over a clear fire ; or, if the fire is not clear, bake or fry them ; but broiling is best. Crabs and Lobsters These should be put into boiling water, that life may be destroyed instantly, and boiled for twenty minutes or longer according to their size. To Dress Crab Pick the meat from the crab, clear away the apron and gills, shred the meat and mix it with two table-spoonfuls of vinegar, two table-spoonfuls oil, a teaspoonful of mixed mustard, and pepper of and salt to taste. Wash and dry the large shell, and put the meat in it. Garnish with parsley. Mussels Wash and brush them well, then put them into a saucepan with a little salt. When they open, remove them from the shell and take care- fully away a small piece of weed from under the black tongue, as this is poisonous. Strain the liquor and pour over them. Stewed Mussels Prepare the mussels as in foregoing recipe, put them into a saucepan with sufficient of their own liquor to cover them, add a blade of mace.
