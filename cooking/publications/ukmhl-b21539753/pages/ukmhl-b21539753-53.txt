75 SAUCES AND GRAVIES SAUCES AND GRAVIES Plain Sauce Ingredients.—2 ozs. of flour, IJ pint of water. 1 oz. of butter or dripping, or even less, pepper and salt. Method.—Put a pint of water in a saucepan on the fire to boil, and mix the flour quite smoothly with the quarter pint. Pour it into the boiling water and boil for three minutes ; add pepper and salt, and stir in the butter or dripping off the fire. A better sauce may be made by adding more butter or dripping, and using new or skim-milk instead of water. Caper Sauce Add a table-spoonful of chopped capers to the plain sauce, and a dessert-spoonful of vinegar. If capers cannot be obtained, chopped gherkin may be substituted. If this sauce is to be used with boiled mutton, make it with the meat liquor.* Anchovy Sauce Add a dessert-spoonful of anchovy sauce to the plain sauce.
