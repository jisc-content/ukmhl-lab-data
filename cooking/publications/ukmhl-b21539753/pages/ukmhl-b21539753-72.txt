COOKERY FOR SMALL INCOMES 94 Light Pudding Ingredients.—A dessert-spoonful of corn-flour. h pint of milk, 1 egg, 1 dessert-spoonful of sugar. Method.—Put the milk on to boil. Mix the corn-flour smoothly with a little cold milk, stir it into the boiling milk ; continue stirring smoothly until it well thickens, add the sugar, and stir in the yolk of egg. Whip up the white to a stiff froth, either with a knife on a plate, or in a basin with a fork. Stir this in lightly, and pour the mixture into a greased pie dish. Bake in a quick oven for a quarter of an hour, until the pudding is thrown up and nicely browned. Semolina Pudding Make like corn-flour. It is very nutritious. Semolina is made from hard wheats.
