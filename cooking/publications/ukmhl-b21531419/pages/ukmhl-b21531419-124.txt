But you must put in all but four handfuls of herbs,which you must steep one night,andoneday, in alittlebowlofwater,beingclosecovered; thenext day take another quantity of fresh water, and boil the same herbs in it, till the colour be very high ; then take another quantity of water, and boil the same herbs in it, until they look green ; and so let it boil three or four times in several waters, as long as the Liquor looketh any thing green.
