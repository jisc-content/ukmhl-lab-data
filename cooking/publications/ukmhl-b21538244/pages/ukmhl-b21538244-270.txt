Mix the chopped veal and ham with the crumbs, onion, lemon rind, mustard, and the egg beaten up with the gravy. Season the mixture carefully and press it down gently but firmly into the mould, taking care not to disarrange the egg. Cover the top with a piece of greased paper, and put a lid with a weight on it over the paper to keep the mixture pressed together. Steam it for about three-quarters of an hour.
