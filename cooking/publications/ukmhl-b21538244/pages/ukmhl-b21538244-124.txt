Boiled Sugar Icing that Granulates Remedy.—Put it back in the sugar boiler, add a little more water, and reboil it again to the right degree. Meat Found to be Slightly Tainted Remedy.—Cut away any part particularly noticeable, then wash the rest well in cold water, containing either a little Condy’s fluid or vinegar. Then dry it and cook it at once, by dry heat; on no account stew or boil it.
