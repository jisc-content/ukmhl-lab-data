403 SAXADS A Good Salad-Dressing Ingredients— Two hard-boiled yolks of eggs. Eight tablespoonfuls of salad oil. Three tablespoonfuls of mixed vinegars. Two teaspoonfuls of French mustard. Two teaspoonfuls of castor sugar. Two tablespoonfuls of cream. One tablespoonful of claret. Seasoning. Method.—Mix together the salt, pepper, mustard, and sugar. Rub the yolks of egg through a sieve and add the mustard, etc., gradually and smoothly to them. Next add the oil and vinegars, stirring the mixture well all the time. Lastly, add the cream and claret. Put all into a bottle and shake it well. Cork it tightly and keep it in a cool, dry place.
