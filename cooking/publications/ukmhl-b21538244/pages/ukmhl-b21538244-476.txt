THE COMPLETE COOK 4o8 Watercress Sandwiches Ingredmits— Three rounded tablespoonfuls of Slices of brown or white bread. butter. Salt and pepper. Two bunches of watercress. Method.—Wash and examine the cress very carefully, and stalk and dry it lightly in a clean cloth. Chop the leaves very finely. Beat the butter to a cream, stir into it the cress and seasoning, spread a thick layer of the mixture on a slice of bread, cover with another and cut into neat shapes.
