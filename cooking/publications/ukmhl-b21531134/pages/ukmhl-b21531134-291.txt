ADELAIDE PUDDING.—E. R. Break seven eggs into a deep pan, leaving out three of the whites. Put ten ounces of loaf-sugar in a pint of water; set it over the fire until the scum rises; pour it over the eggs ; add a tea-spoonful of grated lemon- peel, and the juice of a lemon.
