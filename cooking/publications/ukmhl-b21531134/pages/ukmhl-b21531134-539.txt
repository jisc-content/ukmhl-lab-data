VERDER, OR MILK PUNCH. Pare six oranges and six lemons as thin as you can, grate them after with sugar to get the flavour. Steep Sie peels in a bottle of rum or brandy stopped close twenty-four hours. Squeeze the fruit on two pounds of sugar, add to it four quarts of water, and one of new milk boiling-hot; stir the rum into the above, and run it through a jelly-bag till perfectly clear. Bottle, and cork close immediately. NORFOLK bUNUH. In twenty quarts of French brandy put the peels of thirty lemons and thirty oranges, pared so thin that not the least of the white is left.
