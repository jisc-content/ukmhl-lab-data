Meat, Fowls, and Game, as in January, with the addition of Ducklings and Chickens; which last are to be bought in London most if not all the year, but very dear. Fish.—As the last two months; except that cod is not thought so good from February to July, but may be bought. Vegetables.—The same as the former months, with the addition of kidney-beans.
