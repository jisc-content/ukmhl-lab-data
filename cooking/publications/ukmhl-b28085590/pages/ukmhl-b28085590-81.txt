Tapioca and Apple Pudding Wash a dessertspoonful of tapioca and put it to soak in half a pint of cold water for an hour. Put it in a saucepan and let it boil, then simmer it gently until it is smooth and clear, stirring it frequently to avoid lumps. Pare and core two apples, and cut them into thin slices. Put them into a pie-dish and bake till the apples have become rather soft, in a moderate oven. Put one dessertspoonful oi castor sugar into the tapioca, and one of lemon juice, and pour it over the fruit. Bake till the apples are quite done, and serve either cold
