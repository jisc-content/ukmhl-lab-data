Little Queen^s Favourites Blanch and pound one ounce of almonds with a little sugar, mix them with three ounces of sifted or castor sugar; beat the white of one egg to a stiff froth and add it to the almonds and sugar; then add one ounce of ground rice; beat the mixture all well together, and when very light drop it from a spoon in small round cakes on to the tin, and bake in a slow oven.
