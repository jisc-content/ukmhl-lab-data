School^Room Puddings Make a nice batter of one egg, one ounce of flour, and a quarter of a pint of milk, beaten up well together and mixed by degrees, egg and flour first, then the milk ; butter some patty pans, pour the batter into them till they are three parts full, bake for a quarter of an hour, fill with jam and serve.
