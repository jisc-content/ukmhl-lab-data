Chicken Patties Pick the meat from some cold fowl; take two ounces of it and one ounce of ham, mince them well together, add half an ounce of butter rolled in a teaspoonful of flour, two tablespoonfuls of cream or milk, and one of gravy or stock, a little pepper and salt, a few pieces of rind of lemon, and an eggspoonful of the juice of lemon.
