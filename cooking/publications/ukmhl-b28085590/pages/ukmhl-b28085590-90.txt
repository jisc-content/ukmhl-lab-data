Chocolate Cream One bar of chocolate broken into small pieces, half a pint of milk poured over it to soak till it is dissolved. Put into a cup one tablespoonful of corn flour, mix it well with a little milk, then add the half-pint soaked in chocolate, and a dessertspoonful of sugar, boil for ten minutes, and pour into a wet mould.
