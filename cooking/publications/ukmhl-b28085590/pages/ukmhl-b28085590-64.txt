Cheese Omelet Take a tea-saucer, fill it with small pieces of cheese; mix a saltspoonful of mustard with an eggcupful of milk, add salt and a little pepper; pour it over the mixture and stand it in the oven till the cheese has melted; beat one egg into it, and put it back into the oven till it is a nice brown ; take out and serve.
