Baby^s Kisses To one pound of loaf sugar add a tea- cupful of water, stir it over a slow fire till it is melted. Add a teaspoonful of essence of lemon and stir well. When the mixture bubbles take it off the fire and stir It for a few minutes, then let drops of it fall on an oiled dish. Let the drops cool and then remove them to a warm place to dry thoroughly.
