Stuffed Tomatoes This will make an excellent dish for the schoolroom supper. Get four tomatoes, cut a thin slice from I the stem end, remove the seeds and soft j pulp, and put them into a basin with a small teacupful of soft bread crumbs and a : slice of meat finely minced. Add a pinch I of salt, a sprinkling of pepper, and a piece ! of butter, or beef dripping, the size of a : marble. Mix them well together, fill the ; tomatoes with the mixture, and put them ; on a baking-tin which has been well rubbed ' with dripping. Bake them till they are soft.
