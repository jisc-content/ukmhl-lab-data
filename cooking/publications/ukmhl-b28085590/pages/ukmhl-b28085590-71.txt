Sister Ann^s Fritters Put one ounce of flour into a basin, and mix it to a stiff paste with boiling water, stir quickly and beat the batter well to make it smooth. When the batter is cool break in one egg, both white and yolk, and one with the yolk only; beat well together with a wooden spoon, whisk the white of the second egg till it is well frothed, and, when the lard is ready for frying, add the whisked egg to the mixture, and drop a teaspoonful at a time into the frying-pan.
