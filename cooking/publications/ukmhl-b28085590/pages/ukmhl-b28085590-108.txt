This is done by doubling up your fists and punch- ing it hard till it is a nice ball on your pastry board. Then grease three cake tins, divide your dough into three pieces, knead each piece, and put it into a cake tin. Put it before the fire to rise for an hour, and then bake for about twenty minutes in a good oven, not too hot. Turn out on a sieve to get cool.
