33 c Fish Pie Get about four ounces of the remains of any cold fish, and break it into small pieces, add either an anchovy chopped finely, or a teaspoonful of anchovy sauce, and a tablespoonful of milk. Butter a small pie-dish, and put in this I mixture. Then mash three cooked potatoes I with a small piece of butter and a dessert- spoonful of milk.
