53 Swiss Egg Grate three ounces of cheese, butter a large saucer rather thickly, and cover it with part of the grated cheese. Break three eggs into the saucer, being careful not to break the yolks. Chop a small teaspoonful of parsley and mix it with the rest of the grated cheese, strew this over the eggs, and put a small bit of butter on each egg. Bake in a quick oven till the ep'gfs are set.
