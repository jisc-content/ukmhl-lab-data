Fairy Snow Sweeten the juice of half a lemon, put it in a dish, beat up the white of an egg, and mix it with half a pint of cream, add a little sugar and whisk it well; as the froth rises on the top of the cream take it off with a fork and put it on the lemon ; do this till all the cream is whisked.
