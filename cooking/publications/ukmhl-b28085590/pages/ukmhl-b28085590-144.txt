Beef Tea Cut into small pieces a quarter of a pound of gravy beef without any fat. Put it into a clean two pound jam pot, and just cover the meat with water. Place a saucer or small plate over the top to act as a lid, and put into a slow oven for three or four hours. Look at it occasionally to see that it is only simmering; if it boils, it will waste.
