103 Baby^s Cake Four ounces of corn-flour, three ounces of white sugar sifted, two ounces of butter, one egg, and one eggspoonful of baking powder. Rub the butter into the flour, mix in all the other ingredients, lastly stir in the egg and, if necessaiy, a tiny drop of milk, beat well together and bake on a tin in a slow oven.
