These cakes may be made with almond instead of cocoanut. The Chiefs Favourite Rolls Put half a pound of flour into a basin with a saltspoonful of salt and a tea- spoonful of castor sugar. Dissolve one ounce of butter in a teacupful of tepid milk, mix half an ounce of yeast with a little drop of cold milk, and add it to the tepid milk, &c.
