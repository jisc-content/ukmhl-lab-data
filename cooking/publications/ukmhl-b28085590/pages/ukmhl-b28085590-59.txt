When upon the point of boiling, pour it over the eggs in the dish and serve. Birds in the Nest Boil two eggs for ten minutes, take off the shells, cut the eggs in halves, and cut a tiny piece off each end to make them stand upright, take out the yolks, chop up a slice of chicken into tiny pieces with a small piece of beetroot and the yolk of the egg, and add a little pepper and salt; fill each cup with the mixture, put them on a dish, and surround them with watercress.
