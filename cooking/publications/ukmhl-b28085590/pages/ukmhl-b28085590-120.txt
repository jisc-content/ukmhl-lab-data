Rusks Four ounces of flour, one ounce of butter, half an ounce of lard, half a tea- spoonful of baking powder, half a tea- spoonful of sifted sugar, a pinch of salt, and one egg. Mix with the flour the baking powder, sugar, and salt; rub in the butter and lard, beat up the egg with a very little milk, pour into the flour, and mix as dry as possible. Roll it out, and cut into shapes with the top of the flour-dredger, and bake at once. When half done, split open with a fork, and put back into the oven, till slightly brown and crisp.
