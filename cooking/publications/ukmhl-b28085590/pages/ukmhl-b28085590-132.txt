Brazil-Nut Candy Dissolve a pound of sugar with a tea- cupful of water over the fire, and boil till it forms into threads on the spoon and snaps when you take the spoon out of the sugar. Then stir in a pound of Brazil nuts, chopped, and pour the mixture into a well-buttered tin, and when nearly cold mark with a knife into squares. Stuffed Walnuts Beat the white of an egg to a stiff froth with one teaspoonful of vanilla and one teaspoonful of water, then add gradually one pound of icing sugar, until the mixture forms a stiff paste. Work it very smooth
