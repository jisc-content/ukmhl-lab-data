BROTHS. 17 Time required, two hours. BEOTHS. BEEF BROTH. One pound of lean, juicy beef. One pint of cold water. Half a teaspoonful of salt. Mince the meat, put it into a stew-pan with the water and salt, and boil slowly one hour. Strain, add a shake of black pepper, if allowed, and serve hot, with strips of dry toast. Time required, one hour. MUTTON BROTH. Two pounds of lean, juicy mutton. One quart of cold water. One teaspoonful of salt. Remove every particle of fat and skin from the meat; then cut it up, put it into a stew-pan with the water and salt, and boil slowly an hour and a half. Strain, and set it away to cool; when cold, remove all fat and dregs, and heat the broth as required.
