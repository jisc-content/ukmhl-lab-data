Four tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar. Four drops of any essence preferred. After the tapioca has been well washed, soak it in the cold water seven or eight hours, or until it is tender. Put it into a double kettle with the milk, and cook until dissolved and well blended with the milk. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar together until very light, stir it into the tapioca, and remove at once from the fire. When nearly cold, stir in the whites of the eggs, which have been beaten to a stiff froth. Flavor and serve cold. Time required after soaking, two hours.
