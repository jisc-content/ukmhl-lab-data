Do not consult with a patient as to what he would like to eat; appropriate food should be given with- out his care. When a meal is finished, remove all articles of diet from the room, and do not leave deli- cacies intended for his use within sight. Never allow a patient to become faint or exhausted for want of nourishment. In convalescence, food should be given in great moderation. "Little and often" is a safe rule.
