14 INVALID COOKERY. Time required, about ten minutes. BEEF JUICE AND ICE CREAM. Four tablespoonfuls of beef juice without salt. Four tablespoonfuls of rich, sweet cream. Four teaspoonfuls of sugar. Four drops of essence of vanilla. Put all the ingredients into a freezer, and stir until the sugar is dissolved. Freeze in the same manner as you would any other ice cream.
