97 PUDDINGS. PUDDINGS. BREAD PUDDING. Half a pint of dry, toasted crust crumbs. One pint of boiling milk. One egg. One tablespoonful of sugar. A grate of nutmeg. Grate the crumbs into a small pudding dish, pour the boiling milk over them, cover close, and set aside to cool. Beat the egg and sugar together until very light, and stir it into the bread and milk, which should be nearly cold. Flavor, and bake in a brisk oven. Serve hot, with wine-sauce or cream. Time required for baking, twenty minutes. RICE PUDDING. One quart of cold milk. One-fourth of a pint of rice, well washed. One-fourth of a pint of sugar. Half a teaspoonful of salt. One quart of boiling milk. Nutmeg. Put the cold milk into a pudding dish with the rice, sugar and salt, and bake in a slow oven. 7
