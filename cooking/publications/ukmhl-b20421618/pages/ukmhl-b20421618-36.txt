INVALID COOKERY. 26 STEWED TRIPE. Half a pound of fresh honey-comb tripe. Half a pint of milk. Half a teaspoonful of salt. A shake of black pepper. One teaspoonful of butter. One scant teaspoonful of flour. Three tablespoonfuls of sweet cream. Cut the tripe into short, narrow strips, put them into a stew-pan with the milk, salt, butter and pep- per, and simmer until the milk is reduced to one- third. Blend the flour and cream well together, and stir it into the stew; simmer two minutes longer, and serve in a hot, covered dish. Time required, about half an hour. N.
