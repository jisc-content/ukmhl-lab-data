MISCELLANEOUS. 75 PORTEREE. One-fourth of a pint of cold water. Four tablespoonfuls of porter or brown stout. One teaspoonful of sugar. Four tablespoonfuls of pounded ice. Place all the ingredients in a large goblet, and stir until the sugar is dissolved. Grate a little nutmeg over the top, if desired. Drink through a straw. Time required, about ten minutes. SYLLABUB. Two teaspoonfuls of sugar. One tablespoonful of wine. One pint of new milk. Dissolve the sugar in the wine, and put it into a pint pitcher. Take it to the cow and milk into it, until the foam reaches the top. Time required, about ten minutes. TOAST WINE. One slice of well browned toast. Half a pint of boiling water. One teaspoonful of sugar. Two tablespoonfuls of wine.
