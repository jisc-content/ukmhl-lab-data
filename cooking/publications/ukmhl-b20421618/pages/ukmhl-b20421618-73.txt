Four tablespoonfuls of rice. One quart of cold water. One quart of boiling water. Wash the rice thoroughly, put it into a saucepan or double kettle with the cold water, and place it on the back part of the stove (where it will keep hot but not boil), and let it remain one hour. Acid the boiling water, and simmer slowly until the rice is nearly all dissolved. Strain, and drink either hot or cold. Time required, about two hours.
