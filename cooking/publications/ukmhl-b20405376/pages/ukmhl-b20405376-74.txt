52 COOKERY FOR INVALIDS. Mulled Claret. Put a very small piece of cinnamon and of crushed nutmeg, ginger, and cloves, into a bright saucepan with a quarter of a pint of water. Boil them for a minute, strain out the spice, and add half a pint of claret and four or five lumps of sugar. Let the wine get hot, and take care to remove it from the fire before it reaches the boiling point.
