Soak half an ounce of gelatine in the third of a pint of water, let it boil until dissolved, add a quarter of a pound of sugar, when melted strain and add a quarter of a pint of Madeira. Let the jelly stand until cool, whip it until it becomes a thick froth, put it into a mould, set it in a cool place or on ice until quite firm.
