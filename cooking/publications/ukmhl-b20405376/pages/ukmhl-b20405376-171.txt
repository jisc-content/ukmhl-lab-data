149 COOKERY FOR INVALIDS. Custard Cream. Pour half a pint of boiling milk or cream on to the yolks of three eggs well beaten, sweeten to taste, whip together, and put it in a stewpan; stir over a gentle fire until it thickens, then flavour with extract of vanilla or lemon, or with brandy; whilst cooling, stir occasionally, and when nearly cold, stir in a quarter of an ounce of Nelson's gelatine previously soaked in cold water, according to the directions on the packet and then dissolved in three tablespoonfuls of boiling milk, then put the cream into a mould, and let it stand until the next day.
