VEGETABLES. Fried Potatoes. POTATOES, properly fried, may occasionally be served with the chop or cutlet, and are more digestible and nourishing than when plainly boiled. This remark, however, applies only to potatoes fried in perfection and served immediately they are done, as they lose their crispness by standing.
