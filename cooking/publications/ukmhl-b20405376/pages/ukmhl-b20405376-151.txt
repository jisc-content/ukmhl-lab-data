Serve with sauce, made as directed for boiled rice, or with any kind of stewed fruit. Fig Pudding. Chop half a pound of figs very finely, mix them with a quarter of a pound of coarse sugar, a table- spoonful of treacle, four tablespoonfuls of milk, half a pound of flour, a quarter of a pound of suet, an egg, and a pinch of grated nutmeg. Put the pud- ding into a buttered mould, and boil for four or five hours. Plum Pudding.
