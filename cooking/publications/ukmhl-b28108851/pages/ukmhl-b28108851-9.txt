PREFACE TO NEW EDITION. 4 The present edition has been carefully revised, and a large number of new and original recipes have been added, which are within the power of every girl of average intelligence. The book is intended for middle class families and young persons desirous of acquiring a knowledge of Domestic Cookery. I hope the book will be found useful in awakening attention and im- proving a much neglected and often despised art. Some of the lectures deal with the theory of cook- ing, and reasons are given for doing things, and it is only by connecting the theory with the practice that cookery can be made an educational discipline in girlsâ€™ schools. Next to the art of cultivating the earth, cookery opens up a field of national usefulness and importance. A cookery book may give recipes, but it cannot give brains.
