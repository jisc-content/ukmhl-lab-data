This is perfectly true, and a thoroughly good cook will be no mean artist in every sense of the word. But very few people, even among those who are daily employed in cooking, will give the same amount of patience and diligence to attain the distinction of meriting the term " artist in this most important of professions as they would give to gain a knowledge of music or painting, or to learn a language.
