MAIGRE'' DISHES. 124 Timbale of Macaroni. Line a round mould witli good short paste ; fill it with, the following preparation^ and put on the cover^ closing the edges with beaten egg, and bake for three-quarters of an hour in moderate oven. Slip it carefully out of the mouldy so as not to disturb the shape. Serve hot. Filling: Make a sauce financiered^ (see Chapter V.)^ and stir into it a quarter of a pound of cooked macaroni. Or a white sauce may be made instead^ the macaroni added, also some grated cheese.
