ON THE USE OF INGREDIENTS. Everything used for making ice must be fresh and of the best quality. Use fresh fruit whenever possible, and avoid the use of essences or essential oils. Jam or fruit pulp (pre- served) may be used as a substitute when fresh fruit is unobtainable or out of season.
