The Kirschwasser should be added just before freezing. 47.—GLACE A L’EAU DE FRAMBOISES. (Raspberry Water Ice.) Ingredients. i| lb. ripe raspberries (picked). The juice of 2 lemons. 1J pints syrup. Method.—Rub the fruit through a fine sieve into a basin, add the lemon juice and the syrup ; strain and freeze. A few drops of liquid carmine may be added be- fore freezing if it is found necessary, though this is scarcely the case if sound and ripe fruit is employed. 48.—GLACE A L’EAU DE FRAISES. (Strawberry Water Ice.) Rub \ lb. of small ripe strawberries through a fine sieve, and proceed to add the same ingredients as indicated in recipe No'. 47, omitting' the raspberries. 49.—GLACE A L’EAU D’ABRICOTS. (Apricot Water Ice). This is made exactly the same as peach water ice, in No. 44, using apricot pulp instead of peach pulp ; a little liquid saffron, or saffron paste, should be added in order to give the ice a nicer tint.
