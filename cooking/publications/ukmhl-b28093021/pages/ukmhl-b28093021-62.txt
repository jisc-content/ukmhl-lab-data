(Milk Punch.) Some imitation opaque coloured cups (as mentioned in No'. 56) should be used for serving this punch. Otherwise use little fancy china cups. Pour a quart of boiling milk on to 4 oz. of loaf sugar, strain, and cool. Freeze to the usual consistency, and add two table-spoonfuls of rum and the same quan- tity of Cognac brandy, also a gill of whipped cream. Mix well, and freeze a little longer. Fill up the cups, and grate a little nutmeg or cinnamon over the top of each. 84.—PUNCH A LA NANSEN.
