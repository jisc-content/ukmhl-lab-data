Line one or two plain charlotte moulds with white paper, previously rubbed over with good olive oil. Trim some finger or savoy biscuits, and range them neatly round the inside of the moulds. Mix i oz. of finely cut glace cherries with the ice mixture, and fill the moulds. Place the mould or moulds in a charged ice cave for twoi hours.
