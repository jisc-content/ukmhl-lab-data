All good housewives will have herbs dried in their season duly sifted and put away in bottles for winter and occasional use. Of these parsley is the most important. It should be carefully picked, and freed from grit by shaking in a dry cloth, then laid on an iron sheet or dish on the hob or range until it becomes dry and crisp.
