LITTLE DINNERS. 82 boiling water. Then put to it a quarter of a pound of loaf-sugar and the peel of half a lemon. Let it simmer for half an hour, then strain, and allow it to stand until cool, but not so that it sets. Add to it the juice of a large lemon, and whisk until it is perfectly white and thick. Dip a mould into cold water, drain it. and then put in the sponge. Let it stand until the next day, then dip the mould into tepid water for half a minute, loosen the edges carefully with the top of a teaspoon, and turn out the sponge.
