I'lvsli brciulevumbs 1 clove 1 pint of milk 2 tablespoonfuls cream BREAD SAUCE. 1 small onion 4 peppercorns i oz. fresh butter Peel the onion, stick the clove in it; put the onion and milk in a saucepan, boil up, stir in the breadcrumbs, add the peppercorns, and cook for fifteen minutes. Remove the onion and peppercorns, stir in the cream and butter, season with a pinch of salt, and keep hot until required for serving. If liked, the onion may be cooked longer, passed through a sieve, and added to the sauce; the cream may be omitted; if found too thick, a little hot water may be added. Bread sauce is usually served with roast fowl, turkey, and game birds. 88.
