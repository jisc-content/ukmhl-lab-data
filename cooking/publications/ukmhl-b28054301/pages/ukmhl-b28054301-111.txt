ANCHOVY PATTIES. 12 anchovies 1 yolk of egg Cayenne 4 lb. puff paste 2 ozs. grated parmesan cheese Parsley Scrape, wipe and bone one dozen Gorgona anchovies, roll out some short paste or remnants of puff paste rather thin, enclose each anchovy neatly in the paste; place them on a baking sheet, brush them over with the beaten yolk of egg, mark slightly with a knife, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese and a pinch of cayenne over each. Bake them in a quick oven for ten minutes. Dish them up on a folded napkin, garnish with parsley, and serve quickly.
