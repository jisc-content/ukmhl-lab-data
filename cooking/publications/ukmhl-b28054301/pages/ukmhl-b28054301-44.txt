STEWED PIGEONS WITH OLIVES. 1 Bordeaux pigeon 1 oz. butter Pepper and salt i gill brown sauce 1 shallot 12 stoned French olives 6 preserved mushrooms ^ gill tomato sauce Draw the pigeon, truss it for roasting, and roast for ten minutes in a sharp oven, baste frequently with dripping or butter; untruss and cut it into neat joints. Peel and chop the shallot, fry it to a light brown in an ounce of butter, put in the pieces of pigeon and fry a little longer; season with pepper and salt; pour off the fat, add the brown and the tomato sauces, also the mushrooms cut in dice; boil up and skim; put in the olives, and stew gently for thirty minutes. Arrange the pigeon nicely on a hot dish, dispose of the olives and mushrooms, strain the sauce over the whole, and send to table. I.
