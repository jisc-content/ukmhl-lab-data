THE HOUSEKEEPER’S ORACLE. 97 KNUCKLE OF VEAL Is always boiled, and esteemed for its fat, and tendons about the knuckle. A handsome slice can be carved only in the direction 1, 2. About this part is some deli- cate fat, — and cutting in the direction 4, 3, two bones will be divided, — between these, fine marrowy fat is to be found.
