The inexperienced will scarcely believe what degree of improvement will be visible upon the removal of the first dressing: the Air is the great corrupter of Wounds. People in general have no idea what a healing process is pro- ceeding in large Wounds under a poultice unremoved for five or six days; and then the exclamation is, What a fine cure the Surgeon has made! The customary sticking-plaster is spread with a gum which does not suit an irritable Skin; the Gold-beater's Skin is pre- ferable for slight superficial Wounds.
