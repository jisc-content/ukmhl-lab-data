BREAST OF VEAL ROASTED Should be first cut quite down, in the first line on the left, 4, 3; next cut across, in the direction 1, 3, from 3 to the last 1 on the left, taking care to divide the gristles from the rib-bones. The gristly part being divided into parts, in the direction 1, 2, may be offered to those who prefer it: — (in a breast of Veal stewed, these are particularly tender and in- viting) : — the ribs are to be separated in the direction 4, 3 ; and, with a part of the breast, a slice of the sweetbread, 5, cut across the middle.
