PLAIN AND VERY CRISP BISCUITS. Make a pound of flour, the yolk of an egg, and some milk, into a very stiff paste ; beat it well, and knead till quite smooth j roll very thin, and cut into biscuits. Bake them in a slow oven till quite dry and crisp.
