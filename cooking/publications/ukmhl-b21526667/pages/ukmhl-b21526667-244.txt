TO KEEP ANCHOVIES WHEN THE LIQUOR DRIES. Pour on them beef-brine. TO MAKE SPRATS TASTE LIKE ANCHOVIES, Salt them well, and let the salt drain from them. In twenty-four hours wipe them dry, but do not wash them. Mix four ounces of common salt, an ounce of bay salt, an ounce of saltpetre, a quarter of an ounce of sal prunel, and half a tea-spoonful of cochineal, all in the finest powder. Sprinkle it among three quarts of the fish, and pack them in two stone jars. Keep in a cold place, fastened down with a bladder. These are pleasant on bread and butter, but use. the best for sauce.
