CHEESECAKES, ANOTHER WAV. Mix the curd of three quarts of milk, a pound of currants, twelve ounces of Lisbon sugar, a quarter of an ounce of cinnamon, ditto of nutmeg, the peel of one lemon chopped so fine that it becomes a paste, the yolks of eight and whites of six eggs, a pint of scalded cream, and a glass of brandy. Put a light thin puff paste in the pattypans, and three parts fill them. LEMON CHEESECAKES. Mix four ounces of sifted lump-sugar, and four ounces of butter, and gently melt it; then add the yolks of two and the white of one egg, the rind of three lemons, shred fine, and the juice of one and a half, one Savoy biscuit, some blanched almonds pounded, three spoonsful of brandy ; mix well, and put in paste made as follows: eight ounces of Hour, six ounces of butter, two thirds of which mix with the Hour first, then wet it with six spoonsful of water, and roll the remainder in.
