Beat the breast-bone Hat, and turn the feet close to the legs, and put one into the other. Draw out the gizzard, and run a skewer through the middle of the bodies. Tie the skewer fast to the spit when you put them down to roast. Wheat ears, and other small birds, must be done in the same manner. POULTRY. TO BOIL TURKEY.
