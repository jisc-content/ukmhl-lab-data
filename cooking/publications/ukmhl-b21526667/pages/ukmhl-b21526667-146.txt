Salt it eight or ten days : when it is to be dressed, weigh it; let it lie half an hour in cold water to make it white j allow a quarter of an hour for every pound, and half an hour over, from the time it boils up : skim it as soon as it boils, and frequently after. Allow water enough. Save some of it to make pea-soup. Some boil it in a very nice cloth, floured ; which gives a very delicate look. It should be small and of a fine grain.
