Method.—Beard the oysters and cut each into three or four pieces. Strain the oyster liquor through muslin. Melt the butter in a stewpan. Mix in the flour smoothly. Add a gill of oyster liquor and the cream, and cook well. Add the seasoning, and, off the fire, the yolks of three of the eggs and tho oysters.
