Preserve of different kinds. Method.—Cut the cake into slices, and stamp out the centre with a circular cutter. Spread with different coloured jams. Replace the cake in shape. Cover all over with a white or coloured glace (see Garnishes) and decorate with leaves of angelica or cherries. When about to serve, place in the centre half a pint of whipped cream, sweetened with castor sugar. Baked Lemon Pudding. Ingredients. Yolks of 4 eggs. i lb. of butter. 2 ozs. of castor sugar. Some pastry. 1 large lemon. Method.—Cream the butter, and add the sugar and the eggs one by one. Then mix in the grated rind of the lemon and strained juice. Line a small pie-dish with pastry, and pour in the mixture; bake half an hour. Diplomatic Pudding. Ingredients. ± pint of sweet jelly. 2 ozs. of ratafias. 1 pint of milk. 1 whole egg and 4 yolks. Not quite % oz. of isinglass. 2 ozs. of sugar. 2 sponge cakes. A little flavouring essence. Method.—Soak the isinglass in a little milk. Break the sponge cakes and ratafias, and put them into basin. Boil the milk with the sugar. Beat the eggs, and pour the milk on them.
