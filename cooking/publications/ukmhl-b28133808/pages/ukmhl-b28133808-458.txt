Beat half a pound of butter to a cream, and then beat well with it a quarter of a pound of icing sugar. Mix in very gradually half a gill of chocolate. Coffee icing may be made also in this manner. Almond Icing (for Cakes). Mix two pounds of ground almonds and two pounds of sifted sugar with from five to seven raw whites of eggs, slightly beaten. Do not make the icing too moist.
