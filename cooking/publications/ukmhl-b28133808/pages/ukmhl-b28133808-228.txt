A COMPLETE COOKERY GUIDE. 208 Fish Cakes. Ingredients. 1 lb. of cooked fish (cod, haddock, ling, and hake are best for the purpose). 1 lb. of boiled potatoes. 1 oz. of butter. 2 or 3 eggs. Some white crumbs. Parsley. Pepper and salt. Method.—Eub the potatoes through a sieve. Break the fish into flakes and remove the bones. Mix the fish and potatoes with the butter, pepper and salt and one egg well beaten. Form the mixture into balls or cakes, egg and bread- crumb them. Ee-shape them after the crumbs are on, and fry them in hot fat (see French Frying). Drain well and serve on a folded napkin, garnished with fried parsley. Fish Pudding. Prepare a mixture as for fish cakes. Put it on a dish that will stand the heat of the oven; shape it like a fish. Brush over with beaten egg and bake for half an hour. Fricassee of Cooked Fish.
