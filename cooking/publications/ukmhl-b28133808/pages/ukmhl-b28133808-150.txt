Ingredients. 1 pint of stock. The trimmings from the heart. 1 onion stuck with 2 cloves. 1 oz. of butter. 1 oz. of flour. A little Harvey’s sauce or catsup. A little colouring, if necessary. Pepper and salt. Method.—Put the trimmings into a saucepan with the onion and stock, and simmer gently while the heart is cooking. Then melt the butter in a stewpan.
