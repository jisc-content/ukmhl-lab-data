(diabetic.) 12 sauce oysters. \ gill oyster liquid. ^ gill of cream. 2 yolks of egg. China or paper ramakin cases. Blanch the oysters, remove the beard, and cut the oysters in half. Warm the oyster liquid, beat the yolks of egg with the cream and stir them in. Stir until the mixture slightly thickens, then season and put in the oysters. Serve in the ramakin cases. (Be careful not to boil or the mixture will curdle.)
