98 SICKROOM COOKERY. STEWED TRIPE. ^ lb. of tripe. 1 onion. pint of milk. \ dessertspoonful of flour. Pepper and salt. Blanch the tripe by putting it into cold water, and bring it to tbe boil. Strain away the water. Remove all fat, and cut into neat square pieces. Put the tripe, the onion chopped, and the milk into a saucepan, and season with pepper and salt. Bring to the boil and simmer gently for two hours. Blend the flour smoothly with a little cold milk, and strain in. Stir until it boils up. Serve very hot.
