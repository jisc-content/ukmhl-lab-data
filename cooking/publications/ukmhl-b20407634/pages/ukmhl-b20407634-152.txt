Put the wholemeal and salt into a basin, melt the butter and pour it in. Beat the eggs well, and stir in sufficient to bind to a stiff paste. If more moisture is needed add a little water. Roll out to the thickness of biscuits, stamp into small rounds, and make some holes with a skewer. Bake for fifteen minutes in a good oven, but do not brown. A dessertspoonful of sugar may be used instead of the salt if liked. CAKES. CORNFLOUR CAKES. 2 oz. of butter.
