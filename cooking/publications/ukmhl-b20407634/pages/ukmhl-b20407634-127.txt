APPLE MERINGUE. 1 lb. of apples. 1 lemon, rind and juice. 3 oz. of sugar. 2 oz. of butter. 2 eggs. Put the butter into a white saucepan, add the apples peeled and cut up, tbe sugar, and the rind and juice of a lemon. Simmer until tender. Rub through a hair sieve, then stir in the yolks of eggs, and place in a small piedisb. Bake in the oven for eight minutes. Whip the whites of egg to a stiff froth, place on top of the pudding, shaking- plenty of castor sugar through the white of egg. Bake for three minutes to set the meringue. Serve. APPLES AND SAGO. 4 apples. 1 pint of water. 2 oz. of sugar. 1 oz. of sago or crushed tapioca. \ lemon, rind and juice. Cochineal. Bring the water to the boil, sift in the sago, and cook until clear, stirring all the time. Add the lemon rind grated, the juice, sugar, and the apples, peeled and cored. Draw to the side of the fire, cook very gently until the apples are tender. Serve on a hot dish. Colour the syrup if liked.
