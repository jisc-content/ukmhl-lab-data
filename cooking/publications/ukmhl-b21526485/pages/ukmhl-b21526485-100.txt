To make icing for your wedding cake, beat the whites of eggs to an entire froth, and to each egg add five teaspoonfuls of sifted loaf sugar, gradu- ally ; beat it a great while. Put it on when your cake is hot, or cold, as is most convenient. It will dry in a warm room, a short distance fiom a gentle fire, or in a warm oven. LOAF CAKE. A common sized loaf is made by these proportions.
