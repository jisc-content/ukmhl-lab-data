14 PRACTICAL COOKERY. Vanilla Sauce. 3 yolks. i teaspoonful vanilla essence. ยง gill milk. 2 whites stiffly whipped. 2 oz. castor sugar. Method : Make a custard of the yolks, milk and sugar, add the vanilla essence. Whip the whites to a very stiff froth, and put into a large basin. Pour over them, through a hair sieve, the hot custard, mix lightly but well. Serve either hot or cold.
