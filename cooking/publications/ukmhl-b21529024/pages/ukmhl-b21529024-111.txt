preserves, etc. 95 Bengal Chutney. 1 pint vinegar, li lb. moist sugar. 4 lb. cooking apples, weighed after paring. 1 lb. stoned raisins. 1 lb. sultanas. J lb. salt. 2 oz. powdered ginger. \ teaspoonful cayenne. 1 oz. garlic. Method : Put the vinegar and sugar into a pan, bring to the boil and skim well, then add the other ingredients. The stoned raisins should be cut up roughly, the sultanas left whole, each clove of garlic cut into 4 or 5 pieces, and the apples sliced. Boil gently till thoroughly blended and till the apples and garlic are quite tender.
