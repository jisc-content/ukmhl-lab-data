Meat Jelly. 1 lb. lean beef. 1 chicken. 1 lb. lean veal. Salt. Method: Remove all skin and fat, and cut up both meat and chicken into very small pieces. Put into a stew-jar, with a sprinkling of salt, and cover closely, having no water with the meat. Set in a pan of boiling water and cook for 7 hours.
