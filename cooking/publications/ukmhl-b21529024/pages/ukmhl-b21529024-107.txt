Yanilla Ice Cream. 1 pint milk. 1 pint double cream. 4 eggs. 4 tablespoonfuls vanilla essence. 10 oz. castor sugar. Method: Heat the milk, and pour it over the well- beaten eggs and sugar. Cook till thick, then strain and cool, stirring occasionally to prevent a skin forming. When quite cold, add the flavouring and the whipped cream, and freeze.
