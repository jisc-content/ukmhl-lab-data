To one cupful of water add one cupful of molasses, one cupful of sugar, one beaten egg, one cupful of vin- egar, two and one half Boston crackers, rolled fine, one cupful of stoned raisins, butter the size of an egg, one quarter of a teaspoonful of cloves; two thirds of a teaspoonful of cinnamon; two thirds of a teaspoonful of allspice. This will make three pies. The pies should bake slowlv, taking about three quarters of an hour. A grating of nutmeg should be added to each pie before putting on upper crust. The egg, cracker, and slow baking thickens this apperently thin mixture. FRUIT PIE.
