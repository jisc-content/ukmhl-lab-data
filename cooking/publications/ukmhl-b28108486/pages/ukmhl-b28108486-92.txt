F] FRITTERS (PUMPKIN). FRITTERS 0PUMPKIN). Stew a sweet pumpkin; when soft, add a few ounces of flour to thicken it. For two cups of pumpkin take a good slice of white bread, soaked in milk and squeezed very dry. Mix well together with a pinch of salt, some cinnamon, and a quarter of a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda. Fry in boiling fat. Serve hot, with sugar and cinnamon.
