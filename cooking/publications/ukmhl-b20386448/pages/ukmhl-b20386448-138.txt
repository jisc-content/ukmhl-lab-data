HIGH-CLASS COOKEHY. 128 between two dishes till nearly cold, trim them neatly, egg and bread-crumb them, fry them a light brown, dish them up on cold Tartare sauce, garnish with pickles and serve. Tendrons de Veau a la Villeroy. Ingredients. Two pounds of breast of Veal braised. Quarter pound of Liver Farce (see Cutlets a la Kachel). Quarter pint of thick Bechamel Sauce. Egg and Bread-crumbs. Half pint of Brown Sauce. Macedoine to garnish. Cook the veal carefully until done, about one and a half hours. Then pull out the bones, and press itflat. Wlien cold trim into cutlet-shaped pieces. Cover each piece with liver-farce, and then with Bechamel. Egg and crumb carefully, and fry in sufficient fat to cover. Arrange the tendrons in a circle, pour the brown sauce round, and place the macedoine in the centre. Tete de Veau en Tortue. Ingredients, Half a Calf's Head. One Carrot. One Onion. Two sticks of Celery. Two tablespoonfuls of Flour. One gill of Vinegar. One tablespoonful of Salt.
