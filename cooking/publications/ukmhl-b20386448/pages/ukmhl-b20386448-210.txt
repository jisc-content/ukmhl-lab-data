200 HiaH-OLASS COOKERY. A piece of Slialot. One ounce and a half of Butter. Salt and Pepper. Skin the kidney and cut up into thin slices, break the eggs into a basin, beat well and season with a pinch of white pepper and salt, and add the chopped parsley. Put half the butter in an omelet-pan, when hot add the chopped shalot, fry a little, add the kidney, and saute for a few seconds. Put the remainder of the butter in another omelet-pan, when hot put in the egg- mixture, and proceed the same as for Savoury Omelet. When set and ready for folding, put the stewed kidney in the centre fold, turn on a dish, and serve with a tablespoonful of hot demi-glace sauce poured round the omelet. Oyster Omelet. [ Ingredients. Three Eggs. Six Oysters. Pepper and Salt. Blanch and beard the oysters and cut each into four. Separate the yolks from the whites of the eggs, beat the yolks a little, and add the oysters and pepper and salt. Whip the whites to a stiff froth and stir lightly to the yolks. Put the mix- ture into the omelet-pan for two or three minutes over the fire, stirring all the time.
