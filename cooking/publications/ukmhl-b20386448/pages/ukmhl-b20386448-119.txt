One tablespoonful of Mushroom Ketchup. The grated peel of half a Lemon. One teaspoonful of Cayenne Sauce. One blade of Mace. Half pint of Stock. Two dozen of Oysters. Sippets of Toast. Mince the veal and make it hot in the stock with the ketchup, cayenne sauce, lemon peel, and mace. When thoroughly hot, take out the mace; scald the oysters in their own liquor, taking off the beards; put the mince on a hot dish, the oysters in the centre and the sippets of toast round.
