Half a pint of Water. Two ounces of Buttel*. Four ounces of Flour. One Yolk and two whole Eggs. Essence of Vanilla or Orange. A little Salt. Put the water, butter, and salt into a stew- pan, as soon as these begin to boil, draw the stewpan off the fire and add the flour, stir these well together over the fire with a wooden spoon until it becomes a soft paste; now add the flavour- ing and yolk of one egg, beat these well together and add the two whole eggs one at a time. Place this paste on a baking sheet in round balls, bake a very light brown, and serve either plain or filled with whipped cream. PufF Paste. Ingredients. Half a pound of Flour.
