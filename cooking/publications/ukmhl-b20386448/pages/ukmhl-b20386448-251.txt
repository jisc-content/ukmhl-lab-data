CREAMS, JELLIES, ICES, COLD SWEETS. 241 Meringues. Ingredients. Four Whites of Eggs. A half pound of Castor Sugar. . A flat Meringue Board. Oiled paper to cover the Board. Cover the meringue board with oiled paper. Whip the whites of eggs to a very stiff froth and stir in the sugar. Shape some of this mixture in a dessertspoon, scoop it out with another dessert- spoon and lay it on the oiled paper, sift some castor sugar over each meringue and dry them well in a warm oven. Then turn them over, press in all the soft part underneath, and dry them again. If for immediate use, fill them with whipped cream sweetened and flavoured with vanilla. If not required at once, put them away in a tin box covered over with paper until they are wanted. Pouding k TOrldans. Ingredients, Half au ounce of Gelatine. Half a pint of Milk.
