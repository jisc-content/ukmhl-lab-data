Partridge Pudding. Ingredients. Two Partridges. Half a pound of Veal Cutlet. One gill of good Brown Stock. Pepper and Salt. One Shalot. Chopped Mushrooms. Suet Paste. Grease and line a pint-and-a-half pudding basin with good suet paste, cut up the veal cutlet and place it in the bottom of the basin, cut up the partridges into neat joints, lay them on the cutlet, sprinkle over pepper, salt, chopped shalot, and mushrooms, pour in the stock, wet the edge of the paste, put on the top crust, tie over with a cloth, plunge into boiling water and boil steadily for two hours.
