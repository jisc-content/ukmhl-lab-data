Ingredients, Six ounces of Vienna Flour. Five ounces of Castor Sugar. Kind and juice of one Orange, grated. One teaspoonful of Baking-powder. Three Eggs. Beat the eggs and sugar together to a smooth cream; add the flour and baking-powder sifted, and the orange juice and rind. Mix all well together, and bake in a quick oven for a quarter of an hour. When cold, ice it with French icing made of half a pound of icing sugar worked well with the juice of the orange ; when well mixed, pour this all over the cake. Queen Cakes.
