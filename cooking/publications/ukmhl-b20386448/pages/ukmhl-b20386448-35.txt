SOOTS. 2S Puree de Pois Verts. Ingredients, One quart of Peas. One handful of Parsley. A small bunch of Mint. One quart of Stock. One gill of Cream. Salt and Pepper. Put the stock on to boil. When boiling add the salt, then the peas, and other ingredients except the cream; boil until the vegetables are tender, then pass them through a hair-sieve with the stock they were boiled in, and again pass through a tammy-cloth, if desired very smooth; put all back into a clean stewpan to boil up, and just before serving add the cream and a teaspoonful of castor sugar.
