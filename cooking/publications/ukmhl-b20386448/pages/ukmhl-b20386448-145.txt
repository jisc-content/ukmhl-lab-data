BAUOES. 135 Bread Sauce, Ingredients, One gill of Bread-crumbs, One small Onion. Five Peppercorns. Half a pint of Milk, Salt. One tablespoonful of Cream. Put the milk into a stewpan, when boiling add the crumbs, onion, salt, and peppercorns, and let it stand by the fire for fifteen minutes to soak the bread, then stir it and add the cream.
