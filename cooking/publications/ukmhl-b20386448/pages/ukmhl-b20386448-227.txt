Pour into the mould, and set. Gateau de Riz. Ingredients, One quart of Milk. Four ounces of Kice. Three ounces of Castor Sugar. Three quarters of an ounce of Gelatine. Half a pint of double Cream. Boil the milk and stir in the rice, and cook very gently till quite tender; turn this into a basin, and when nearly cold dissolve the gelatine with a gill of milk, then add the sugar, and strain into the rice, add the cream half-whipped, and mix very lightly. Decorate a mould with a little jelly and pistachio nut, set it in ice; pour in the rice and set. Serve with a compote of fruit in the centre.
