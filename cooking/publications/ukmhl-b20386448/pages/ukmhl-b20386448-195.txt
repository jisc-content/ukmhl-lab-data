185 SAVOUEIES. Eight thin slices of Gruyere Cheese. Two ounces of grated Gruyere Cheese. Nutmeg. Mignonette Pepper. Salt. One gill of double Cream. A little chopped Parsley. Spread the butter on the bottom of a dish and lay the slices of cheese on it; break the eggs upon the cheese, being careful not to disturb the yolks. Sprinkle some grated nutmeg, mignonette pepper and salt over them ; mix the chopped parsley, the cream whipped, and the grated cheese together, and pour over them; bake in a quick oven about ten minutes. Hand very thin dry toast with the eggs. CEufs Farcis a la Russe. Ingredients. Six hard-boiled Eggs. Caviare. Tomatoes. Oil. Vinegar. Cut the eggs in half, and take out some of the yolk from the centre of each half; fill this space in with caviare, and cover over with the yolk rubbed through a sieve. Serve on slices of tomato, with a little salad oil, vinegar, and pepper and salt over them.
