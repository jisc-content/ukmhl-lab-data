( 245 ) FANCY BEEAD AND CAKES, Arrowroot Cake. Ingredients, One pound of Butter. One pound of Arrowroot, One pound of Sugar. Eight Eggs. Rind of one Lemon. Beat the butter to a cream; beat the yolks and whites of the eggs separately a long time; add the sugar rubbed through a sieve and the grated lemon-peel. Beat all well together for one hour^ and bake in a moderate oven. Brioche. Ingredients for the Sponge, Four ounces of Flour. Half an ounce of Yeast. Half a gill of warm Water.
