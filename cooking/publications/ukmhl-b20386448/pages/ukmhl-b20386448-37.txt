Then put this into a pan with the stock and cream, season, and get quite hot, but care must be taken that it does not boil, or the colour will be spoilt. Serve with fried bread. Restorative Soup. Ingredients, One Calf s Foot. Three pounds of Shin of Beef. One pound of Knuckle of Veal. Three quarts of Water. Salt. Blanch the foot and cut it up into pieces. Cut up the veal and beef, and put all on to boil in three quarts of water. Skim well, and let it simmer gently till reduced to three pints.
