Filets de BcBuf a la Bearnaise. Ingredients, Two pounds of FiUet of Beef, cut up into neat round fiUets. Two ounces of Glaze. Two ounces of Butter. One chopped Mushroom. Pepper and Salt. Try the fillets in butter with the mushroom and seasoning. When cooked, brush each one over with a little glaze. Dish the fillets in a circle on mashed potato or spinach. Serve Bearnaise sauce in the centre, with some glaze poured round.
