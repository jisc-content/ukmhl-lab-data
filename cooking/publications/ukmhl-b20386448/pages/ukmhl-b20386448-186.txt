HIGH-CLASS COOKERY, 176 Choux Marin au Parmesan^ Ingredients. One basket of Seakale. Three ounces of Parmesan, Two ounces of Butter. Seasoning. Wash the kale, cut it across and boil in salted water until tender. Then drain and place it on a greased dish in layers with grated cheese and seasoning between each. Sprinkle cheese over the top, pour some butter melted over all, and bake for about ten minutes or until slightly browned. Cold Cheese Souffle. Ingredients, One and a half ounces of Gruyere Cheese. One and a half ounces of Parmesan. Half a pint of Cream. One giU of Aspic Jelly. Cayenne and Mustard. Grate the Gruyere and Parmesan, whip the cream and stir in the cheese, season to taste with salt, cayenne, and made mustard, whisk the jelly and mix it in lightly; serve in Eame- quin cases with a layer of aspic and a little grated cheese on the top.
