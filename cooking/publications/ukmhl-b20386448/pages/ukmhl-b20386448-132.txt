122 HIGH-CLASS COOKEKY. Croquettes a la Victoria. Ingredients. One gill of Supreme Sauce. Half a Chicken, minced. Half a Sweetbread „ Six Truffles Six Mushrooms „ Three Eggs. Mix all this into the sauce; stir in, when over the fire, the yolks of three eggs. Let this thicken, pour out on a dish, and when cold make up into croquet shapes. Egg and bread-crumb, and fry them a nice brown in boiling lard or fat. These should be dished on a napkin, served with parsley fried ; or they can be made into cutlet shape, and served with brown sauce, and peas or macedoine in the centre. Souffle de Perdrix.
