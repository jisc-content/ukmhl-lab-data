Turn the pudding into a mould or floured pudding- cloth, put it into boiling water, and let it boil for five or six hours. Pine-apple Souffle. Ingredients. Three ounces of Pine-Apple. Three ounces of Flour, sifted. Three ounces of Sugar. Two ounces of Butter. Half a pint of Milk. Yolks of three Eggs. Whites of four Eggs. Melt the butter in a stewpan, and add the flour and milk; cook well; add the sugar and the pine- apple, previously cut into dice ; add the yolks one by one; whip the whites very stiff; stir in the mixture very lightly; pour into a prepared soufile mould; steam one hour.
