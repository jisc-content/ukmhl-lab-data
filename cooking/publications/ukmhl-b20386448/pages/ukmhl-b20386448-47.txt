Dish the cutlets on a napkin and garnish with fried parsley. Lobster Souffle. Ingredients. Two Whitings. One Hen Lobster. One gin of Cream. Two ounces of Butter. Two ounces of Flour. One gill of Fish Stock made from bones of the Whitings. Four Eggs. Take the meat of the whitings (half a pound) off the bones and the coral from the lobster, pound these two together in a mortar, then pass through a wire sieve. Make a sauce of one ounce of butter, two ounces of flour, and the gill of fish stock ; this must be well cooked. Take the meat of the body of the lobster (half a pound) and pound in a mortar with the whiting and the sauce, adding the eggs one at a time; when well pounded, pass all through a hair sieve, add a little cayenne pepper and the cream well whipped.
