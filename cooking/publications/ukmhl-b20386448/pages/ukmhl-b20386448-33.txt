Put the pieces of lobster, and some small quenelles of whiting into the tureen, pour the soup on them, and serve. Puree de Pommes de Terra. Ingredients, One pound of Potatoes. One Onion. Two leaves of Celery. One ounce of Butter. One pint of Stock. Half a pint of Milk. One gill of Cream. Salt. Fried Bread. Wash all the vegetables, and cut them into thin slices. Melt the butter in a stewpan ; put in the sliced vegetables. Place the stewpan on the fire and let the vegetables cook for five minutes ; then add the stock and milk, and boil slowly until the vegetables are quite tender. Now rub the vege- tables through a tammy-cloth or hair-sieve. Put the soup back in a saucepan, add the cream, and when hot it is ready to serve. Take two or three slices of stale bread; remove the crust, and cut the crumb into dice. Fry either in fat or butter, and serve with the soup.
