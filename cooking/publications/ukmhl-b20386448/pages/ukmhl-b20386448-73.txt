CURHIES AND INDIAN DISHES. 63 Clear Mulligatawny. Ingredients. One quart of good Stock. Two ounces of Butter. One sliced Onion. Two sliced Tomatoes. Three tablespoonfuls of Curry Paste. Two green Chillies cut fine. Three Eggs. Fry the onion in the butter, add the tomatoes, chillies and curry. Then pour in the stock and boil for about half an hour. Skim well. Let the soup cool. Beat the whites and yolks of the eggs together, wash and crush the shells, and add all to the soup. Stir over the fire until boiling, and strain in the usual way.
