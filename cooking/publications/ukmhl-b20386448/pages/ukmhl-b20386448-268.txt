Vienna Bread. Ingredients, One pound of Flour. Half an ounce of Yeast. Quarter of an ounce of Castor Sugar. Quarter of an ounce of Salt. One Egg. One ounce of Butter. Half a pint of warm Milk. Mix the sugar and yeast together ; beat up the egg, add it to the milk and pour it into the yeast. Have the flour, salt, and butter in a basin. Pour the dissolved yeast to these, and beat all together. Let the dough rise about two hours; make the mixture into rolls ; let them prove, and bake in a quick oven. Walnut Oake. Ingredients.
