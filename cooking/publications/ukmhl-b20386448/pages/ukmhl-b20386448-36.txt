Ingredients, Three pints of Stock. Two ounces of lean Ham. One Onion. A Uttle Celery. One Shalot. One ounce of Butter. Three pounds of Tomatoes. One tablespoonful of Vinegar. Salt, Pepper, and Nutmeg. Put all these ingredients, except the tomatoes and the stock, into a saute-pan and saute them for five minutes, then add the tomatoes sliced; let them simmer, and when cooked pass through a fine sieve; add to the stock, and let it boil about five minutes. This soup can be made of tinned tomatoes. Purde de Ohoux de Bruxelles. Ingredients, One pound of Brussels Sprouts. One quart of boiling White Stock. One gill of thick Cream.
