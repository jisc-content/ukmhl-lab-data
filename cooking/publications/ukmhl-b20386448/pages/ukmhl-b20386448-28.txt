Four tablespoonfuls of ground Rice. Half a pint of boiling Cream, One teaspoonful of Sugar, Salt to taste. Put the stock, which must be white and freed from fat, in a stewpan; when nearly boiling, stir in the ground rice, or better still, French flour of rice, which is smoother; stir well, and let it boil for ten minutes. Add the seasoning but not any pepper; then, when boiled sufficiently, add the cream, and strain before serving. Potage a la Tortue Fausse. Ingredients, Half a Calf s Head. One Carrot. One Turnip.
