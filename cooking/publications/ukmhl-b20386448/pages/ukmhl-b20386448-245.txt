One dessertspoonful of Castor Sugar. Thirty drops of Essence of Vanilla. Take a pint souffle-tin and line it carefully with the biscuits, fitting them close to each other; cut off the tops of the biscuits that stand above the tin. Put the gelatine and milk in a stewpan on the fire to melt. Whip the cream to a stiff froth; add the sugar and vanilla to it. Strain the melted gelatine into the cream, mix it well, and pour it carefully into the mould, not to dis- arrange the biscuits. When set, turn out and serve. Chartreuse of Oranges. Ingredients, Six Tangerine Oranges. One pint of Jelly. Peel the oranges and take away every morsel of white skin very carefully, quarter them carefully not to break the skin and let the juice out.
