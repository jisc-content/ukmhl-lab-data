FANCY BREAD AND CAKES. 259 Half a pound of Walnuts or Almonds. One teaspoonful of Baking-powder. One teaspoonful of Vanilla Essence. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream, then add the flour, sifted, and mix well; add the walnuts or almonds chopped, and lastly the whites only of the eight eggs, whipped to a stiff froth. Bake in a moderate oven for one hour.
