HiaH-OLASS COOKERY. Sauce Hollandaise. Ingredients. Half a pint of melted Butter. Yolks of five Eggs. Two teaspoonfuls of Lemon-jiiiee, Salt and Cayenne. Put the butter and yolks of eggs into a stewpan; stand it in a saucepan of hot water, and whisk it well over the fire till it thickens; it must not boil, or it will curdle and be spoilt. Season to taste with salt and cayenne, and at the last stir in the lemon-juice. Horse-radish Sauce. Ingredients. One gUl of whipped Cream. Two ounces of grated Horse-radish. Salt, Pepper, mixed Mustard. One tablespoonful of White Yinegar. Mix together and serve. Sauce Italienne. Ingredients. Two Shalots. Six Mushrooms. One ounce of Butter. A sprig of Thyme. Bayleaf.
