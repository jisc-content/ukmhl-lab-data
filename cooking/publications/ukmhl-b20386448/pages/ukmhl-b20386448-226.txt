216 HiGH-CLASS COOKERY. CEEAMS, JELLIES, ICES, COLD SWEETS. Apricot Cream. Ingredients. Half a pint of Apricot Puree. One gill of Syrup. Half a pint of whipped Cream. Three ounces of Castor Sugar. One ounce of French Gelatine. One teaspoouful of Lemon-juice. Take a tin of apricots and strain them from the syrup, pass them through a hair-sieve. Take half a pint of this puree, and mix lightly with half a pint of double cream whipped. Dissolve the gelatine with a gill of the syrup you have strained from the apricots, add the sugar and lemon-juice, and strain into the cream. Decorate a mould according to taste, set the movild in ice, pour in the cream, and set in ice till quite cold, then turn out and serve. Coffee Cream. Ingredients. Two Eggs. One ounce of Gelatine.
