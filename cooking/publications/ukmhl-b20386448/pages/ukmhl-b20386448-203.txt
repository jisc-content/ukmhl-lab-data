( 193 ) PASTRY. Cheese Pastry. Ingredients, Two ounces of Butter. Two ounces of Flour. Two ounces of Parmesan Cheese. One ounce of Cheddar Cheese. Yolk of one Egg. Salt and Cayenne. One dessertspoonful of Water. Put the flour on a paste-board, sprinkle in a little salt and cayenne, and rub in the cheese, grated, and the butter; mix all these together with the yolk of an egg and the water into a smooth stiff paste. EoU this out into a strip one eighth of an inch thick and five inches long; cut this into strips of equal sizes, also some rings. Grease a baking sheet, put the straws and rings on it, and bake in a hot oven ten minutes till they are a pale brown. For serving, stand the straws through the rings like a bundle of sticks. 0
