Scrape the sieve well underneath, and add this paste to the sauce. 108.—BROWN SAUCE. (Fr. Sauce Espagnole.) Ingredients— i oz. Butter or Dripping, i oz. Flour, i pint Brown Stock, i small Onion. A small piece of Carrot and Turnip, i stick of Celery or ^ teasp. Celery Seed. Method— Melt the butter or dripping in a saucepan, and let it brown. Add the onion, skinned and cut in thin slices, and keep stirring with an iron spoon until it is brown. Add the flour, and brown it also. Then draw the pan to one side, pour in the stock, and stir over the fire until boiling. Boil for a few minutes, and remove all scum with an iron spoon.
