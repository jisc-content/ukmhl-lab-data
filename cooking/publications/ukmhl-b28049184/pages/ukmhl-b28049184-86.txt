Brown in the oven or with a salamander, and serve hot. 95.-SOLE A LA ROUENNAISE. (Fr. Sole k la Rouennaise.) Ingredients— 1 Sole. Pepper and Salt. Lemon Juice. Lobster Cutlet Mixture. Cardinal or White Sauce. Method— Prepare and season the same as in last recipe, but before doubling them over, put a little lobster cutlet mixture (see Recipe 58) on to each. Double over, and shape the pieces neatly. Place them on a greased baking tin, cover with greased paper, and bake in a moderate oven for fifteen minutes. Lift out and dish the pieces, one overlapping the other, on a hot dish. Coat them with white or cardinal sauce (see Recipes for Sauces) made of stock from fish bones. Decorate with chopped truffle, parsley, or lobster coral, and serve hot. 96.—TROUT (TO BAKE). (Fr. Truite Rotie.) Required— Trout. A squeeze of Lemon A little Butter. Juice. Pepper and Salt.
