Method— Clean the trout thoroughly, and dry them well in a cloth. Split them open, remove the bone, and season with pepper, salt, cayenne, and a little lemon juice. Then rub over with salad oil, and broil in front or over a clear fire from five to ten minutes, according to the size of the fish (see page ix.). Serve very hot.
