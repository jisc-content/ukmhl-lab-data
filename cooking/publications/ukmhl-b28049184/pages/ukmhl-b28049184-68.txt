OYSTERS AU NATUREL 64 64.-OYSTERS AU NATUREL. (Fr. Hultres au Naturel.) . Required— Oysters. Cayenne. Pepper and Salt. Lemon Juice. Parsley to garnish. Method— Open the oysters carefully, and serve them in their shells arranged on a folded serviette. Season them with pepper, salt, and a pinch of cayenne. Garnish with parsley or cut lemon. Serve directly they are opened, or the flavour will be spoilt, and hand thin brown bread and butter separately. 65.—OYSTER CUTLETS. (Fr. Cotelettes d’Huitres.)
