Note.—This may be steamed in a border mould, and the inside afterwards filled with green peas, or oysters mixed with a little of the sauce. 20.-FISH CURRY. {Fr. Poisson au Cari.) Ingredients— 6 oz. cooked Fish. \ oz. Butter. | oz. Flour. 1 teaspoonful Curry Powder. \ teaspoonful Chutney. 1 Onion. 1 small Apple. | pint Fish Stock or Water. A squeeze of Lemon Juice. Pepper and Salt. Boiled Rice. Method— Remove all skin from the fish, break it into flakes, and then weigh it. Melt the butter in a small stew- pan, chop the apple and the onion rather small, and fry them in it for a few minutes. Then add the curry powder, chutney, and the rice flour, and mix with an iron spoon until quite smooth. Add the stock or water, and stir until boiling, seasoning to taste with pepper and salt.
