A nice supper-dish. Sweetbreads. Half boil them, and stew them in a white gravy : add cream, flour, butter, nutmeg, salt, and white pepper. Or do them in brown sauce seasoned. Or parboil them, and then cover them with crumbs, herbs, and seasoning, and brown them in a Dutch oven. Serve with butter, and mushroom ketchup or gravy. Sweetbreads roasted.—Parboil two large ones,when cold, lard them with bacon, and roast them in a Dutch- oven. For sauce, plain butter and mushroom-ketchup. Sweetbread Bacjout.—Cut them about the size of a walnut, wash and dry them, and fry them of a fine brown; pour to them a good gravy, seasoned with salt, pepper, allspice, and either mushrooms or mushroom-ketchup: strain, and thicken with butter and a little flour.
