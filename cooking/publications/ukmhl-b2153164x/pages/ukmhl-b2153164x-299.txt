Beat two ounces of fresh candied orange in a mortar, with a little sugar, to a paste ; then mix one ounce of powder of white ginger with one pound of loaf-sugar. Wet the sugar with a little water, and boil all together to a candy, and drop it on paper the size of mint-drops. Peppermint Drops. Patifia Drops. Blanch and beat in a mortar four ounces of bitter, and two ounces of sweet almonds, with a little of a pound of sugar sifted, and add the remainder of the sugar, and the whites of two eggs, making a paste ; of which put little balls, the size of a nutmeg, on wafer- paper, and bake gently on tin-plates. liaspherry Calces. Pick out any bad raspberries that are among the fruit, weigh and boil what quantity you please, and when mashed, and the liquor is wasted, put to it sugar the weight of the fruit you first put into the pan, mix it well off the fire until perfectly dissolved, then put it on china-plates, and dry it in the sun. As soon as the top part dries, cut with the cover of a canister into small cakes, turn them on fresh plates, and, when dry, put them in boxes with layers of paper.
