Rice Flummery. Boil with a pint of new milk, a bit of lemon-peel, and cinnamon ; mix with a little cold milk as much rice- flour, as will make the whole of a good consistence, sweeten, and add a spoonful of peach-water, or a bitter almond beaten ; boil it,observing it doesn’t burn ; pour it into a shape or pint-bason, taking out the spice.
