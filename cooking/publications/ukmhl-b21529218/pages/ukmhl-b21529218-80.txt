HOW TO MAKE GOOD BREAD SAUCE PUT one pint of new milk to boil with one onion and a few white peppercorns. Let it simmer for a while; steam it, and return it to the fire. Add two handfuls of bread crumbs, one gill of cream, pepper and salt and serve hot.
