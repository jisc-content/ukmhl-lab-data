THE DUDIJEY RECIPES 128 CEPS A LA BORDELAISE Having dried the ceps in a napkin, warm them on both sides in the freshest oil. Then in another stewpan heat some oil, into which you have thrown some chopped parsley and a little eschalot. Cook the ceps in this on a moderate fire, taking care to turn them fre- quently. When they are done add a very little cayenne and a little glaze. Any mushrooms can be dressed in this way, and if fresh and new oil is not at hand good butter will do.
