To ^‘glacer” with coffee use icing sugar with essence of coffee rather thick. This is to be used cold. ALMOND CHEESECAKES Blanch four ounces of sweet almonds. Pound them in a mortar with four ounces of loaf sugar, four ounces of butter, the outer rind of a lemon grated, a tablespoonful of sherry, have your patty pans ready lined with puff* paste. Then beat up the pounded mixture with the yolks of six and the whites of three eggs. Fill up the patty pans and bake for quarter of an hour. CHOCOLATE CHEESECAKES A QUARTER pound of pounded almonds, a quarter pound of fresh butter, a quarter pound of pounded sugar, three eggs, some grated chocolate. Place in patty pans previously lined with puff* paste and bake in a moderate oven.
