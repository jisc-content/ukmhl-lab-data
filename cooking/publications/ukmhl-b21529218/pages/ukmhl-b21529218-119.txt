CHICKEN, GAME AND LIGHT DISHES 91 liquor made as follows: six pounds of shin of veal, one pound of fresh pork, the bones and trimmings of the fowl. Boil this for five hours, then use it for cooking the galantine. When it is cooked let it cool, roll in a clean napkin and tie again very tightly, place under a six pound weight. Trim and glaze, and it is ready for serving cold next day, garnish with aspic jelly.
