Three hours after wash them. Next day strain off the liquor that will flow from them. Put it into a stewpan and boil it till it is reduced one half. It will not keep long, but it is preferable to any of the ketchups con- taining spice, &c., to preserve them, which overpowers the flavour of the mushrooms. An artificial mushroom bed will supply these all the year round. ¥ WALNUT KETCHUP Take two sieves of green walnut shells.
