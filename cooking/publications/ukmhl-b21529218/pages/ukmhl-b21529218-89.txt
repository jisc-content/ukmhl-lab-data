61 CHICKEN, GAME AND LIGHT DISHES CHICKEN VILLEROI POACH a young white chicken without completely cooking it. Let this cool and cut it into neat joints, then mask it well with the following villeroi sauce. Dip the pieces into some well-beaten egg and bread crumbs and fry in clean fat. This must be served very hot with the white sauce. SAUCE FOR CHICKEN VILLEROI PUT into a stewpan one pint of Allemande sauce, two tablespoonsful of truffle essence; let this reduce to half. When it is very stiff, coat over your pieces of chicken before egging and bread-crumbing them. Fry them a light golden brown, and serve with them a white sauce made of chicken, in a sauce- boat. I
