Moisten a dessertspoonful of arrowroot with a little cold water; boil three dessertspoonfuls of sugar in a gill of water for ten minutes; mix it with the arrowroot; stir it over the fire for two minutes; add two tablespoonfuls of brandy and twenty drops of the essence of sweet almonds. Serve in the dish, not poured over the pudding. 536. SUMMER PUDDING.
