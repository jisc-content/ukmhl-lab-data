lxxiii BILLS OF FAMILY FARE. June 14. BREAKFAST. Potted prawns, eels made hot, slices of venison, broiled, fruit. DINNER. Tapioca soup. Grenatins of veal, rissoles of venison, peas, potatoes. Cold sole, cold beef, hot cake, fruit. DINNER. Mayonnaise of lobster, cold beef, lamb chops, peas, potatoes. Fresh fruit pudding. Cheese, &c. KITCHEN. Baked Irish stew.
