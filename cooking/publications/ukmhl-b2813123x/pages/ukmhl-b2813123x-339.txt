Serve very hot. 375. ROAST PARTRIDGES. Hang the birds before a brisk fire; baste constantly with butter (a quarter of a pound for a brace) and roast for about eighteen minutes. Serve garnished with fried crumbs; and with gravy and bread sauce in tureens. Put three ounces of crumbs of bread into a small fryingpan or stewpan, with an ounce and a half of butter, and fry over a gentle fire (stirring all the time) till of a pale yellow-brown colour ; drain on a sieve before the fire. Put a quarter of a pound of crumbs of bread into a stewpan, with one clove, two allspice, three shalots, and three-quarters of a pint of milk ; boil, and stir for a quarter of an hour; then rub through a sieve. Put the sauce again into the saucepan ; add either two tablespoonfuls of thick cream or an ounce of fresh butter; boil two minutes; then serve.
