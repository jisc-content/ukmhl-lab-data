CALF’S HEART, ROASTED. Thoroughly clean and soak the heart in cold water for half an hour. Make a stuffing as follows:—A quarter of a pound of beef-suet, chopped small, the hard yolk of one egg, pounded, half an ounce of fine crumbs of bread, a saltspoonful of mixed sweet herbs, a dessertspoonful of chopped parsley, half a saltspoonful of salt, the same of pepper, the tenth part of a nutmeg, grated, and one well-beaten egg; mix these ingredients thoroughly, and fill the heart with the stuffing; spread one ounce of butter on a sheet of foolscap paper, fasten it round the heart, and roast for an hour and a quarter. Take off the paper a quarter of an hour before serving; dredge ovep a little flour, and baste constantly.
