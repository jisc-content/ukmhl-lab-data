Beat the yolks of three fresh eggs; add two ounces of sifted loaf sugar and half a pint of Marsala; stir over a slow fire till it thickens; add half a gill of cream, stirred in by degrees. Serve immediately. 565. MIXED FRUIT PUDDING.
