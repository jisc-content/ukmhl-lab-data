To be kept in a cool place. 200. sheep’s tongues. Well wash the tongues, and rub into each a tablespoonful of salt, a teaspoonful of moist sugar, and a teaspoonful of vinegar. Let them lay in pickle four or five days ; put them into boiling water, and simmer for three-quarters of an hour; take off the skin, trim round the root, and serve cold for breakfast.
