6 SOUPS, BROTHS, ETC. 18. TAPIOCA SOUP. (Two quarts.) Well wash three ounces of tapioca ; rub a stewpan across the bottom three times with garlic, put in the tapioca, with five pints of stock (No. 1), and boil for two hours, stirring frequently. Add a wineglassful of Marsala, and serve at once. 19. SEMOLINA SOUP. ' Follow the preceding receipt, using semolina instead of tapioca, and boiling half an hour less. 20. REAL TURTLE SOUP. It is not intended to give a receipt for making real turtle soup, but simply to instruct the uninformed how to send it to table, should they ever require the knowledge. Tie down the jar in which the turtle is sent, and place it in a pan of hot water; let it boil slowly, till the soup is quite hot; put a glass of old Madeira into the tureen, pour in the turtle, and serve immediately, with cut lemons on a plate.
