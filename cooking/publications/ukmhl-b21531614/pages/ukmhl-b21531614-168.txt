One pound of dry flour, one tea-spoonful of baking powder, and two table-spoonfuls of fine salad oil. Mix the baking powder well with the flour, adding the salad oil, and a sufficient quantity of cold water to form a paste. Roll it out, and spread a little oil over ; dredge with flour, and fold it in three ; then roll it, adding oil and flour as before, and when rolled again, it will be ready for use.
