No. 660.—To Bottle Gooseberries. Pick gooseberries of the small round kind, put them in clean, dry bottles, and cork them loosely; put them in a pan of cold m water, and set them on a moderate fire; when the gooseberries change colour and begin to shrink a little in the bottles, take them off the fire, and let them remain in the water till cold; then cork them tightly and cement the corks.
