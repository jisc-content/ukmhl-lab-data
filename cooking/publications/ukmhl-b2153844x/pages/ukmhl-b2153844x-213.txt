Orange Cake (2). The weight of two good-sized eggs in butter, sugar and flour. Rub butter and sugar together till like a cream; beat the eggs well with a tablespoonful of milk; add these to the other two ingredients; add the grated rind of an orange, and half the juice, then the flour and a teaspoonful of baking powder. Bake in a quick oven half an hour. Icing for it may be made by squeezing the juice of the oranges and mixing in sufficient icing sugar to make it the con- sistency of double cream.
