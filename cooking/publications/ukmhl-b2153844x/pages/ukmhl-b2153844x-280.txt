IN AID OF THE FUND FOR THE REMOVAL OF To Sweeten Rancid Butter. If, from any cause, butter becomes rancid, to each pint of it, add one tablespoonful of salt and one tea- spoonful of soda. Mix well, then add one pint of cold water, and set on the fire until it comes to the boiling point. Now set away to cool, and when hard take off the butter in a cake, wipe it dry, and put it away for cooking purposes. It will be perlectly sweet.
