Chocolate Sponge. Cut 30ZS. of chocolate in small pieces, put it in a saucepan with 2 tablespoonfuls of water and let it melt; add a few drops of vanilla, let it get nearly cool, beat the whites of 3 eggs to a very stiff froth, and stir slightly into the chocolate. Heap the mixture in a dish, cut half a tin of pineapple into small squares, and arrange round the sponge. Miss M. S. Owen. Caledonian Cream. The whites of 2 Eggs, beaten stiff. 2 tablespoonfuls of sifted Sugar. 2 tablespoonfuls of Raspberry Jam. 2 tablespoonfuls of Red Currant Jelly. To be beaten together with a silver spoon till so stiff that the spoon will stand upright in it. Put it in a glass dish and ornament with ratafias, if liked. Mrs. H. W. Price. Chocolate Cream (1).
