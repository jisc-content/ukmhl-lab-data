CHICKENS, A l’iNDIENNE, Tehss two chickens for boiling, lard the breasts closely, and place them in a conveniently-sized stew-pan with a carrot, an onion stuck with two cloves, and a garnished faggot of parsley, &c.; add some good stock in sufficient quantity to reach up to the larding, place a buttered paper over the chickens, and having put the Lid on the stew- pan, set them to braize gently, with some live embers of charcoal upon the lid of the pan: about forty muiutes will suffice to do them.
