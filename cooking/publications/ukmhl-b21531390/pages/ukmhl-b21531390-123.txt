Strain this mixture; and add two tablespoonfuls of vinegar, and one of brandy. Apply it warm, night and morning. RECEIPT FOR MOCK CREAM. Mix half a table spoonful of flour with a pint of new milk ; let it simmer five minutes to take, off the rawness of the flour; then beat up the yolk of an egg, stir it into the milk while boiling, and run it through a fine sieve.
