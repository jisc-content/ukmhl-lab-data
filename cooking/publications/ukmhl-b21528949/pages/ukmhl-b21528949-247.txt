SICKROOM COOKERY. 239 BAKED CUSTARD PUDDING. (diabetic.) 1 gill of cream. 1 gill of water. 2 whole eggs. 1 (i-grain) tabloid of saccharine. Beat the eggs up well, dissolve the saccharine in the water and. stir in, then add the cream. Mix well ami put into a small piedish. Bate in a moderate oven for thirty minutes. STEAMED CUSTARD.
