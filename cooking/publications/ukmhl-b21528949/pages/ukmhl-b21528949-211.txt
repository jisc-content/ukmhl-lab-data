(diabetic.) 1 lb. of fresh beef, veal, or a chicken. 2 oz. of butter. 1 large onion. 1 tablespoonful of curry powder. 2 tablespoonfuls of almonds. 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice. -J, pint of stock or water. The almonds should be blanched, chopped, and rubbed through a wire sieve. They should then be put to steep ' in the stock or water. Cut the meat into square blocks, or, if a chicken or rabbit, into small joints. Chop the onion and fry a golden brown in the butter. Strain it out and put aside. Return the butter to the saucepan, and fry the meat lightly. Add the curry powder and lemon juice. Fry without browning for five minutes. Add the stock and almonds and bring to the boil.
