STEWED MUSHROOMS. ^ lb. of mushrooms. 1 oz. of butter. \ pint of stock or milk. 1 teaspoonful of lemon juice. 1 dessertspoonful of flour. Pepper and salt. Rinse and peel the mushrooms. Melt the butter and stir them in it without breaking. Add the stock, season with pepper, salt, and the lemon juice. Simmer gently for twenty minutes. Blend the flour with a little cold liquid, and strain into the saucepan. Stir gently until it boils up, but be careful not to break the mushrooms. Serve on a hot dish with triangles of toasted bread round. TOMATOES AU GRATIN'. 1 lb. of tomatoes. Breadcrumbs. 2 oz. of butter. Pepper and salt. Slice the tomatoes and put them into a buttered pie- dish with a few scraps of butter, pepper and salt. Put plenty of breadcrumbs on top and the rest of the butter in pieces. Bake in the oven for twenty-five minutes until the tomatoes are tender. Serve.
