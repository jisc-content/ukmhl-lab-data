SHREWSBURY BISCUITS. 2 oz. of butter. 2 oz. of castor sugar. f an egg. 4 oz. of flour. \ teaspoonful of grated lemon rind. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream, add the egg and grated lemon rind. Stir in the flour lightly. Roll out one eighth of an inch in thickness. Stamp into round cakes, place on a greased baking tin, and bake in a moderate oven for about fifteen minutes. Be careful in adding the egg, as they must be only moist enough to bind, or the biscuits will be difficult to roll out.
