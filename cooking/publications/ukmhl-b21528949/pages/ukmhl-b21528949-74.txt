FRIED PLAICE. 1 plaice. 1 tablespoonful of flour. Pepper and salt. 1 egg. Breadcrumbs. Fillet the plaice. Cut each fillet across into three or four pieces, according to size. Pat the flour on to a plate, season with pepper and salt. Dip each piece of fish into this, then brush over with the egg beaten up and dip into breadcrumbs. Flatten these on with a dry knife.
