SICKROOM COOKERY. 158 BEATEN EGG. 1 yolk of egg. 1 glass of skerry, or 1 cup of tea. A yolk of egg can be beaten up, and either a glass of wine added to it or a cup of tea. WHITE OE EGG AND SODA WATER. 1 white of egg. 1 tablespoonful of brandy or lemon juice, tumblerful of soda water. Beat the white of egg to a froth and put it into a tumbler. Add the brandy and fill up with soda water. This is a nutritious drink, sometimes given in acute disease. The yolk of egg can be used in the same way. , WHITE OF EGG AND MILK. 1 white of egg. 1 gill of milk. Flavouring. Boil the milk and let it cool. Whip the white of egg and put into a tumbler, add the milk, and flavour with vanilla or a tablespoonful of brandy. Both nutritious and digestible as a beverage.
