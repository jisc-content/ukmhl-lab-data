EGG FLIP. 1 egg. 1 dessertspoonful of castor sugar. 1 glass of skerry or brandy. Stir the raw yolk of egg and sugar together in a tumbler until creamy. Add the wine. Whip the white of egg to a stiff froth and lightly stir in. This is both nutritious and stimulating in cases of weakness or collapse from fatigue. EGG CORDIAL. (Dr. Burney Yeo.) 1 white of egg. 1 tahlespoonful of cream. 1 tablespoonful of brandy. “ Beat the white of egg to a froth, add a tahlespoonful of cream to a tahlespoonful of brandy.” Serve in a tumbler. EGG NOG. (Bartholdw.) (a nutritive drink in acute disease.) “ Scald some new milk by putting it, contained in .a jug, into a saucepan of boiling water, but it must not bo allowed to boil. When quite cold, heat up a fresh egg with a fork in a tumbler with some sugar; beat up quite to a froth. Add a dessertspoonful of brandy and till up the tumbler with scalded milk.”
