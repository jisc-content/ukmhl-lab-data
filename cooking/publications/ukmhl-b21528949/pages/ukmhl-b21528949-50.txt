Vegetables. Cold water. For second stock all scraps of meat, bones from which the meat has been cut, and all meat and bone from which first stock has been strained, can be used. Put all into a large saucepan, fill with cold water, and simmer all day. Any trimmings of meat and vegetables should be added throughout the day.
