The next morning the whole lump will he well fdrmented or leavened; add a little salt, and as much warm water (not warmer than new milk) as will mix it, knead it up quite stiff and firm till it be smooth and pliable, the more it is kneaded the better : let it stand by the fine about two hours, then make it up into loaves and bake them.
