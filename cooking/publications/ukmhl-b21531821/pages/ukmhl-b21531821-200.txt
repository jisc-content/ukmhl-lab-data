Three pounds and a half of flour, half a pound of sugar, some grated nutmeg or pounded mace, eight eggs well beaten, a wine-glass of rose-water or rose-syrup, half a pint of yeast, a pound of butter melted in a pint and a half of milk, and put just warm to the other ingredients; let it rise an hour before the fire, then mix it well together ; add two pounds of currants, butter a tin and bake it.
