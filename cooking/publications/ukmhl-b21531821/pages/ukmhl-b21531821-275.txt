Oatmeal Flummery. Put a pound and half of very fine white oatmeal to steep a day and a night in cold water, then pour it off clear, add- ing as much more water, and let it stand the same time ; then strain it through a fine hair sieve, and boil it till it be as thick as hasty pudding, stirring it all the time. When first strained, put to it one large spoonful of white sugar, and two of orange-flower water ; pour it on dishes, and serve to eat with new milk, or cream and sugar. It is very good. 942.
