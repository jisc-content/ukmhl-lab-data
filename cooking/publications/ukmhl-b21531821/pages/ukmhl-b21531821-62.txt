Cut off as much of the white end as will leave the aspara- gus about six inches long; scrape the remaining white part very clean, and as they are done put them in fresh water; tie them in small even parcels, put them in boiling water, and boil them till tender, but do not over-boil them; take them up with a slice into a sieve to drain a little; havereadv a thin toast to dip into the water ; lay it in a dish and the asparagus upon it the white ends outward ; pour melted butter over the green part when you serve it up. 124. Asparagus like Green Pease, After cutting the tender part of asparagus the size of small pease, wash them in spring water; scald them a moment in boiling water, drain them well and dress them, as pease, with white sauce, only omitting the lettuce. 126, Gourds. Pare a gourd, then boil it in water, when it is done enough and there is very little water remaining, put to it some milk, butter, a little salt, and sugar; add some slices of bread if agreeable; do not set it on the fire after adding these ingredients.
