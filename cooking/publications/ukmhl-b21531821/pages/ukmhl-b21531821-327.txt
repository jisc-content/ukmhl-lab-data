For Inflammation of the Eyes, or removal of Film. Boil an egg hard, and divide it in the middle, take out the yolk very clean, and while the egg is hot, fill the cavity with very clear honey, and after putting the parts of the egg together, wrap it in fine muslin, and let the honey fil- ter through it into a clean vessel, being careful to exclude dus:, &c.
