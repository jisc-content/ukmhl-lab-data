I may mention particularly the Rev. W. (He supplies distribution copies at Cd, per lOO.) His ‘Penny Dinner Cooker/ now commercially supplied by Messrs. Walker and Emley, Newcastle, overcomes the difficulties I have described in the slow cookery of Rumford’s soup. It is a double vessel on the glue-pot principle, heated by gas.
