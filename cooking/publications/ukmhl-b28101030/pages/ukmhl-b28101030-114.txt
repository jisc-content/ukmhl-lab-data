IIO white stock or water, ten peppercorns, one table-spoonful of French wine vinegar,, one table-spoonful of tarragon vinegar, the thin rind and juice of half a lemon, and the white and shell of one egg. Put all the ingredients in a stew-pan, add a good pinch of salt and the white and shell, previously beaten, and whisk into the stew-pan; bring the contents slowly to the boil, whisking all the time, and boil slowly for five minutes; allow to stand for ten minutes and strain twice or three times through a cloth or jelly bag.
