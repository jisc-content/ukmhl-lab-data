Oil the inside of paper cases, and place them on a baking-tin in the oven for a few minutes. Peel the shallot and chop finely, fry a little in olive oil, then drain and put it, equally divided, into the cases. Mix the bread-crumbs,, half the parsley, and Par- mesan cheese, adding a little pepper. Put about a dessert-spoonful of this and a tiny piece of butter into each of the cases; carefully break an egg into each case ; sea- son with pepper and salt. Divide thecream equally and pour over each egg, sprinkle with a little Parmesan cheese, and bake in a moderate oven for about six minutes. Take out, and brown the surface under a salamander or hot shovel, sprinkle with a little chopped parsley, dish up on a folded napkin, and serve quickly.
