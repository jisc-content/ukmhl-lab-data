INTRODUCTION. These little breakfast menus are designed for a family or party of six : each will be found to contain a dish of fish, a meat dish, and a dish of eggs, any two of which can be selected if three be considered too many. Seven of them are composed for days of abstinence. A plate of fancy bread is suggested for every menu to facili- tate choice, recipes for which will be found in the Appendix.
