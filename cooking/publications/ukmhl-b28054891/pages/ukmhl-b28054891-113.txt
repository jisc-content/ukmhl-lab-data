Sauce Book 101 Veal Broth A knuckle of veal A chicken 2 quarts of water 2 table-spoons well-boiled rice Put a knuckle of veal and a ehieken (an old one will do) into a large stew-pan. Cover with two quarts of water. Let it boil up gently. Skim. Simmer for three hours. Strain through a napkin. xVIIow it to cool. Remove the fat. Serve with a little boiled rice.
