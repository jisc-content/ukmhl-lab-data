We put the jam into a stewpan on the fire, and stir it until it has melted. 15. We take the pan out of the oven. 16. We take a knife and pass it round the edge of the omelette, to ease it from the pan. 17. We give the pan a shake to loosen the omelette. 18. We turn the omelette on to a hot dish. 19. We spread the jam on the omelette, and fold it over like a sandwich. 20. We sprinkle about a teaspoonful of white castor sugar over it, and it is ready for serving.
