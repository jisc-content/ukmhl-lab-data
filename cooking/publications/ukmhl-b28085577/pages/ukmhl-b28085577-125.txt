Time required, about two hours and a quarter. Now we will show you how to make a Rabbit Pie. 1. We must first make the paste, in the same way as for veal and ham pie, from note 1 to note 8. N.B.—Clarified dripping may be used, instead of the butter and lard, for economy’s sake. 2. We take a rabbit (which has been skinned and properly prepared for cooking), wash it well in cold water, wipe it dry, and put it on a board. 3. We take a sharp knife, and cut it up in neat pieces, about equal sizes. 4. We take half a pound of bacon, or salt pork, put it on a board, and cut it slices. 5. We put half a pound of beef-steak on a board, and cut it in small pieces. 6. We arrange the rabbit, bacon, and beef in a pie-dish, and sprinkle over them a teaspoonful of chopped parsley, and a teaspoonful of pepper and salt mixed, and pour in about a tablespoonful of cold water, or stock. 7. We finish the pie in the same way, as for veal and ham
