We return the liquor to the saucepan, add the meat, vegetables, the soaked barley, and pepper and salt to taste, and put the saucepan by the side of the fire, to simmer gently, for about half an hour; until the vegetables are tender. 10. We take a sprig or two of parsley, wash it, and chop up finely on a board. 11. When the broth is finished, we must skim off all the grease, and then add the chopped parsley. 12. For serving, we pour the broth into a hot soup tureen.
