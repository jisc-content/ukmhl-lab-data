Now we will show you how to make Mutton Broth. 1. We take two pounds of the scrag end of the neck of vmttony and wash it well until it is quite clean. 2. We put the meat into a large saucepan with three pints of cold watery and put it on the fire to boil. 3. We take one ounce of pearl barley or rice, and wash it well in cold water. 4. When the water boils we put in the pearl barley or rice and half a saltspoonful of salt to help the scum to rise. 6. We now draw the saucepan to the side of the fire and let it simmer gently for two hours and a half. 6. We must watch it and skim it occasionally with a spoon. 7. If the meat is required’for immediate use we should make sauce to pour over it. 8. We take a sprig or two of parsley, wash it and wring it in a cloth, put it on a board, and chop it up finely with a knife.
