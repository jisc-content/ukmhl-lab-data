Now we will show you how to dress Potatoes. For a dish of Mashed Potatoes. 1. We take two pounds of old potatoes, wash them, and steam them as for steamin<gpotatoes (see “Vegetables, Lesson No. 1 ”). 2. We take a stew-pan and put in it one ounce of butter, one gill of milk, and pepper and salt to taste. 3. We put the stew-pan on the fire to boil. 4. We place a wire sieve over a plate. 5. We take the steamed potatoes, one at a time, out of the steamer, put them on the sieve, and pass them through on to the plate as quickly as possible, rubbing them with a wooden spoon. 6. We take the sifted potato and stir it into the boiling milk, in the stew-pan. 7. We must now beat it all lightly together, and then turn it into a hot vegetable dish for serving.
