194 National Training School for Cookery. SOUPS. Lesson No. io. SEMOLINA SOUP. Average cost of “ Semolina Soup ” (about one quart). INGREDIENTS. d. 1 quart of second stock 2 tablespoonsful of semolina i J | pint of milk ) ^ Salt and pepper / 9% (Stock already made.) Time required, about half-an-hour. Now we will show you how to make Semolina Soup. 1. We put one quart of second stock (or stock from pot-au- feu) into a saucepan, and put it on the fire to boil. 2. When the stock is boiling, we stir in very gradually, two tablespoonsful of Semolina. 3. We must stir it well while it boils, for ten minutes. 4. We now add half a pint of milk, and pepper and salt to taste. N.3.—The milk might be omitted if not required. 5. We let the soup warm through, stirring well all the time, and then pour it into a hot soup tureen, or basin, for serving. N.B.—Toasted, or fried bread, cut in the shape of dice, should be served with this soup.
