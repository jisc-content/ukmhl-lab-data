Time required, about one hour. Now we will show you how to make Croquettes or Ris- soles of Chicken.. 1. We take the half chicken (either roasted or boiled), cut away all the flesh from the bones, and put it on a board. 2. We cut away the skin, and mince the meat up very finely. 3. We take the mushrooms, wash them, peel them, and mince them and the ham up finely on the board, mixing them with the minced chicken. 4. We put one ounce of butter in a stewpan, and put it on the fire. 5. When the butter is melted, we stir in half an ounce of flour, and mix it to a smooth paste.
