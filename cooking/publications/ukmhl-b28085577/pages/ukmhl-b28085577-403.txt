Now we will show you how to make a Seed Cake. 1. We take ten ounces of flour and put it in a basin. 2. We mix into the flour, one teaspoonful of baking poivder, and half a saltspoonful of salt. 3. We take two ounces of clarified dripping and rub it well into the flour with our hands, until there are no lumps remaining. 4. We add two ounces of crushed loaf sugar and one tea- spoonfid of carrazvay seeds. 5. We mix these well together with a wooden spoon. 6. We break one egg into a cup and beat it up with two tablespoonsful of milk. 7. We pour this into the basin and mix all quickly together into a stiff paste, stiff enough to allow a spoon to stand up in it. 8. We take a cake tin and grease it inside with a piece of dripping. 9. We pour the mixture into the tin and put it at once in the oven (the thermometer at the oven door should rise to 240°) to bake for one hour.
