Lessons on Cooking Meat. Stewed Breast of Lamb, g g 7. For serving, we must carefully remove all grease {either with a spoon or by putting a piece of clean blotting- paper or whitey-brown paper on the top of the stew, which will absorb the grease), and turn the stew on to a hot dish; the meat should be arranged in the centre, and tho. peas and gravy round.
