Now we will show you how to make Ox-tail Soup. 1. We take the ox-tail’ put it on a board, and cut it through the joints into pieces with a knife. 2. We put the pieces of ox-tail into a stewpan, cover them with cold water, and put the stewpan on the fire to boil. 3. We let it boil for five minutes, take the stewpan off the fire, strain off the water, and wipe the pieces of ox-tail dry with a clean cloth. 4. We put the ox-tail back in the stewpan, with two .1ounces of butter. 5. We take one carrot, wash it, scrape it clean with a knife, cut it in half, and put it in the stewpan.
