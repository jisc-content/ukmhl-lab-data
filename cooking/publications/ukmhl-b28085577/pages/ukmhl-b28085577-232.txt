Average cost of “ Oyster Sauce!' s. d. i 6 o of o if 0 of 1 8f Time required alout twenty minutes. Now we will show you how to cook Cod Fish, and make Oysier Sauce. 1. We take a slice of cod weighing one pound. 2. We put it in a basin of cold water, and wash it well. 3. We take a small fish-kettle and fill it with warm water, and add to it as much salt as will make the water taste salt. 4. We put the fish-kettle on the fire to boil. 5. We take the cod out of the basin, and place it on the drainer in the fish-kettle, and let it boil for fifteen minutes. N.B.—It must not boil fast. N.B.—While the fish is boiling, we should make the sauce: see below. 6. When the slice of cod is sufficiently cooked, the flesh will leave the bone. N.B.—The bone is usually left in, or the fish would break to pieces. 7. We serve the slice of cod on a folded napkin on a hot dish, with oyster sauce.
