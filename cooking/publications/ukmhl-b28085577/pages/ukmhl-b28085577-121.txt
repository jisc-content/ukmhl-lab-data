Now we will show you how to make Sausage Rolls. 1. We take half a pound of meat (cooked or uncooked), put it on a board, take away .all the fat, and mince the meat up as finely as possible. 2. We take the mince meat, put it in a basin, and season it well with pepper and salt. 3. We take four sage leaves, put them on a board, and chop them up as finely as possible with a knife. 4. We take half a shalot and one small onion, peel them, and chop them up upon the board. 5. We mix the chopped sage, shalot, and onion well into the mince meat with a spoon. 6. We put one pound of flour into a basin. 7. We add to it one teaspoonful of baking powder, a quarter of a saltspoonful of salt, and half a pound of clarified dripping. 8. We rub the dripping well into the flour with our hands. N.B.—We must mix it thoroughly and be careful not to leave any lumps. 9. We add enough water to the flour to make it into a stiff paste.
