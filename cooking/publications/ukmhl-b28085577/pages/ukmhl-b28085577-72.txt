We cut some thin slices of bacony about two inches wide, and about four inches in length. 2. We roll up these strips of bacon. 3. We take a skewer and run it through the centre of each roll of bacon. 4. We place this skewer, with the bacon, on a tin and put it in the oven for six minutes. 5. For serving, we take the rolls of bacon off the skewer, and arrange them in the centre of the cutlets, as described above.
