We whip the whites of the two eggs to a stiff froth with a knife, and then stir them lightly into the stewpan, mixing all the ingredients together. 11. We then pour the contents of the stewpan into the basin with the carrot and bread crumbs, and mix them well together. 12. We take a pie-dish (about one pint) and line the edge of it with puff paste (see “ Pastry,” Lesson No i). 13. We pour the pudding into the pie-dish, and put it into the oven (the thermometer at the oven door should rise to 240°) to bake for half an hour; it is then ready for serving.
