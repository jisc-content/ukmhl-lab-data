We now add the fish, the whites of the eggs, a few grains of cayenne pepper (about as much as would thinly cover one side of a threepenny-piece), and salt to taste; mix it all well together, and turn it out on a hot dish. 8. We must now place a sieve over it, take the yolks of the eggs, and rub them through the sieve all over the fish and rice: it is now ready for serving.
