Now we will show you how to make Jelly with Iceland Moss. 1. We wash one oz. of Iceland Moss well in cold water. 2. We then put it in a basin of cold water, and let it soak all night. 3. After that time we take it out of the water and squeeze it dry in a cloth. 4. We then put it in a saucepan with one quart of cold water. 5. We put the saucepan on the fire and let it boil for one hour; we must stir it frequently. 6. We then strain it through a sieve into a basin, and sweeten it with loaf sugar. 7. It can be taken with either wine or milk according to taste.
