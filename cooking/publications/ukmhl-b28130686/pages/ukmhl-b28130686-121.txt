Two fried sausages or two grilled kidneys can be added to this dish. CHEESE SCALLOP, Ingredients Bread Crumbs. Milk. 1 oz. Butter. \ lb. Cheese. 2 Eggs. Take a small cupful of bread crumbs, and soak them in a little milk. Mix with this the eggs, well beaten, and the cheese, grated. Then add the butter in small pieces. Sift over the mixture a layer of bread crumbs, and bake till a nice brown. CHEESE PUDDING, Ingredients 1 pint of Milk. 2 Eggs. % lb. of Cheese. Salt. 2 oz. of Butter. ir lb. of Bread Crumbs. Mustard. Heat the milk, melting the butter in it, mix together the cheese, grated, bread crumbs, and a little mustard and salt. Add the milk and yolks of the eggs, beaten. Beat the whites to a stiff froth and stir in lightly. Bake in a greased pie dish for twenty minutes.
