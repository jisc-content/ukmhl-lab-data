Castor Sugar. 2 Eggs. Brown Sugar. Salt. Vanilla Essence. 1 oz. of Cornflour. Put the milk, with a tablespoonful of castor sugar and a pinch of salt, into a basin, and place the basin in a saucepan of hot water over the fire. Mix the cornflour with a little cold milk and bring to the boil. Beat up the eggs in a large basin. Then take a table- spoonful of brown sugar, place it in a saucepan and stir over the fire till it is thoroughly scorched, taking care not to burn it. Pour the hot milk on to the eggs, then pour the mixture in a saucepan, thicken with the cornflour, and immediately before removing from the fire stir in the scorched sugar.
