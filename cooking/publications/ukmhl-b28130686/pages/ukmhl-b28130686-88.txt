84 PARSNIP FRITTERS* INGREDIENTS Parsnips. Flour. Salt. Milk (or Cream). 2 Eggs. Butter. Boil three or four parsnips till tender, and mash. Take half a cup of milk or cream, and mix with it two tablespoonfuls of flour, two eggs, salt and an ounce of butter. Add to the mashed parsnips, and fry a delicate brown in butter or dripping. Serve very hot. SCALLOPED CAULIFLOWER* Ingredients Cauliflower. Bread Crumbs. Butter. Cheese. Potatoes. Boil a cauliflower till tender. Take away the flower from the leaves, mix with half its weight of mashed potatoes. Put a tablespoonful in some scallop shells with a piece of butter, sprinkle with bread crumbs and bake. A little grated cheese may be added to the mixture if approved. TOMATO PIE, Ingredients : Butter. Salt. Paste. Potatoes Tomatoes. Milk. Pepper. Thyme. 1 Egg.
