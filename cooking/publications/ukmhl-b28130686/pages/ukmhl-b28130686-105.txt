Very good with batter or suet pudding. SAUCE FOR BRAWNS, COLD MEATS, &c. Ingredients Vinegar. Sugar. Mustard. Thoroughly mix three tablespoonfuls of vinegar, one teaspoonful of made mustard, and a dessertspoonful of castor sugar. Excellent with brawn, &c.
