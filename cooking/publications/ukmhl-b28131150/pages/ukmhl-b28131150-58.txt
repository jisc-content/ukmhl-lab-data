PISTACHIO CREAM.* Put into a mortar a pound of blanched pistachio nuts, and a wine-glassful of brandy. Beat to a smooth paste. Add to it a quart of thick cream, and the beaten and strained yolks of four eggs. Place it upon a slow fire, and stir it until it is sufficiently thick. Pile it high in a glass dessert-disli, or put it into small glasses. "When cold, decorate it with neatly-cut pieces of pistachio nuts ; or you may put it into your ice-pot and freeze it if preferred.
