CHAPTER III. CLEAR SOUPS. CONSOMME A EES TRA CON. No. 85. To three pints of Consomme (No. 1 or 2), throw in a handful of shredded green tarragon leaves, make very hot and serve. CONSOMME A UN FINE HERBS. No. 86. To three pints of Consomme (No. I or 2), add a table-spoonful of chopped parsley, a tea-spoonful of chopped chives or green onions and chervil. Make very hot and serve. CONSOMME A LA ROYAL. No. 87. Poach in a frying-pan two or three dozen small pieces of chicken Quenelle meat (No. 174), and throw into the tureen three pints of Consomme (No. 1 or 2). CONSOMME A LA JARDINIERE. No. 88. Boil in three pints of Consomme (No. 1 or 2) until tender, one carrot, one turnip, a little celery and spinach, cut into different shapes with a cutter, and add a quarter of a pint of peas and some small sprays of cauliflower, cooked in the Consomme.
