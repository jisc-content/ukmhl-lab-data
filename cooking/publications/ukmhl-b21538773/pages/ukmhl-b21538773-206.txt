Cocoanut Cakes 435 Mix well together two well beaten eggs, 3 oz. of sifted sugar, 2 oz. of desiccated cocoanut, one dessertspoonful of flour. Bake a pale brown on floured baking tins. Welsh Cakes 436 1 lb. of flour, 6 oz. of butter, 6 oz. of castor sugar, 2 oz. of currants, half a pint of raw cream. Mix the butter and sugar well into the flour, then add currants and cream. Roll out until half an inch in thickness, cut the cakes with the cover of the flour dredger and bake both sides in a Dutch oven before a clear fire. Essex Scones 437 \ lb. of flour, 2 oz. of butter, one large tablespoonful of baking powder, a pinch of cream of tartar. Rub the butter well into the flour and baking powder till it is like breadcrumbs, then mix with milk to a light dough, knead it with the hands, flatten it round to the size of a plate, stamp out and bake twenty minutes in a hot oven. Cut in quarters, then through lengthwise, butter and serve very hot.
