To Fillet Fish It is more economical to do this at home, as then the trimmings may be used for rissoles and the bones and head for a stock or for sauce. To fillet a sole, lemon sole or plaice, skin the fish and lay it flat on the table, and with a cook’s knife cut the flesh right down the back bone and then round the fins. Ease the flesh from the back-bone by means of the knife, and, so to speak, peel the four pieces of fish away from it. A small sole will make four fillets.
