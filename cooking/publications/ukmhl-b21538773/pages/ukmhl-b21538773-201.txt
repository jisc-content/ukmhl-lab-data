Luncheon Cake 424 Put 6 oz. of butter into a basin and work it with a wooden spoon until it is creamy. Then add 6 oz. of powdered sugar and work again until the butter becomes quite white. Beat the yolks of three eggs until they are frothy, and add these to the butter and sugar and mix well. Scatter in by degrees 4 oz. of sultanas which have been washed and dried and floured, and 4 oz. of raisins which have been coarsely chopped, and two ounces of minced mixed peel.
