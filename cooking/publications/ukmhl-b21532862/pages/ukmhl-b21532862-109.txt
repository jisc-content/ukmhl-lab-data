You may boil fome whole pepper in it; cover the pafty-pan with puff’d pafte; be fure to roll out the pafte very thick; lay in the meat in the pan, and put fome of the gravy in with it; if the venifon is lean, take a flap of fat mutton; let it lie all night in fome red wine and vinegar; lay it on the top of the venifon; then clofe it up, and fend it to the oven: it takes a long time of baking. When you draw it out of the oven, fhake it. If you think it has not enough of gravy about it, pour in more at the top. A mock Venifon Pafty.
