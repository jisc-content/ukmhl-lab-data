Fold the fillets and put them on a greased tin, sprinkle with pepper and salt, and squeeze lemon-juice over them ; cover with a buttered paper and place in a moderate oven for about six minutes. Melt in a stewpan an ounce of butter, and add three-quarters of an ounce of flour and mix well; pour in the fish stock and boil for five minutes. Now add a little salt and pepper, half a gill of cream, and a teaspoonful of lemon-juice, and one teaspoonful of chopped parsley. Arrange the fillets in a circle and pour the sauce over them.
