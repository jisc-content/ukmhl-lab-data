SOVPS A LA MODE. 35 Tapioca Soup a la Crfeme. Potage au Tapioca a la Creme. Put one pint of white stock on to boil in a stew- pan ; then take one ounce of prepared crushed tapioca, and when the stock boils stir it in gradu- ally, after which move the saucepan to the side of the fire, and let it all simmer till the tapioca is quite clear. Then put the yolks of two eggs in a basin, add to them two tablespoonfuls of cream, and stir it with a wooden spoon, and then strain the mixture into another basin. Now take the stew- pan with the stock off the fire and let it cool; when cool add by degrees two or three tablespoonfuls of the egg liaison^ stirring well all the time, being very careful the eggs do not curdle ; after which add this mixture to the rest of the stock, stirring well and put in pepper and salt to taste.
