213 FARINACEOUS DISHES. 775. “ Nebengiisse fur Mehlspeisen ”—Sweet, or Fruit Sauces. Select any sweet or fruit sauce from among those given in the Index, either to be poui'ed over the dish when served, or handed in a sauce-tureen. 776. “ Obstschaumfiir Mehbpeisen”—Fruit Whips. Whisk' the whites of two or three eggs to a stiff snow ; choose a rich coloured fruit syrup, and, by degrees, whip in among the snow enough to give colour and flavour. Put the foam round the dish as a wreath. Hand more of the fruit syrup if requii'ed.
