It may then he glazed or not, according to taste. Marrow Toast. Let the butcher break up a marrow bone. Take out the marrow in as large pieces as possible, and put them into a stewpan with a little boiling water rather highly salted. When the marrow has boiled for a minute, drain the water away through a fine strainer. Have ready a slice of light toasted bread, place the marrow on it and put it into a Dutch oven before the fire for five minutes, or until it is done* Sprinkle over it a little pepper and salt, and a small teaspoonful of parsley chopped very fine The toast must be served very hot.
