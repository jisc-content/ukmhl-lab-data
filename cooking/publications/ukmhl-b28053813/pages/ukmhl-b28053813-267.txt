To render gum dragon more easy to dissolve it should be soaked for an hour in lemon juice. A nice inexpensive cream for filling meringues may be made as follows :—Boil two ounces of sugar with a pint of milk and an ounce of Nelson’s gelatine previously soaked, strain it, let it get cold, and flavour it with vanilla. When the cream appears likely to set add by degrees the juice of half a lemon, and whisk it until it becomes frothy and thick. Put it aside and use as required.
