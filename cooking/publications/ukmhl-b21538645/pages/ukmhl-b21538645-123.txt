Method.—Rub the butter into the flour, then add the lemon rind, castor sugar, and the candied peel chopped. Mix with the egg and milk, form into buns, and place a few comfits on the top of each. Method.—Cream the butter and sugar, then add the egg, and after it is well beaten the citron chopped finely and the flour, baking powder, and corn flour mixed.
