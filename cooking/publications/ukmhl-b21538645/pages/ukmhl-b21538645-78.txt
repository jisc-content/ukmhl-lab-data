Ingredients.—3 gills of Pine-apple Purde, 3 gills of double Cream, 2 ozs. Castor Sugar, ozs. of leaf Gelatine, a few Pistachio Kernels. Method.—Take a tinned pine-apple, remove the brown specks, and chop it well. Dissolve the gelatine in a little water and add to the puree with the castor sugar. Now whip the cream stiffly, and mix all lightly together and pour it into a mould, decorated with pistachio nuts and a little clear jelly. When set, turn it out by dipping the mould into warm water.
