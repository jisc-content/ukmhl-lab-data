VEGETABLES. Bsparaaus. Scrape the asparagus, wash it well, and cut it all one length. Have ready a stewpan of boiling water, with water about three inches in depth, and a little lemon juice in the water. Tie the asparagus in bundles and stand it upright, so as to leave the tender green above the water. Fit a lid tightly on, and boil rapidly for about 20 minutes. Dish on toast and serve with oiled butter, lemon juice and cayenne. Carrots. Scrape the carrots and let them lie in cold water until wanted ; then plunge them into boiling water with a little salt.
