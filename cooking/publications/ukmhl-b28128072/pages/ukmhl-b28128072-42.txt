Chop and pound 1 lb. of raw hare, add to it 3 oz. of finely chopped fried bacon and 2 oz. of ham, mix in 3 egg yolks, and \ gill of cream ; rub it all through a sieve, and mix with it enough reduced Espagnole sauce to make a very light puree, season with pepper and salt, put into a buttered mould, and steam for 45 minutes, then turn out and serve.
