Serve on a very hot dish with a tiny piece of butter in each, and a dust of minced parsley, a squeeze of lemon juice, and pepper and salt. Saut6d Kidneys (Hot). Skin and core the kidneys as before, cut them in slices. Put 1 oz. of butter into a pan, and melt this, then fry a small shallot in this (cut up), put in the sliced kidneys and fry for about 5 min- utes, tossing them occasionally, then sprinkling in rather less than 1 oz. of flour, and stir it all for 3 or 4 minutes longer over the fire, add 1 gill of good brown stock if at hand (water will do, but it is not so nice), stir it all until it boils, then simmer gently for 10 minutes ; serve on a wall of mashed potato, with the sauce round.
