Mince 1 lb. of any remains of cold meat (two or three kinds can be used)/a piece of fat bacon, or, if not handy, some bacon fat, and mix with this half the amount of breadcrumbs soaked in stock, a little grated lemon peel, a grate of nutmeg, and 2 eggs. Pack this into a buttered mould, and steam for 1| to 2 hours. Turn out, and serve with a good brown sauce round it. Rissoles (Hot).
