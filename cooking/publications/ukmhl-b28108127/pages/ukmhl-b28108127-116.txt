A green sauce for use with all kinds of cold fish and meat.—Take a handful of cher- vil, tarragon, chives, pimpernel, and garden cress ; wash in cold water ; blanch by putting the herbs into hot water for a while, to deprive them of rankness or bitterness of taste ; refresh them by plunging them in cold water. Now add four yolks of hard-boiled eggs and two anchovies, and pound the whole well in a mortar.
