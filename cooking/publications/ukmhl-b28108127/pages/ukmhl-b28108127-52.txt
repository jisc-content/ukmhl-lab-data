3<5 DELICATE DINING. Of the vegetable tribe, lentils, beans, and peas are the most nourishing. Fruit, when perfectly ripe, is most easy of digestion, because the juice of fruit consists of pure grape-sugar (glucose) and water, and it is in the form of grape-sugar that all starchy food is finally absorbed into the system. It may be said that the starch of the fruit, hav- ing been already changed into glucose by the process of ripening, requires no digestion after it is eaten by man, inasmuch as it is already in the state in which this element of nutri- tion is immediately absorbed into the system.
