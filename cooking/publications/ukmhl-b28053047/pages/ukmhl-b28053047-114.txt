108 Fifteen pounds of potatoes have their skins removed in one minute, by the simple operation of turning on the water spray and then turning the handle of the machine. It is practically noiseless in action, and is valuable, not only to the cook, as a labour saver, but also to the owner, because of the saving in cost of potatoes due to reduction of waste. It is well known that quite 20% of potatoes are wasted when they are peeled in the ordinary way by hand, but where this machine is in use the average loss in weight is only 5%.
