176 RECIPES FOR USING OXO. 0X0 AND BROWN BREAD SANDWICHES. These delicious sandwiches are of the highest nutritive value. They make an appetising, strengthening lunch, and are specially welcome as a quickly-prepared school meal for children. To prepare, spread Oxo thinly over well-buttered brown bread. 0X0 SANDWICHES WITH MUSTARD AND CRESS. A unique sandwich of great delicacy. Prepare brown bread sandwiches as above, and immediately before serving add finely- chopped mustard and cress, well rinsed and free from superfluous water. 0X0 ON HOT BUTTERED TOAST. The perfection of toast for cold days. Spread Oxo lightly over well-buttered toast. Serve hot.
