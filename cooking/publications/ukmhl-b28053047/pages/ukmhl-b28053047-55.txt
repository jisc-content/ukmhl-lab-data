49 YAM SOUP. 1 lb. of peeled yam 1 onion Pepper and salt 2 ozs. dripping Bacon rind Grate of nutmeg 3 pints of water 1 gill Fussell’s unsweetened milk Melt the dripping. Cut up the yam and stir in the dripping for ■ten minutes, do not brown. Add the water, onion, and bacon rind, bring to a boil, and as the yam softens, mash it up. Pass through a sieve, return to the saucepan, add the milk, nutmeg, and seasoning. Serve with sippets of toast.
