Take the stones from eighteen ripe fleshy apricots, cut into thin shoes and put them into a basin with the juice of three lemons; have ready boding a pint and a half of clarified syrup, pour it over the apricots, cover the basin with paper, and let them remain until quite cold, then drain the syrup through a napkin, add an ounce and a half of clarified isinglass, (half cold,) mix well in, and pour into your mould.
