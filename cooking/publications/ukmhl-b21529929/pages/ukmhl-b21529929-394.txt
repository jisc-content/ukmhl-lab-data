No. 808. Filets de Volatile saute au Supreme. Eillet three fowls as before, making nine fillets from the three, saute the same, dress them in crown on a border of mashed potatoes, sauce over with a sauce supreme (No. 57) and serve; should you require a larger entree use four fowls instead of three.
