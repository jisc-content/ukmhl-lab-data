All thin sauces are or will become very much in vogue; they invigorate the appetite without overloading the stomach; and are, consequently, more wholesome; all brown sauces are preferable for meat or game entrees, and in some instances, which you will see, for poultry; but of course the arrangement of your tables would prevent you serving all white or all brown entrees.
