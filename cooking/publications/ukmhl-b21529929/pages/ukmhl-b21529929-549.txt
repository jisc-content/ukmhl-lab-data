Gateau fourre, ou d’ Artois, aux Pommes. Peel and cut ten apples into slices, put them into a preserving-pan with two ounces of butter, six ounces of powdered sugar, some thin lemon-peel cut in strips, and a a little powdered cinamon, pass them over a sharp fire till tender, then take them off, mix four tablespoonfuls of apri- cot marmalade with them, and put by till cold; make half a pound of puff paste and proceed as before, using the above preparation instead of the sweatmeats before‘men- tioned.
