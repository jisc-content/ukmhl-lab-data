No. 924. Caittes aux petits pois. Proceed exactly as above with the quails, but make the stand entirely of green peas nicely boiled; have ready a pint of stewed peas (No. 1077) with which you have put the quails a few minutes, fill the centre with them, dress the quails round as above, and pom the remainder of the peas in the dish. No. 925. Turban des Cailles a la Financiere. Roast eight quails in vegetables, but without the vine- leaf and bacon, make a border of forcemeat as for ris de veau a la Turque (No. 673), stand it in your dish, then make a ragout financiere (No. 50), but using game sauce instead of brown sauce; put your quails five minutes in the sauce, then dress them round on the border of forcemeat; garniture in the centre, sauce over, and serve.
