The Strasburg bacon being very dry, requires soaking at least twenty-four hours; it must be allowed to simmer until very tender; place it be- tween two dishes, with a weight upon it, and when cold cut it into the shapes required, and make them hot in good white stock. Good streaky bacon may be used instead of the Strasburg, if it is difficult to obtain. No. 445. Fillet of Veal a la Frinciere.
