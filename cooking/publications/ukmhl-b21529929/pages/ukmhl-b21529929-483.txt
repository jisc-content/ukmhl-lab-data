No. 1015. Pate de Volaille aux truffes. Line a raised pie-mould with pate fine (No. 1136) as di- rected, but you will not require so large a mould; line the pie with forcemeat (No. 120), you have previously boned a small fowl, which stuff as for galantine a la voliere (No. 1032), seasoning it rather highly, but it will not require sewing up ; having filled it, place it in your pie, cover with forcemeat, forming a dome, finish the pie as directed for pate de veau, bake two hours and a half in a slow oven, take it out, cut off the lid, lay a sheet of tin upon the meat (made for that purpose), upon which place a seven pounds weight, let remain until cold, then take your pie out of the mould and serve with croutons and chopped aspic (No. 1360) upon the top.
