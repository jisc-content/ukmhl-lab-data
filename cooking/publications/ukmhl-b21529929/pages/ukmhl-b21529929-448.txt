No. 940. Turkey Bcircled. Trass a turkey as described in the last, have a square piece of fat bacon the eighth of an inch in thickness and large enough to cover the breast, upon which tie it with pack- thread ; roast and serve as in the last, taking away the pack- thread but leaving the bacon; it will require a little longer to roast as the bacon prevents the fillets from being done so soon.
