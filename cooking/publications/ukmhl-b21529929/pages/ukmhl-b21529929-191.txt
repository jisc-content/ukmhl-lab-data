141 POISSONS. No. 345. Pike en matelote. Stuff and bake the fish as before; when done, dress it without a napkin, and pour a sauce matelote (see Saumon en matelote mariniere, No. 239) in the middle and round the fish, and serve very hot. Or the fish may be stewed as in the last. No. 346. Pike a la Hollandaise. Boil the fish in salt and water, in the same manner as cod-fish; drain it well, dish it without a napkin, pour a sauce Hollandaise over it. (For sauce, see Turbot a la Hollandaise, No. 206.) No. 347. Small Pike a la Meuniere. Crimp a small pike, it must not weigh more than two pounds, but smaller if you can get it, and proceed exactly as for Sole a la meuniere (No. 260), but allow it more time.
