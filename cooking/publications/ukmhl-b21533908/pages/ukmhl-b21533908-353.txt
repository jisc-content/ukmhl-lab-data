Take three parts of soup-herb powder to one part of savoury powder. Obs.—These powders should be kept ready prepared ; they will save a great deal of time in cooking ragouts, stuffings, forcemeat-balls, soups, sauces, &c.; kept dry and tightly corked down, their fragrance and strength may be preserved undiminished for several years.
