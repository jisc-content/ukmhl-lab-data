Dress your potatoes exactly as before directed, put them on a gridiron over a very clear, brisk fire; turn them as they brown, till they are done all over, and send them up dry, with melted but- ter in a cup. Potatoes fried in slices. (No. 104.) Peel large potatoes, and slice them the thick- ness of a two penny piece; dry them well in a clean cloth, flour them, and fry them in lard. Take care that your lard and fryingpan are quite clean ; put it on a quick fire, watch it, and as soon as it boils, and the lard is still, put in the slices of potatoe, and keep moving them till they are crisp; take them up and lay them to drain on a sieve, send them up with a very little salt sprink- led over them.
