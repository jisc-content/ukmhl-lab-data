Put three ounces of fresh butter into a three quart stewpan ; when it is melted, till it about one third full with carrots and turnips cut into small squares; shake these over the fire for ten minutes, qr a quarter of an hour ; then add as much water as will nearly fill the saucepan, and after letting it stew gently for an hoifr, slice in a couple of onions, stew for two hours longer; a quarter of an hour before you serve up, stir in a teacupful of bread raspings, some salt, and either some Cay- enne, or common pepper.
