SAUCES, GRAVIES,- &C. Stuffing for Hare. (No. 379.) Two ounces of beef suet chopped fine, three n ounces of fine bread crumbs, parsley a drachm, shallot half a drachm, or a small anchovy, a drachm of marjoram or lemon-thyme, or winter- savory, half a drachm of grated lemon-peel, half a drachm of nutmeg, pepper and salt; mix with the white and yolk of an egg, put it in the hare, and sew it up.
