When yon want oyster sauce, put some of the oysters, with a spoonful of the pickle, into good melted butter. Bottled Oyster Sauce. (No. 280.) To make half a pint of sauce, put two ounces of butter into a stewpan, with three drachms of oysters, two drachms of flour, and half a pint of new milk ; set it on a slow fire, stir it till it boils, and season it with salt.
