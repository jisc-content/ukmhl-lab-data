Roast Potatoes. (No. 109.) Wash and dry your potatoes, put them in a tin Dutch oven, or cheese toaster; take care not to put them too near at first, or they will get burnt oa the outside before they are warmed through. Large potatoes will require two hours to roast them. Roast Potatoes under Meat. (No. 110.) Peel and wash large potatoes, put them on the fire in cold water, and let them boil till they are half done; drain the water from them, and put them into an earthen dish, or small tin pan: set them under the meat that is roasting, and baste them with some of the drippings ; when they arc browned on one side, turn them and brown them all over; send them up round the meat, or in a small dish.
