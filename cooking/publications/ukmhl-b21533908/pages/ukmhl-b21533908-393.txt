Minced Veal. (No. 511.) * Mince ready dressed veal as fine as possible, but do not chop it; put it into a stewpan with a few spoonsful of veal or mutton broth, a little lemon-peel minced fine, a spoonful of milk or cream : thicken with butter and flour, and season it with salt, a tablespoonful of lemon pickle, or catsup.
