Have ready a large stewpan, half full of fine lard, quite hot, and drop the batter into it, the size of large nuts, until the surface be filled : as they brown, turn them : they will be very large : when done, remove them on clean paper in a dish before the fire, and do the remainder. Serve as quickly as possible. They are excellent. ORANGE FRITTERS.—E. R. Take off the rind of two oranges, removing all the white skin ; then cut the oranges in slices across, and take out all the pips ; dip the slices of orange in batter, and fry them. Serve with powdered sugar upon each fritter. PINK-COLOURED FRITTERS. Boil a large beet-root until it is tender; beat it fine in a marble mortar.
