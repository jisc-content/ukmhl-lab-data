Rub smooth a large spoonful of oatmeal with two of* water, and pour it into a pint of water boiling on the fire; stir it well and boil it quick, but take care it does not boil over. In a quarter of an hour strain it off, and add salt and a bit of butter when eaten. Stir until the butter be incorporated. BARLEY GRUEL. Wash four ounces of pearl-barley; boil it In two quarts of 'water with a stick of cinnamon, till reduced to a quart; strain and return it into the saucepan with sugar and three-quarters of a pint of port wine. Heat up, and use as wanted. A VERY AGREEABLE DRINK. Into a tumbler of fresh cold water pour a table- spoonful of capillaire and the same of good vinegar.
