GRAVY FOR A FOWL WHEN THERE IS NO ME.\T TO MAKE IT OF. Wash the feet nicely, and cut them and the neck small: simmer them with a little bread browned, a slice of onion, a bit of parsley and thyme, some pepper and salt, and the liver and gizzard, in half a pint of water, till half wasted. Take out the liver, bruise it, and strain the liquor to it. Then thicken it with flour and butter, and add a tea-spoonful of mushroom-ketchup, and it will be very good. VEAL GRAVY.
