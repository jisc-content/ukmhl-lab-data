BRANDY CREAM. Boil two dozen of almonds blanched, and pounded bitter almonds, in a little milk. When cold add to it the yolks of five eggs beaten well in a little cream, sweeten, and put to it two glasses of the best brandy ; and when well mixed pour to it a quart of thin cream: set it over the fire, but do not let it boil; stir one way till it thickens, then pour it into cups or low glasses. ^V hen cold it will be ready. A ratafia-drop may be put in each, if you choose it. If you wish it to keep, scald the cream previously. RAT.AFIA CREAM.
