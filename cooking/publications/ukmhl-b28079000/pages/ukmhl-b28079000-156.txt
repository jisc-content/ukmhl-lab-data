SICK-ROOM RECIPES 144 1. Cut up the gelatine, place in a large bowl, pour the boiling water over, add the sugar and stir until both dissolve. 2. Add the grated rind of half a lemon and beat the eggs well. 3. Pour the milk into the eggs, stir and add the dissolved gelatine and sugar ; stir well. 4. Strain the lemon juice and pour gradually into the jelly, stirring all the time. 5. Pour into a mould and serve when set. INVALID EGG PUDDING Ingredients : One Egg One teaspoonful of Flour One tablespoonful of Milk A pinch of Salt. 1. Mix the flour and milk together and add the salt. 2. Stir in the egg well beaten. 3. Strain into a buttered cup. 4. Stand in boiling water and simmer for twenty minutes. 5. Serve with castor sugar. LINSEED TEA Ingredients : Two ounces of Linseed One ounce of Sugar Candy Strips of Lemon Peel One and a half pints of Water. 1. Put all the ingredients into an enamelled saucepan, bring to a boil and simmer from one to two hours. 2. Strain, and add the juice of half a lemon. 3. Serve hot or cold.
