Shred and chop the suet. 2. Put the flour in a basin, add the suet and a pinch of salt. 3. Make into a light paste with cold water. 4. Roll out on a floured board. 5. Spread with jam, roll up and close the ends. 6. Wring a cloth out of cold water and tie the pudding in it. 7. Place in a pan of boiling water and simmer for two hours. 8. Remove the cloth and serve on a hot dish.
