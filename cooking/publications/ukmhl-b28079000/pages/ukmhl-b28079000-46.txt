BOILED CAULIFLOWERS 1. Choose medium sized cauliflowers and as white as possible. Cut away the outer leaves, and trim off the tops of the inner. Soak in plenty of cold water for one hour, adding one tablespoonful of salt to the water. 2. Stick three kitchen forks into the stalk of the cauliflower, and place in a pan of boiling water, the head of the flower downwards. Add a tablespoonful of salt. Boil from fifteen to twenty minutes. Drain and serve whole with melted butter. N.B.â€”The forks should rest on the edges of the pan, and are used to support the cauliflower and to facilitate the taking up and draining.
