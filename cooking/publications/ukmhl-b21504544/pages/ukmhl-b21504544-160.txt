WHOLESOME COOKERY. 142 358.—Caramel Rice Mould. I Prepare the mould with caramel according to recipe 326. Take 4* ounces of Carolina rice; put it in 1 quart of milk, and let it gradually swell till cooked and the milk absorbed; then add a small lump of very fresh butter and stir in 4 eggs. Put this in your mould; do not quite fill the mould. Steam it from 20 minutes to half an hour. Serve hot, or cold with a custard round 359.—Plain Cold Rice Pudding. 1 ounce of Carolina rice, 1 tablespoonful of sugar, a very small lump of butter, 1 pint of milk. Place all in a deep pie-dish and bake till quite set. Must be done very slowly. Serve cold. When the spoon is put in the dish it ought to come out with a cream upon it; if it does not, the pudding has not succeeded. 360.—Caramel Semolina Mould.
