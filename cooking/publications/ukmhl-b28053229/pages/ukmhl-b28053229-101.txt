An interesting article, entitled “ La Vanille des colonies francaises et la vanilline chimique,” appears in IdAgriculture pratique des pays cliauds for October 1909. In this it is pointed out that, next to Mexico, the French colonies have become the most important exporters of vanilla. The production of this substance is, however, becoming a matter of greater difficulty year by year, owing to the lowering of the prices that has been brought about by the competition of the artificial product vanillin.
