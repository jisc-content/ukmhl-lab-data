PRESERVED FRUITS. 51 66. Dried Fruits. All the Fruits I have been describing as capable of preserving, may also be Dried, Drain them from the syrup (in which they should have been warmed), then wash in luke-warm water, place them on hair-sieves, and put in the stove for four hours to dry ; then let them cool, and varnish witli a little of their own syrup.
