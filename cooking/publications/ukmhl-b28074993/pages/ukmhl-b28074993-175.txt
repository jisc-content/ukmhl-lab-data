(Wife of SeDHtor from Ohio.) pNE pound of sugar (light weight), one pound of flour (light weight), a little more than half a pound of butter, whites of sixteen eggs beaten to a stiff froth; beat butter and sugar to a cream and add the flour and eggs alternately until all are used; flavor with peach or lemon.
