SRATE a package of sweetened chocolate, add two tablespoonfuls of water, and set the bowl in a tin of water on the stove to melt. While melting, roll some of the cream (see page 204) into balls; dip these one at a time in the chocolate, lifting out with a fork. Put on a buttered dish to harden. Use any kind of flavoring desired in cream.
