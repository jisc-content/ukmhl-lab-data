218 AND HOW TO COOK TEEM. DEVILED ALMONDS. BT MBS. W. D. OWBir. (Wife of RepresentstlTe from Indiana.) JLANCH one-half pound of sweet almonds and wipe them dry; then put into a frying-pan two ounces of butter; make it hot; add the almonds; fry them gently till of a good brown color; drain them on a hair sieve; strew cayenne pepper and salt over them and serve hot.
