114 STATESMEN'S DISHES; CHESTNUT STUFFING. BT MRS. F. M. COCKBELI.. (Wife of Senator from Missouri.) ^KY bread crumbs, sweet herbs, chopped celery, a little grated nutmeg, pepper and salt. Put ^ a small quantity of butter in a stewpan; when hot stir in a small minced onion, brown slightly and add the other ingredients, all thoroughly mixed, with two eggs and a little melted butter or stock. Cook through without browning. The chestnuts are put on the fire to burst their skin, boiled in salted water, and added to the dressing.
