STATESMEN’S DISHES; 118 MUSHROOM SAUCE. BT MBS. C. A. BEBOSN. (Wife of Representative from New Jersey.) »AKE one tablespoonful of butter, melt it and stir into it while hot two tablespoonfuls of flour, sifting it gradually, and stirring con- stantly till smooth. Pour in a glass of milk or milk and cream, and stir continually till it boils; then drain off the liquor from ajar of French mushrooms, and taking the mixture from the fire put in the mush- rooms, pepper and salt and steam it. Served with steak or chops.
