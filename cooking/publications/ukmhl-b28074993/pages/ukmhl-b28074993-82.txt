STATESMEN'S DISBES; 78 VEAL LOAF. BY MBS. J. O. 8AWYBB. (Wife of Representative from Now York.l pNE and a half pints cold veal well minced, one pint powdered crackers, one teacup milk, one-half teacup butter, two eggs. Stir veal and cracker dust together, seasoning with salt and pepper; beat eggs in milk and add one-half pound of butter, melted; mix all together and pour in buttered baking-dish; press down until smooth and put rest of butter on top; cover and bake one- half hour; remove cover and brown. Serve hot in dish, or sliced when cold.
