(Wife of Senator from Kansas, and President pro tern, of tlie Senate.) pound of butter, one pound of flour, one- quarter of a pound of sugar (flne granulated), one dozen eggs, two pounds of citron, two small cocoanuts (grated), two pounds blanched almonds, one wineglassful of wine, one wiueglassful of brandy, one teaspoonful of ground cinnamon, one grated nutmeg, one teaspoonful of ground mace, two teaspoonfuls of baking powder, cream, butter and sugar and eggs, thoroughly beaten, but not separated.
