AND HOW TO COOK THEM. 78 BEEF COLLAR. BT HRS. JA8. Z. GBORGB. (Wife of Senator from Mississippi.) JAKE a piece of flank, lay it in salt a day or two, according to the weather. Then wash and cut off all the loose fat and gristle; score it on the inside; season with a little pepper, allspice and nutmeg; roll it up tightly in a cloth, with ends well secured, and boil from six to eight hours, accord- ing to weight, not letting it boil violently. Set it away to cool; slice thin and serve cold. Excellent for lupchcon.
