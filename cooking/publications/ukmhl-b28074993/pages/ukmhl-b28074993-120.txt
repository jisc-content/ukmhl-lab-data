110 STATESMEN’S DISHES; CATSUP. BT MBS. JOHN H. EKAGAN. (Wife of Senator from Texas.) SNE peck of ripe tomatoes, peeled and sliced. Add six good-sized onions, sliced fine. Lay in a jar first a layer of tomatoes, then a layer of onions; add a little salt with each layer; let stand over night, then boil well, stirring almost constantly. When done add one pint of vinegar and two garlics chopped fine; add also red pepper, cinnamon, cloves, spice and ginger, all ground fine. Bottle for use. Note.—This is especially appetizing for baked whiteflsh.
