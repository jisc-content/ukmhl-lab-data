103 AND HOW TO COOK THEM. BAKED CUSTARDS. mbs. ALFRED C. HARMEB. (Wife of Representative from Pennsylvania.) pint of cream, four eggs, cinnamon, almond WMW flavor, and three ounces of sugar. Boil the cream with a piece of cinnamon; pour it into a bowl, and when cold add the eggs well beaten and strained, the sugar powdered, and a few drops of almond flavor. Bake in small cups in slow oven.
