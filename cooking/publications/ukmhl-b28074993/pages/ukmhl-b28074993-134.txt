(Wife of the President of the United States.) sNE cup of black molasses, one cup of chopped suet, one cup of milk, three and a quarter cups of flour, two eggs, one teaspoonful of soda, one of cinnamon, half a teaspoonful of nutmeg, one pint of flgs. Mix together the molasses, suet, spice and the figs, cut fine; dissolve the soda with a tablespoonful of hot water and mix with the milk; add to the other ingredients; beat the eggs light, and stir into the mixture; add the flour and beat thor- oughly. Butter two small or one large brown-bread mold; turn the mixture into the mold and steam five hours. Serve with cream sauce.
