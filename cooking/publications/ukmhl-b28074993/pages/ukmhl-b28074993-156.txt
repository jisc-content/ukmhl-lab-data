STATESMEN'S DISHES; 162 SPONGE PUDDING, BT KBS. W. K. KASOH. (Wife of Bepteaentatlve from Illinois.) pint milk, six eggs, two ounces flour, two ounces butter, two ounces sugar. Boil the milk, add the flour and then the sugar. When nearly cold add the butter. Separate the eggs, beat the yolks, and add the whites just before baking. Set pudding-dish into water to bake. Eat with sauce.
