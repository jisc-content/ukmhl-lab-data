21B AND HOW TO COOK TEEM. ORANGE FOOL. BT XB8. DABXBZ. X. BABBDBIX. (Wife of Uolted Stntes Marshal of the District of Oolomhia.) JIX the juice of three Seville oranges with three whole eggs well beaten; one-half pint of cream, and cinnamon, nutmeg and finely sifted sugar to taste. Set this over a slow fire and stir, one way, until it becomes of the consistency of melted butter. Serve ice-cold.
