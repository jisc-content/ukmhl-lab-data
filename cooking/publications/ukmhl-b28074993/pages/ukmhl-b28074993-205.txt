201 AND HOW TO COOK TEEM. ANGEL FOOD. BT Mias MABY B. TCBPIE. (Daughter of Senator from Indiana.) the whites of seventeen eggs; one pound pulverized sugar, one-half pound flour, three- quarters of an ounce cream of tartar. Sift the flour, sugar and cream of tartar together seven times. Beat whites of eggs stiff; then sift the flour slowly into the whites. Bake in new pan that has never been used. Do not butter the pan.
