AND HOW TO COOK THEM. 123 MARYLAND EGG-NOGG. BY MBS. STEPHEN J. FIELD. (Wife of Associate Justice of the Supreme Court.) gallon of milk, one dozen eggs. Divide tlie yolks from the whites and beat them. Add fifteen tablespoonfuls of sugar, one grated nutmeg, one pint of brandy, one pint of Jamaica rum. Beat the yolks and sugar until light, add the brandy and rum, stirring constantly. Last of all put in one gallon of milk or cream and cover with the beaten whites of eggs.
