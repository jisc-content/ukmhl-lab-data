164 STATESMEN'S DISHES; PIE CRUST. BT HB8. J. W. YAK SOHAIOK. (Wife of Kepresentatlve from Wisoonaln.) ^WO cups of flour, three-fourths cup of lard, one-half cup of butter, a pinch of salt; mix with ice-cold water—using a knife instead of the hand—into a firm dough. Roll it in a napkin and lay it on ice till the filling is ready; roll very thin, spread with butter and dredge with flour, repeating the process three times. Prepare the pie, and bake in quick oven.
