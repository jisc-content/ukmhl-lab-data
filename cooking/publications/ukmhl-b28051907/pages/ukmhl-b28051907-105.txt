OATMEAL MUSH. Oatmeal, Indian meal, and hominy all require two things to make them perfect: that is, plenty of water when first put on to boil, and a long time to boil. Have two quarts of boiling water in a saucepan, and stir into it one cup of oatmeal; let it boil one hour; then add a generous half table-spoonful of salt. Boil one hour longer, stirring often. HOMINY. Wash in two waters one cup of hominy, stir it into a quart of boiling water, add a table-spoonful of salt, and boil from thirty to sixty minutes. Sixty are better than thirty. Stir often, and be careful that it does not burn. The fine hominy cooks more quickly than the coarse. To be eaten as a vegetable with meat, with butter and sugar, or with milk.
