Probable cost, 5d. or 6d. 41.—FISH SOUFFLE, Ingredients— X lb. uncooked fish. % oz. butter. % oz. flour. Yz gill of milk, or fish stock 2 eggs. Pepper and salt. A squeeze of lemon juice. Metbod— Weigh the fish free from skin and bone, and scrape it down finely with a knife. Make a panada with the butter, flour, and fish stock. Melt the butter in a small saucepan, add the flour, and mix till smooth with a wooden spoon. Then pour on the milk or fish stock, and stir until the mixture is thick and free from lumps, and leaves the sides of the pan quite clean. Put this panada into a mortar with the fish, season- ings, and yolks of eggs. Pound well together, and rub through a fine wire sieve, scraping the sieve well underneath.
