32 LIBERAL LIVING UPON NARROW MEANS. One half cup butter. Two cups brown sugar. One cup molasses. One cup cold water. One tablespoonful ground ginger. One tablespoonful cinnamon. Two small teaspoonfuls soda dissolved in hot water. Put sugar, molasses, and shortening into a bowl and set in the oven until the contents are warm enough to mix readily. Stir in the spices, water, soda, and flour. Plour thickens so variously that it may be necessary to add more in order to make the dough stiff enough to roll out. INIake the cakes very thin and bake quickly. This recipe is for a large quantity.
