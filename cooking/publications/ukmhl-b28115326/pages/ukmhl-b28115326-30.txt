Thicken the milk with a teaspoonful of butter creamed with one of flour, add a teaspoonful of sugar, and pepper and salt to taste. Potatoes au Gratin. Prepare mashed potato as usual, and mound it in a pie plate or a dish that will stand the heat. Sprinkle fine dry crumbs over the top and brown on the upper grating of the oven. Lemon Pie. Make pastry as directed in the chapter treating of the Christmas dinner. Line the pie plate with the paste and fill with the following mixture : — One teacupful boiling water. One tablespoonful cornstarch. One lemon. One cup sugar. One egg. Two teaspoonfuls butter. Wet up the cornstarch in a little cold water and stir, into the boiling water. Cream the butter and sugar, and stir into the cornstarch and water. Set aside to cool, and when quite cold, add the egg beaten light, the juice of the lemon, the grated peel, and the inside chopped very fine. Set aside a little of the pastry in a cold place to use for Monday’s dessert.
