Simmer (very gently, or the onions will shrink,) for about three hours. 53. Mandram. Two cucumbers, two large onions, two red peppers : chop all up together into very small pieces, add a little salt, and let the cucumber, etc., drain for about an hour ; add some vinegar and oil. To be eaten with fish or cold meat. 54. Tomato Salad. Slice ripe tomatoes, Spanish onions, and a small cucumber ; sprinkle with salt and black pepper, and add oil and vinegar.
