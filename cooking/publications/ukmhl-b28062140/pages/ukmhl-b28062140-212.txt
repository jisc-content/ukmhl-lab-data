XVIII. ON BEING INVITED TO DINE. In the grammar in which I learned the ele- ments of the Spanish tongue, one of the ex- ercises, I remember, began as follows: “ I like to dine always at home; an invitation incon- veniences me. Nevertheless, it is necessary to take account of the requirements of soci- ety.
