156 DELICATE FEASTING. a means of fixing pastel, invented for Marie Antoinette’s service, at Trianon, a table far superior to the Margravine’s system of lifts. It was a table which rose through the floor, all served, and accompanied by four little tables, or dumb-waiters, on which were placed the various utensils necessary. A similar ar- rangement is described in Bastide’s “ La Pe- tite Maison,” already mentioned.
