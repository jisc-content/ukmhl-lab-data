Keep them by the fire till they would peel, but don’t peel them; then pour the water oflF till both are quite cold. Place the codlins then in a stone jar with a smallish mouth, and pour on them the water that scalded them. Cover the pot with bladder wetted, and tied very close, and then over it coarse paper tied again. It is best to keep them in small jars, such as will bo used at once when opened. To keep Goosebernes.
