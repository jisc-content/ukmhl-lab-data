Forcemeat, to force Fowls or Meat, Shred a little ham, or gammon, some cold veal, or fowl, some beef-suet; a small quantity of onion, some parsley, very little lemon-peel, salt, nutmeg, or pounded mace, and either white pepper, or Cayenne, and bread crumbs. Pound it in a mortar, and bind it with one' or two eggs beaten and strained. For forcemeat patties, the mixture as above. For cdd savoury Pies. The same; only substituting fat, or bacon, for suet.
