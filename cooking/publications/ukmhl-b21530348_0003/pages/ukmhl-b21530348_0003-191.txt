ARTISTIC SUGAR WORK. 1.—Lilt Stand, for holding preserved fruits or sweets. Stems and leaves in boiled Sugar, flowers in sugar paste. Lilies round base of stand holding sweetmeats. 3.—Wheat-sheaf Stand, made of boiled Sugar, with band of sugar paste; sickle and rake of caramel. Filled with preserved fruits and sweetmeats. Surrounded by smaller sheaves filled with preserved cherries. Base made of boiled Sugar straw. 2.—Norse Battle-ship, made of boiled Sugar, sugar paste, and piping. Vessel filled with fruits arranged as a cargo. 4.—Peacock Vase, holding preserved fruits. Bodies and tails of birds moulded in boiled Sugar, finished off with coloured piping. Stand and vase of boiled Sugar piped over in design.
