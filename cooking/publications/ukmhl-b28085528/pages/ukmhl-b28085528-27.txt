Fried eggs, bacon, hung beef, potted shrimps, preserves. DINNER. Scolloped fish. Calf’s heart, roasted, pork cutlets, tomato sauce, potatoes, greens. Lady Betty’s pudding. Cheese, &c. KITCHEN.
