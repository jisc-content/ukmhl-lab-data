LOBSTEK CUTLETS. Take the meat out of one large or two small hen lobsters; pound it and season with a saltspoonful of salt, three-quarters of a salt- spoonful of white pepper, half a grain of cayenne, and two ounces of dissolved fresh butter. Poimd the coral and spawn; add the well-beaten yolks of two eggs, and a teaspoonful of baked floiu; stir over a gentle fire for two minutes ; then mix it with the lobster. Set it in a cool place for an hour. Make up the meat into four or six small cutlets; dip each into beaten egg, then into fine dried bread crumbs; let them stand ten minutes in a cool place ; then redip them in egg and crumbs.
