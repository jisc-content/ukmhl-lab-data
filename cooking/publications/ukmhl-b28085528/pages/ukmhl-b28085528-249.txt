The tripe must be quite fresh. Have about two pounds ; cut it into pieces three inches long and two broad ; wash it in cold water, and dry it with a cloth. Lay the tripe in a bright stew- pan, with a pint and a half of new milk; simmer as gently as possible for two hours; stir frequently to prevent the tripe burning to the bottom.
