Beat the white of one and the yolks of two fresh eggs ; mix in two tablespoonfuls of luell-hahed flour and half a pint of new milk ; beat the batter for twenty minutes, cover it over, and let it stand two hours in a cold place. Butter a basin, beat the batter again for ten minutes, and pour it in.
