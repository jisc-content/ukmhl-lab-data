Cut the foot into 4 or 6 pieces and remove all marrow and fat. Wash and scrape it well in warm water: put it into a deep pan and cover it with cold water. Bring it to the boil and rinse well; then return it to the pan with the 5 pints of water. Simmer slowly from 6 to 7 hours, then strain it through a hair-sieve into a basin and allow it to become cold.
