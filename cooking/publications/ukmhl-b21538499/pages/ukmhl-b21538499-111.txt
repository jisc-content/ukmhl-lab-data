Sheep’s Head Pie.—Required: Short crust; 1 sheep’s head; y lb. bacon; an egg; 1 teaspoonful finely chopped parsley; l/2 pint pot liquor; y tea- spoonful salt; y teaspoonful pepper. Soak the head all night in salt and water; wash thoroughly in warm water, removing the soft bones from the nostrils. Boil with sufficient water to cover it, till it is quite tender (about three hours). Place the egg in hot water, boil for ten minutes; plunge the egg in cold water. Remove the shell and cut up into small pieces. Cut the meat, gristle, and bacon into small pieces, and put them, with the egg, parsley, pepper, salt, and half a pint of the liquor the head has been boiled in, into a pie dish; cover with paste as in last recipe, and bake as directed.
