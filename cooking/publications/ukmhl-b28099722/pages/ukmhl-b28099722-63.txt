When so tender in the stalk that you can easily put a fork in, it is ready; lift it out on a drainer and press the water well out of it. It may be served with French sauce or plain melted butter. N.B.—A piece of bread, tied in a linen rag and put in the water, prevents the unpleasant smell the boiling of green vegetables causes in a house. TO BOIL GREEN PEAS. Shell and wash the peas, and put them in boiling water with a little sugar, salt, and a sprig of mint in it; let them boil for about 20 minutes, or perhaps a little longer if the peas are old.
