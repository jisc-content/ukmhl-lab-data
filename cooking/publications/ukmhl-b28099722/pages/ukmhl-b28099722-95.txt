SAUCES. BROWN SAUCE. 1 teaspoonful Butter or Mustard. Flour. Dripping. 1 Onion. Put into a small pan the teaspoonful of butter or dripping; let it get quite hot, then slice a small onion, and fry it in the fat till brown. Stir in one teaspoonful of flour, add a teacupful of gravy or water, and let it boil; then add half a teaspoonful of made mustard, and a few drops of either vinegar, ketchup, or Harvey sauce; boil all for one minute. Strain it to keep back the onion. Boil the egg hard, i.e., for ten minutes ; take off the shell, and chop it up. Put into a small sauce-pan the butter and flour, and mix over the fire ; then pour in a breakfast-cupful of milk, and stir till it boils; then add to it the chopped up egg, which mix well, and serve. EGG SAUCE. 1 oz. Butter. 1 oz. Flour. 1 Egg. 1 breakfast-cupful of Milk. A little Pepper and Salt.
