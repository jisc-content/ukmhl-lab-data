Bub the yolks of three or four hard boiled eggs to a smooth paste, with a little melted butter, pepper and salt, to these add two raw ones, beat in light, add enough flour to hold the paste together, make into balls, with floured hands, and set in a cool place until just before your soup comes off, when you put them in carefully and boil one minute. ANOTHEB SOUP FOB SUET. Mrs. G. B. Wyllie. One tea-cup pearl barley in about one pint cold water, bring to the boil till it swells, boil in a tin pail, in hot water, four quarts milk, put in your barley and keep simmering for three or four hours, add, then serving, one teaspoonful of salt.
