CREAM TAPIOCA PUDDING. Mrs. A. T. Hall. Soak three tablespoons of tapioca in water over night; put the tapioca into a quart of boiling milk, and boil half an hour; heat the yolks of four eggs with a cup of sugar : add three tablespoons of prepared cocoanut; stir in and boil ten minutes longer; pour into a pudding dish; beat the whites of the four eggs to a stiff froth, stir in three tablespoons of sugar ; put this over the top and sprinkle cocoanut over the top and brown for five minutes.
