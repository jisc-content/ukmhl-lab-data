Mix one pint of cream with nine tablespoons of fine Bugar and one gill of wine in a large bowl; whip these with the cream dasher, and as the froth rises, skim into the dish in which it is to be served. Fill the dish full to the top, and ornament with kisses or macaroons. SPANISH CREAM. Mrs. J. H. Brown.
