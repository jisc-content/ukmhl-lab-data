FRIED BREAD IN BATTER. M. A. T. Take one tablespoon sweet light dough; dissolve it in one cup sweet milk ; add three or four eggs, one and a half cups flour, one teaspoon of salt; cut some thin slices of light bread, dip in this batter, and fry in hot lard'; sprinkle with powdered sugar, and garnish with jelly.
