VALUABLE recipes. 193 BAKED BATTER PUDDING. Mrs. WyUie. Beat separately the yolks and whites of three eggs, inix three tablespoons of flour with a pint of milk and a small piece of butter; add the eggs; bake in a quick oven. Serve with sauce. STEAM PUDDING. Mrs. Wyllie. One and a-half cups flour, one and a-half cups suet, one cup of currants, one cup of golden syrup, spices to taste, two teaspoons cream of tartar. Steam four hours. CHERRY PUDDING. H. N. Jenk*. A pint of bread crusts or soft crackers, scalded in a quart of boiling milk, piece of butter the size of an egg, one teaspoon of salt, three eggs, one and a half teacups of sugar if eaten without sauce, and if with sauce a tablespoon of sugar; a pinch of pulverized cinnamon, and a quart of stoned cherries ; bake quickly.
