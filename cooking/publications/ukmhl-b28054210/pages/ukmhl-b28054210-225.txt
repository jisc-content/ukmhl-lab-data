BOILED CUSTARD. Mrs. R. M. Tickering. One quart milk, eight eggs, one-half pound of sugar; beat to a good froth the eggs and sugar. Put the milk in a tin pail and set it in boiling water; pour in the eggs and sugar and stir it until it thickens. TAPIOCA CREAM. Mrs. Bendelar5. One teacup of tapioca, soaked over night in a pint of milk; in the morning add one quart more, and when boiled till the tapioca is clear add the yolks of three eggs, well beaten, with one cup of sugar. Beat the whites to a foam. Sweeten and flavour the whole with vaniila. This pudding is better eaten cold.
