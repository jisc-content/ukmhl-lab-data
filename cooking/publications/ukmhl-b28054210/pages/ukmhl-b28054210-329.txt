VALUABLE RECIPES. 323 : Take one cup of brown sugar, butter size of wal- nut, one cup flour, one cup chopped figs, one-half cup sweet milk, one egg, one teaspoon baking powder; when done place the fig cake between the light cake, with a little frosting. SPONGE JELLY CAKE. 4- Mrs. Carson. Three eggs beaten separate, one cup sugar, one cup flour, two teaspoons baking powder put into the flour, and three tablespoons boiling water. Mix all together and cook in jelly tins in a quick oven ; place either jelly or chocolate frosting between the cakes. CHOCOLATE FROSTING. Mrs. Carson. One-half cup chocolate grated, one cup sugar, yolk of one egg, small cup half full of sweet milk; put on stove and stir while cooking till it will candy when dropped in cold water.
