Six oranges, juice of all, and grated peel of three, two lemons, the juice only, one pint of sugar, dissolved in one pint of water; prepare and freeze as you would lemon ice. ICE CREAM. M. One pint milk, yolks of two eggs, six ounces sugar, one tablespoon corn starch; scald until it thickens, when cool, add one pint whipped cream and the whites of two eggs, beaten stiff; sweeten, flavour, and freeze. ICE CREAM. Mrs. W. H. Ovington. Scald one quart of milk with one sheet of isinglass (broken), and a vanilla bean; when cool, strain, mix with one pint of cream whipped to a froth; sweeten to taste and freeze. \
