THE ART OF LIVING IN AUSTRALIA. 362 ITALIAN CABBAGE. 1 Cabbage ..... 2 d. 1 oz. Butter ..... Id. 2 oz. Dry Cheese 1 spoonful Flour Pepper and Salt Total Cost—4cd. Time—15 Minutes. Boil the cabbage by directions given, strain away the water and press it very dry. Put the butter into a saucepan, and when it is dissolved, chop up the cabbage and put a layer at the bottom of the saucepan. Sprinkle over some grated cheese, pepper, and salt, then more cabbage and cheese, until all are used up. Simmer gently for fifteen minutes, slip it on to a hot dish, and serve. SAVOURY POTATOES. 5 or 6 Large Potatoes 2 oz. Cheese 1 spoonful of Milk 1 Egg Pepper and Salt Total Cost—2>\d. Time—Two Hours. Scrub the potatoes and bake them in the oven. Cut off the end, scoop out all the meal; grate up some dry pieces of cheese, beat it into the potatoes with the yolk of the egg, and some seasoning. Whip the white till stiff and stir lightly in ; fill the potatoes with this mixture. Lay in a baking sheet and bake for about twenty minutes. Garnish with parsley, and serve. CAULIFLOWER FRITTERS.
