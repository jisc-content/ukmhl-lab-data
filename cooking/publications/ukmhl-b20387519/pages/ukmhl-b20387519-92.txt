Ingredients.—1 oz. beets, 1 oz. parsley, \ oz. leeks, \ lb. bread crumbs, 2 ozs. butter, 5 eggs, 1 oz. tapioca. Rub the butter and a dessertspoonful of flour into the bread crumbs; add the herbs chopped, seasou with pepper, salt, and a little mace ; mix all together with two eggs well beaten ; roll it into rather small balls and lay them in a pie-dish. Steep the tapioca ten minutes in half a pint of water; pour it over the balls; add three eggs, boiled and cut in small pieces ; cover with paste, and bake it. It may be used either hot or cold. Baked Mushrooms. Ingredients.—20 mushrooms, 1 oz. butter, pepper, salt. The button mushrooms should not be used for this dish. Choose good-sized ones, cut off the stalks, peel the tops, and wipe them. Lay them in a tin baking-dish, putting a small bit of but- ter into each, and sprinkling them with pepper
