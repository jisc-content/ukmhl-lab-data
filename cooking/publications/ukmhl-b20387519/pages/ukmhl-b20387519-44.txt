4o PRACTICAL VEGETARIAN RECITES. Swiss Sandwiches. Ingredients.—2 ozs. Gruyere cheese, 1 oz. butter, a little cream, mustard. Pound all the ingredients well together in a mortar, spread on thin slices of bread and cut into three-cornered sandwiches. Polenta Sandwiches. Ingredients.—2 ozs. polenta, 1 oz. semolina boiled together with one onion cut up, 2 ozs. grated Parmesan, \ oz. butter, pepper and salt. Boil rather stiff and turn out on a plate to get cold. Then work in the grated cheese with a small piece of butter, and press smoothly into a pot. Make into sandwiches as in previous recipes. Curry Sandwiches. Ingredients.—3 hard-boiled eggs, 3 ozs. butter, a tea- spoonful of salt, a teaspoonful of Worcester Sauce, a tea- spoonful of curry powder, a tablespoonful of bread crumbs. Pound the whole to a smooth paste, until tho- roughly mixed, and moisten with a little tarragon vinegar. Spread the mixture between thin slices of buttered bread and serve. Bahette Sandwiches.
