Macaroni Soup. Ingredients.— I lb. Naples macaroni, 1 quart mush- rooms, 2 turnips, 1 onion, 3 Jerusalem artichokes, 1 dessert- spoonful potato flour, \ pint cream, 4 ozs. bread crumbs. Boil the macaroni in two quarts of water, with a teaspoonful of salt, and a small piece of butter ; when tender, drain the water from it; wash it m fresh water; lay it in a clean cloth for a short time, and cut it in pieces about an inch in length. Wash the mushrooms without paring them, or cutting off the stems; put them in a pan with three quarts of water, the turnips, onions, arti- chokes, bread crumbs, mace, pepper, and salt; boil them till the vegetables are perfectly soft; rub them through a sieve ; return the soup into a pan; put in the macaroni; set it on the fire again ; stir in the potato flour, mixed with a little cold water till quite smooth; add a little cayenne pepper and salt as required, and when it has boiled ten minutes add the cream.
