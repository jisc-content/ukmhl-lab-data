PEACTICAL VEGETARIAN RECIPES. BEEAKFASTS. PORRIDGES. Oatmeal Porridge. Set on the fire a pan containing a quart of cold water; add to this one teaspoonful of salt, and when it comes to the boil strew into it, slowly and carefully, about four tablespoonfuls of oatmeal. If the oatmeal is very fine rather less will be required. It should be allowed to dribble through the fingers so as not to form lumps, and the porridge must be carefully stirred with a stick or wooden spoon.
