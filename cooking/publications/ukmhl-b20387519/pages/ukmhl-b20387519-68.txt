64 PRACTICAL VEGETARIAN RECIPES. Carrot Marmalade. Ingredients.—2 lbs. carrots, 2 lbs. sugar, 2 large lemons, •| oz. tartaric acid. Scrape or pare the carrots, then weigh them, allowing an equal weight of bruised loaf sugar; boil the carrots till perfectly tender, mash them quite fine, and rub them through a hair sieve. Boil the pulp half an hour, adding the sugar, and squeeze thoroughly the juice from lemons, and when taken from the fire stir in the tartaric acid. Vegetable Marrow Marmalade. Ingredients.—6 lbs. of grated marrow, 6 lbs. of broken ■ loaf sugar, the juice and grated rinds of 2 lemons. Peel the marrows and grate them, then weigh six pounds; add the broken loaf sugar, the lemon juice, and grated rinds of lemon ; boil it half an hour, stirring it frequently, and pour it into small moulds. Pineapple Marmalade. Ingbedients.—1 quart sliced pineapple, 1 lb. powdered sugar. Pare and cut into small slices enough pineapple to measure one quart, add the powdered sugar; boil half an hour, and pour into sweetmeat pots.
