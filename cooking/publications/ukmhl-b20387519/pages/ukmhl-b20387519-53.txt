LUNCHEONS. 49 Gooseberry Custards. Ingeedebnts.—8 pints green gooseberries, \ lb. sugar, 4 eggs, 2 tablespoonfuls orange-flower water. Set the gooseberries in cold water over a slow fire, and simmer till soft; then drain the water away and rub tbem through a sieve. To a pint of pulp add the eggs, the sugar, and orange-flower water. Set it over a fire, stirring constantly till it becomes thick, and when cold serve in custard glasses. Lemon Custards. iNOEEDrENTS—8 eggs, 6 ozs. sugar, 2 lemons, 1 teacupful cream, 1 pint boiling water, 2 tablespoonfuls of orange- flower water. Beat the yolks of eggs till quite frothy; pour on them the boiling water, stirring quickly all the time ; add the sugar, and the rind of the lemons grated; stir it over a slow fire till thick, adding the cream and orange-flower water. When hot stir in the lemon juice; pour it into a basin; stir till nearly cold, and serve in custard glasses. Baked Custards. Ingeedients.—1 pint cream, 4 eggs, cinnamon, almond flavour, 3 ozs. sugar. Bod the cream with a piece of cinnamon ; pour it into a basin, and when cold add the eggs, well beaten and strained, the sugar powdered, and a few drops of almond flavour. Bake in small cups m a cool oven.
