Fry it in fritters nicely browned on both sides, and serve with sifted sugar over. Vegetable Sausages. Ingredients.—3 carrots, 4 onions, 2 parsnips, \ pint split red lentils, 2 eggs, \ pound bread crumbs, sago, parsley, garlic, salt and pepper. Boil the vegetables and mash fine. Cook the lentils until soft, pound them well, and add to the mashed vegetables, together with a little boiled sago, chopped parsley, a small portion of finely- chopped garlic, salt and pepper. Mix the eggs and bread crumbs, and add a portion to the other ingredients. Make up into pieces about the size of ordinary sausages, roll in egg and bread crumbs, and fry in boiling oil until browned.
