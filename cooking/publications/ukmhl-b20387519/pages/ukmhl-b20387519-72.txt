Wash and pick the beans, put them in an earth- enware dish with three quarts of boiling water, cover, and let them simmer in the oven four hours. When they have been in the oven two hours and a half add the tapioca, and about half an hour before they are done add the parsley and thyme; season with pepper and salt, and just at the last stir in a little butter.
