Ingredients.—1 pint of mushrooms, \ pint of bread crumbs, 2 ozs. butter. Kub the butter into the bread crumbs, adding pepper and salt, and as much as will just moisten the bread; add the mushrooms cut in pieces ; line a basin with paste; put in the mixture ; cover with paste, tie a cloth over, and boil an hour and a half. It is equally good when baked. Green Bean Pudding. Ingredients.—1 quart of full-grown mealy green beans, 2 tablespoonfuls of cream, 2 yolks of eggs. Boil the beans till quite tender ; peel and mash them fine with a little pepper and salt till quite smooth; add the cream and the yolks of eggs, well beaten. Boil it an hour in a basin that will just hold it, and serve with parsley sauce poured over it.
