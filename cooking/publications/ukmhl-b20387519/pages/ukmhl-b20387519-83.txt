Ingredients.—1 pint beans, 3 ozs. butter, juice of 1 lemon, 1 oz. parsley. Steep the beans two hours in cold water, drain, and set them on the fire in two quarts of cold water, adding a saltspoonful of salt, and one ounce of butter. When they boil simmer them slowly two hours or more ; put them into a stew- pan with a little pepper, salt, chopped parsley, two ounces of butter, and the lemon juice; set them on the fire a few minutes and stir them well.
