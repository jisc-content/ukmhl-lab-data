DINNERS. Chestnut Soup. Ingredients.— \ lb. chestnuts, 1 onion, 1 stick celery, \ pint milk, 4 sprigs parsley. Boil the chestnuts just long enough for the rind and inner skin to be easily taken off, and when mashed put the chestnuts into a pan with the onion and celery, cut in very small pieces, and a pint of water, and let all boil together till suffi- ciently done ; then add the milk, the parsley finely chopped, and season with pepper and salt. Dried Green Peas Soup. Ingredients.—lj pints dried green peas, \ pint boiled spinach, 1 lettuce, 4 ozs. butter, 1 teaspoonful flour. After steeping the peas in soft water twelve hours, set them on the fire in a quart of boiling soft water, with a teaspoonful of salt, a piece of soda the size of a large pea, and two ounces of butter; simmer gently till the peas are perfectly soft, and rub them through a fine colander or wire sieve; add the lettuce and boiled spinach j put all together into the pan, with two quarts of boiling water; simmer till nearly ready; take out the lettuce and add some heads of asparagus, or a few leaves and young stalks of spinach, cut in very small pieces; stir in a spoonful of flour, mixed with two ounces of butter; season with pepper and salt, add a piece of sugar the size of a walnut, and boil the soup twenty minutes.
