The materials should be all ready, viz., the pans buttered, the flour and sugar sift- ed, the lemon grated, strained, etc., so that no time will be lost in mixing them together and getting them quick- ly into the oven. For robust persons a sponge cake is often covered with a wafer thickness of icing, made by stirring a heap- ing cupful of pulverized sugar into the white of an egg (not previously beaten), and flavored with lemon, va- nilla, or rum, etc.
