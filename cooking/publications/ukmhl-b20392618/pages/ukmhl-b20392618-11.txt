PEEFAOE All the works on Cookery hitherto published are full of Recipes to gratify the taste and provoke the appetite of persons in the enjoy- ment of good health; but few of the recipes contained in them are intended for those who, unfortunately, do not enjoy that blessing. It is for such persons that this little work has been written; and both the simplicity and the economy of its arrangements will, it is hoped, place it within the reach of all classes of society.
