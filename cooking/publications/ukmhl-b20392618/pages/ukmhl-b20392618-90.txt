66 THE invalid's OWN BOOK. It is said an egg is equal in nourishment to a quarter of a pound of meat. —♦ POACHED EGGS. Boil water with a very little vinegar in it. When you break the egg keep it together neatly in the shell before you turn it into the pan. It takes about two minutes to poach. Lay it upon a piece of toasted bread, and serve it hot.
