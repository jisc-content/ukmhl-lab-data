THE invalid's OWN BOOK. 4 DANDELION AND PARSLEY TEA. Wash and scrape six roots of dandelion, and six of parsley ; add a pint of boiling water; set it by the fire to infuse for three hours. Add a pinch of salt to render it more palatable ; or, what is more efficacious, a little saltpetre (nitre). This is an excellent remedy in dropsy, &c., acting powerfully on the kidneys.
