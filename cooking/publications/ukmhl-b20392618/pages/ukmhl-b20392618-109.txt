I FISH. 85 A FISH PUDDING. Pound some cold fish in a mortar, with a few spoonfuls of broth, till it is in a paste. Add a small lump of butter, and some crumbs of grated bread. Mix it with the yolks of four eggs, well beaten, and strain it through a sieve. Put it into a basin, with the whites of the egg^ well beaten. Put it into a souffle dish, or mould, into an oven, and bake it. O 3
