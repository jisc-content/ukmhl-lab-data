C 2 EMULSIONS AND DRINKS OF A MORE NUTRITIOUS NATURE. MILK SODA WATER. Half fill a tumbler with milk, and pour upon it soda water. —+— STRENGTHENING DRINK. Boil a tea-spoonful of pearl barley in two pints of water, changing the water several times. When it becomes clear, add a little cinnamon or nutmeg, and some lemon-peel cut very thin. Strain it' and add a small quantity of milk. If approved, sweeten with sugar or honey. ORGEAT.
