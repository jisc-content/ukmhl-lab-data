For a small quantity use the following: % Cup of sugar. 1 Teaspoon of vanilla. 1 Tablespoon of brandy. 1 pint of scalded sweet cream. Mix and freeze. The whites of two eggs beaten stiff is a valuable addition to this cream. Heat the milk until it boils; meanwhile mix the flour, sugar, and salt in a little cold water, and when the milk reaches the boiling-point pour it in; stir it for a minute over the fire in a saucepan, and then turn it into a double boiler and cook it for twenty minutes.
