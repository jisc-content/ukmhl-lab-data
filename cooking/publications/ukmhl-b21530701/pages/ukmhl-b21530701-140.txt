PREPARATORY REMARKS ON MUTTON. .c The finest mutton is that of the mountain, or black- faced sheep of Scotland, and that of the South Downs and Welsh sheep. Sheep are in perfection from the age of four to five. In May and June, or just before shearing,mut- ton tastes strongly of the coat, or what is called woolly.
