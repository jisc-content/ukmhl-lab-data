PREFACE. This Volume contains a Complete System of Practical Cookery, so carefully adapted to the purposes of every-day life, that although a person, totally unacquainted with the art here treated of, may not be able at first to dress a dinner, merely by referring to this book, any cook or housekeep- er, possessed of ordinary experience, will certain- ly have the means of knowing, without further help than what these pages afford, how to pre- pare, in the best manner, every dish at present in general use.
