Serve them with melted butter and a little lemon pickle in it, or a brown sauce thickened. Garnish with cut lemon. To Dress a Breast of Lamb. Cut it into pieces, and stew it in weak stock, with a glass of white wine ; add pepper and salt. When it is perfectly tender, thicken the sauce with butter and flour. Have cucumbers stewed in gravy ready to put over the lamb before serving. A breast of mutton may be served up in the same way.
