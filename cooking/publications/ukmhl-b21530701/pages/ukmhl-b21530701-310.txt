Another way to make Plum Pudding. Half a pound of raisins stoned, the same of currants cleaned, six ounces of minced suet, three table-spoonfuls of flour, the same of grated bread, five ounces of brown sugar, eight well-beaten eggs, three cloves pounded, half a tea-spoonful of Jamaica pepper ground, and a small grated nutmeg, a little salt, and a glass of brandy ; mix it all extremely well, and boil it for three or four hours, Serve with wine and sugar sauce.
