Cut into joints a hot roasted fowl, sprinkle over it of pepper and salt a dessert spoonful each, and two or three onions cut small, pour on it nearly a pint of boiling water, cover it with another dish, and let it stand till cold; a cold fowl may be prepared in the same way, and put into a sauce-pan for a few minutes to heat. Poulet a la Frarujaise. Cut a chicken up the back, fill it with thick slices of fat bacon, and put a slice of bread over the bacon, sew it up, and tie it round with a bit of pack thread, and then roast it.
