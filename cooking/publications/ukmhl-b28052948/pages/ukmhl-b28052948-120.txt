BUTTERED CRAB. Pick the meat from the inside of a medium-sized crab, add to it some bread-crumbs, chopped parsley, cayenne, and pieces of butter; put it again into the shell, sprinkle it with lemon-juice, strew it over with bread-crumbs, and bake it. Serve it quite hot. BUTTERED SHRIMPS. Put some pickled shrimps into a saucepan with a good piece of fresh butter, and enough eggs beaten up in white wine to thicken with. Carefully turn them one way while cooking them, and when quite hot servo upon sippets of toast.
