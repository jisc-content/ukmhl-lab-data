Forcemeat as for Turtle, at the Bush, Bristol. A pound of fine fresh suet, one ounce of ready dressed veal or chicken, chopped fine, crumbs of bread, a little shalot, or onion, salt, white pepper, nutmeg, mace,penny- royal, parsley, and lemon-thyme finely shred; beat as many fresh eggs, yolks and whites separately, as will make the above ingredients into a moist paste; roll into small balls, and boil them in fresh lard, putting them in just as it boils up.
