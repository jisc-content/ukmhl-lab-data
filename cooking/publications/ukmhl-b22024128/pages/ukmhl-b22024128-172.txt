Pick, singe, and wash the birds nicely: then dry them; and season, inside and out, pretty high, w'ith pepper, mace, nutmeg, allspice, and salt. Pack them in as small a pot as will hold them, cover them with but- ter, and bake in a very slow oven. When cold, take off the butter, dry them from the gravy, and put one bird into each pot, which should just fit. Add as much more butter as will cover them, but take care that it does not oil.
