Boil some spice in a little water till the flavour is gained, then add an equal quantity of port, some sugar and nutmeg ; boil together, and serve with toast. Another way.—Boil a bit of cinnamon and some grated nutmeg a few minutes, in a large tea-cupful of water; then pour to it a pint of port wine, and add sugar to your taste: beat it up and it will be ready.
