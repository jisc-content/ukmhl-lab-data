Chop the bone of a knuckle of veal small; put it in a stewpan with a quart of good stock, 2 large onions, 1 teacupful of rice, 1 tablespoonful of curry powder; stew from 4 to 5 hours. 46.—Curried Soup. Fry some chopped onion in butter, toss it up with some curry powder, add some stock or milk, pass it through a hair sieve, put it back on the fire with some 1 reviously boiled rice, boil up once, and serve.
