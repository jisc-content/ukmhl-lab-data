Make exactly as the coffee cream ice, simply using 6 ounces of chocolate boiled in water or milk instead of the coffee. 388.—Vanilla Cream Ice. Make exactly like the coffee ice, but leave out the coffee; use an extra half-pint of milk and some .essence of vanilla. 389. —Fruit Cream Ice. Pound any fruit liked with sifted sugar; rub it through a sieve; add cream. Put it in a mould and freeze. 390. —Parfait au Cafe. Boil 1 pint of milk; take it oft’ the fire, and stir in 1 pint of strong coffee. Now put in a saucepan 8 yolks of eggs and 10 ounces of sugar. Stir the milk in. Place the saucepan on the fire, and whisk the mixture until it is just on the boil; then take it off the fire, let it become cold, and strain it. Put it in a mould and freeze it. When half frozen, stir in a wine-glassful of curagoa or rum; finish freezing it. Serve immediately on a d’oyley.
