When yeast is used and not baking powder, a medium coarse oat- meal may be added to the wheat meal instead of fine oatmeal, which is necessary in the foregoing receipt. The butter and milk supply fatty matter in which the wheat is somewhat deficient; all the saline and mineral matters of the husk are retained ; and thus a more nutritive form of bread cannot be made, combined with a desirable proportion of inert matter.
