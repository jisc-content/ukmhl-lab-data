Keep them in boxes with sheets of paper between each layer. Lemon-biscuits may be made in the same way. 760. White Currant Jelly. Bruise the currants with the back of a wooden spoon, and run the juice through a jelly bag; to every pint of juice take a pound of double-refined sugar; clarify, and boil it to candy ; then put in the currant juice ; boil it ten minutes ; skim well, pass through a fine sieve, and pot it. 761. Black Currant Jelly.
