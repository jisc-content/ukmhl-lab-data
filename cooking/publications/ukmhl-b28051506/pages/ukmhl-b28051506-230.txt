Put into a sweetmeat-pan five ounces of sugar and five eggs ; mix as for a biscuit; add a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds beaten, and the same quantity of flour; butter a baking-sheet, roll the paste the thickness of a crown-piece, lay it on the sheet, and bake of a fine colour in a quick oven.
