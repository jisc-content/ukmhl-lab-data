Laver Is usually bought prepared in pots, and then merely re- quires heating over a lamp and a squeeze of lemon added to it. Serve over a lamp that it may be very hot. If you pick it fresh by the seaside it requires most careful washing in many waters to get rid of the sand. Salt water is best, if you can get it quite clear, to wash it in. It should then be slowly stewed for many hours in weak veal broth till it is quite a pulp; add more broth if it gets too dry. 414. Grilled Mushrooms. Choose large fresh mushrooms ; skin them and remove the stalks; lay them on a dish with a little fine oil, pepper, and salt over them ; let them stand an hour, and then broil on a gridiron over a clear sharp fire.
