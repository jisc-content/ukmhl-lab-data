Take out the crop, and with the middle finger loosen the liver and other parts of the breast end. 4. Cut off the vent. 5. Draw the fowl clean. 6. Wash out and wipe the inside quite dry. 7. Beat the breastbone flat with a rolling-pin. 8. Put a skewer in the first joint of the pinion, and bring the middle of the leg close to it. g. Put the skewer through the middle of the leg and through the body, and do the same on the other side. 10. Put another skewer in the small of the leg and through the sides- man, and do the same on the other side. 11. Put another through the skin of the feet, and cut the nails off. 12.
