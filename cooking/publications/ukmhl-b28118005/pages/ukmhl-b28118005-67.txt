Then put in the steak and fry to a nice brown over a quick fire. Stir in an ounce of flour and let it get slightly browned. Season with salt and pepper, and half a teaspoonful of ground ginger. Moisten with a pint of good brown stock ; bring it to the boil and skim well. Now put in six ounces of macaroni or spaghetti (a species of very small macaroni) ; these must be previously blanched in salted water and drained; put in also the carrots. Cover the casserole and let the whole simmer slowly for about an hour or a little less, till the meat, vegetables and maca- roni are quite tender. Before serving, skim off the fat from the sauce.
