Take up, drain, dish up and garnish with slices of lemon and parsley, and serve hot. Soles a la Parlskmie. Skin and fillet two small soles, season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice, and roll up each fillet. Melt half an ounce of butter in the chafing dish, place in the fillets of sole, sprinkle over a small finely minced shallot and a dessert-spoonful of finely chopped parsley. Moisten with half a glass of white wine, Chablis or Sauterne, also two table-spoonfuls of well reduced bechamel sauce ; then cover the dish and cook the fish slowly from 15 to 20 minutes, according to the size of the fillets.
