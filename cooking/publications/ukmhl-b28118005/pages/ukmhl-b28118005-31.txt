Cut some cold boiled or roast chicken, free from bone, skin and gristle, into small dice, season to taste and heat up in a little white sauce. Beat up six eggs with one table-spoonful of milk and season to taste with salt and pepper. Melt three-quarters of an ounce of butter in a chafing dish, pour in the egg mixture and stir over a quick fire till the eggs begin to set, then put the chicken mixture in the centre. Fold in the sides and shape neatly (oval cushion shape), allow the omelet to take colour, and serve. mutton Cutlets a la minute.
