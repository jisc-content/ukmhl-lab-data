As soon as it boils skim it well, and then sim- mer it an hour and a half: then take the best end of the mutton, cut it into pieces (two bones in each), take some of the fat off, and put as many as you think pro- per : skim the moment the fresh meat boils up, aud every quarter of an hour afterwards.
