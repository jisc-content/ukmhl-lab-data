LEMON CHEESE-CAKES. {Christ-Church-College Receipt.) Rasp the rind of a large lemon with four ounces of fine sugar, then crush, and mix it with the yolks of three eggs, and half the quantity of whites, well- whisked; beat these together thoroughly ; add to them flour tablespoonsful of cream, a quarter of a pound of oiled butter, the strained juice of the lemon,—which must be stirred quickly in by degrees,—and a little orange-flower brandy. Line small patty-pans with thin puff-paste, half fill them with the mixture, and bake them thirty minutes in a moderate oven.
