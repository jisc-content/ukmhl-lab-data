A plainer sort. Turn three quarts of milk to curd, break it, and drain the whey: when dry, break it in a pan, with two ounces of butler, till perfectly smooth ; put to it a pint and a half of thin cream, or good milk, and add sugar, cin- namon, nutmeg, and three ounces of currants.
