Clean half a pound of small eels, and set them on with three pints of water, some parsley, one slice of onion, a few pepper-corns; let them simmer till the eels are bro- ken, and the broth good. Add salt, and strain it off. The above should make three-half-pints of broth. Tench Broth. Make as eel-broth above. They are both very nutritious, and light of digestion.
