Three hours will bake it. Very good common Plum Cakes. Mix five ounces of butter in 3 lbs. of dry flour, and five ounces of fine Lisbon sugar ; add six ounces of currants, washed and dried, and some pimento finely powdered. Put 3 spoonfuls of yeast into a Winchester pint of new milk warmed, and mix into a light dough with the above. Make it into twelve cakes, and bake on a floured tin half an hour. Little Plum Cakes, to keep long.
