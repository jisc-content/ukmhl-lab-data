Beef d-la-vingrette. Cut a slice of underdone boiled beef three inches thick, and a little fat; stew it in half a pint of water, a glass of white wine, a bunch of sweet herbs, an onion, and a bay leaf; season it with three cloves pounded, and pepper, till the liquor is nearly wasted away, turning it once.
