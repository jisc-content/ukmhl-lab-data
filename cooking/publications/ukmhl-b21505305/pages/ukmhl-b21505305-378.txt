Hungary Water. To one pint of highly rectified spirit of wine, put an ounce of oil of rosemary, and two drams of essence of ambergris ; shake the bottle well several times, then let the cork remain out twenty-four hours. After a month, during which time shake it daily, put the water into small bottles. Honey Water. Take a pint of spirit as above, and three drams of essence of ambergris; shake them well daily.
