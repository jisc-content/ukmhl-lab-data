Orange Fritters. Peel two oranges, removing all the white part, and cut them into rounds across, five slices each; set them in a teacup with a tablespoonful of brandy and a little powdered sugar; let them remain an hour. Then make a batter of a quarter of a pound of flour, a pinch of salt, one ounce of butter, and a tablespoonful of the juice and the brandy from the orange, a teaspoonful of the orange rind scraped, and a tablespoonful of white sugar; beat up the yelks of two eggs and the white of one to a froth separately; put in the yelks, and beat the batter for a quarter of an hour; add the wdiite froth, and beat for another quarter of an hour.
