Stewed Apples and Rice Pare the apples, and place them either in the oven or over the fire, putting with them a slice of lemon-peel and sugar to taste, and enough water to cover them. Have ready the boiled rice, make a wall of it round the dish, and place the apples in the centre. A little nutmeg may be grated over if liked. 308. Stewed Normandy Pippins When there is a scarcity of fresh fruit, one is glad to turn to tin or dried for a sweet. Nor- mandy pippins make either a sweet or a dessert dish. They need soaking over night, and putting into a stewpan with enough water to cover them, sugar, and a slice of lemon-peel.
