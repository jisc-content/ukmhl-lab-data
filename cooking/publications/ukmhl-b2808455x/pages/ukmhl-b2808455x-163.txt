Dough Nuts Put six ounces of flour in a basin, and well mix with it a teaspoonful of baking powder; then rub in very thoroughly and finely two ounces of butter, and two tablespoonsful of castor sugar. Beat two eggs together with two tablespoonsful of milk. Pour them into the centre of the other ingredients, making all into a paste. Turn it out on to a floured board, knead very lightly, and form into small balls about the size of an egg. Make a hole in the centre of each, put in a teaspoonful of jam and close up the hole again, and drop the nuts into boiling fat for five minutes. Put them out on kitchen paper, and roll in sifted or crystallised sugar. 332. Cocoanut Buns Rub two ounces of butter into half a pound of flour, add one teaspoonful of baking powder, a quarter of a pound of castor sugar, and a quarter of a pound of dessicated cocoanut.
