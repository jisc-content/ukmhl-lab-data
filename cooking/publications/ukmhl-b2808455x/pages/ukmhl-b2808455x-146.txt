Baked Custard Pudding Allow six eggs to each pint of milk. Beat the yolks and whites separately. Then beat together, adding a slight dusting of flour or cornflour, and any flavouring liked. Line the pie-dish with a nice fight crust. Introduce the custard, and grate thereon some nutmeg. Cover with small pieces of butter. Place in the oven, and cook very slowly, and serve cold. 292.
