Merlans au gratin are breadcrumbed and fried with their tails in their mouths. d la Frangaise—dipped in milk, floured and fried. Served with anchovy or any other sauce. Filets de merlans a la Hollandaise — fillets served with Hollandaise sauce. d Vltalienne are served with Italian sauce. d la Orly are floured, fried, and served with tomato sauce. WHITING POLLOCK. Colin frit is cut in slices, fried, and served with oil and lemon.
