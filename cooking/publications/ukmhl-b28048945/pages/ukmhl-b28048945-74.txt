Poulet d la Princesse is a cold entree prepared with fillets of chickens cut in oval slices, coated with a sauce of aspic and cream, set in aspic and cut out; served on an aspic border, with salad. Salade de filets de volaille a la Brunow—small round slices of fowl and slices of cucumber stewed with white sauce, aspic and peas. Cut in square pieces, and dressed on a salad border, with white tartare sauce. Blanc de volaille aux concombres — slices from the breast of a roast fowl, with pieces of cucumber, white sauce, cream, &c. Served with a dressed vegetable in the centre and white sauce.
