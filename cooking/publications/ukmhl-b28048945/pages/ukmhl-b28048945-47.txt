Grenadine de filet de bceuf d la Financiere— cut in slices, larded, cooked in mirepoix, and served in the sauce with Financiere garnish. Tournedos d la sauce poivrade—slices of cooked fillet dressed in a crown alternately with fried slices of bread of the same size. Poivrade sauce in the centre. This may be served with olives, or French beans, instead of the Poivrade sauce; and would then be called Tour- nedos aux olives, or aux haricots verts. Slices of the fillet served with Piquantc, Ilollandaise, or other sauce, are called after the sauce with which they are served.
