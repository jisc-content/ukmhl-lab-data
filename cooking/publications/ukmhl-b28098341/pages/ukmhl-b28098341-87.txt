When the fritters are done, serve with spiced sugar apart. SWEETMEAT PUDDING. Take a small tart-dish, line it with a thin puff- paste; slice one ounce each of candied lemon, orange, and citron peel; lay them in the dish and pour over them the beaten yolks of six eggs, and the whites of three, a quarter of a pound of sugar, six ounces of fresh butter melted, and the juice of two sweet oranges. Bake it in a moderately-heated oven for half an hour. BLANC MANGER.
