Take half a pound of fine Tafilat dates, stone them, and pound them to a paste ; add the juice of a lemon, a quarter of a pound of fresh butter, the beaten yolks of six eggs, a gill of brandy, four ounces of powdered and sifted loaf-sugar and two ounces of stale bread- crumbs. Thoroughly mix all together, put it into a buttered mould, and bake for twenty minutes in a moderate oven. BAKED CUSTARDS.
