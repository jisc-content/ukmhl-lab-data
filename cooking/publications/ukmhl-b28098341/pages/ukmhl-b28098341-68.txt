Beat the whites and yolks of four eggs; strain them, and add two large tablespoonsful of lemon- juice, four ounces of powdered loaf-sugar, the grated rind of one lemon, and a glass each of white wine and brandy. Mix all well together, and stir it in a small saucepan over the fire until it becomes suffi- ciently thick; then pour it into small cups, and serve. COWSLIP PUDDING.
