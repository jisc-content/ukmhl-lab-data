SIIELFORD PUDDING. AVell beat six eggs, and add them to one pound of suet, one pound of flour, three-quarters of a pound of currants, carefully cleaned, a little salt, some sugar, grated lemon-peel, and a teacupful of milk. Mix it well and boil i t in a pudding-shape for six hours. Serve with wine or brandy sauce.
