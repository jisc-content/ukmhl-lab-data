Take a quarter of a pound each of bread-crumbs, chopped suet, and cleaned currants, a little grated lemon-peel and ginger, and the yolks of four and the whites of two eggs: mix well together, and make into balls the size of a hen’s egg. Throw them into boiling water, and in twenty minutes they will be done. Serve with sweet sauce.
