Boil a pint of new milk, then mix two ounces of saloop powder with a dessert-spoonful of peach-water and two ounces of sugar : stir it into the milk; let it thicken over the fire; add two ounces of butter, and when it is cool, beat in it two eggs. Put it into a tart-dish edged with paste, and bake it half an hour. CHRISTMAS PUDDING. One pound of chopped beef-suet, one pound of stoned raisins, one pound of washed currants, half a pound of bread-crumbs, a quarter of a pound of sugar, half an ounce of mixed spice, a quarter of a pound of candied lemon and orange peel, a gill of brandy, a gill of cream, four eggs well beaten. Mix thoroughly and boil in a cloth for four hours. Serve with wine sauce or burnt brandy. NEW CABINET PUDDING.
