RICHMOND PUDDING. Take one pound of chopped suet, one pound of Mus- catel raisins, stemmed but not stoned, one glass of Malaga wine, four ounces of flour, four ounces of brown sugar, half a nutmeg, and three eggs : mix well, and boil in a cloth for three hours. THE SHREWSBURY PUDDING. Take half a pound of dressed and peeled beetroot; pound it in a mortar till quite smooth, add a quarter of a pound of fresh butter, six ounces of loaf-sugar in powder, the juice of two lemons, and the grated rind of one, two penny sponge-cakes, a glass of brandy, and three eggs : beat these ingredients perfectly well together; put them into a buttered mould dusted over with bread-crumbs, and either bake for forty minutes or boil it for an hour.
