The best tools are the cheapest in the end, and in skilled hands the best are the only right tools to use ; but one does not like to see fine tools blunted and carelessly used by the unthinking. So the decision as to which make of cooking stove you will adopt resolves itself into the question of whether you or your maids are to be its users. And here, having shown you the possibilities of the “ more excellent way,” I must leave you either to take it or to fall back upon the old conventions and suffer their drawbacks, if such is your election.
