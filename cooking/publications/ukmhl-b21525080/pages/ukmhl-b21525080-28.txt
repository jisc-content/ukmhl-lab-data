This is the second book of ‘ Countrey Contentments by Gervase Markham, who was born about 1568 and died in 1637. He has been called ‘ the earliest English hackney wTiter Other editions are 1623, 1631 (4), 1637 (5), 1648, 1649, 1656 (6), 1660 (7), 1664, 1668 (8), 1675, 1683 (9). An edition of 1653 was reprinted by Constance, Countess De La Warr, in 1907. The cookery recipes seem more modern than those of preceding books, although many of the dishes are obsolete, e. g. leach and jumbal.
