LEMON MOULD. 4 oz. leaf gelatine Rind of 3 lemons Juice of 3 lemons 4 pint cold water 3 eggs 4 lb. lump sugar Rub the sugar on the lemons to get off all the rind, put into a saucepan with the lemon juice, gelatine, and water, allow this to stand by a gentle heat until all is dissolved, let it cool slightly, then add to the eggs, which must be beaten; pass through a fine hair sieve or muslin; pour into a mould, and when firm dip in warm water and turn out.
