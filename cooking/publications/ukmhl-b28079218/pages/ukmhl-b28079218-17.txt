CURRIED COD. 1 Ib. cod 1 onion pint milk I pint gravy 1 tablespoonful lemon juice 3 oz. butter 1^ tablespoonful curry powder 1 dessert-spoonful sugar 1 teaspoonful salt 3 tablespoonfuls cocoanut Fry the fish in 2 oz. of the butter ; put the remaining 1 oz. of butter in a saucepan, and brown in it the onion ; add the curry, cocoanut, milk, gravy, sugar, salt; boil 15 minutes ; divide the fish into large flakes, add to curry, also the lemon juice, and boil five minutes. Serve with a dish of rice. 2 tablespoonfuls bread crumbs Clean, scrape, and wash the fish; mix all the above ingredi- ents together with the egg or a little milk; lay this inside the fish and sew up, to keep it in its place. Lay in a well- greased dripping tin, and put some bits of dripping on the top ; bake 20 to 30 minutes. When done, dish, and sprinkle over the top some brown bread crumbs. Serve with parsley sauce.
