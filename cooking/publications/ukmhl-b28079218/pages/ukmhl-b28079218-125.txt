SOUTHPOET SAUCE, 1 oz. butter I 1 oz. flour 4 pint water 2 medium apples A strip of lemon rind | 2 cloves 2 tablespoonfuls sugar | Peel, core, and cut in small quarters the apples, put into a pan with the water, sugar, cloves, and lemon rind; boil until soft, pass through a wire sieve, rubbing the pulp through with a wooden spoon; dissolve the butter with the flour, and add the pulp from the apples, stir until smooth; boil five minutes, and serve.
