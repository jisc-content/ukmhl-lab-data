STEWED BEEF STEAK. li to 21b. steak 1 onion 1 carrot 1 turnip ^ pint water 1| oz. butter or dripping 1 oz. flour A bunch of sweet herbs A sprig of parsley Put the butter into a pan, and when hot brown in it the steak, then the onion and flour ; add the water gradually, and when boiling add the vegetables, herbs, and steak ; simmer very slowly to 2 hours. When done, dish the steak and vegetables on the top, or the gravy may be strained over the steak, and garnished with fancy cut carrots and turnips, which should be boiled in separate pans containing boiling water, a little sugar and salt.
