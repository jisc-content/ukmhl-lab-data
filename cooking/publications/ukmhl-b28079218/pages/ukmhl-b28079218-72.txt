PUDDINGS. No. 149. APPLE PUDDING. ^ lb. apples 6 oz. butter Whites of 5 eggs ^ lb. castor sugar 2 lemon rinds, grated Peel, core, and weigh the apples, put them into an enamelled saucepan with one tablespoonful of water, and stew gently until soft; pass through a wire sieve, allow the apples to cool, beat the butter and sugar to a cream, add the pulp of the apples, the lemon rind, and the whites, whipped; pour into a small pie dish lined and decorated with rough puff pastry (page 84), made from 6 oz. flour; bake 30 to 40 minutes ; cover with castor sugar and serve hot or cold.
