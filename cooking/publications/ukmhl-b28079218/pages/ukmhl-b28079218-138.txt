128 No. 301. BREAD (Vienna). 3 lb. Vienna flour 1 good teaspoonful salt 1 oz. German yeast 3 oz. butter Enough milk to make into a dough Crumble the yeast into a basin, add to it one teaspoonful sugar and one teaspoonful flour, cover with lukewarm milk, cover and set in a warm place to rise ; rub the butter into the flour, add the salt, make a well in the centre, miY the yeast, and add with enough warm milk to make a dough ; knead this lightly with the hands until the dough leaves them quite clean; turn the smooth side uppermost, cut across, cover and set in a warm place, to rise, two hours; knead lightly, divide into small rolls, plaits, twists, or according to taste; set them on a greased tin in a warm place, to rise, 10 minutes; paint over with a little milk and sugar. Bake in a rather hot oven a light brown. No. 302.
