APPLE CHEESECAKES. i lb. apples 3 cloves 1 oz. butter Some scraps of short or puff pastry 3 oz. brown sugar 2 eggs 1 tablespoonful castor sugar Eoll out the pastry, cut in rounds, and line some small patty tins ; peel, core, and cut in quarters the apples, put into an enamelled pan with one tablespoonful of water and the 'cloves; stew until soft, add sugar and butter, when dis- solved rub all through a fine wire sieve ; beat up the eggs and add to apples; put a teaspoonful of this mixture into each patty case; bake 10 to 15 minutes.
