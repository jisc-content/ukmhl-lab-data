Vanilla Cream (Cold). 1 white of egg, 3 yolks, | pint milk, 1 table- spoonful of castor sugar, 1 pint good double cream, 1 oz. leaf gelatine, and | teaspoonful vanilla essence. Make a custard of the eggs, milk and sugar in the usual way. When cool stir in the cream, whipped to a froth, and the gelatine, previously dissolved. Pour this into a mould rinsed out in cold water. Place it on ice, or in a cool place, to set. Turn out, and serve. Chestnut Cream (Cold). Pound some boiled chestnuts, and pass them through a sieve. Mix in some whipped cream flavoured with some cura^oa syrup, and some gelatine previously dissolved in milk. Add sugar to taste. Pour into a mould and leave to set.
