Turn out and serve. Strawberry and Custard Pudding (Hot or Cold). I lb. strawberries, 2 tablespoonsful castor sugar, 3 eggs, and 1 pint of milk. Butter a piedish, lay in the strawberries, stalked and halved, at the bottom of this, dust with castor sugar. Beat up the eggs and milk, and strain this over the strawberries. Bake in the oven till the custard is set. Dust the top with castor sugar, and powdered cinnamon, if liked. Serve either hot or cold. Almond Custard (Cold).
