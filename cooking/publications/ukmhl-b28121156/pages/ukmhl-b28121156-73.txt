CUSTARDS AND CREAMS 69 Coffee Cream or Bavarois (Cold). I pint cream, 1 gill strong coffee, 1 gill milk, o yolks of egg, 1 white, and 2 oz, sugar. Make a custard with the coffee, milk, and eggs, and strain it into a basin. Melt the gelatine with a little coffee, strain it into the custard. Whip the cream, and stir this in ; when the cream is just beginning to set, turn it into the mould, and leave it to set.
