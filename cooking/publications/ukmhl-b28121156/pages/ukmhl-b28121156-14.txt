This is economical and useful for fruit pies. Sift together 4 oz. of flour and 2 oz. of castor sugar, and mix to a smooth paste with 2 well -beaten egg yolks, with a scrupulously clean wooden spoon. This mixture needs a hot oven. About 10 minutes before it is finished, take it out of the oven, brush it over with stiffly whipped white of egg, sprinkle with castor sugar, and return to the oven to finish.
