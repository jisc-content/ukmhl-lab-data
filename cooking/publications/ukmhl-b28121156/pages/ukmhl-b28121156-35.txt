Hasty Pudding (Hot). 2 oz. flour, 1 pint of milk, 1 egg, ^ oz. of butter, and a spoonful of jam. Boil the flour and milk together for a minute, then beat in the egg and butter, and flavouring to taste. Place the jam at the bottom of a piedish, pour in the mixture, and bake in a sharp oven till the pudding is browned. Saxony Pudding (Hot). 4 oz. of fine, sifted flour, 1 gill of milk, 4 oz. fresh butter, 2 oz. of sugar, vanilla flavouring, 5 egg yolks, and 4 whites.
