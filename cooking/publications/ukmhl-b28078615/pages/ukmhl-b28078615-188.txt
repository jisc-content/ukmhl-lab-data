GREEN GOOSEBERRY SAUCE. Boil some green gooseberries in water till soft, and sweeten them with brown sugar. PUDDING SAUCE. Mix with half a pint of melted butter two wine glasses of sherry, and a table-spoonful of pounded loaf sugar ; make it quite hot, and serve in a sauce-tureen, with grated nutmeg on the top. YANKEE PUDDING SAUCE. Beat together a large table-spoonful of fresh butter, four of pounded sugar, and a little grated nutmeg; add a dessert- spoonful of brandy, or sherry; heap it up upon a small dish, and grate a little nutmeg or orange-peel over the top.
