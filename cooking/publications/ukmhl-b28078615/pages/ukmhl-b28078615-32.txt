TOMATA SOUP. Wash, scrape, and cut small the red part of three large carrots, three heads of celery, four large onions, and two large turnips; put them into a sauce-pan, with a table- spoonful of butter, and half a pound of lean new ham ; let them stew very gently for an hour, then add three quarts of brown gravy soup, and some whole black pepper, with eight or ten ripe tomatas ; let it boil an hour and a half, and pulp it through a sieve; serve it wdth fried bread cut in dice.
