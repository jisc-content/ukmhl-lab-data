Sprinkle over it and the tongues of four pigs, a little common salt and a very little saltpetre. Let them lie four days, wash them, and tie them in a clean cloth. Boil them until the bones will come easily out of tiie head; take off the skin as whole as possible, place a bowl in hot water and put in the head, cutting it into small pieces.
