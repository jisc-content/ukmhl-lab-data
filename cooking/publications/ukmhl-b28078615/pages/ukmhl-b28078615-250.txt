Pare, core, and mince fourteen or fifteen French rennet apples; put them into a frying-pan, with sonie pounded loaf sugar, a little pounded cinnamon, grated lemon-peel, and two ounces and a half of fresh butter ; fry them a quarter of an hour over a quick fire, stirring them constantly. Butter a shape of the size the charlotte is intended to be ; cut strips of bread about the width of two fingers, and long enough to reach from the bottom to the rim of the shape, so that the whole be lined with bread ; dip each bit into melted butter, and then put a layer of the fried apples, and one of appricot jam or marmalade, and then one of bread dipped into butter; begin and finish with it.
