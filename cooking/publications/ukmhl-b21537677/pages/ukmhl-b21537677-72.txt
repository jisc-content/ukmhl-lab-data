Mix \ lb. of boiled rice with two tablespoonfuls of white sauce, the yolk of a raw egg, and a little nutmeg, pepper and salt. Stir this over the fire for a few minutes, then set it aside to cool. When it is cold take half a dessert- spoonful of it, spread it out hollow with a teaspoon, and put in a small quantity of any kind of cooked fish, cut into very small pieces, and seasoned. Cover it with the rice, and mould it into small shapes like quenelles ; dip them in beaten egg and bread crumbs, and fry in boiling fat, and serve with crisp parsley.
