Veal Cutlets a la Talleyrand. One and a half lb. veal cutlet, 2 shalots finely minced, 2 or 3 mushrooms finely minced, teaspoonful chopped pars- ley, 2 oz. butter, 1 bay leaf, salt and pepper, and a tiny blade of mace, 1 pint of white sauce, 1 yolk of egg. Cut the veal into rather large neat cutlets. Make the butter hot in a saucepan, fry the veal cutlets in it, with the shalots, parsley, bay leaf, mushrooms, mace, pepper and salt, for three minutes. Stir on to this the white sauce, and let the whole gently stew for twenty minutes. Remove the cutlets, and dish in a circle. Pass the sauce through a sieve, stir in it the yolk of egg, and pom* over. Put boiled peas or French beans in the centre, and sprinkle over all some hard-boiled egg which has been rubbed through a sieve. Veal Cutlets aux Concombres.
