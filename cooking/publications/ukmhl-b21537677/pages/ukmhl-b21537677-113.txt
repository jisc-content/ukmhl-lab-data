One pint of white sauce, a teaspoonful of lemon juice, a tablespoonful of finely-chopped parsley which has been scalded. Heat the sauce, add the lemon juice, and season with pepper and salt. Just before sending to the table stir in the parsley. N.B.—The sauce must not be allowed to boil after the parsley is added. R .aw Marinade—No. 1.
