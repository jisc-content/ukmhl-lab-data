Salmon Cutlets, Grilled or Baked. Cut the salmon in slices 1 inch thick, lay each slice in a strip of buttered paper, and grill or bake from 6 to 10 minutes. Serve with anchovy or caper sauce. Salmon Cutlets, Fried. Cut the slices 1 inch thick, slightly flour them, egg and breadcrumb, and fry 10 minutes in a bath of hot fat. Send to table dished on paper mats, garnished with fried parsley. Serve Remoulade sauce with them. See “ Sauces.”
