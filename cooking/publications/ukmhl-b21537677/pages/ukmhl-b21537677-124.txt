Two yolks of eggs, 1 white of egg, 1 oz. butter, 4 oz. grated Parmesan cheese, salt and cayenne, ^ lb. puff pastry. Mix in a basin the eggs, the butter melted, seasoning, and the cheese. Roll the pastry out very thin, spread the mixture on, fold over like a sandwich, mark in squares with the back of a knife, brush over with beaten-up egg, and bake in a quick oven.
