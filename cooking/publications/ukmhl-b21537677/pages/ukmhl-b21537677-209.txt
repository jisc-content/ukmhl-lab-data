Mix these with the butter and treacle into a stiff paste, adding a little milk if required. Roll out and shape in rounds. Bake in a brisk oven. Russian Cake. Two lb. of walnuts, 1 lb. sugar, 10 eggs, 1 large table- spoonful of manna, the grated rind of a lemon. Put the yolks of the eggs and the sugar into a large basin, and whisk for fifteen minutes. Peel and pound the walnuts, and mix them in; add the lemon rind. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth, and mix them in lightly. Butter a cake tin, and sprinkle it over with the manna. Porn* the mixture into it, and bake in a moderate oven about an horn*. Do not turn the cake out till cold. Manna is to be had from the chemist. Gold Cake.
