MISS CAMERON'S COOKERY BOOK. 29 Plain Pea Soup. One pint of split peas, 3 quarts of liquor from porlc or tongue, 2' tablespoonfuls of prepared peas powder, 1 carrot, 1 onion, and 1 stick of celery, \ teaspoonful of salt, a pinch of cayenne pepper. Soak the peas in cold water for twelve hours, then put them in a saucepan with the stock and vegetables; boil for two hours, and strain through a tammy; return the pulp to the saucepan, and add 1 carrot grated and the salt and pepper. Simmer another half hour, and serve with fried sippets of bread. Tomato Soup.
