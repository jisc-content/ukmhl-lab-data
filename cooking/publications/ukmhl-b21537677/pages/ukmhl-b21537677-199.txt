Four oz. butter, 4 oz. castor sugar, 5 oz. flour, 3 eggs, | teaspoonful essence of vanilla, some jam syrup, some cocolanka (desiccated cocoa-nut), 1 small teaspoonful baking powder. Rub the butter and sugar to a frothy cream, add the eggs and flour alternately, then the vanilla and baking powder ; whip well together, and half fill little plain dariole moulds, which have been buttered and dusted with sugar and flow in equal quantities. Bake in a moderate oven till a golden brown, turn out, and allow to cooL Then dip in the syrup, and roll in the cocolanka, stand them bottom upwards, and place on each a glace cherry, dipped in the syrup to make it stick.
