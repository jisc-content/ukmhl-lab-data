Marple Pudding. ^ lb. breadcrumbs. 2 eggs. Butter. A little lemon-rind, i pint milk, i tablespoonful sugar. Enough raisins to line mould. Butter a plain mould, and place the stoned and halved raisins round it. Mix the breadcrumbs and the milk (warm). Let these soak for a little while, then add the butter, sugar, lemon-rind, and eggs. Beat all well together; pour the mixture into the mould, cover closely, and steam for two hours. Apple Amber Pudding. 3 apples. 3 oz. sugar. The peel of half a lemon cut thin, i grating of nutmeg.
