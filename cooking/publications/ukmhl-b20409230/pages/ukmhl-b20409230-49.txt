Remove all fat, skin, etc., from a quarter of a pound of raw beef, mutton, game, or poultry; mince very finely, and sprinkle over it a little salt, and (if allowed) pepper. Put it in a jar, cover closely ; stand it in a saucepan of hot water, and keep the water simmering. When the meat is perfectly hot through it is done. Serve piled up on small rounds of toast.
