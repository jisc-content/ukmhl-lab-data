Put the bran into a jug, pour over it the boiling water; let it stand in a warm place for a quarter of an hour. Then strain through a hair-sieve or piece of muslin. Sweeten to taste, and add wine or lemon- juice, if allowed. White Wine Whey (Milk). i pint milk. i wineglass sherry or Madeira. 1 dessertspoonful castor sugar. Put the milk and sugar in a saucepan ; when it boils add the wine. The milk must be boiling when the wine is added, or it will not curdle. Let it stand a minute, then strain and serve very hot. It must be strained, as the curds are very indigestible. Treacle posset is made in the same way, leaving out the sugar and using the same amount of treacle as wine. White Wine Whey (Eggs). 2 yolks of eggs. i gill sherry or Madeira. i dessertspoonful castor sugar. Beat the yolks and sugar together; stir into the wine when it boils. All possets should be taken as hot as possible.
