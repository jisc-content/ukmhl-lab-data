TO MISS DAVENPORT HILL, m.l.s.b., WHOSE INTEREST IN THE WORK OF COOKERY FOR MANY YEARS, AS CHAIRMAN OF THE COOKERY COMMITTEE OF THE LONDON SCHOOL BOARD, ENDEARED HER TO ALL WITH WHOM SHE CAME IN CONTACT, THIS LITTLE WORK IS, WITH HER KIND PERMISSION, MOST RESPECTFULLY DEDICATED BY A GRATEFUL TEACHER.
