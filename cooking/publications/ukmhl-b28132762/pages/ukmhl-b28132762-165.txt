BREAD, SCONES AND CAKES. 157 1. Put the flour and baking powder into a basin, rub the butter well in, add the sugar and currants. 2. Beat up the eggs in a basin, add the milk, then pour it into the basin with the flour and mix well (do not beat it) with a fork. 3. Pour the mixture into a cake tin, place it in a hot oven, if possible do not open the oven door for the first twenty minutes, and bake for one and a half hours.
