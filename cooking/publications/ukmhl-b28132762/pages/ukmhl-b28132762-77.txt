69 MACARONI AND RICE. Baked Macaroni. (I). 1 breakfastcupful macaroni. 1 pint boiling stock or water, 1 good teaspoon butter or lard. 1 tablespoonful milk (if Ideal, a teaspoon mixed with water). 1 dessertspoonful of bread crumbs. 1 teaspoonful salt, i teaspoonful pepper. A pinch of red pepper. 1. Throw the broken macaroni into the boiling stock or water, add the salt and pepper and boil for half-an-hour or longer. 2. Empty it from the saucepan into the colander and drain off all the water. 3. Put the macaroni into a small pie dish, lightly mix in the butter and milk, sprinkle over with the bread crumbs and put in the oven for about ten minutes or a quarter of an hour, until the bread crumbs are just browned.
