Then add two onions sliced and browned in a little butter, two leeks, one large parsnip, two turnips, two small carrots, quarter of a cabbage, a stalk of celery, and a bay-leaf. The dish is improved by adding any remains of poultry or roast meat. It should not be boiled to rags, but cooked so slowly that it retains shape and flavor.
