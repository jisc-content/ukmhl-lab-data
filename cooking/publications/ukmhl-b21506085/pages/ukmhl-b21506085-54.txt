Then stir in the six beaten eggs; spread about an inch thick on an oiled platter, and allow to cool When cool, cut in strips an inch wide and three long; roll carefully in fine crumbs, dip in beaten egg, and then again in crumbs, and fry in boiling lard. Drain on brown paper, dust with powdered sugar, and serve hot. Very delicious.
