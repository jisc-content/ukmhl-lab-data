Savouries or Entremets Savoureux. These are commonly known as “After-Dinner Savouries” ; the dishes selected may be hot or cold, but they are more often served hot than cold. They must always be dressed in very small portions, and, above all, daintily. The most popular savouries are Cheese Savouries, Egg Savouries, and those made from Smoked Fish. ENTREMETS SUCRES.
