Pound the flesh of the whitings and the lobster coral together in a mortar, and rub them through a wire sieve Melt the butter in a stewpan. Mix in the flour and add the milk, stir and cook well. Beat the cream stiffly, mix it lightly in and pour into a prepared souffle tin. Covered with buttered paper and steam for half an hour. ►serve with a supreme sauce poured over.
