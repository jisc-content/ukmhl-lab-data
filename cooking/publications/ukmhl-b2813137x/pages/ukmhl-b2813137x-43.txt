Put the butter into a saucepan and melt it, add the vegetables, cleaned and cut in pieces, fry about five minutes, add the flour, spice and seasoning, the tomatoes cut in slices, also the ham ; lastly add the extract dissolved in a quart of warm water with the salt and pepper, stir until the soup boib, then allow it to simmer one hour, rub through a wire sieve, boil up again, and serve with fried bread.
