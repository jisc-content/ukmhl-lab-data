141 No. 349. PUNCTUAL PUDDING. INGREDIENTS. 2 ozs. of butter i 2 ozs. of Coombs’ flour 2 ozs. of castor sugar 2 eggs £ pint of milk A little flavouring METHOD. Beat the batter and sugar to a cream, beat in the eggs. Stir in the flour, and gradually add the milk. Add the flavouring, beat thoroughly, pour into a greased pie-dish, and bake in a quick oven 45 minutes. No. 350. VIENNOISE PUDDING. INGREDIENTS. a ozs. of stale bread cut into dice 3 ozs of sultanas £ lb. of castor sugar 2 ozs. of candied peel Grated rind of a lemon 1 wineglass of sherry V pint of milk 2 whole eggs 1 oz. of lump sugar METHOD. Put the one ounce of lump sugar into an old saucepan, and burn it to a dark brown. Pour in the milk, and stir until it is well-coloured, and the sugar dissolved. Beat the eggs well, strain the coloured milk on to them, and add the sherry. Put all the dry ingredients into a basin, and pour the eggs, milk and sherry over them. Let the pudding soak for half an hour. Then put it into a well-greased pint mould. Cover with buttered paper, and steam for one hour and a half. This pudding is to be served with German sauce.
