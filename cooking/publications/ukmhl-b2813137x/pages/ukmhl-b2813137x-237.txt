AUCHENCAIRN SCONES. INGREDIENTS. 1 lb. of Hour | 4 teaspoonful of salt 1 teaspoonful cream of tartar 1 teaspoonful of sugar 4 teaspoonful of carbonate of j 1 oz. of butter or lard soda J A little milk METHOD. M ix all the dry ingredients well together, then make into a stiff paste with the milk. Roll out to about half an inch in thickness, cut into small rounds and bake in a moderate oven about 20 minutes. N.B. In Scotland these are cooked over the fire upon a gridiron. No. 442. BUTTERMILK CAKES. INGREDIENTS. 1 quart of buttermilk | 1 teaspoonful carbonate of soda H ozs. of Hour j A pinch of salt METHOD. Put the flour and salt into a basin, add the soda to the buttermilk and mix into the flour, gradually, beat well, until quite smooth. Fry like pancakes and serve hot, with plenty of butter on them.
