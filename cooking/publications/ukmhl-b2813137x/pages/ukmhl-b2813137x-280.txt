Cut the meat into small pieces, break the bones. Put meat and bones into a saucepan with the water. As soon as it boils remove the scum, cover closely, and simmer for nearly three hours. Strain the broth into a basin, place the basin in cold water, which will cause the fat to quickly rise. Remove the fat carefully, re heat the broth, add the parsley and a little salt. Serve immediately. No. 536. MUTTON TEA. INGREDIENTS. 1 lb. of lean mutton Salt to taste 1 pint of cold water METHOD. Remove all skin and fat, and chop the meat finely. Place it in a jar, pour over the water, and let it stand for quite two hours. Cover closely, place the jar in a sauce- pan of cold water, and bring to the boil. Remove at once, strain the tea, take off all fat, add the salt, and serve.
