Cut into round cakes and bake in a quick oven about 15 minutes. Serve hot, well buttered. No. 478. GENOESE CAKE. INGREDIENTS. 6 ozs. of Coomb’s flour 4 ozs. of butter A few drops of flavouring essence 5 ozs. of castor sugar 3 eggs A pinch of salt METHOD. Beat the butter to a cream, add the sugar and flour gradually, moistening with the yolks, and stiffly beaten whites of the eggs. Stir in the salt and flavouring. Pour in a cake tin lined with paper, not greased, and bake in a moderate oven about 45 minutes. When cold, coat with chocolate icing, and sprinkle over half an ounce of pistachio nuts, blanched and very finely chopped. No. 479. FRANKS CAKE. INGREDIENTS. 8 ozs. of Coomb’s flour 3 eggs 4 ozs. of butter l grated rind of lemon 6 ozs. of castor sugar METHOD. Rub the butter into the flour add the sugar and lemon rind; beat the eggs separately, add to the dry ingredients and mix well. Bake in a lined cake tin about 45 minutes.
