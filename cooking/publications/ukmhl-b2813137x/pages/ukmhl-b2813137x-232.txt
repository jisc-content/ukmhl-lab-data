178 No. 430. ICE CREAM (Plain). INGREDIENTS. 1 quart of milk 1 oz. of cornflour 2 ozs. of castor sugar Yolks of 2 eggs Flavouring essence .METHOD. Mix the cornflour with a little milk quite smoothly, put the remainder into a saucepan to boil with the sugar, when boiling add the cornflour, stir well, and let it boil two or three minutes; let it cool a little, then add the yolks of the eggs and some flavouring essence; when quite cold freeze in the usual way. A few drops of cochineal may be added. N.B.â€”When making ices always let the custard, etc. be quite cold before freezing.
