133 No. 329. QUEEN PUDDING. INGREDIENTS. \ pint of bread crumbs 1 pint of milk 1 oz. of butter 2 eggs 1 dessertspoonful of sugar | A little jam I Grated rind of half a lemon METHOD. Put the milk on to boil. Place the bread crumbs in a basin, grate over them the lemon rind, add the butter, then pour over the boiling milk. Allow to stand a few minutes, to cool, beat in the yolks of the eggs, add the sugar, and put the pudding into a greased pie-dish, and bake in a moderate oven until set. .Remove the dish from the oven, spread the jam over the top, and pile the stiffly-beaten whites of the eggs upon the jam. Return the pudding to the oven for a few minutes to set the meringue. Sift over castor sugar, and serve. No. 330. IMPERIAL PUDDING.
