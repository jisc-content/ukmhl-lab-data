INGREDIENTS. 4 tablespoonfuls of vinegar 4 ozs. of butter 1 tablespoonful of water Pepper and salt 5 eggs, yolks of METHOD. Put the vinegar into a saucepan and reduce it three-fourths. Add the water and yolks, and stir until the sauce thickens. This is best done in a double saucepan. Now add in small pieces (off the fire) the butter. Flavour with pepper and salt. Note.—If allowed to get too hot the sauce will curdle. No. 241. MAITRE-D’HOTEL SAUCE. To one pint of velout6 or supreme sauce, add two tablespoonsful of finely chopped parsley.
