Apple Amber Pudding. 1 lemon. 2 large eggs. Pastry (6 oz. flour, etc.). (Enough for 7 or 8 persons.) Peel, core, and slice the apples, put them into a saucepan with the butter, and 3 oz. of the sugar, cook until a pulp; add the lemon rind (grated) and juice, and yolks of eggs. Line a pie-dish with the pastry, decorating the edge, pour in the apple mixture; bake for about half an hour. Beat up the whites of eggs stiffly, mix in the rest of the sugar, and pile roughly on the top of the pudding.
