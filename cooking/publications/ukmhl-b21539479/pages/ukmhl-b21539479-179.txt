Brown Sauce. 2 oz. butter. 6 mushrooms. 1 carrot. 1 shallot. oz. flour. 1 pint brown stock. Pepper and salt. 1 tablespoonful tomato sauce. Chop the vegetables and fry in the butter until a golden brown colour ; brown the flour, add the stock gradually, stirring all the time; bring to boiling-point, skim, add pepper and salt; put on the lid and cook for half an hour, stirring occasionally. Add the tomato sauce, strain, and it is ready to use. Italian Sauce. \ pint brown sauce. % gill sherry. Bunch of herbs. Add the sherry and herbs to the sauce, cook fifteen minutes.
