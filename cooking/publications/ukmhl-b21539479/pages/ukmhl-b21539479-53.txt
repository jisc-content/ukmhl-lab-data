Pepper, salt, cayenne. 1 pint aspic jelly. Small cress. Tarragon or chervil. {Enough for 5 or 6 persons.) Separate the meat from the lobster, putting on the shell with a little water and a few peppercorns to make the stock. Make a panada with the butter, flour, and stock. Cut up the lobster meat. Pound the spawn, rub it through a hair sieve and mix with the panada. Cook till it turns a rich red, add the lobster, seasoning, and lemon-juice. Spread the fillets with this, roll up, put on to a buttered tin, cover with buttered paper, and bake ten to fifteen minutes. When cold cut into slices. Decorate a mould with a little jelly and cress, put in some slices of fish, more jelly, and so fill up the mould. When set turn out and serve with salad. Fish Pie.
