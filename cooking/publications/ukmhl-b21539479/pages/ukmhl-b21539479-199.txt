Mix the dry ingredients together, and moisten with the liquid. Put into a pint basin, boil for four hours. Treacle Sponge. i lb. flour. Pinch of salt. £ teaspoonful ground ginger. Suet crust (see p. 22C). I 1 oz. sugar. 2 lb. apples. | 3 cloves. [Enough for 8 people.)
