Drain the syrup from the peaches, add the water and 4 oz. of the sugar. Reduce, put in the peaches, and simmer for ten minutes. Drain, and put syrup and peaches aside separately. Dissolve the gelatine in the milk, add the remainder of sugar, and flavour. When at the point of setting put a teacupful into a 3-pint mould, then a layer of peaches; when set add more mixture and another layer of peaches; fill the mould in this way.
