Put on to the hot toast and serve at once. Eggs with Tomatoes. 4 tomatoes. 1 oz. butter. 1 shallot (chopped finely). 6 eggs. Pepper, salt, and cayenne. A slice of hot buttered toast. (Enough for 4 persons.) Skin tomatoes and cut into pieces. Melt the butter in a frying-pan, cook shallot in it for a few minutes, add tomatoes, cook; add eggs (whole) and seasoning. When the eggs begin to set stir well, when cooked turn into a hot dish in which you have placed the toast. Serve at once. Ham Toast. 1£ oz. butter. 3 eggs. 3 oz. lean ham.
