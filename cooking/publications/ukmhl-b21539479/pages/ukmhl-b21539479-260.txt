232 KING EDWARD'S COOKERY BOOK Method No. 4. 1. Pass the flour through a sieve with the salt. 2. Add the fruit and spice. 3. Put the butter, sugar, and treacle into a saucepan, and stir over the fire until hot. 4. Dissolve the soda in the milk, and add to the eggs, which should be well beaten. 5. Add the butter, sugar, and treacle to the dry ingredients, also the eggs, milk, and soda. 6. Mix well together. 7. Put into buttered and papered tins. 8. Bake in a moderate oven.
