Bread Sauce. 1 gill milk. 1 gill chicken stock. 1 small onion. 1 clove. peppercorns. 15 gills breadcrumbs. 1 tablespoon ful cream. Pepper and salt. Put the milk, stock, onion, clove, and peppercorns into a saucepan, simmer for thirty minutes. Strain, add crumbs, cook for five minutes, add seasoning and cream. Custard Sauce. 1 egg. \ oz. sugar. £ pint milk. Essence of almonds. 1 teaspoonful cornflour. Beat up the egg, mix the cornflour with a little of the cold milk, put the rest of the milk on to boil. When boiling stir in the cornflour, cook for three minutes. Take off the fire, add sugar, add gradually to the egg, return to the saucepan, and stir over the fire till it thickens; add flavouring.
