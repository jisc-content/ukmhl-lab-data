Cabbage Soup. 1 white cabbage. 1 Spanish onion. 2 teaspoonfuls parsley. 2 quarts stock (made with beef bones and a ham bone). Pinch of thyme. 2 teaspoonfuls marigold petals. 1 tablespoonful flour. Browning. (Enough for 6 or 7 persons.) Shred the cabbage, not too finely, cut up the onion finely. Put these ingredients into a saucepan with the parsley and boiling stock. Boil gently for two hours, add the thyme and marigold petals, cook for ten minutes. Thicken with the flour, add seasoning and browning.
