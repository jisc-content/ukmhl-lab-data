THE HOUSEKEEPER SERIES, I.—ENGLISH AND FLENCH COOKERY. PUBLISHERS’ PREFACE. The present volume is the first of a series intended to embrace subjects of practical interest in connection with dojnestic life, and to be reliable and useful guides to every class of housekeeper in those matters inti- mately associated with the necessities, pleasures, health, happiness, comforts, and enjoyments of families. This, the first of the series, is devoted to food and cookery—English and French. The next volume will deal with Furnishing, Upholstery, Interior Decoration, etc. Each volume will be written and compiled with care, under critical direction, and issued at a price which will place it within the reach of all classes.
