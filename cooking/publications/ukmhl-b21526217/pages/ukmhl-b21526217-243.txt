CONFECTIONER. 193 N®. 235. PINE APPLE CHIPS, DRIED. T'ake your slices of pine apples that you have in syrup, and wash them in warm water, then put them on your sieves in such a manner that they do not touch each other, then take some fine powdered sugar, put it into a fine linen bag and just shake it over them; keep turning of them every day for three or four days in a hot stove ; when dry put them into your papered box with writing paper between every layer.
