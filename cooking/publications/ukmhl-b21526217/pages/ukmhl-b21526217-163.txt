N°. 125. RASBERRY ICE CREAM. Take a large spoonful of rasberry jam; put it into a bason and squeeze one lemon in; add a pint of cream and a little cochineal to colour it; pass it through a sieve into a bason; put it into your freezing pot, and do as above directed in your barberry ice cream.
