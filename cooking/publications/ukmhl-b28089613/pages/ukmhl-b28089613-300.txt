274 Ale, Beer, Cider, and Perry. In seasons of dearth, the Paris brewers were forbidden by ordonnance to make beer. Ordonnances of the Prevot de Paris appeared to this effect in 1415, and again in 1482. An arret of the council renewed this interdiction in 1693, and two others of the par- liament to a like effect appeared in 1709 and 1740.
