BANQUETS OF THE NATIONS 480 Mulbhas. When semi-cold, put white peas, which have been soaked, boiled, and then roasted as you would coffee beans, in rows on it, then pour the rest of the sugar over. When cold enough to handle roll into balls, a pea being in the centre of each ball. (Almond Sweets.) Proceed as above, only colouring with saffron, and using skinned almonds instead of peas, and cut into small pieces.
