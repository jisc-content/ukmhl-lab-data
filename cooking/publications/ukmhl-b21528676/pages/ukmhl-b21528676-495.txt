Tortillas de Queso. (Cheese Cakes.) Dissolve two teaspoonfuls of salt in one pint of cold water, in which put one tablespoonful of cornflour smoothly, and beat until it froths, then stir in a spoonful of grated cheese and enough flour to make a rather thin batter. Fry the tortillas in a hot frying-pan rubbed with a piece of butter tied in a cloth.
