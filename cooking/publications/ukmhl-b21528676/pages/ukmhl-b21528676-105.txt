Langue de Beouf a la Bruges. (Braised Ox-1 ongue.) Put some cut vegetables in a stewpan, and place a mildly pickled ox-tongue on top; put in a little stock and cook slowly till tender. Remove the skin. Cut it in thin slanting slices, put on a dish, mask with ham cullis, and garnish with little heaps of stewed cabbage, carrots, and turnips, small pieces of lean ham, and slices of small smoked sausages. Cullis of Ham.
