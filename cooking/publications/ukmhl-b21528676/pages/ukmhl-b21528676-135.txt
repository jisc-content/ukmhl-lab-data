Remove the fillets from a couple of plump teal. Put some peanut oil into a pan, and when quite hot slice an onion very thin into it, and fry till brown. Then put in the fillets of teal and brown, after which throw in a table- spoonful of pounded bacon which has been fried till brown and quite brittle, a large cupful of very strong stock made of the bones and trimmings of the birds, a tablespoonful of oil, half a tablespoonful of flour ; season with a table- spoonful of soy, a little sugar and pepper, a teaspoonful of pounded ginger, and a tablespoonful of ground green chillies.
