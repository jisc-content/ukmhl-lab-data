BRAZIL 79 Icing. walnuts. (A chestnut cake can be made in the same way, only you will decorate with Marrons glace.) Pass four ounces of icing sugar through a sieve, and then put in a lined saucepan, add one and a-half table- spoonfuls of cold water by degrees, and stir over a moderate fire until the mixture is warm and quite smooth ; if heated too much the icing will be spoilt.
