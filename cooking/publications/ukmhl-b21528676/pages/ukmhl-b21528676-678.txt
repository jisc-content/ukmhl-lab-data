BANQUETS OF THE NATIONS 642 Po-bae Ja. (Tea with salt and butter.) In making Thibetan tea, pour boiling water into a large bamboo cylinder. A piece of brick tea is broken off and thrown into this. Salt and wood ash are added, butter also. The whole is then churned vigorously.
