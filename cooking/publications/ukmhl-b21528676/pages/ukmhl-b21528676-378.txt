When cooked, put them in a dish and bruise into a soft mass and mix with the syrup of sugar; pass through a sieve, return to the pot, and put in the almonds (which have been blanched and fried in ghee), the rosewater, cardamoms, and nutmeg. Stir over the fire till thoroughly cooked, and of the consistency of thin porridge. Murenboo Aninas No. (Preserved Pine-apple.) Boil half a pound of the pulp of a pine-apple with the same weight of very heavy sugar syrup for a quarter of an hour. Eat when cold.
