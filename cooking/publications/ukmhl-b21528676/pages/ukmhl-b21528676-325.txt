Cocoanut Honey. Scrape a cocoanut and put it into a bowl; pour half a pint of boiling water over it, and fifteen minutes after- wards squeeze all the liquor out. Then mix with this a quart of honey previously heated. When well mixed and cold put into a bottle for use.
