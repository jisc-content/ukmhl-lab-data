Boil a large crab ; take all the meat out of the shell and legs ; grind this with some salt, a pinch each of mace, ginger, cardamoms, garlic, and red chillies, a spoonful of boiled rice, a tablespoonful of oil, and the pulp of a large tomato. Fill the shell, and roast in the oven for half an hour. Serve with rice. Kerrie^oedang. (Curried Shrimps.) Take two pounds of fresh shrimps and put them in a saucepan with a good hard cocoanut cut up into small pieces, a seasoning of onions, ginger, and salt; pour over one pint of water, and boil until the whole is done. Take out the shrimps and shell them, and strain the gravy into a basin. Put four ounces of oil or fat into a saucepan, boil it, add twelve onions cut in slices, cook them to a light brown, and take them out.
