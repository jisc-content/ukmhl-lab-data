Kopt chonnoye Kabannoe Myasso. (Roast Wild Boar.) Choose the saddle or haunch of a young wild boar, remove the swarth, put the meat in a kitchen basin, moisten nearly to height with cooked pickle consisting of oil, red wine, vinegar, chopped onions, bay leaves, pepper and salt, and steep for two days.
