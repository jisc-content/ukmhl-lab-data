BANQUETS OF THE NATIONS Akhrote-Ka-Mithai. (Almond Tablet.) Mix two pounds of sugar and four ounces of ghee in a pound of water, and boil to crackling height. Having reduced three pounds of milk to a fourth of its bulk by boiling, remove the sugar from the fire and stir in the milk along with a pound of cleaned and finely- chopped almonds. Stir till thick and creamy, and pour into any kind of oiled dish fancy may dictate. Walnut Tablet. This is made the same way, substituting walnuts for almonds.
