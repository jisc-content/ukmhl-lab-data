Divide this into pieces about the size of a walnut, wrap them up separately in pieces of the leaves, put them close together in a saucepan, sprinkle well with salt, pour over a little melted butter, and add broth to half their height. Cover them over, and cook until the liquor is reduced to a glaze, then remove the pan to the side. Boil in stock ten ounces of rice, and finish with butter and grated Parmesan cheese. Place a thick layer of this rice in an entree dish, and very carefully arrange the dolmas on top. Pour a rich lemon sauce over all, and put in a very hot oven for three minutes. Lemon Sauce.
