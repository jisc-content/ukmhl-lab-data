BANQUETS OF THE NATIONS 362 Amriti. (Vanilla Tablet.) 1 ake a pound of ghee, melt it in a pot, stir in eight ounces of gram flour, four ounces of sugar, and four ounces of milk curd. Boil for a few minutes, stirring all the time. Remove from the fire, add some essence o! vanilla, and beat till thick and creamy. Balushai. (Orange Tablet.) Boil quarter of a pound of ghee, the same quantity of thick curd, and one pound of sugar, till it balls when you take a piece between the fingers. Remove from the fire, flavour with essence of orange, colour with a touch of saffron, and beat till it is thick and creamy. Pour into an oiled form and cut into handy pieces.
