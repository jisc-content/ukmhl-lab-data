Thinly pare six apples, and core carefully. Put them into boiling syrup, adding some lemon juice and red colouring matter, and dish when tender but not too soft. Kalakand. (Milk Sweets.) Boil equal quantities of milk and sugar until quite thick and creamy, and, when cold, cut in squares ; decorate with silver and rose leaves.
