BANQUETS OF THE NATIONS 634 Yellow Chartreuse. Mix in four pints of spirits (at least 62 overproof) eighty minims of oil of angelica, twelve minims of oil of melissa, twelve minims of oil of mace, twelve minims of oil of hyssop, eight minims of oil of coriander, eight minims of oil of cloves, eight minims of oil of cazuput, and four minims of oil of calamus. Add four pints of syrup, and colour yellow with a little saffron. Filter till clear. Cannot be used under two years. I
