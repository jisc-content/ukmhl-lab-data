The above tables will enable you to calculate how much your meat, grocery, and other weighable articles cost at so much a pound. The number of pounds is given in the first column of the first table, and, by running the finger along the line till the price per lb. is reached, you will there find the total cost of the pounds. Do the same in the case of the ounces ; add the two sums together, and you get the total cost of the article.
