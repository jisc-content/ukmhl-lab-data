Boiled Icing (No. i).—One cup granula- ted sugar, one-half cup water, one-quarter tea- spoonful cream of tartar. Set on the back of the stove and stir occasionally until dissolved. Then boil until it will harden when dropped into cold water (or about eight minutes). Let cool a little and beat until stiff enough to spread on the cake. 3. Boiled Icing (No. 2).—Boil one teacupful of granulated sugar with four teaspoonfuls of water until it drops from the spoon in threads. Have ready the beaten white of one egg, and pour the syrup slowly into it, stirring all the time. Flavor, and spread on the cake while warm. 4. Chooolate Icing.—One cup sugar, one- quarter cup chocolate, one-half cup milk, yolk of one egg, one teaspoonful vanilla. . 5. Boil together all the ingredients, until the chocolate and sugar are dissolved. When cool, spread the icing between the layers and on top of the cake.
