Breakfast Rolls.—One quart whole wheat flour, one-half pint milk, one-half pint water, one-third yeast cake, two tablespoonfuls sugar, two tablespoonfuls shortening, pinch salt. Mix thoroughly at night and set' to rise. In the morning mix again and make into small rolls. Put in a greased pan and let rise twenty minutes, or until light'. Brush over the tops with melted butter or milk and bake one-half hour. Or take some risen bread dough, work into it in the morning one tablespoonful butter and proceed as above. 12. Whole-Wheat Gems (Good).—One quart whole-wheat flour, one quart water, one tablespoonful melted shortening, two table- spoonfuls sugar, two teaspoonfuls baking jiowder, a pinch salt. Beat thoroughly, put into hot greased gem pans and bake twenty minutes in a hot oven. 13.
