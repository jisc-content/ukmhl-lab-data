Hollandaise Sauce.—Rub one-half cup butter to a cream; add the yolks of two eggs, one at a time, and beat well; stir in the juice of half a lemon, one salt'spoonful salt; when ready to serve add one-half cup boiling water, place the bowl in a pan of boiling water or in the top of teakettle and cook until thick as custard, stirring all the time. 20. Sauce Tartare.—Make a mayonnaise dressing and add one tablespoonful finely chopped Gherkins pickles. 21. Maitre d’Hotel Sauce.—To one cup drawn butter No. i add one teaspoonful minced parsley and the juice of a lemon. Boil up and serve.
