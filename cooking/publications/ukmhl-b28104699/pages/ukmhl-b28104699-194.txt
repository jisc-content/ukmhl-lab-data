Marble Cake.—One-half cup butter and one cup sugar beaten to a cream, one-half cup sweet milk, one and one-half cups flour, one t'easpoonful baking powder, whites of four eggs added last. Take one cup of this mixture, add to it five tablespoonfuls grated chocolate wet with milk and flavor with vanilla. Put a layer of white batter in cake pan, drop the chocolate batter with a spoon in spots; pour over the re- maining white batter, and bake. Ice with choc- olate icing. 9.
