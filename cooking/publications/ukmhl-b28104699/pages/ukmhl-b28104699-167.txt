Beat yolks of two eggs, and add one cup cooked pitted prunes, mashed, one pint milk and two tablespoonfuls sugar. Pour this mixture over the bread, and bake in a slow oven one hour, or until the custard is set. Then beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth with two tablespoonfuls powdered su- gar, spread over the top and brown. 42. Prune Pudding.—One pound stewed prunes, white of four eggs, one cup sugar. After the prunes are stewed, drain off the juice, remove the stones, and chop.
