Bake in a well-greased pudding dish, and eat at once, be- fore it falls. 15. Cheese Ramekin.—Put one cup of bread crumbs and one gill of milk on the fire to boil. Stir and boil until smooth. Then put' in four tablespoonfuls of grated cheese, a little piece of butter, and salt. Stir till the cheese is dis- solved, then remove from the fire. Beat two eggs, the yolks and whites separately. Stir the yolks into the mixture and then the whites of the eggs. Put in a pudding dish and bake fifteen or twenty minutes. 16.
