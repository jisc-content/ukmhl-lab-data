Rice Pudding (Good).—Take two table- spoonfuls rice, wash, pour boiling water over and let stand five minutes; throw off, add a cup of sugar, a little vanilla and two quarts of milk. Bake slowly about two hours, stir- ring occasionally until last half hour, then brown. 38. Steamed Rice.—One quart of sweet milk, two-thirds of a cup of uncooked rice, and a lit- tle salt. Put into cups, set in a steamer over boiling water, and cook until the rice is almost’ like jelly. When cold turn out of the cups, and serve with sugar and cream or with pud- ding sauce. 39.
