Cabbage Salad.—Two large raw eggs well beaten, six tablespoonfuls of cream, one- half teaspoonful salt, six teaspoonfuls of vin- egar and a small piece of butter. Put on the fire and cook, stirring constantly until quite thick. Have a half head of cabbage chopped fine, sprinkled with salt. Add to the dressing when cold, two tablespoonfuls of cream and pour over the cabbage. 5.
