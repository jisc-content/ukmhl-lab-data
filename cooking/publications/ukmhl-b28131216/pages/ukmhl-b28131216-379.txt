Black Ink. Take a gallon of rain or soft water, and three quar- ters of a pound of blue galls, bruised; infuse them three weeks, stirring daily. Then add four ounces of green copperas, four ounces of logwood chips, six ounces of gum arabic, and a wine-glassful of brandy.
