Roll out some thin puff-paste, and lay it in a patty- pan of what size you choose ; put in raspberries; strew over them fine sugar ; cover with a thin lid, and then bake, Cut it open, and have ready the following mix- ture warm : Half a pint of cream, the yolks of two or three eggs well beaten, and a little sugar; and when this is added to the tart, return it to the oven for five or six minutes.
