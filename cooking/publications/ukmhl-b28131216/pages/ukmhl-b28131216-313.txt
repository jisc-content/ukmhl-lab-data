Mix five ounces of butter in three pounds of dry flour, and five ounces of fine Lisbon sugar ; then add six ounces of currants, washed and dried, and some pimento finely powdered. Put three spoonfuls of yeast into a Winchester pint of new milk warmed, and mix into a light dough with the above. Make it into twelve cakes, and bake on a floured tin half an hour.
