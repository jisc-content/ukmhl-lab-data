Forcemeat may be put both under and over the meat, if preferred to balls. Eel Pie. Cut the eels in lengths of two or three inches, season with pepper, and salt, and place in the dish, with some bits of butter, and a little water ; and cover it with paste. Cod Pie. Take a piece of the middle of a small cod, and salt it well one night : next day wash it; season with pep- per, salt, and a very little nutmeg, mixed; place in a dish, and put some butter on it, and a little good broth of any kind into the dish.
