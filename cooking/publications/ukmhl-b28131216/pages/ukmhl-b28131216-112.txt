Serve with brown gravy, in which boil some mushrooms, pickled or fresh. Gar- nish with balls fried. Veal Cake. Boil six or eight eggs hard ; cut the yolks in two, ana lay some of the pieces in the bottom of the pot; shake in a little chopped parsley, some slices of veal and ham, and then eggs again ; shaking in after each some chop- ped parsley, with pepper and salt, till the pot is full. Then put in water enough to cover it, and lay on it about an ounce of butter; tie it over with a double paper, aud bake it about an hour. Then press it close together with a spoon, and let it stand till cold. It may be put into a small mould ; and then it will turn out beautifully for a supper or side dish. Veal Sausages. Chop equal quantities of lean veal and fat bacon, handful of sage, a little salt, pepper, and a few an- chovies.
