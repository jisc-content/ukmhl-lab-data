Tie down with bladder, and keep in a dry place, or in paper. Mushroom Powder. Wash half a peck of large mushrooms while quite fresh, and free them from grit and dirt with flannel; scrape out the black part clean, and do not use any that are worm-eaten; put them into a stew-pan over the fire without water, with two large onions, some cloves, a quarter of an ounce of mace, and two spoon- fuls of white pepper, all in powder; simmer and shake them till all the liquor be dried up, but be care- ful they do not burn.
