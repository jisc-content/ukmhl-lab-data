89 WEST INDIAN, ETC. l^olasses Pie Take one cup of molasses, one cup of sugar, four whole eggs and four teaspoonfuls of butter. Mix together the sugar, butter and eggs, stir in the molasses, and bake in a rich crust. Squash Stewed Peel the squashes and steam them till tender, and season with milk and cream. Add a little butter and salt, also add an onion cut fine and a slice of ham. They should stew till as thick as mashed potatoes.
