Chicken. (Neapolitan) II re dei Cuochi Cut up a fowl and cook it in a stewpan with a little butter and salad oil till light brown ; then add six tomatoes, some mushrooms chopped very fine, and two table-spoonfuls of very good broth; add salt to taste. Cook slowly for two hours, keeping the stewpan tightly covered, adding a very little garlic. Serve very hot.
