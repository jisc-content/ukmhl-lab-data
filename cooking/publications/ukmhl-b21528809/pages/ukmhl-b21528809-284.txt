COOKERY AND HOUSEKEEPING Saturdays.—Turn out kitchen, and area passages, etc., thoroughly. Be dressed by four o’clock. Nights out. House-parlourmaid’s Duties—Every Day Down 6.30 winter. 6 summer. Take hot water, tea and letters to mistress’ bedroom. Take hot water to master’s dressing-room, brush clothes, etc. Sweep and dust stairs. Dust hall, dining-room, drawing-room and study. Have own breakfast. After breakfast, lay dining-room breakfast. Wait at breakfast if required. Afterwards clear away. Strip all beds. Then wash all china and silver. Do bedrooms. Attend to Special Duties for the day. Dress. Lay dining-room lunch. Wait at lunch.
