A Genoese Sauce is made by adding to Genoese Sauce the brown sauce above described a glass of cooking port, a table-spoonful of mushroom catsup, a tea-spoonful of anchovy, a lump of sugar, a little grated nutmeg, and some finely minced parsley. Boil these well to- gether, and add a tiny pat of butter, say, half an ounce. Bring to the boil again, skim carefully, make hot, strain through a heated gravy strainer into a hot sauce tureen, and it is ready for use.
