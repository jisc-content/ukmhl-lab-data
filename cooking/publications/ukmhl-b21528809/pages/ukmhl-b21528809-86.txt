Butter a fireproof china dish rather thickly, place the fish upon it, add a few bits of butter here and there, and moisten with a glass of cooking sherry and a half a glass of mushroom catsup, and bake in a moderate oven for from twenty to thirty minutes ; then pour over it a gill and a half of aurore sauce, and rub over it the yolk of a hard-boiled egg; replace in the oven for two or three minutes, and serve at once.
