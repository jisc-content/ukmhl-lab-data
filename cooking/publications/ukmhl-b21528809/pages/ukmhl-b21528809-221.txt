209 SAVOURIES Yet another and even nicer variation on An American Savoury the above are CroCtes A l’Americaine.— Take the brown meat from a lobster. Chop it finely, and mix with it a tea-spoonful of French mustard, a tea-spoonful of English mustard, a few drops of strained lemon juice, and an oz. of grated Parmesan cheese. Mix all well together and make hot in a sauce- pan with a bit of butter about the size of a walnut. Have ready some small neatly cut squares of fried bread, allowing one for each person, pile the mixture upon these, dust each with a little cayenne pepper, make very hot in the oven, and serve at once.
