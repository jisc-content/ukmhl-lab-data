Mix all together and place in a perfectly clean rather wide- mouthed jug, and put the jug into a deep saucepan nearly full of hot water. Bring the water to the boil, and let it continue boiling during the whole of the time. Stir the cream for full twenty minutes, then pour into a basin, and continue to stir till the mixture is cool.
