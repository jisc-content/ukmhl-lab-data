HOUSEKEEPING 273 Have own lunch. Clear away. Wash up all glass and silver. Attend to Special Duties for the day. ■ Make and serve afternoon tea. Have own tea. After tea, wash up. Lay dinner table. Take hot water to bedrooms. Serve and wait at dinner. After dinner, clear away. Have own supper. Wash all glass and silver. Tidy bedrooms for the night. Take up hot water. Light fires if required. House-parlourmaid’s Special Duties Mondays.—Turn out dressing-room. Help sort linen for wash. Mend all house linen. Tuesdays.—Turn out mistress’ bedroom. Do mending.
