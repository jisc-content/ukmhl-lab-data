Pudding A la Norvegienne Equally nice, in a rather different way, is “ Pudding a la Norvegienne.” Rub a pound of red currants through a hair sieve, and reserve in a clean basin. Peel, core, and slice thinly a pound and a half of apples.. Place a pound of loaf sugar in a clean enamelled iron stew-pan. Add to it the sieved currants and apples, and bring gently to the boil. Continue to boil until the apples are quite tender, but do not allow the syrup to reduce.
