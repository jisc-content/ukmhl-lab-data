Apropos of potato ribbons, an ingenious little instrument for cutting these may be had at any ironmonger. Potatoes a la Duchesse are delicious. They are made as follows : Bake six large potatoes. Then rub them through a very fine wire sieve. Place the puree thus obtained in a clean stew-pan, and add to it an ounce of fresh butter, the well-beaten yolks of three eggs, pepper and salt to taste, and a tiny grate of nutmeg.
