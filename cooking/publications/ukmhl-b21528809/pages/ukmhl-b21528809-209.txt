197 SWEETS For the sauce whip the yolks of the eggs a custard till they froth, then add to them sifted sugar to taste and a pint of boiling milk. Mix well. Place in a clean enamelled iron stew- pan and stir till the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the saucepan, but do not allow it actually to boil. Then add to it a few drops of essence of vanilla, and rub through a heated gravy strainer around the pudding as directed. An even better way to make the sauce, if time permits, is to place the ingredients in a large jug, stand the jug in a‘saucepan full of water, and bring it slowly to the boil, stirring all the time. Then finish as directed.
