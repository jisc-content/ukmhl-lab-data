See first that the irons are very hot and thoroughly clean. No one can iron well with a dirty iron. The very best way to clean an iron is to rub it well on several thicknesses of brown paper sprinkled with knife powder, clean on a fresh piece of paper, and smear lightly with yellow soap. Wipe again, and use.
