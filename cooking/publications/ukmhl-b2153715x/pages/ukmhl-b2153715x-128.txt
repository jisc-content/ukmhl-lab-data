How to Turn out the Drawing-Room 124 leave it until the room is nearly finished. When it is quite dry remove it, and dust with a clean duster. Brush all the cushions well. Beat them lightly, and wipe them gently with a clean white duster if they are chintz. Unpin the curtains, and the room is done. A room cleaned in this wise every week will only take very little time to do, as it will be practically clean when you start. Wash your hands ; finish the lunch. Lay the table. Take in the lunch, and have your own dinner.
