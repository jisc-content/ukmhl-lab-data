24 BORWICR'S COOKERY BOOK. SNOWBALLS. 1 lb MILLENNIUM flour I teaspoonful BORWICK'S BAKING POWDER. 3 ozs. sugar. 4 ozs. margarine. I egg. A little milk. Sieve flour and baking powder, and rub margarine well into it. Mix in the sugar, then add the egg and milk* and bind to a stiff dough. Divide the mixture into equal (small) portions, and roll into balls. Place on a flat tin, and bake in a moderate oven. When cold, brush over with warmed apricot jam, and roll in desiccated cocoanut.
