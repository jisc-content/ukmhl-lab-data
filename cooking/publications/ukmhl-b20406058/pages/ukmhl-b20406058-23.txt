PRINCESS CAKE. 9 ozs. MILLENNIUM flour. 2 teaspoonfuls BORWICK'S BAKING POWDER. 6 ozs. margarine. 4 ozs. sugar. 2 eggs. 2 tablespoonfuls milk. Beat margarine and sugar to a cream, and add the eggs, beating in one at a time ; then add the sifted flour and baking powder. Lastly stir or beat in the milk. Line tin with greased paper. Pour in mixture, and put two rings of citron on top. Bake in moderate oven.
