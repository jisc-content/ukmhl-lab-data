QUEEN CAKES. 1 lb. MILLENNIUM flour. i-l teaspoonfuls BORWICK'S BAKING POWDER. 4 ozs. margarine. 2 ozs. castor sugar. 2 ozs. currants. 2 eggs. 2 tablespoonfuls of milk. Warm sugar and beat with margarine to a cream, put in eggs, and well beat together for a few minutes. Then add milk, and mix in the flour, baking powder, and currants. Bake in fancy tins in a brisk oven for about 10 minutes.
