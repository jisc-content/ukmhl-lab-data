When you reach the wing bone, disjoint it from the carcase, and then, slitting it along inwards down the wing joint, take out the bone ; now work Fig 20. down to the leg, and disjoint this at the thigh bone. The other side of the body is done in the same way. Fig. 19 gives the appearance of the bird at this point.
