To remove the remaining bones, make a cut along the thigh, as in the dotted line of Fig. 20, and then carefully remove the flesh from the thigh bone, and scrape back the flesh from the leg bone, as in the other side of the figure; then pull this leg bone out, and it will bring the rest of the sinews with it. The so-called “wish- bone” and the two large white sinews in the breast must now be removed, and the boned fowl is ready.
