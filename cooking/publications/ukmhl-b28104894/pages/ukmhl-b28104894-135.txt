125 POULTRY. The proper way of plucking' the bird is to hold it in the left hand, and begin plucking under the wing, carefully removing all feathers, but leaving the down to be singed off. You then reverse it, and pluck down the other side in the same way. The next process, is to singe the bird, which is effected by means of a lighted paper, being very careful not to bring it so close as to actually singe the skin.
