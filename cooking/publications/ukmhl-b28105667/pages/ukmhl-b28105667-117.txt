They invariably dishke calves heads and cold boiled beef, or, in fact, any cold meat, especially Australian. Like Mrs. Gamp, they scorn hash, but somehow like stewed steak, both dishes affording admirable opportunities for displays of dexterity with the blade of the knife. The world, thanks to love of dress, drink, etc., is changing very rapidly.
