Ingredients.—4 ozs. block chocolate, 1 gill milk, 2 ozs. sugar if unsweetened chocolate is used, 1 pint vanilla cream (No. 2 or 3), y2 gill cream. Method.—Cut the chocolate into small pieces, put it in a stewpan with the milk, add the sugar, if needed, and cook till the chocolate is dissolved. Now strain, and add it to the prepared custard cream. Let cool. Lastly, add y2 gill whipped cream. Freeze, and mould. 26.—GLACE A LA CREME D’ABRICOT. (Apricot Cream Ice.) Ingredients.—12 ripe apricots, 6 ozs. castor sugar, 1 lemon, 1 pint fresh cream. Method.—Stone the apricots, and put them, with 6 of the kernels, in a stewpan, cover with water, and cook till tender; add the sugar, and rub through a hair sieve as soon as the sugar is melted. Add the juice of a lemon to the fruit puree. Whisk the cream till stiff, and mix when the former is cold. Freeze as usual.
