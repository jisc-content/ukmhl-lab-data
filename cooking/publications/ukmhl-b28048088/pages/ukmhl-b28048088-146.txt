132 DAINTY DINNER TABLES. where it is desired to make them at home, they should first be cut out of stiff cardboard, then painted over with gum in order to stick the cotton- wool, and finally outlined with either holly, ivy, or mistletoe, as fancy may dictate. It is worthy of note in this connection that the artificial holly and ivy give by far the best results to amateurs at this kind of decorative work.
