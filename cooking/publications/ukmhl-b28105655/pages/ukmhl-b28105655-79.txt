This pudding, having no egg, is suitable when the eggs are objected to, or in winter, when they are very expensive. Urney Pudding. 2 Eggs. Flour. Butter. Sugar. Jam. Carbonate of Soda. Take 2 eggs, with their weight in butter and flour, and the weight of 1 egg in sugar. Put the butter and sugar in a basin, and beat them to a cream ; add the eggs and beat smooth ; then add the flour, in which should be mixed half a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda. Mix till smooth, and add a table-spoonful of strawberry or raspberry jam; pour into a buttered mould, which cover with a piece of greased paper, and steam gently for 1J hours. Soak the tapioca for a quarter of an hour in a breakfast-cupful of hot water. Put the milk on to boil, and when it boils stir in the soaked tapioca, and let it also boil, stirring it constantly for nearly 10 minutes.
