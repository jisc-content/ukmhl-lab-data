BEST MATERIALS ONLY. 23 When I say “ best materials,” I mean everything that is used for cooking. If it be butter, let it be the best, such as you would yourself eat for break- fast. Your cooking butter cannot be too good, be- cause the inferior article is apt to spoil any dish beyond hope, and you would only be “ spoiling the ship for a ha’p’orth of tar.” Now, this principle applies to all materials used in cooking.
