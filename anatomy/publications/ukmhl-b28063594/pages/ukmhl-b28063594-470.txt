LABORATORY MANUAL OF HUMAN ANATOMY 466 Fig. 245. Schematic representation of the course and arrangement of the peritoneum, mesentery, and omental bursa in normal condition. The blue line indicates the primary parietal peritoneum ; the red line, the mesogastrium ; and the yellow line, the visceral peritoneum and peritoneal covering of the mesentery. The continuous lines show the free surface of the peritoneum ; the dotted lines show those parts of the peritoneum of which the free surfaces have been lost by fusion. (After Toldt, Anat. Atlas, Wien, 1900, 2 Aufl., p. 440, Fig. 737.)
