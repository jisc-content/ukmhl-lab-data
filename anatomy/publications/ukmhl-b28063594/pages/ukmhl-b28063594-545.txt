Pelvic Fascia (Fascia pelvis) in the Female. (Vide Spalteholz, Fig. 667.) To expose the pelvic fascia from above, remove the perito- neum by blunt dissection from the viscera and the pelvic wall. This should be done on one side only. Avoid injury to the ves- sels and nerves. The anterior leaf of the broad ligament may, also be left. Study now the following divisions of the fascia: {a) Superior fascia of pelvic diaphragm {fascia diaphragmatis pelvis superior). {b) Tendinous arch of pelvic fascia {arcus tendineus fasciae pelvis).
