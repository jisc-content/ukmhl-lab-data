The bulb will be found just beneath the M. bulbocavernosus. Note its relation to the labia minora and urethra. Trace these ante- riorly and note that the two oblong bodies forming the bulb are united below the clitoris by an intermediate venous plexus {pars intermedia of Kobelt).
