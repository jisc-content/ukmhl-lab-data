(Fig. loi.) Sections of the spinal cord {sectiones medullae spinalis) should next be studied. With a sharp, thin knife, cut through the cord transversely at the following levels: (1) just below roots of fourth pair of cervical nerves, (2) through middle of cervical enlargement, (3) through middle of thoracic cord, (4) through middle of lumbar enlargement, and (5) ..through conus terminalis. In the sections observe the differentiation into white matter (substantia alba) and gray matter (substantia grisea).
