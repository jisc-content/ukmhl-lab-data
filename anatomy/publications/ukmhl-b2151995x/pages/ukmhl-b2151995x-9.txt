A Second Edition of this work having been called for, the Author has endeavoured to make it more complete than its predecessor by availing himself of the most recent improvements in the science of Anatomy, Physio- logy, and Surgery. It is earnestly requested that no student should imagine that this work is intended to be a substitute for the numerous comprehensive works on such subjects, but he should resort to it as a collection of notes, in which simplicity and clearness of description have been employed to render some difficult points more easily un- derstood. Numerous complaints were made that, in the former Edition, a sufficient Index was wanting.
