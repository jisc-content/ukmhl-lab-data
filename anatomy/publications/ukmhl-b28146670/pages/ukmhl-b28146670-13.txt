PREFACE. My attention having been directed to the subject of Deformities, I was surprised to find that there was no work in the English language, which professed to treat generally of these maladies. We have some excellent works on particular varieties of spinal dis- tortion, but with regard to deformities of the limbs, excepting a few scattered papers in our periodicals, I can find no good des- cription of these maladies, nor is there any English writer on surgery, who enters into a detailed account of their nature and treat- ment.
