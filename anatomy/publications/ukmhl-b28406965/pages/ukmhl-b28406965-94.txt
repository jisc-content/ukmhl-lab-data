He may feel pain when no stimulus is applied to the limb, or he may feel ease when it is. Nay, he continues to feel pain, or sensations, in the limb when it is rotten, or no longer in existence ; which seems to shew the integrity of the sentient principle remaining in the brain. In vegetables, and in some moluscse, no traces of a nervous system are discoverable, yet the irritability of life is manifest in all.
