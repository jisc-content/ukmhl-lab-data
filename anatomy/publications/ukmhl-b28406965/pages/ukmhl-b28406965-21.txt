9 There was a time when medical men entertained so determined a dislike to the word theory, that they could scarcely to- lerate the term. If any such remain, I would beg them to reflect that hypothesis and theory are the natural and inevi- table result of thinking; so that if they refuse to allow of any theory, they must prohibit all thought. The antipathy which some have en- tertained to the term theory, has arisen from its misapplication.
