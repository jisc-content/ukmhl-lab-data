The Os Uteri contracted, and closed up. The OS uteri has been found to be so con- tracted, as to have its passage in a great measure obliterated;* and it has even been known to be closed up, by the growth of an adventitious membrane.-f Uterus bony. The substance of the utextis is sometimes more or less converted into bone. This arises from a particular morbid action of its blood vessels, by which they secrete from the blood bony matter, and it is a very rare disease. The Uterus changed into an earthy Sub- stance.
