There would often seem to be slight de* grees of inflammation in the pleura, where the symptoms above stated do not exist at all, or are so obscurely marked as to be altogether overlooked. In examining the chest of adults after death, it rarely happens that adhesions are not discovered in some part of it, uniting the surface of the lungs to the pleura which lines the pa- rietes of the chest. The marked symptoms of pleurisy, however, are by no means so frequent.
