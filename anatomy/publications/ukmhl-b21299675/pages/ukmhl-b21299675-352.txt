Cysts communicaling with the Bladder. Cysts are sometimes found connected very intimately with the bladder, and com- municating with its cavity. These in some instances have been observed to be of a large size, being perhaps half as large as the usual size of the bladder itself There is some difficulty in explaining the manner in which they are formed.
