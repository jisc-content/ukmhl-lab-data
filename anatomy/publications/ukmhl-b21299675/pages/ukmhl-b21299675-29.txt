ADVERTISEMENT TO THE THIRD EDITION For the last seven years I have been so much engaged in the practical duties of my profession, as only to have had occa- sional opportunities of inspecting dead bodies. I have, therefore, scarcely been able to make any additions to this work, but I have made such corrections of it, as more reflection and experience have sug- gested to me, and I hope it will be found to be considerably improved. The Second Edition of this Treatise was translated at Paris about three years ago hy Dr. Ferrall into the French language.
