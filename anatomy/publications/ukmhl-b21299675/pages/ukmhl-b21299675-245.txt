These are the most common appearances of diseased, or preternatural structure in the intestines : but I have likewise had an opportunity of observing others, which are of rarer occurrence. In one or two in- stances, I have seen a sort of bony matter, formed upon the surface of the inner membrane of the gut; I have even seen an adhesion between two portions of intestine converted into bone. It would appear, that almost every part of the body is occa- sionally subject to this process. It may not improperly be considered, as a natu- ral process misplaced. An adhesion being once formed, has the same power (as far as we know) of running into different pro- cesses, as the cellular membrane, which makes a part of the original structure.
