The symptoms which belong to a scir- rhous state of the kidneys are unknown to me, and I do not find that they are dis- tinctly marked by authors. There would seem to be no particular symptoms which belong to the formation of hydatids in the kidneys. Pain is com- monly felt in the loins during their for- mation; there has been remarked to be symptomatic fever, nausea, and vomiting; but these symptoms belong also to some other diseases. This disease, therefore, can only be ascertained by hydatids passing oc- casionally through the urethra along wit' the urine.
