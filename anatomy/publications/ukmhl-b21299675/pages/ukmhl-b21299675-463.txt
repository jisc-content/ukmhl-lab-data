The internal surface of the vagina, near the outward opening, is frequently in^- flamed, especially from the application of the venereal poison, but this hardly ever becomes the subject of examination after death. Adhesion of the Sides of the Vagina. A very violent inflammation has some- times been known to take place in the va- gina, which has terminated in the mutual adhesion of the sides of the cavity. This adhesion is sometimes extended over a great part of the cavity, but I believe is often more confined, producing a stricture in some one part.
