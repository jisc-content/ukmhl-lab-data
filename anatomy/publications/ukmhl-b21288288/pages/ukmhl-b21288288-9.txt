TO JOHN HEAVISIDE, Esq. SURGEON EXTRAORDINARY TO THE KING, F.R.S, F.A.S., &c. My dear Sir, It is with the liveliest pleasure that I ac- knowledge, upon every occasion, your having very early in life honoured me with your friendship. From the first commencement of my professional studies, you have most kindly patronized my exertions, and assisted me in my various pursuits. I cannot but re- collect also, that to you I have for very many years been in the habit of looking up, as to one from whom I might acquire principles of right conduct and integrity, as well as those relating to knowledge in my profession.
