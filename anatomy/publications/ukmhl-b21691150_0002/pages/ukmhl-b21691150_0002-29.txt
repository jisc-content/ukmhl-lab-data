Explanation of Plate 5. Multiple Embolic and Purulent Nephritis from Septico-Py^emia. After the capsule had been removed the surface of the kidney presented a number of scattered spots corre- sponding to abscesses, which occupied the outer portions of the cortex. In addition to these isolated spots, there were larger purulent foci with red contours where the tissues were just breaking down into pus. When cut open with the knife these foci were found to be wedge- shaped collections of pus of embolic origin. The kidney tissues outside of these abscesses were found to be in the condition known as cloudy swelling.
