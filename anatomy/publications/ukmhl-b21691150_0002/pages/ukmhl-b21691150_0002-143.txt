Explanation of Plate 43. Chronic Osteitis of the Femur; Deforming Hy- perostosis of the Bone, and the Formation of a Sequestrum at the Lower End. The bone, throughout its entire extent, is enlarged, and covered with an abundance of osteophytic growths (periostitis ossificans). Here and there the opening of a cloaca may be seen. In the lower jjart of the bone, throughout a length of several centimetres, a smooth sequestrum is visible.
