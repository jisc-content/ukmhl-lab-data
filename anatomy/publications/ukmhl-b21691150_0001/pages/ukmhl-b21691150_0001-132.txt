Explanation of Plate 40. Chronic Enteritis Pigmentosa. The mucous membrane of the jejunum appears much folded, swollen, succulent, reddened, and pigmented with slaty punctiform spots. The entire intestinal wall is friable like tinder and thickened; the contents of the bowel are watery and abundant.
