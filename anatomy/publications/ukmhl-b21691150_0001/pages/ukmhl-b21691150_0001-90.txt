Explanation of Plate 26. Croupous Pneumonia. (Stage of red hepatization.) The lower lobe of the right lung is of a uniform, al- most liver-like consistence and brownish-red color; the cut surface is finely granular, and the air is expelled. On pressure a considerable amount of a gray pyoid matter escapes from the large and medium-sized bronchia. The patient (No. 677, 1895) was of delicate build and badly nourished, weight 42.5 kgm. = 94 lb., and suc- cumbed about the sixth day of the disease. 26
