Descent of the colon is caused not only by abnormal stretching of its ligaments but also by long-continued or oft-repeated overfilling. Slighter grades of enteroptosis are physiological and give rise to no disturbances. Unsuitable clothing (tight lacing), frequent pregnancy, and former obes- ity are evidently important etiological factors.
