Explanation of Plate 35. Carcinoma of the (Esophagus, Perforation into the Trachea. (Two-fifths natural size.) Below the larynx is an annular and oval carcinomatous ulcer of a gray color; the bottom of the ulcer communi- cates with the lumen of the trachea by a large opening. Some distance farther down is a similar, somewhat smaller non-perforating ulcer. Death by gangrenous inhalation pneumonia of both lower lobes. 35
