Beauty, then, and in that country, was un- rivalled. The women who possessed It, in the highest degree, were absolutely adored. Helen, as every one knows, caused the famous war of Troy; the beautiful Aspasla of Miletus, set Greece on fire under Pericles ; and, according to Athenaeus, the carrying away of three beautiful courtezans, was the germ of the Peloponnesian war.
