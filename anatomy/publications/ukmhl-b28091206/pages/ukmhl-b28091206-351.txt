When, now, the hours of recrimination or of gloom are relieved, by the accidental call of a youthful, and perhaps attractive, male or female visitor, the features of the young wife or husband, are lighted with a smile to receive them, partly from grati- tude for the relief they bring, partly from contrariety. The lightened features and glad welcome are instantly observed by that individual of the married couple, whose sex resembles that of the visitor, who is consequently, in imagination, trans- muted into a rival. The other member of the married couple, now probably coquets with a fourth person by way of retaliation ; and that which began in capricious spite or sport, sometimes ends in dangerous at- tachment.
