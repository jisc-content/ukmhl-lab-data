He took her by the hand, led her into the middle of the army, and pulling off her veil, demanded of the Pashas with a fierce look, whether they had ever beheld such a beauty? 'After an awful pause, Mahomet, with one hand laying hold of the young Greek by her beautiful locks, and with the other pulling out his scimitar, severed the head from the body at one stroke. Then turning to his grandees, with eyes wild and furious, “ This sword,” said he, “ when it is my will, knows to cut the bands of love.”
