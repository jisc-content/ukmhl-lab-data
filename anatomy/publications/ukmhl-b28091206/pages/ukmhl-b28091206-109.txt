LAWS OF FEMALE BEAUTY In the INTELLECTUAL SYSTEM, woman has the organs of sense proportionally larger, and more delicately outlined, and the whole nervous matter, participates in the softness and mobility of the other parts. Hence, the sensibility and intellectual quickness of woman are greater. Her im- pressions succeed with rapidity ; and the last of these generally predominates. She has, therefore, more finesse and penetra- tion than depth or force of thought. This is well adapted to her perpetual interest to observe men and her rivals ; and that practice again gives to this species of in- stinct a quickness and a certainty which the reasoning of the profoundest philoso- pher could never attain.
