Let us append to this section some ob- servations on eunuchism. The inventions of this sort have been sufficiently cruel and absurd. Such is infibulation among men, which, preventing them from indulging in love, preserves the delicacy and flexibility of their voices. This was practised by the Romans upon their players, by passing a ring of metal through the prepuce. Such also is the cincture of virginity and the attachment of parts among the African and Asiatic women.
