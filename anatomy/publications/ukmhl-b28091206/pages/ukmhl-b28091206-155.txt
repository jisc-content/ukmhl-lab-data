Thus it is impossible absolutely to de- termine any definite period of puberty, because it varies with variety of climate and of temperament. Upon the whole, how- ever, it is a little more early in the female than in the male; so that in this country, young women are said to be puberes about the fifteenth year ; young men, on the con- trary, nearly at twenty.
