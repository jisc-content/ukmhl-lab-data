The large and expanded eye, was, perhaps, the most dis- tinguishing feature of the Greeks ; and it is still to be found among their descen- dants. The women of Turkey possess con- siderable beauty. They paint the eye- brows black, as did the Greeks ; and they remove, as do also the men, the hair of the pubis by means of a depilatory of orpi- ment and lime, called rusma. This was also a practice of the Egyptians.
