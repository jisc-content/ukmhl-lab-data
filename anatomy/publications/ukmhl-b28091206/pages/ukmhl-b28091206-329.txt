275 LAWS OF FEMALE BEAUTY. We have already observed, that variety is essential to the high enjoyment of every sensual pleasure. The varied surface of the sphere in which no one point lies in the same plane with another, and, on the same account, the female mamma, are most agreeable to the sense of Touch. The Indian anana, or the honey of Hy- mettus, or any one of the most exquisite viands which the vegetable or animal world presents, if perpetually used, would pall upon the appetite, and, after nauseating and disgusting, would at best terminate in a happier insensibility; while the due succession and blending of a few such viands, would gratify the most luxurious Taste.
