This is an error. The purpose of society is to define and secure inheritance, and this it most effectually attains ; but it wages not war against nature; it treats ^neither the concubine nor the courtezan as criminal; and, if it degrade either, it is rather by the operation of a social and con- ventional, than of a moral law. Now having enquired into the founda- tion and established the justice of marri- age, let us examine into the causes of con- cubinage ; for, wherever a practice pre- vails in society, it is likely to have natural causes.
