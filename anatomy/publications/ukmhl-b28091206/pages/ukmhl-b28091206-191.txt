Cressida. Boldness comes to me now, and brings me heart :— Prince Troilus, I have loved you night and day. For many weary months. Troilus. Why was my Cressid then so hard to win ? Cressida.—Hard to seem won ; but I was won, my lord, With the first glance that ever—Pardon me— It I confess much, you will play the tyrant. I love you now ; but not, till now, so much But I might master it:—in faith, I lie ; My thoughts were like unbridled children, grown Too headstrong for their mother :—See, we fools ?
