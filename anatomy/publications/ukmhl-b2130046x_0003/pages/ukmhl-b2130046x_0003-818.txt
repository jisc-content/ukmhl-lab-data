(This work contains a confirmation and extension of Savi's statement respecting the subdivision of the primitive fibres of the nerves in the elec- trical organ of the torpedo. Wagner shows that the subdivision takes place by the breaking up of one primitive fibre into numerous filaments, from each of which a network is formed among the ele- ments of the electrical organ. He describes a some- what similar arrangement of the nerves in muscles.) (R. B. Todd.)
