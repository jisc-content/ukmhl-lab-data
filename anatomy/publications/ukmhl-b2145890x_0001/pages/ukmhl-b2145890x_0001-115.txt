In this dissection the dissector is required to exam- ine the following parts :— (i.) Superficial veins. (2.) Cutaneous nerves. (3.) Deep fascia and its intermuscular septa. (4.) Tibialis anticus. (5.) Extensor longus digitorum. (6.) Peroneus tertius. (7.) Extensor longus hallucis. (8.) Anterior tibial vessels. (9.) Anterior peroneal artery. (10.) Anterior tibial nerve. (11.) Recurrent articular branch from the external pop- liteal nerve. (12.) Extensor brevis digitorum. (13.) DorsaHs pedis artery. The limb should be placed in a convenient position for the dissection of this region. A block should be introduced beneath the knee, and the foot should be extended and fastened firmly to the table by means of hooks.
