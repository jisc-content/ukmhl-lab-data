STERNUM AND BIBS. 16 Interims, Transversalis, and Rectus Abdominis, to the ensiform cartilage. The Ribs have twenty-six muscles attached to them, of which twelve arise, twelve are inserted, and two both arise from and are inserted into them. Arise the Pectorales, Major et Minor, Serratus Magnus, Latissimus Dorsi, Accessorii ad Sacro Lumbalem, Cervicalis Ascendens, Obliquus Externus Abdominis, Transversalis Abdominis, Diaphragma, Subclavius, Sterno Hyoideus, Sterno Thyroideus : Inserted the Obliquus Internus Abdominis, Rectus Abdominis, Sterno Costalis, Scaleni, Anticus, Medius, et Posticus, Serrati Postici, Superior, et Inferior, Sacro Lumbalis, Longissimus Dorsi, Levatores Costarum, Quadratus Lumborum. The Inter-costales Externi et Interni both arise from and are inserted into the edges of the ribs.
