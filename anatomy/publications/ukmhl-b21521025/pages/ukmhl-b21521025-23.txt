3 BONES OP THE HEAD AND PACE. Digastricus, Stylo Hyoideus, Stylo Glossus, Stylo Pharyngeus, Levator Palati, Constrictor Pharyngis Superior, Stapedius, Tensor Tympani: Inserted the Sterno Cleido Mastoideus, Splenius Capitis, Trachleo Mastoideus. The Temporalis is attached to the squamous portion of the bone; the Attrahens Aurem and Mas- seter to the zygoma; the Occipito-frontalis, Sterno- mastoideus, Splenius Capitis, and Trachleo-mas- toideus, to the mastoid process; the Digastricus to the digastric fossa; the three Styloid Muscles to the styloid process; the Superior Constrictor of the Pharynx, Tensor Tympani, and Levator Palati, to the petrous bone; and the Stapedius, within the Tympanum, to the pyramid.
