MUSCLES ON THE POSTERIOR PART OF THE LEG, SEVEN. These are arranged into a superficial and a deep layer. The superficial layer has three muscles. 1. Gastrocnemius, 2. Plantaris, 3. Solseus. The deep layer has four muscles. 1. Poplitseus, 2. Flexor Digitorum Longus vel Perforans, 3. Tibialis Posticus, 4. Flexor Pollicis Longus.
