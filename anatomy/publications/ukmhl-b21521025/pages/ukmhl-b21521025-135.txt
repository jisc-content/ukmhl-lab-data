115 ACCORDING TO THEIR ACTIONS. MUSCLES WHICH ATTACH THE SCAPULA TO THE TRUNK, SEVEN DIRECTLY, THREE INDIRECTLY. Directly,—seven. Trapezius, Rhomboidei, Major et Minor, Levator Anguli Scapulae, Omo-hyoideus, Serratus Magnus, Pectoralis Minor. Indirectly,—three. Subclavius, Pectoralis Major, Latissimus Dorsi. MUSCLES OP THE SHOULDER JOINT,—NINE. Pectoralis Major, Latissimus Dorsi, Teres Major, Deltoides, Supra-spinatus, Infra-spinatus, Subscapularis, Teres Major, Coraco-brachialis.
