26 INFERIOR EXTREMITY. The third Phalanges of the four fingers have two muscles inserted, the Flexor Digitorum Profundus, Extensor Digitorum Communis. INFERIOR EXTREMITY. The Femur has twenty muscles attached to it; of which seven arise, thirteen are inserted. Arise the Vasti, Externus et Interims, Crurseus, Biceps Flexor Crusis, (short head.) Gastrocnemius, Plantaris, Poplitaeus : Inserted the Glutaei, Maximus, Medius, et Minimus, Pyriformis, Gemelli, Superior et Inferior, Obturatores, Externus et Internus, Quadratus Femoris, Iliacus Internus, Psoas Magnus, Pectinseus, Triceps Adductor Femoris, The Glutseus Maximus is attached below the great trochanter and to the upper part of the outer lip
