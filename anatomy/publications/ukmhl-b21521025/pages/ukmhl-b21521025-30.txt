VERTEBRiE. It) Complexus, Recti Capitis Postici, Major et Minor, Obliquus Capitis Superior, Levatores Costarum : Inserted the Cervicalis Ascendens, Quadratus Lumborum : Both arise from and inserted into the Vertebral Column, the Longus Colli, Longissimus Dorsi, Spinalis Dorsi, Splenius Colli, Transversalis Colli, Semi Spinalis Colli, Semi Spinalis Dorsi, Obliquus Capitis Inferior, Multifidi Spinae, Inter-transversales Colli, Dorsi, et Lumborum, Inter-spinales Colli, Dorsi, et Lumborum.
