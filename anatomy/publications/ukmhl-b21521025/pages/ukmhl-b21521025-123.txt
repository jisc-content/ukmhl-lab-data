The Dorsal, or superior set, are the largest; they are four in number. The first arises from the first and second metatarsal bones; it is inserted into the inner side of the first phalanx, and into the extensor tendons of the second toe. The second muscle arises from the second and third metatarsal bones; it is inserted into the outer side of the first phalanx, and into the extensor tendons of the second toe. The third muscle arises from the third and fourth meta- tarsal bones; it is inserted into the outer side of the first phalanx, and also into the extensor tendons of the third toe. The fourth muscle arises from the fourth and fifth metatarsal bones; it is inserted into the outer side of the first phalanx, and into the extensor tendons of the fourth toe.
