Such changes must have exerted their influence upon the heart; unfortunately they are not explained in Pirogoff’s text. The change in position of the right lung was probably brought about by the organisation of the pleuritic effusion, especially noticeable in the sinking-in of the left half of the thorax, as seen at about ■ the section of the second rib.
