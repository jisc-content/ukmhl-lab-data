70 Bones of the Trunk. 00. The First Rib, superior view. The first rib shows an upper and an under surface, an outer and an inner border. It has a round head, Capttuhim, with a single articular facet, when the first dorsal vertebra alone forms the articular fossa; when the articular fossa is formed by the seventh cervical and first dorsal vertebrae, the articular facet of the head is also a double one. At the inner border there is a tubercle, the Tuberculwm IAsfrancii, which is not always very well developed.
