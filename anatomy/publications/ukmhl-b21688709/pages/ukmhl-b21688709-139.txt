Ill Bones of the Lower Extremity. 152. The Right Hip Bone, Os innominatum, outer surface, with the insertions of the muscles. At the place where the ilium, ischium and pubes meet, the acetabulum is situated (Fig. 150); its rim, the Supercilium acetabuli, is interrupted by a notch, the Incisura acetabuli. The bottom of the cavity of the acetabulum has a rough depression, free of cartilage, the Fossa acetabuli, which is continuous with the incisura.
