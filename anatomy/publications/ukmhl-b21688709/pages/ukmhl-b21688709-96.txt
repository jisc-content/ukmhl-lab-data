V 68 Bones of the Trunk. 88. The Bony Portion of a True Rib. Each rib consists of a bony portion and a costal cartilage; the bony portion has an external convex and an internal concave surface, a superior rounded border, and an inferior border containing a groove, Sulcus costalis, which deepens especially toward the vertebral extremity of the rib; the part where the outer edge of the groove is highest, is called Crista costae.
