The Rig lit Milk Teeth, Denies lactei s. caduci The eruption of the temporary or milk teeth commences in the sixth or seventh month after birth; at the end of the second year of life the child has twenty teeth, ten in each jaw, namely, four incisors, two canines, and four molars. In the seventh year the milk teeth begin to fall out and make room for the permanent set. 337. The Teeth of a Child with the Calcified Parts of the Permanent Teeth Exposed.
