132 Knee-Joint. 182. The Knee-Joint, Articulatio genu, in section. The knee-joint was sawed through, after injection of a hardening mass through an aperture made in the patella; after the sawing the mass used for injection was removed. In this way the sac-like diverticula of the synovial capsule become visible, and it can be seen how high up the insertion of the synovial capsule on the anterior and posterior side of the knee-joint reaches.
