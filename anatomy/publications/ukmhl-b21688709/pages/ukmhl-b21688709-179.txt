Orig.: External oblique line of the lower jaw. Insert.: Lower lip and integument of the chin. M. levator menti (Levator labii inferioris); runs from the incisive fossa of the lower jaw down to the integument of the chin. MM. incisivi Cowperi; two upper and two lower, arise from the alveolar processes of the incisor teeth (not illustrated). M. buccinator. Orig.: Alveolar processes of both maxillary bones and Hamulus pterygoid, of the sphenoid bone. Insert.: Into the upper and lower lips, the central fibres intersecting each other.
