From the Art. dorsalis pedis arise (see Fig. 581): a) The tarsal, Art. tarsea, to the outer border of the foot, where it anastomoses with the external anterior malleolar and the metatarsal arteries; b) The metatarsal, Art. meta- tarsca, arises further down either alone or together with the tarsal artery, and forms with it, at the outer border of the foot, an arch, the Arcus pedis dor- salis.
