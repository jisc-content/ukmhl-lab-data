Ligaments of the Spine. 64 83. Ligaments between Sacrum and Coccyx. Between the apex of the sacrum and the first piece of the coccyx there is a disc of fibro-cartilage; also between the separate pieces of the coccyx. The strenghtening ligaments are the anterior, posterior and lateral Ligamenta sacro-coccygea. The Ligamentum sacro-coccygcum posttrius lies between the sacrum and cornua of the coccyx and closes the Hiatus sacro-coccygeus.
