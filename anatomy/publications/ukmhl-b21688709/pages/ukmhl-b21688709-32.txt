Bones of the Cranium. 4 2. Occipital Bone, Os OCcipitis, from behind. 2. Occipital portion with the external occipital protuberance, Protuberantia occipitalis externa, from which runs the external occipital crest, Crista occipitalis externa, to the Foramen magnum; transversely run both Lineae semicirculares externae, i. e. the superior and inferior curved lines (for the insertion of muscles and ligaments). The borders are the superior, Margo lambdoideus, for articulation with both parietal bones, and the inferior, Margo mastoideus, for articulation with the mastoid portion of the temporal bone.
