Ligaments of the Spine. 60 78. The Posterior Obturator Membrane, Membrana obturatoria posterior. The space between the posterior arch of the atlas and the posterior border of the Foramen occipitale magnum is closed by the Membrana obturatoria posterior; this membrane is weaker than the anterior obturator membrane, and is perforated on both sides at its outer border by the Arteria vertebralis, which fills out the space shown in the figure, runnig upwards from the Foramen transversa riuiti of the atlas to the Foramen occipitale. magnum.
