72 Bones of the Trunk 92. The Eleventh and Twelfth Ribs, Costae fiuctuantes. The eleventh and twelfth ribs end free and are of all the ribs the two most moveable; they have a round head with a single articular "facet. The tubercle and neck are wanting in both, the rough line for- ming the Angulus costae only slightly marked. Their cartilages are very short, round and pointed. The twelfth rib is the shortest of all.
