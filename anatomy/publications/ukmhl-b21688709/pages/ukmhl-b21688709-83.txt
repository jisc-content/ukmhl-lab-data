Bones of the Trunk. 55 70. The Coccyx, Os coccygis, 71. The Coccyx, Os coccygis, anterior view. posterior view. The coccyx consists of 4 (very seldom 5) pieces of bone, rudi- ments of the vertebral bodies. The first piece has the Comma coccygea, rudimentary ascending articular processes; also rudimentary transverse processes. Between the cornua is the place of articulation of the coccyx with the apex of the sacrum.
