Female Organs of Generation. THE external Organs of Generation in the female, are the mons Veneris, the labia majora and minora, the clitoris, the meatus urinarius, and the orifice of the vagina. The term ‘ vulva ’ or ‘ pudendum,’ as generally applied, includes all these parts. The vions Veneris is the rounded eminence in front of the pubes, formed by a collection of fatty tissue beneath the integument.
