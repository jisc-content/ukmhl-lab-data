The posterior surface of the suprarenal capsules is in each divided into two parts, by a fairly prominent ridge. The outer and lower portion is in contact with the kidneys, and this is due to the fact that the capsule on each side rests rather on the anterior surface than on the border of the kidney. The remaining portion of this surface is in contact with the corresponding crus of the diaphragm. There is usually a considerable amount of fat surrounding the capsules. 2.
