802 ANATOMY. THE TESTES AND SPERMATIC CORDS. The Testes or Testicles are two glands situated in the Scrotum, and the Spermatic Cord is the collection of vessels, nerves, and the excretory duct connected with each testis. As the testicle and the cord in the scrotum are invested by several structures, technically called their coverings, these will first be described.
