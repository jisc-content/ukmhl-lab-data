THE NINTH PAIR. The nerves which we shall consider in this chapter are purely motor in their function; and, on this account, are conveniently classed together. We shall see, however, that from their occa- sional anastomosis with sensitive nerves, they contain sentient fila- ments which serve to inform the mind of the state of the muscles to which their motor filaments are distributed.
