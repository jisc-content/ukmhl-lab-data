Insertion : Into the Transverse Process of the first Cervical Vertebra. Action: To roll the Head. Obliquus Capitis Superior, Vel Atloido-suh-mastoideus. Origin: From the Transverse Process of the first Cervical Vertebra, passing upwards and ^ little in- wards. Insertion : Into the Occipital Bone, at the outer part of the Insertion of the Rectus Major. Action : To assist in drawing the Head backwards, and a little to one side.
