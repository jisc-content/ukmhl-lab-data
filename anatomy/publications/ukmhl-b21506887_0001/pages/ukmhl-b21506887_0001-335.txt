Origin : Fleshy, from the middle of the posterior parts of the Ulna, Radius, and Interosseous Ligament. It runs obliquely over the Radius, sending one, or more frequently two Tendons, through an Annular Sheath. Insertion : Into the Os Trapezium, and upper and back part of the Metacarpal Bone of the Thumb. Action : To extend the Metacarpal Bone of the Thumb, and draw it from the Fingers.
