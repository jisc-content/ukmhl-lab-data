The Situation of the Bone, at the under and outer part of the Pelvis. The oblique situation of the Bod?/ of the Bone; the under end being considerably nearer than the upper one to its fellow, which is favourable for the passages at the bottom of the Pelvis, for the origin of Muscles, and for walking.
