The Fore-arm is bent by the Biceps Flexor Cubiti and Brachialis Internus, the following Muscles, on ac- count of their attachments to the under end of the Os Humeri, assisting, though only in a small degree, viz. Palmaris Longus, Flexor Carpi Radialis, Flexor Carpi Ulnaris, Flexor Digitorum Sublimis, Supinator Radii Longus, et Pronator Radii Teres;—contended, by the Triceps Extensor Cubiti, and Anconeus.
