From this attach- ment the fascia descends as far as a line drawn from the spine of the ischium to the symphysis pubis. Along this line, which corre- sponds with the origin of some of the fibres of the levator ani, the fascia divides into two layers, an inner, called the recto-vesical fascia-, an outer, called the obturator fascia.
