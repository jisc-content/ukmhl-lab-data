This state continues for a considerable length of time, and is very little relieved by medicine ; which may serve as some ground of distinction between this complaint and a temporary deranged action of the stomach. Cancer of the stomach is attended with a sense of pain in that organ, which varies a good deal in its degree in different indi- viduals. What is swallowed is often rejected by vomiting, and there is frequently thrown up also a dark coloured fluid, which has sometimes been compared to coffee grounds. The patient commonly becomes emaciated, and the countenance sallow ; the pulse is frequent, and hectic symptoms are formed.
