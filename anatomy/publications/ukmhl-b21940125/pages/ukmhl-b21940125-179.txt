SYMPTOMS. The symptoms which belong to ascites are almost too well known to require being mentioned. There is a distinct feeling of fluctuation upon applying one hand to the belly, and striking it with the other; the urine is in small quantity, and of a deep colour; there is thirst, often some feeling of heat, and a pulse more frequent than in health.
