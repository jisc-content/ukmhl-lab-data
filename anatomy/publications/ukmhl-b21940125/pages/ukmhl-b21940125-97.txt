When the pleura is inflamed which covers the lungs, the substance ot the latter is frequently in- flamed to some depth. Adhesions in the Cavity of the Thorax. Adhesions are often found between that portion of the pleura which covers the lungs, and that other portion of it which lines the ribs, the intercostal spaces, and the convex surface of the diaphragm, while there is no sign whatever of present inflammation.
