It is a very common appearance, upon dissection, to find the gall-bladder connect- ed by adhesions, either to the small end of the stomach, or the beginning of the duo- denum. These are the consequence of a previous inflammation in the outer coat of the gall-bladder, and resemble exactly the adhesions which we have already described. Ulcers in the Gall-bladder.
