C 326 : One of the Vesicula Seminales wanting. One of the vesiculse seminales is occa- sionally wanting altogether. Under such circumstances I believe that the extremity of the vas deferens upon that side is ge- nerally enlarged and tortuous, becoming a sort of substitute for it. This was at least the case in the instance which I have seen of this mode of formation. The ex- tremity of the vas deferens has at all times a structure similar to that of the vesiculae seminales, and renders therefore this con- jecture very probable. Vesiculcc Seminales schirrous.
