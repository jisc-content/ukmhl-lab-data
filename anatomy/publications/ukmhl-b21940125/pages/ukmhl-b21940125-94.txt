This may take place at any period of life, but it is more frequent at the age when the body is just arrived at the adult state, and all its actions are carried on with vigour, than either in childhood or in advanced age. The pleura appears to be more liable to inflammation than any mem- brane lining those cavities which have no external opening ; as the peritonaeum, the tunica vaginalis testis, and some others.
