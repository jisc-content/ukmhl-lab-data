[ 1*1 3 CHAP. VI. DISEASED APPEARANCES WITHIN THE CAVITY OF THE ABDOMEN. Ascites. Ascites, or dropsy of the cavity of the ab- domen, is a very frequent disease, and is not confined to any sex or age. I have seen several instances of it in children under ten years old ; but it is much more common at the middle, and the more advanced periods of life. It is also more common in the male than the female sex.
