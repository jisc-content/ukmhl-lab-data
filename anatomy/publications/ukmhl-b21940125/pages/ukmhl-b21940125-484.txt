Bony Ridges irritating the Brain. Upon the inner surface of the basis of the cranium there is always some irregu- larity. This consists in numerous ridges and small eminences with depressed sur- faces interposed between them. It happens occasionally that there is a morbid growth of these eminences and ridges, forming sharp spicula and sharp edges of bone.
