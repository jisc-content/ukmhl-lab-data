Ossification of the Pleura. It sometimes happens, although I believe rarely, that a portion of the pleura is con- verted into bone. This consists of a thin plate, and sometimes extends over a pretty broad surface of the pleura. In all the cases which I have seen, the bony matter seemed to me to be exactly like ordinary bone. I have never seen it form a thick irregular knob, but always a thin plate.
