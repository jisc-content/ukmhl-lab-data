C 456 : In cases where the veins of the pia mater have been found turgid with blood, stupor has very frequently occurred, sometimes delirium, and sometimes, it has been said, even apoplexy in its perfect form. The symptoms of inflammation in the pia, mater, are the same with those which attend inflammation of the dura mater, and they have been already noticed. In inflammation of the substance of the brain, there is pain in the head, delirium, symptomatic fever, and sometimes coma. Where an abscess has been formed in the brain; pain, delirium, and coma, have been remarked, sometimes a paralysis of a part of the body, and sometimes convulsions.
