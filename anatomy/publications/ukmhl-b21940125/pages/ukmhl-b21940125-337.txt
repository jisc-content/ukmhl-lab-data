Diabetes is distinguished by the urine be- ing much increased beyond the natural quantity, by its being more or less sweet to the taste, and of a colour somewhat re- sembling whey. There is great thirst, and a voracious appetite for food. The pulse is also somewhat quicker than natural, and the body becomes at length much ema- ciated.
