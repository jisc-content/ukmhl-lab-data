The inferior phrenic branches of the abdominal aorta are two in number and are distributed to the diaphragm, the sides of the thorax and the suprarenal capsule. The cCELIAC AXIS springs from the aorta between the two crura of the diaphragm. It gives off three principal branches viz., the gastric, the hepatic and the splenic.
