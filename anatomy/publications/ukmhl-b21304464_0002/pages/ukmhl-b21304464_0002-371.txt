Voluptuous ideas determine the blood to the penis, and produce erection : Anger impels the blood into the smaller Arteries, and produces red- ness of the face. Excessive grief or joy promotes the flow of tears, and impels the blood into the finer vessels of the skin. An unusual flow of blood to a part increases Its sensibility ; as in an inflamed eye, or when the penis or clitoris are erected. Inflammation renders the sense of touch more acute. . Grief impedes digestion, and diminishes the secretion of the Gastric Juice : On the other hand, a loaded Stomach blunts sensibility, and produces sleep. Fear also augments the secretion of the mucus of the Intestines, and causes Diarrhoea. The influence of the Nerves upon the Arteries is evident. By throwing a ligature upon a Nerve, the quantity of the fluid secreted by the gland is diminished. . Nuck found, that the division of the Nerves leading to the Salivary Glands diminished the u- sual quantity of Saliva.
