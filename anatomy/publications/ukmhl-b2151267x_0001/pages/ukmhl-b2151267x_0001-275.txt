See also in Series 50, Monsters. {a) Supernumerary Fingers and Toes. 9.1. Supernumerary Finger. Hunterian. MM. 35. "The forearm and hand from a child at birth. There are six fingers instead of five, the sixth one growing out of the little one." The specimen shows a fairly common type of this condition, viz. a small finger, consisting of a terminal phalanx and nail, and part of a second phalanx, attached to the outside of the first phalanx of the little finger. 1-4 5-9 11-18
