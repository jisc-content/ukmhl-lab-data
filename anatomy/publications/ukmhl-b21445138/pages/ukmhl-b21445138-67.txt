The Transversus Perinei—Arises from the tough fatty membrane that covers the tuber ischii, immediately behind the attachment o£ the erector penis; thence its fibres run transversely inwards. Inserted into the central point of union where the sphincter ani touches the accelerator urinae, and where a kind of tendinous projection is formed, common to the five muscles. Use: To dilate the bulb of the urethra, to prevent the anus from being too much protruded, and to re- tract it when protruded.
