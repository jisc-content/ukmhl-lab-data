The fourth sesamoid is found near the outer end of the cuboid tuberosity in the tendon of the peroneus longus, and frequently a fifth is present at the inner end of the head of the astragalus, and a sixth is occasionally found on the inner surface of the internal cuneiform. But instead of the sesamoid bones only fibro-cartilage may be present.
