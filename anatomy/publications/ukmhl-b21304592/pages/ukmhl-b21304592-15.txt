PREFACE. The following Work is intended chiefly for the use of those, who are attending Lectures on Anatomy, or who have access, in their private studies, to Anatomical Preparations. I could wish to have given it a more popular form ; and to have endeavoured, by the aid of a series of Engravings, to have rendered it, in some degree, intelligible to the general reader. But the experience of the last four years has fully satisfied me, that, in this part of the island at least, it could not have been executed according to such a plan, without the most inconvenient delay, and without an expence, which, were the work otherwise calcu- lated to be useful, would have deprived it of half its utility.
