The Delivery of the Course of Anatomy in the University having been divided between its two Professors, the following Lecture was Introductory to the General Anatomy which forms part of the division assigned to the Author. He deems it not unnecessary to apprize the reader, that he is fully sensible of the insufficient manner in which so important a subject has been treated in the following pages. He trusts, therefore, for an apology in the circumstance that it was not intended for publication, and that its appearance now has been permitted solely to gratify the expressed wishes of the Students who were the auditors.
