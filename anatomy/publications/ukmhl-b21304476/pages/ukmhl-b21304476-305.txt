The fleshy fibres terminate in a tendon which passes through a groove in the outer edge of the lower extremity of the radius. Inserted, generally by two tendons, into the os trapezium, and into the upper and back part of the metacarpal bone of the thumb. Use: To extend the metacarpal bone of the thumb outwardly.
