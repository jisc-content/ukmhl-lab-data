227 OTIC GANGLION. Small oval shaped flattened body of a reddish grey colour situated on inner surface of inferior maxillary nerve & around origin of its internal pterygoid branch, just below foramen ovale, close to tensor tympani muscle & cartilaginous portion of Eusta- chian tube which lie on its inner side, and to middle meningeal artery which lies behind. It presents: THREE ROOTS: Sensory Root - From auriculo-temporal nerve, and also from glosso- pharyngeal through small petrosal nerve. Motor Root - From inferior maxillary nerve & its internal pterygoid branch ; also from facial nerve through small petrosal. Sympathetic Root - From plexus on middle meningeal artery. " BRS.
