Such processes as may be followed either with the naked eye, or with relatively low powers, and may therefore be grouped together under the above heading, are alone introduced into this Edition of the Tablets. The macroscopical outlines submitted may not be without some advantages, which fuller descriptions would not present. They may better disengage the leading points, and thus allow the reader to follow more easily what one may perhaps call the thread of the subject. When once main lines are well grasped, details are easily filled in.
