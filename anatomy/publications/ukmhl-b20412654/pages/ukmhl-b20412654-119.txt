107 PUDIO NERVE. From lower part of sacral plexus. With pudic artery through lower part of great sacro-sciatio foramen on inner side of great sciatic nerve. Winds round spine of ischium, and re-enters pelvis through lesser sacro-sciatic foramen, where it gives off inferior hemorrhoidal nerve. Forwards along outer wall of ischio-rectal fossa above pudic artery, both nerve & artery being covered by obturator fascia, and divides into perineal nerve & dorsal nerve of the peni*
