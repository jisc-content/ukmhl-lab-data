It is, therefore, by the constant presence of one of the teachers, and by the direc- tions which will be given as to the order of the studies, that I intend to obviate the waste of time and labour. The advantages of observing a certain order in the dissections will be explained to the pupils, and will, I hope, be made so clear to them, that they will feel no temptation to depart from it.
