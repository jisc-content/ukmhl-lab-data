To recapitulate in a few words, I propose to economize the time of the student, by directing the order of his studies; to econo- mize it also, and to fix his attention to the proper objects, by the constant presence and superintendance of one of the teachers in the dissecting room; to root the acquire- ments more firmly in his mind by frequent examinations, and to stimulate him, if he need it, by special honorary rewards.
