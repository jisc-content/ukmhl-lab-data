INTRODUCTORY LECTURE ON ANATOMY, &c. Delivered at the New Medical Schooly October 2d, 1826. Gentlemen, As the teacher of Anatomy in this School, I have the honour of being the first to ad- dress you; and as this is the commencement of a new undertaking, I shall, before I pro- ceed with the particular objects of my own department, endeavour to explain to you the reasons for forming this School, and the plans that will be adopted for the be- nefit of those gentlemen who come to us for instruction.
