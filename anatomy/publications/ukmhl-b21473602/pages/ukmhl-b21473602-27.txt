It may be here also proper to state our intention of keeping open our rooms for dissections during the summer, although we are assured, by the illustrious promoters and encouragers of sound chirurgical know- ledge, that anatomy cannot be learnt at that period of the year. Our winter pupils will, of course, have the advantage of such an arrangement. The intimate connexion between Ana- tomy and Physiology, renders it necessary for me to say a few words respecting the latter science. It treats of the various func- tions and uses of the different textures, structures, organs, &c. which it is the object of Anatomy to examine.
