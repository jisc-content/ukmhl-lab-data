At the different Vertebrae, they are joined by cross Branches, which have a semilunar form, like the Sur- face of the Bones which surround them. NERVES OF THE NECK AND SUPERIOR EXTREMITY. Nervus Accessorius. The Accessory Nerve belongs in some respect to this Class of Nerves; but having part of its origin within the Head, and from its passing out with one of the Cerebral Nerves, it has been already described along with these.
