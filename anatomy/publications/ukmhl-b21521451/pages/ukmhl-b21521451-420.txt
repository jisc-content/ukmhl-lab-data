PULMONARY VEINS. 412 pulmonary veins. there are three pulmonary veins on the right side. The right pul- monary veins pass behind the right auricle to the left auricle ; the left pass in front of the descending aorta ; they both pierce the pericardium. Within the lung the branches of the pulmonary veins are behind the bronchial tubes, and those of the pulmonary artery in front; but at the root of the lungs the veins are in front, next the arteries, and then the bronchi.
