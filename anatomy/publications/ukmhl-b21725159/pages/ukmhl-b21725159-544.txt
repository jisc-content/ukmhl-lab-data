V, is upon the great root of the fifth with the ganglion in front ; a and b, in connection with VII, indicate respectively the facial and auditory nerves ; a, b, and c, in connection with VIII, point respectively to the glosso- pharyngeal, pneumo-gastric, and spinal accessory nerves ; C I, the suboecipital or first cervical nerve ; C VIII, the eighth.
