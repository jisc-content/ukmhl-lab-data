134 POISONOUS SNAKES OF CEYLON. I can offer no decided opinion. Oil seems to have been useful, both applied externally, and taken internally, in many instances of the bite of the viper ; and arsenic seems to have done good in some instances of the bite of the hooded-snake. Eau de luce does not appear to have deserved the high character that was first given to it, indeed I am not aware of any satisfactory proof of its ever having been beneficial.
