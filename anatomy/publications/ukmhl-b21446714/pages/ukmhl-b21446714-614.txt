Flat Foot, from Injury.—Plaster of Paris cast of a, right foot, illustrating the above. The patient, a healthy man of 28 years, stated that when 10 years- of age he fell from a height of twenty feet, and landed on his feet. For nine months after the accident he had been a patient in the Dundee- Infirmary, and for nine months more he had been unable to walk properly. After that, however, he quite recovered the use of his feet, although they remained deformed. He was under treatment in the Royal Infirmaiy,. Edinburgh, for fracture of the pelvis when this cast was taken. G. C. 2771. Presented by Charles W. Cathcart, F.R.C.S.E., 1887. 11. 42.
