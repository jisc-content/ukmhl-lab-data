105 Part I.] OF MAMMALIA. The Vessels and Nerves of the Ear are in propor- tion to the parts on which they are dispersed. They do not differ materially from those in a Human Body. In Mammalia, as well as in man, the Portio Mollis passes, with the Portio Dura, into the Meatus Internus, and enters the Labyrinth by several Holes, the number and size of which vary in different Af nimals.
