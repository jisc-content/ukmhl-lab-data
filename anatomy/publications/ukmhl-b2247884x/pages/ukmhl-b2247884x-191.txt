This view of the danger will convince every thinking man of the duty of consenting at once to do all that may be required of him without running away, or, if he has run away, before he is allowed to come back. Those who run away should go to single houses, or to tents pitched in the fields; and if those who stay will avoid intercourse with the sick, and submit to, and encourage all the use- ful measures which the public safety demands, the number of deaths will soon become very small indeed.
