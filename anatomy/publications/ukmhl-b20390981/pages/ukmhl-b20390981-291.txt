281 MUSCLES MOVING THE THUMB. The movements of the first metacarpal bone are so closely associated with those of the phalanges whicli it carries, that it is profitable to consider its special muscles in the group with those of the thumb. The longer name of the meta- carpal flexor is preferred as indicating its action as well as " opponens pollicis," and at the same time expressing much more clearly the antagonism between it and the metacarpal extensor.
