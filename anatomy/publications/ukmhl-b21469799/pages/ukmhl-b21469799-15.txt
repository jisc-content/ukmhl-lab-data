The priesthood having many features of resemblance with the professors of the healing art, exercising equally an influence over the imagination in proportion to its weakness, and employing the same springs, hope and fear, did not fail to usurp the empire of me- dicine, and to arrogate to itself the exclusive privilege of practising Physic and Surgery.
