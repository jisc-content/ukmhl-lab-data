And there are others who have conceived, and successfully performed operations, from the boldness of which, our ancestors would have shrunk back in dismay. If at any former period Surgeons did not uphold the dignity of their profession, and allowed themselves to be under the control of Physicians, that is not likely to happen again. The Physicians of the present day are too enlightened, and too liberal, to wish to exer- cise any such superiority over Surgeons.
