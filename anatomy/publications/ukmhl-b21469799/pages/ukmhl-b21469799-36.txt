And you will not fail to be struck with a singular resemblance, in the early part of his career, to that of Ambrose Pare. Like Pare, he was in the outset of life an army surgeon, and possessing, like him, a similar mind for observation and for reflection on the powers of nature, he converted every hint presented by accidental circumstance, to useful purpose.
