A description of the arteries. 63 Meningeal Branch. Somewhere in the space between the second of the Cervical Vertebrae, and where it enters the cavi- of the Fascia, adheres closely to the convex aspect of the Dura Mater, but is loosely connected with the central aspect of the Vertebral canal. Weitbrecht, offended that two parts so different in appearance, structure, and function, should be confounded, and nature herself misshapen and distorted, to suit the mistaken ideas of Anatomists, denies the existence of such a Tuyau Ligamenteux.
