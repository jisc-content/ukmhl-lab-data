Irregular Contraction of the Colon. Hunterian. H. 13. " A portion of the colon arrested by death in its peristaltic motion. At two places it is seen contracted almost without cavity, and at two other parts it is seen distended to much more than thrice the size of the contracted part." It was filled with spirit and hardened. Shows the relative size of the gut in the contracted and in the relaxed condition. 35.67. The Mucous Membrane of the Colon. Hunterian. R. 12.
