This last circumstance, however, is very rare. The state in which these productions have most generally been found, is that in which they were ossified in the centre, and cartilaginous in the circumference. I have met with one in the articulations of the os pisiforme with the os pyramidale, the size of the head of a large pin, and the whole of which was harder than ivory.
