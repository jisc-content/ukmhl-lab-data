Part IV.] OF THE VISCERA, &c. OF THE ORGANS OF URINE AND GENE- RATION IN THE MALE. KIDNEYS. The Kidneys are two Glandular Bodies of a pale Ted colour, situated in the upper and back part of the Abdomen, in the Lumbar Region. They are placed one on each side of the Spine, ex- tending from the eleventh pair of Ribs to near the Crista of the Ossa Ilia, and rest upon the Diaphragm, large Psose, Quadrati Lumborum, and Transversales Abdominis. The Right Kidney is situated at the under and back part of the large Lobe of the Liver, behind the Colon, and is commonly a very little lower than the Left, be- ing supposed to be affected by the Great Lobe of the Liver.
