This part of the Gland is also taken notice of in the works of Mor- GAGNI. The Prostate has a red Fleshy appearance, and is of a Spongy Substance, but is one of the firmest Glands of the Body. It generally sends out Ten or Twelve Ducts, which open obliquely near the beginning of the Urethra, at the sides of the Caput Gallinaginis; the Orifices of these Ducts surrounding the terminations of the Seminal Canals.
