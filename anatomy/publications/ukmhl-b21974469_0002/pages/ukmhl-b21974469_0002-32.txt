My thanks are also due to many professional friends who, in past years, have kindly invited me to assist them in their examinations of the dead body, or to perform the inspection for them. I shall always gratefully avail myself of such means of extending my acquaintance with morbid anatomy, and shall thankfully accept any specimens of diseased structure. I exceedingly regret that my announcements relative to the timp of completing the work, were not strictly complied with ; but the delay of publication has arisen from a variety of circumstances altogether uncontrollable. w. c, 67, Great Prescot-Street, June 20, 1822.
