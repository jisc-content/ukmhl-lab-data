SECTION III. DISSECTION OF PARTS ABOUT THE PELVIS AND LOWER EXTREMITY. Before the dissection of the parts situat- ed within and about the pelvis, it is proper to take a review of its bony structure. Without an accurate knowledge of its form, dimensions, and relative position, as well as the situation and size of its openings, no just idea can be formed of the relative position of the organs contained within, of the manner in which they are protected from external injury, or the modes of ac- cess to them.
