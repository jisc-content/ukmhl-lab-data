513 GENERAL PATHOLOGY. Presented by Sir James Paget, Bart. 3382. The halves of a Calcareous Body, removed from a cyst in the ham. It has a laminated structure. Fragments of it treated with weak acid show under the microscope a granular organic structure like fibrin. The cyst, which occurred in a man, aged 48 years, lay along the tendon of the biceps at the outer margin of the ham, and contained half an ounce of serum. The patient had had no necrosis or abscess, and there was no large vein in the neighbourhood of the cyst.
