The present Catalogue contains a description of the Spe- cimens in the Museum formerly described in the ' Cata- logue of the Contents of the Museum, Part V. compre- hending the Preparations of Monsters and Malformed Parts, 1831,' as well as of the Specimens of a similar nature which have been added to the Collection since the publication of that work. The whole have been rearranged and redescribed by Mr. Lowne, to whom this duty has been entrusted by the Council of the College. W. H. FLOWER, Conservator. October 1st, 1872.
