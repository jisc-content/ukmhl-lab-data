Those which fly metamorphose, or pass through cer- tain changes in their Body, before they become com- plete Insects. They breathe by means of Tracheae, but have no Cellular Lungs. They have a Nervous System and Blood-vessels, but no Heart. They are divided into the following orders : 1. Coleoptera.—Having a hollow horny Case, under which the Wings are folded.
