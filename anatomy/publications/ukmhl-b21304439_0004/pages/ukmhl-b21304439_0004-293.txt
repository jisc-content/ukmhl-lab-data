Among those of the Reptile tribe are to be observed, the Spongy Ball upon the Thumb, and part of the Palm, of the Frog kind. They are composed of hard Papillae. By means of these, the Male fixes firmly upon the Back of the Female, which he squeezes when in season, and conti- nues there for days, or even weeks, in the water, till the Semen is ejected, which takes place in both sexes about the same time; the two fluids mixing together without any copulation.
